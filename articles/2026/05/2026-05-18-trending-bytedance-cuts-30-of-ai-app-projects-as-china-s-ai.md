# Trending ByteDance Cuts 30% of AI App Projects as China's AI Layer Enters "Self-Correction Cycle"

An internal ByteDance update revealed the company cut roughly 30% of its AI application projects, explicitly abandoning its "spray-and-pray" product strategy. Tencent is simultaneously rotating capital toward fewer, deeper AI bets. Forbes contextualizes this as China's AI application layer entering a structural reset — moving from breadth to focused vertical execution, paralleling Western consolidation but driven by distinct unit-economics constraints and mounting regulatory pressure.
