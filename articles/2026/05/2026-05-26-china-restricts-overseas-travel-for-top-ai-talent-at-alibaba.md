# China restricts overseas travel for top AI talent at Alibaba and DeepSeek

Chinese authorities are imposing travel restrictions on personnel doing advanced AI work at private firms deemed strategically important, notably Alibaba and DeepSeek. Affected employees must now obtain government approval before traveling abroad. The move reflects Beijing's broader push to shield its AI industry from data leakage, technology loss, and foreign poaching.
