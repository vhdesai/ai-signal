# Greg Brockman testifies his OpenAI stake is worth close to $30B

OpenAI President Greg Brockman testified in the Musk trial that his stake in OpenAI is worth close to $30B, in response to questioning from Musk's lawyer. Brockman acknowledged he had not put any of his own money into OpenAI in the early days. The testimony lands as OpenAI's $122B latest funding round values the company at $852B post-money.
