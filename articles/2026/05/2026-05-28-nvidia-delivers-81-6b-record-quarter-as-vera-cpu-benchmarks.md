# NVIDIA delivers $81.6B record quarter as Vera CPU benchmarks debut

NVIDIA reported record Q1 FY27 revenue of $81.6B (up 20% sequentially, 85% year-over-year). Phoronix's first independent Vera CPU benchmarks this week confirmed substantial leadership over x86 incumbents on agentic AI workloads. Jensen Huang's recent appearances continue to project demand as "utterly parabolic," reinforcing the company's $1T outlook through 2027.
