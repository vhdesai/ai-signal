# OpenAI expands ChatGPT advertising toward smaller marketers

The Information reports that OpenAI is moving beyond large-brand launch partners and offering ChatGPT ad products to smaller advertisers. The shift matters because it suggests conversational AI may become a performance-ad channel, not just a premium brand surface. If successful, OpenAI would be competing more directly with Meta’s small-business advertising engine.
