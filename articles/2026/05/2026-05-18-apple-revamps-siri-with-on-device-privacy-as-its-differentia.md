# Apple revamps Siri with on-device privacy as its differentiator

Apple previewed a revamped Siri built around an on-device foundation model and a private-cloud-compute fallback. The pitch leans hard on data-handling guarantees as the consumer assistant market becomes increasingly commoditized at the capability tier.
