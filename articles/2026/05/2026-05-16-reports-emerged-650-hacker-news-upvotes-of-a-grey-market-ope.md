# Reports emerged (650 Hacker News upvotes) of a grey market operating within China offering deeply discounted access to…

Reports emerged (650 Hacker News upvotes) of a grey market operating within China offering deeply discounted access to Anthropic's Claude API tokens, circumventing standard pricing structures. The phenomenon raises concerns about API terms enforcement, potential misuse at scale, and the broader challenge of AI pricing arbitrage in markets where frontier models are officially restricted or expensive. Anthropic has not publicly commented.
