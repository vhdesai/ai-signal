# The US House of Representatives has opened an inquiry into Airbnb's use of open-source Chinese AI models in its products

The US House of Representatives has opened an inquiry into Airbnb's use of open-source Chinese AI models in its products. CEO Brian Chesky stated publicly that Airbnb is not sharing data with Chinese firms and that it uses open-source model weights, not API access — a distinction that may be legally significant in the legislative proceedings. This is part of a broader Congressional focus on Chinese AI integration in US consumer applications. Source: Bloomberg/Techmeme (May 23, 2026)
