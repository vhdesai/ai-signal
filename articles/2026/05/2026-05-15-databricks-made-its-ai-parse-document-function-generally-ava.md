# Databricks made its ai_parse_document function generally available by default for compliance-security-profile…

Databricks made its ai_parse_document function generally available by default for compliance-security-profile workspaces — including those with HIPAA, HITRUST, C5, and TISAX controls. The function parses structured content from PDFs, images, Word, and PowerPoint files. This lowers the barrier for regulated industries to integrate unstructured document AI into data pipelines on Azure Databricks, with direct relevance for Microsoft's enterprise customers in financial services and healthcare.
