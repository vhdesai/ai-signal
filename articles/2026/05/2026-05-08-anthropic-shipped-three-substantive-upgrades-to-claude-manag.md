# Anthropic shipped three substantive upgrades to Claude Managed Agents on May 7

Anthropic shipped three substantive upgrades to Claude Managed Agents on May 7. Dreaming (research preview) enables agents to review past sessions, extract patterns, and self-improve over time — either automatically or with human review gating. Outcomes lets developers define a success rubric; a separate grader evaluates results in its own context window and drives iterative refinement until the rubric is met. Multiagent Orchestration allows a lead agent to decompose tasks and delegate to specialist sub-agents working in parallel on a shared filesystem. Netflix has already deployed multiagent orchestration for its platform engineering team. The additions come alongside Anthropic's SpaceX Colossus-1 compute deal, which has already doubled Claude Code rate limits for Pro, Max, and Team subscribers.
