# New Meta Launches Hands-Free AI Glasses Features for Accessibility

Ahead of Global Accessibility Awareness Day, Meta unveiled new hands-free capabilities for Ray-Ban Meta and Oakley Meta Vanguard glasses: Be My Eyes group-calling integration, voice controls for WhatsApp/Messenger/Instagram calls, a customizable one-touch action button, and real-time captioned calls on Ray-Ban Meta Display glasses. Meta also opened its Wearables Device Access Toolkit to third-party developers building accessibility extensions. The announcement strategically positions Meta's AI wearables as an assistive-technology platform alongside their consumer device narrative. 📈 Industry News
