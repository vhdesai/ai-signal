# Stanford HAI 2026 AI Index: Capability Is Accelerating, Not Plateauing

The Stanford Human-Centered AI Institute released its 2026 AI Index, finding that AI capability is compounding rather than plateauing. Industry produced over 90% of notable frontier models in 2025, and several now match or exceed human baselines on PhD-level science questions, multimodal reasoning, and competition mathematics. Performance on SWE-bench Verified (agentic software engineering) rose from 60% to near 100% in a single year. Enterprise adoption reached 88%, and 4 in 5 university students now use generative AI. The Index frames this as "the most transformative technology transition since the Industrial Revolution." 🛠️
