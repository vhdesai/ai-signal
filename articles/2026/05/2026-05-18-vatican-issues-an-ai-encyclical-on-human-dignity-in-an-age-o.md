# Vatican issues an AI encyclical on human dignity in an age of automation

The Vatican released an encyclical addressing AI's implications for labor, dignity, and discernment — explicitly cautioning against treating displaced workers as residuals of efficiency. The document is likely to feed into European policy framing and into corporate governance conversations about AI's social license.
