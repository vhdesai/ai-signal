# Anthropic Institute Publishes Research Agenda — Economic Diffusion, Threats, AI in the Wild, R&D Acceleration

Anthropic's newly established Anthropic Institute (TAI) published its formal research agenda, organized into four pillars: economic diffusion (who benefits from AI, and how?), threats and resilience (AI-enabled security risks), AI systems in the wild (behavioral analysis from within a frontier lab), and AI-driven R&D (recursive self-improvement signals). TAI will publish findings including granular data from the Anthropic Economic Index and early warning signals for job market disruption. Anthropic disclosed that software engineering roles inside the company are "changing radically" — a signal the public can expect detailed analysis on as TAI matures.
