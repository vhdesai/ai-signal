# Amnesty says major generative AI data pipelines are privacy-invasive by design

Amnesty International argued that enormous data pipelines powering major generative AI systems are rooted in mass invasions of privacy by design. The critique targets the upstream data collection and processing practices behind model development, not just downstream misuse. It adds pressure for stronger transparency, consent, and data governance rules around foundation-model training.
