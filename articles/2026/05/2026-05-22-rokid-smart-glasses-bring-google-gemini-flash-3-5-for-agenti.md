# Rokid Smart Glasses Bring Google Gemini Flash 3.5 for Agentic Wearable AI

Rokid, a global smart eyewear manufacturer, announced it will integrate Google's Gemini Flash 3.5 into its smart glasses platform following Google's recent I/O announcements. The upgrade enables higher-precision, lower-latency agentic AI interactions via voice commands, making Rokid one of the first wearable platforms to bring continuous contextual AI experiences to users in over 100 countries. The Rokid Agent Store has already seen 3,000+ developer submissions with 400+ approved agentic workflows, and the store will soon open to international markets. 📊 4 · Industry News
