# Alibaba's Qwen team unveils Qwen3.7-Max with 1M-token context

Announced at the Alibaba Cloud Summit in Hangzhou, Qwen3.7-Max is positioned as Alibaba's most advanced agent model, featuring a 1M-token context window, extended-thinking mode, and a 35-hour autonomous demo chaining 1,000+ tool calls. It scored 56.6 on the Artificial Analysis Intelligence Index — the highest-ranked Chinese model on that index and #13 overall on LM Arena. The flagship Max variant remains closed-weight; the smaller Plus is promised open-weight.
