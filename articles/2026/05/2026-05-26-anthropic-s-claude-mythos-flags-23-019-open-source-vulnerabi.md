# Anthropic's Claude Mythos flags 23,019 open-source vulnerabilities under Project Glasswing

Anthropic's Claude Mythos Preview scanned more than 1,000 open-source projects and surfaced 23,019 candidate vulnerabilities — 6,202 estimated high- or critical-severity. Of 1,752 high/critical findings reviewed by outside firms, 90.6% were judged valid. Anthropic warns that verification, disclosure and patching are becoming the new bottleneck as AI-assisted vuln-hunting outpaces defenders. N Research
