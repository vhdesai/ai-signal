# Salesforce's lukewarm outlook fuels AI-disruption fear

Salesforce posted softer-than-expected forward guidance, prompting renewed concern that incumbent SaaS suites are being squeezed by AI-native and agentic-AI alternatives. Analysts highlighted slowing seat growth in customer service and sales clouds as the most visible symptom of the displacement. The print weighed on broader software multiples Wednesday.
