# China's ByteDance developing AI chips modeled on Nvidia partner Groq

TikTok owner ByteDance is developing a new AI inference chip whose structure resembles Groq's language-processing units, part of an aggressive build-out of homegrown AI infrastructure. The move highlights how Chinese hyperscalers continue to design around U.S. export controls and points to broader pricing pressure on Nvidia in inference-heavy production workloads.
