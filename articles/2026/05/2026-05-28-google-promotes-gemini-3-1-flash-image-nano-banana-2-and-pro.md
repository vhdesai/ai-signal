# Google promotes Gemini 3.1 Flash Image (Nano Banana 2) and Pro Image to GA

Google moved its native visual models — Gemini 3.1 Flash Image (Nano Banana 2) and Gemini 3-Pro Image (Nano Banana Pro) — into general availability. A new video-to-image capability lets developers pass a video file or public YouTube URL alongside a text prompt to generate cinematic posters, thumbnails, or summary infographics. The previous preview SKUs are deprecated and will shut down June 25, 2026.
