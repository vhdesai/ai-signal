# Breaking White House Expected to Sign AI Frontier Model Vetting Executive Orders Within Two Weeks

The White House is finalizing multiple AI executive orders and sources indicate at least one will be signed within the next two weeks — the centerpiece being a federal vetting system for frontier AI models prior to public release, the first such mechanism in U.S. history. Internal debate is active on the stringency of the review: some officials prefer a light-touch regime while others advocate aggressive pre-release oversight. The move represents a notable policy reversal from the administration's earlier deregulatory posture and reflects intensifying bipartisan concern about frontier model risks.
