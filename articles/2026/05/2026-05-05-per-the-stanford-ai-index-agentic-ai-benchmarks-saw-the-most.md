# Per the Stanford AI Index, agentic AI benchmarks saw the most extreme capability gains of any category in 2026 —…

Per the Stanford AI Index, agentic AI benchmarks saw the most extreme capability gains of any category in 2026 — Terminal-Bench real-world task completion improved from 20% in 2025 to 77.3%, and cybersecurity agent success rates jumped from 15% (2024) to 93%. Google's updated Deep Research Agent (released April 21) now supports collaborative planning, MCP server integration, and file search — with two variants optimized for speed and maximum comprehensiveness respectively. Gemini Deep Think notably earned a gold medal at the International Mathematical Olympiad, marking a milestone in AI mathematical reasoning.
