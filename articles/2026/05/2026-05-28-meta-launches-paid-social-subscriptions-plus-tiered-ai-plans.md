# Meta launches paid social subscriptions plus tiered AI plans (Meta One Plus & Premium)

Meta began rolling out worldwide paid add-ons (Instagram Plus and Facebook Plus at $3.99/mo, WhatsApp Plus at $2.99/mo) alongside two AI tiers — Meta One Plus ($7.99) and Meta One Premium ($19.99) — that bundle higher compute, longer reasoning, and expanded image/video generation. The shift signals Meta's intent to monetize Muse-class AI directly rather than rely solely on ad revenue.
