# Mira Murati's Thinking Machines Lab introduces TML-Interaction-Small, a 276B MoE for real-time multimodal collaboration…

Mira Murati's Thinking Machines Lab introduces TML-Interaction-Small, a 276B MoE for real-time multimodal collaboration — MarkTechPost, May 13, 2026 Thinking Machines Lab unveiled a 276B-parameter MoE (12B active) built on a multi-stream, time-aligned micro-turn architecture that processes 200ms chunks of audio, video, and text simultaneously. The system runs a real-time interaction model in parallel with an asynchronous reasoning model that handles tool use. This is the lab's first concrete public artifact and signals a shift from turn-based to full-duplex AI interfaces.
