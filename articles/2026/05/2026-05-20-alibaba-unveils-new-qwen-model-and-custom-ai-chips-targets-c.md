# Alibaba unveils new Qwen model and custom AI chips, targets "China's AI factory"

Alibaba unveiled a next-generation Qwen model alongside custom-silicon designs at its Apsara event, positioning itself as the AI infrastructure backbone for Chinese enterprise. The company forecasts ¥30 billion in AI revenue in 2026, with agents driving more than half of cloud sales. A follow-up SCMP analysis framed the announcement as Alibaba moving from AI investment to commercialization — a shift to monetize its aggressive model-development spend.
