# Trump Clears Nvidia H200 Sales to Alibaba, Tencent and 8 Others — Beijing Halts Deliveries

The Trump administration approved Nvidia H200 GPU exports to 10 Chinese firms including Alibaba, Tencent, ByteDance, and JD.com — a sharp reversal of earlier export controls. Despite the US clearance, Beijing has ordered a halt to deliveries pending its own review, layering bilateral regulatory complexity onto what would otherwise be several billion dollars of incremental Nvidia revenue.
