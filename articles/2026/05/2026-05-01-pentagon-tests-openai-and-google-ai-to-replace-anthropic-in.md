# Pentagon tests OpenAI and Google AI to replace Anthropic in classified defense workflows

The Pentagon cleared eight tech firms (AWS, Google, Microsoft, OpenAI, SpaceX, NVIDIA, Reflection, Oracle) to deploy frontier AI on IL6/IL7 classified networks. Anthropic, whose Claude was previously the favored defense vendor, was conspicuously absent — though the NSA is reportedly using Anthropic's not-yet-public Mythos model for cyber operations. Pentagon CTO Emil Michael took a thinly-veiled shot at Anthropic: "it's irresponsible to be reliant on any one partner, and we learned that that one partner didn't really want to work with us."
