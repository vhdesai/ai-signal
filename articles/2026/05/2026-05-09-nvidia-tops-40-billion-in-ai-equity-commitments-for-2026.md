# Nvidia Tops $40 Billion in AI Equity Commitments for 2026

Nvidia has now committed more than $40B to AI equity investments in early 2026, anchored by a $30B check into OpenAI plus multi-billion-dollar stakes in Corning ($3.2B) and IREN ($2.1B). CEO Jensen Huang told reporters Nvidia "tries to invest in all" foundation-model companies because "we don't pick winners." Analyst Matthew Bryson (Wedbush) calls the strategy the "circular investment theme" — Nvidia finances its own demand. May 20 Q1 earnings will be the first financial test of this portfolio approach. Reported by TechCrunch and CNBC independently. ⚡
