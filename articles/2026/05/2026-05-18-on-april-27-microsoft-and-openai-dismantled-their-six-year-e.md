# On April 27, Microsoft and OpenAI dismantled their six-year exclusive cloud agreement, replacing it with a…

On April 27, Microsoft and OpenAI dismantled their six-year exclusive cloud agreement, replacing it with a non-exclusive license running through 2032; the "AGI clause" was also removed. OpenAI immediately began deploying models to AWS and launched "DeployCo," a $10B AI consulting arm targeting enterprise deployments. Separately, over 600 OpenAI employees cashed out $6.6B in shares (75 employees hit the $30M cap), while Sam Altman's business dealings face scrutiny from the House Oversight Committee ahead of a potential IPO. Former OpenAI Chief Scientist Ilya Sutskever has reportedly testified against Altman in federal court, alleging a year's worth of documented deceptive behavior.
