# AlphaEvolve Paper: Gemini-Powered Agent Scales Scientific Algorithm Discovery Across Domains

DeepMind published detailed research on AlphaEvolve showing its Gemini-powered agent autonomously discovering novel algorithms across chip design, databases, genomics, logistics, and model training. Key results: 20% improvement in Spanner database write efficiency and 30% fewer errors in DeepConsensus genomics variant detection — both production systems at Google scale. The paper frames AlphaEvolve not as a specialized code optimizer but as a general-purpose scientific discovery engine, positioning it alongside AlphaFold as a milestone in AI-augmented science. 🛡️ AI Safety & Policy
