# xAI Ships Grok 4.3; Now Available in Palantir AIP

xAI released Grok 4.3 on May 6, posting 53+ on the Artificial Analysis Intelligence Index. Palantir added it to AIP on May 14 for U.S. and supported-region enrollments. The model release follows xAI's controversial 10x API price increase on Grok 3 in early May — now the most expensive model in major API catalogs at $30/$150 per million input/output tokens.
