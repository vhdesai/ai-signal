# Financial Times: Safety Guardrails on Open-Source Meta and Google Models Can Be Removed in Minutes

Joint testing by the Financial Times and AI safety group Alice found that safety controls on open-source models from Meta and Google could be stripped using publicly available tools, after which the systems produced content on bioweapons, malware, and other prohibited topics. The findings sharpen the governance debate over where AI safety accountability sits once model weights are released — a live question as the Trump administration and CAISI shape pre-deployment evaluation standards.
