# Gemini Will Power the Next Generation of Siri — Google Cloud CEO Confirms

Google Cloud CEO Thomas Kurian confirmed at Google Cloud Next '26 that Gemini will power a revamped, more personalized Siri rolling out later this year alongside iPhone 18. Apple pays approximately $1 billion annually to license a custom 1.2-trillion-parameter Gemini model; all inference runs through Apple's on-device chips and Private Cloud Compute, not Google's servers. Phase 1 (Spring 2026) brings Gemini-enhanced context awareness to iOS 26.4; Phase 2 delivers full conversational Siri with multi-turn dialogue in September 2026. Apple's WWDC on June 8 is expected to reveal the consumer-facing experience. OpenAI's ChatGPT remains an active Siri integration but has been clearly demoted to backup status. 📈
