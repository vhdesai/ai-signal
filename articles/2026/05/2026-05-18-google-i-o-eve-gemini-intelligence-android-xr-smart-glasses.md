# Google I/O Eve: Gemini Intelligence, Android XR Smart Glasses & "Googlebooks" Unveiled Hot

With the developer conference opening tomorrow at Shoreline Amphitheatre (keynote 10 a.m. PT), Google has already fired its biggest shots. Pre-announced headline items include Gemini Intelligence—a proactive agentic AI layer embedded system-wide into Android 17—and Android XR smart glasses co-developed with Samsung, Warby Parker, and Gentle Monster, running Gemini 2.5 Pro natively on-device. Google is also retiring the Chromebook category in favor of Googlebooks, a new line of premium Android-native laptops. The simultaneous hardware-software-model integration push is Google's most aggressive consumer AI move since the original Pixel launch.
