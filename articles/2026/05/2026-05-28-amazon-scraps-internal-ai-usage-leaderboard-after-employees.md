# Amazon scraps internal AI usage leaderboard after employees gamed it

Amazon is pulling an internal ranking system designed to measure employees' AI tool usage after staff inflated scores with meaningless prompts — driving up internal AWS spend in the process. The episode highlights a broader enterprise pattern: incentive metrics that reward AI-adoption volume rather than business outcomes tend to produce expensive theater.
