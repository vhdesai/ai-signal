# Google announced its most sweeping Search update in 25 years at I/O, with AI-powered answers becoming the default experience. The shift transforms Search from a link-finding engine into an AI-first answer engine, sparking debate about the impact on web publishers and the broader internet ecosystem. Business Insider's Katie Notopoulos argues the change "is about to ruin the internet" by turning it from "a place you go" into "a place that comes to you."
