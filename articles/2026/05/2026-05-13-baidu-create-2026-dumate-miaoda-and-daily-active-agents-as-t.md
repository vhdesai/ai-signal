# Baidu Create 2026: DuMate, Miaoda, and "Daily Active Agents" as the New Growth Metric

At its annual developer conference in Beijing, Baidu CEO Robin Li proposed "Daily Active Agents" (DAA) as the defining agent-era metric — predicting global DAA could surpass 10 billion. The company rolled out DuMate (general-purpose agent, now mobile with PC sync), Miaoda (coding agent app with enterprise edition), an upgraded Yijing digital-human platform, and a full-stack AI Cloud designed for large-scale agent deployments. ERNIE 5.1 reportedly cuts training costs by ~94% vs. its predecessor.
