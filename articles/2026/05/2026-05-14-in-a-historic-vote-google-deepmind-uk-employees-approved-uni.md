# In a historic vote, Google DeepMind UK employees approved unionization by a 98% majority — the first union formed at…

In a historic vote, Google DeepMind UK employees approved unionization by a 98% majority — the first union formed at any frontier AI lab. The primary catalyst was a classified Pentagon AI contract that staff were not consulted on. The vote signals a growing tension between AI lab leadership (which increasingly pursues government and defense contracts) and technical staff who joined for safety-oriented research missions. The development has immediate implications for talent recruitment and retention across DeepMind, with potential ripple effects at Anthropic, OpenAI, and other labs.
