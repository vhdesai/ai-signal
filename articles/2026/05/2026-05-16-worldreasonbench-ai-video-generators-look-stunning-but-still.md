# WorldReasonBench: AI Video Generators Look Stunning But Still Can't Reason

A new benchmark called WorldReasonBench tests AI video generators not on image fidelity but on physical plausibility and logical consistency. ByteDance's Seedance 2.0 topped the leaderboard ahead of Google's Veo 3.1 and OpenAI's Sora 2. The findings confirm that today's generators excel at aesthetics but routinely violate basic physics and causal reasoning — a key gap for enterprise video, simulation, and training-data applications. 🛠️ 3 · Products & Tools
