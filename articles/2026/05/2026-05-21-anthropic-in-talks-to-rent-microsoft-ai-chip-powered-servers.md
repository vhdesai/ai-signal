# Anthropic in talks to rent Microsoft AI-chip-powered servers — MSFT shares up 1.5% premarket

Anthropic is in active discussions to rent servers powered by Microsoft's AI chips for complex workloads, per two people who spoke with executives involved. Microsoft shares rose ~1.5% in premarket trading on the news. A partnership would be a significant win for Microsoft as it pushes to emulate Alphabet and Amazon's custom-silicon strategies — and would further diversify Anthropic away from reliance on any single compute provider.
