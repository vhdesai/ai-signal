# A joint team from UC Berkeley and MIT published optany (optimize_anything) — a single LLM-based optimization system…

A joint team from UC Berkeley and MIT published optany (optimize_anything) — a single LLM-based optimization system that frames all problems as improving a text artifact evaluated by a scoring function. The system achieves state-of-the-art results across six diverse tasks simultaneously: nearly tripling Gemini Flash's ARC-AGI accuracy, cutting cloud scheduling costs 40%, and matching AlphaEvolve on circle packing — without any task-specific specialization. The results directly challenge the assumption that domain-specific optimization tools are necessary, with implications for how enterprises architect AI workflows.
