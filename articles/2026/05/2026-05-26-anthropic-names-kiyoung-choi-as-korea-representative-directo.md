# Anthropic names KiYoung Choi as Korea Representative Director, prepares Seoul office

Anthropic appointed Choi (ex-Snowflake Korea GM, with prior leadership roles at Google Cloud, Adobe, Autodesk, and Microsoft) as its first Korea country head ahead of a Seoul office opening. Anthropic's Economic Index shows Koreans use Claude at 3.5× the rate expected for the population. Customers cited include Law&Company and SK Telecom; the appointment was confirmed across five Korean outlets.
