The Cowork newsletter highlighted OpenAI’s confidential S-1 process as a defining moment for AI capital markets. A public listing would force unprecedented transparency around revenue, compute spend, model margins, and safety obligations, creating the benchmark against which other frontier labs and AI infrastructure companies will be measured.
