# Fastino Labs Open-Sources GLiGuard — 300M-Parameter Safety Moderator Beats Models 23–90× Its Size

Fastino Labs released GLiGuard, an Apache 2.0 safety-moderation model running prompt safety, jailbreak detection, harm classification, and refusal detection in a single forward pass. The encoder-only architecture delivers up to 16x higher throughput and 16.6x lower latency while matching or exceeding accuracy of decoder-only guardrail models orders of magnitude larger across nine benchmarks.
