# OpenAI briefs White House officials on GPT-Rosalind biodefense work

OpenAI’s reported GPT-Rosalind biodefense program positions frontier models as national-security and public-health infrastructure, not just commercial productivity tools. The program is described as supporting pandemic preparedness, vaccine discovery, and biothreat detection, and it expands the pattern of frontier labs pairing technical capability releases with government-facing safety narratives.
