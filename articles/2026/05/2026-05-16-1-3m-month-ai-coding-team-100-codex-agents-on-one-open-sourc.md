# $1.3M/Month AI Coding Team: 100 Codex Agents on One Open-Source Project

OpenClaw founder Peter Steinberger is running approximately 100 parallel Codex instances around the clock on his open-source project, driving OpenAI API spend to $1.3 million per month with a team of just three humans. The agents write code, review pull requests, and find bugs. Steinberger's operation is the most extreme public example to date of agentic AI as a force multiplier for small engineering teams — and a leading indicator of where enterprise software development economics may be heading. 📈 4 · Industry News
