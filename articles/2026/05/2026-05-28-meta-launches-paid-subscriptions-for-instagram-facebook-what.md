# Meta launches paid subscriptions for Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp — plus a tiered AI plan

Meta began rolling out worldwide paid add-ons (Instagram Plus and Facebook Plus at $3.99/mo, WhatsApp Plus at $2.99/mo) alongside two AI tiers — Meta One Plus ($7.99) and Meta One Premium ($19.99) — that bundle higher compute, longer reasoning, and expanded image/video generation. The shift signals Meta's intent to monetize Muse-class AI directly rather than fund it solely through ad revenue.
