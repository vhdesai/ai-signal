# AI Search Startups Surge: Exa Labs at $2.2B, Parallel Web at $2B

Following Google's I/O announcement that it will rebuild traditional Search around AI, a wave of startups is racing to claim the next discoverability layer. Andreessen Horowitz-backed Exa Labs raised $250M at a $2.2B valuation; Parag Agrawal's Parallel Web Systems raised $100M at a $2B valuation led by Sequoia. Amazon, LinkedIn, and Reddit are also reworking their internal search around AI — broadening the universe of potential acquirers. Compiled May 26, 2026.
