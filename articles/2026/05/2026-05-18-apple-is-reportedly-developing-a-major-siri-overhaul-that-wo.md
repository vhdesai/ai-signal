# Apple is reportedly developing a major Siri overhaul that would automatically delete conversation histories to address…

Apple is reportedly developing a major Siri overhaul that would automatically delete conversation histories to address privacy concerns — a direct differentiator from Google Assistant and ChatGPT. The update integrates more advanced large language models and is part of Apple's broader on-device AI strategy. The move is expected to ease regulatory pressure and rebuild user trust, particularly in regulated markets in the EU, while reinforcing Apple's hardware-software privacy narrative.
