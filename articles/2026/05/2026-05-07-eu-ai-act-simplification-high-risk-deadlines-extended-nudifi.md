# EU AI Act Simplification: High-Risk Deadlines Extended, Nudification Apps Banned

The EU reached a provisional deal on May 7 to simplify portions of the AI Act, extending compliance deadlines for high-risk AI systems (now December 2027 for Annex III, August 2028 for Annex I) while leaving substantive obligations unchanged. The deal also explicitly bans non-consensual AI-generated explicit content ("nudification" apps). Separately, NIST in the US launched development of a Trustworthy AI Profile for Critical Infrastructure — signaling imminent sector-specific guidance expected to function as a de facto US standard for energy and defense AI operations.
