# A new paper analyzing over 160,000 product launches on Product Hunt found that entrepreneurial entry surged sharply…

A new paper analyzing over 160,000 product launches on Product Hunt found that entrepreneurial entry surged sharply following the public release of ChatGPT-3.5, disproportionately driven by solo founders using generative AI. However, the data shows that at the top of the quality distribution, team-based ventures continue to outperform AI-assisted solo founders — suggesting that generative AI lowers entry barriers but has not yet closed the collaboration advantage in building high-quality products.
