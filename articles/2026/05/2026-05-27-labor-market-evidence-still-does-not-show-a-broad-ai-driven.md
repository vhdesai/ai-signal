# Labor-Market Evidence Still Does Not Show a Broad AI-Driven White-Collar Jobs Collapse

A synthesis of current labor-market data finds no broad evidence of AI-driven displacement in white-collar occupations through Q1 2026 — counter to several high-profile prior forecasts. The piece is being widely cited and aligns with Altman's same-day reversal (Section 3). Useful empirical anchor for internal workforce-planning conversations.
