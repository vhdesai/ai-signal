# Apple to make on-device AI a centerpiece of WWDC, distill Gemini into local models

Apple plans to use next month's WWDC to position 15 years of custom silicon as a privacy- and cost-advantaged path to local inference. Under its existing agreement with Google, Apple will use a large Gemini model to train smaller, distilled variants capable of running on iPhone, Watch, and Mac. Apple is also evaluating acquisitions — including Liquid AI — to accelerate model-shrinking work.
