# Cisco announced it is cutting nearly 4,000 positions while simultaneously reporting record quarterly revenue — a…

Cisco announced it is cutting nearly 4,000 positions while simultaneously reporting record quarterly revenue — a pattern increasingly common in enterprise tech as companies reallocate headcount budgets toward AI infrastructure and tooling. The company framed the cuts as enabling increased AI investment. This follows a broader industry trend: AI-driven productivity gains are being captured in margin expansion rather than workforce retention, a dynamic with direct implications for SaaS and enterprise software M&A valuations.
