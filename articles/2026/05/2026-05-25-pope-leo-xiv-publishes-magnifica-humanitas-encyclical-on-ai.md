# Pope Leo XIV Publishes "Magnifica Humanitas" Encyclical on AI

Pope Leo XIV released "Magnifica Humanitas," an encyclical letter setting out the Catholic Church's framework for AI development, with explicit teaching on human dignity, labor displacement, autonomous weapons, and the moral responsibilities of AI developers and deployers. The document is being read alongside the EU AI Inventory and U.S. executive order context as part of a broader normative consolidation around AI accountability.
