# OpenAI Model Disproves Erdős Unit Distance Conjecture After 80 Years

An OpenAI internal reasoning model autonomously produced a counterexample to Paul Erdős's 1946 unit distance conjecture in discrete geometry — a problem that had stood for eight decades. The result is one of the most consequential AI-assisted mathematical findings to date and intensifies debate about frontier models as legitimate scientific collaborators.
