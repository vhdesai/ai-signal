# MIT Study: Will AI Create Jobs the Way Past Technologies Did?  Trending

A new MIT study of the postwar U.S. labor market examines which categories of workers historically filled new tech-enabled jobs as transformative technologies were introduced, positioning the findings as a framework for evaluating who will benefit most from AI-driven job creation. The research addresses the labor-economics angle currently dominating policy discussion around generative AI deployment at enterprise scale. It is among the most policy-relevant academic publications in the 24-hour window.
