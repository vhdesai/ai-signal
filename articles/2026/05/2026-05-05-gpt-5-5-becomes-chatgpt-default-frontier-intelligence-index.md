# GPT-5.5 Becomes ChatGPT Default; Frontier Intelligence Index Hits 60.24

OpenAI made GPT-5.5 Instant the new default model in ChatGPT, following its April 23 launch where it posted 60.24 on the Intelligence Index — a three-point leap over the previous ceiling held by Claude Opus 4.7 (57.28). GPT-5.5 also scores 59.12 on coding benchmarks and 82.7% on Terminal-Bench 2.0. The shift to GPT-5.5 Instant as default brings the highest-capability model to all ChatGPT users at no extra charge.
