# Trending Anthropic Tops CNBC Disruptor 50 for 2026

CNBC published its 2026 Disruptor 50 ranking with Anthropic in the No. 1 position, cementing AI-native companies' dominance of the private-disruption narrative. The ranking coincides with Anthropic's week of consecutive enterprise wins — the PwC partnership, the Stainless acquisition, and the SandboxAQ integration — and arrives as Anthropic's secondary-market valuation approaches $900B. The full list is heavily weighted toward generative-AI companies.
