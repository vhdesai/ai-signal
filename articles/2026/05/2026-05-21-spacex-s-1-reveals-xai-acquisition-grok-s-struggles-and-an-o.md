# SpaceX S-1 Reveals xAI Acquisition, Grok's Struggles, and an Orbital AI Pivot  Trending

Ars Technica unpacks the SpaceX S-1, which positions the SpaceXAI division (formed after the xAI acquisition) and orbital data centres as the long-term growth story — with Grok acknowledged to lag ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini in user preference. Notable disclosures include the $1.25B/month Anthropic compute contract and a $60B option to acquire Cursor. The filing frames SpaceX's AI ambitions as a multi-year infrastructure play, not a consumer AI product story.
