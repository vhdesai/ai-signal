# Tencent Cloud Begins Paid Commercial Services for Hy3 Preview and DeepSeek-V4-Pro

Tencent shares jumped 4% as the firm transitioned its Hunyuan-3 preview and DeepSeek-V4-Pro hosting from free-tier to paid commercial service tiers. The move signals that Chinese frontier-model unit economics are crossing into commercial-viability territory and gives Tencent Cloud a credible Azure-equivalent enterprise pitch inside China. Watch for follow-on pricing signals from Alibaba Cloud and Baidu within the week.
