# TrendingNvidia Tops $40 Billion in Equity Investments in 2026 — Now Financing Its Own Supply Chain

Nvidia has crossed $40 billion in equity commitments in 2026 alone — including a $3.2 billion pact with glass maker Corning and a $2.1 billion right-to-invest deal with data center operator IREN announced this past week, alongside a previously disclosed $30 billion check to OpenAI. With $97 billion in free cash flow generated last fiscal year and a $5.2 trillion market cap, Jensen Huang is systematically financing the entire AI supply chain to guarantee Nvidia hardware runs from chips to applications. Analysts at Wedbush described the strategy as a "circular investment theme" that builds a competitive moat — though critics draw comparisons to vendor financing dynamics seen in the dot-com era.
