# WSJ: AI market signals are getting “soapy,” but not yet fully overheated

The Wall Street Journal’s Intelligent Investor column examined whether the AI trade is becoming a bubble, using AIAI Holdings’ Nasdaq listing and repeated “AI” branding as a case study. The column argued that market enthusiasm is heating up, but has not yet reached the extreme speculative behavior of late-stage bubbles. For technology executives, the piece is a useful reminder that AI capital markets can remain rational in the aggregate while still rewarding weak narratives at the margin.
