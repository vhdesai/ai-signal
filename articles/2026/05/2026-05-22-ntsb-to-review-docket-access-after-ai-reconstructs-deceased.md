# NTSB to review docket access after AI reconstructs deceased pilots' voices

Third parties used spectrogram data from public NTSB investigation files to reconstruct the voices of deceased pilots in cockpit-voice audio, triggering a Board review of what investigative materials should remain publicly accessible. The case is a milestone in synthetic-voice misuse and will likely be cited in upcoming biometric-data rulemaking.
