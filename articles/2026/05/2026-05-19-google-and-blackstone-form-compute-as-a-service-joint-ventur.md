# Google and Blackstone form compute-as-a-service joint venture

Google and Blackstone unveiled a joint venture to offer AI data-center capacity, networking, and computer hardware as a "compute-as-a-service" product. Google contributes TPUs, software, and services; Blackstone brings capital, project debt, power procurement, and institutional demand. The structure lets Google expand the addressable market for TPUs beyond Google Cloud while Blackstone owns the compute inside data centers, not just the real estate.
