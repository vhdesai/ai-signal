# Xreal, Google's Smartglasses Partner, Says It Has Finally Cracked the Form Factor

Xreal, Google's official smartglasses hardware partner for the Android XR platform, says it has cracked the wearable category's long-standing tradeoff between weight, optical quality, and battery life. The reveal complements Google I/O's Gemini-powered Samsung XR glasses announcement and signals that smartglasses will be the next major AI hardware battleground.
