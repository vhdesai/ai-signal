# Isomorphic Labs (DeepMind Spinout) Raises $2.1B Series B for AI Drug Discovery

Isomorphic Labs — the DeepMind spinout applying AI to pharmaceutical research and drug design — closed a $2.1 billion Series B led by Thrive Capital. The round is one of the largest AI biotech raises of 2026, reflecting surging investor appetite for applying frontier AI to life sciences. Isomorphic builds on AlphaFold's protein-structure breakthrough, extending into full drug candidate discovery pipelines. The raise underscores how AI's most valuable near-term applications may lie in high-stakes scientific domains rather than consumer products.
