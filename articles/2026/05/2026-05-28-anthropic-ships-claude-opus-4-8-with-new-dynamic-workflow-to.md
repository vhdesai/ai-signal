# Anthropic ships Claude Opus 4.8 with new "dynamic workflow" tool

Anthropic shipped Claude Opus 4.8 the same day it announced its $65B Series H. The model posts 57.9% on Humanity's Last Exam and 69.2% on SWE-Pro, with materially stronger agentic and long-horizon performance and a higher propensity to flag uncertainty rather than fabricate progress. A new "dynamic workflow" tool extends agentic orchestration for enterprise deployments. Pricing matches Opus 4.7.
