# Recursive Superintelligence Raises $650M at $4.65B Valuation — Stealth Debut

Recursive Superintelligence emerged from stealth with a record $650 million Series A at a $4.65 billion valuation — the largest stealth debut in AI history. The company's positioning around "recursive" self-improving AI architectures places it in the AGI-adjacent space, competing for talent and capital with Anthropic, OpenAI, and xAI. The raise signals sustained LP appetite for frontier AI bets at high valuation multiples despite the coming IPO liquidity events. 🌐 Hardware & Geopolitics
