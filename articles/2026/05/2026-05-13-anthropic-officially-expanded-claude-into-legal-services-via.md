# Anthropic officially expanded Claude into legal services via a new set of Cowork plugins announced on May 12,…

Anthropic officially expanded Claude into legal services via a new set of Cowork plugins announced on May 12, integrating with Westlaw for case research, DocuSign for contract execution, and Harvey for legal-workflow automation. The capabilities range from contract review and clause flagging to bar exam preparation tools. The move signals Anthropic's push into high-value professional verticals and follows a February release that unsettled legal-tech stocks. By bundling skills, sub-agents, and matter-aware context, Anthropic is targeting law firms that want a single AI system across the full matter lifecycle rather than point-solution tools.
