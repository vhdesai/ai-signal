# Meta cuts 8,000 jobs starting May 20 to fund $115–$135B AI capex

Meta began its third 2026 layoff wave on May 20, eliminating ~10% of its workforce and reorganizing remaining staff into "AI pods" under CAIO Alexandr Wang's Superintelligence Labs. The reductions free payroll to fund $115–$135B in 2026 AI capex — including the 1GW Prometheus supercluster in Ohio and the 5GW Hyperion campus in Louisiana. Several other hyperscalers reported parallel headcount actions the same week. Products & Developer Tools
