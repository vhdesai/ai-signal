# EU AI Act Simplification Deal: High-Risk Rule Delays, Nudification App Ban

The EU reached a provisional agreement to simplify the AI Act's compliance obligations, delaying some high-risk AI rules for smaller operators while enacting an immediate ban on non-consensual explicit AI content (so-called nudification apps). The deal reflects political pressure to avoid over-regulating European AI competitiveness while maintaining hard lines on the most harmful use cases. U.S. state-level regulation meanwhile continued advancing in Colorado, Connecticut, and California with distinct governance frameworks, creating a growing patchwork compliance challenge for multinational AI deployments.
