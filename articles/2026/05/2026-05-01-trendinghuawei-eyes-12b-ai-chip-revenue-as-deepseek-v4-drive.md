# TRENDINGHuawei Eyes $12B AI Chip Revenue as DeepSeek V4 Drives Orders from Nvidia

Huawei expects 60% YoY growth in AI chip revenue to roughly $12B in 2026, driven by Chinese tech giants' orders for Ascend 950PR processors. DeepSeek V4 — optimized for Ascend hardware — is accelerating the substitution from Nvidia in China amid continued US export restrictions.
