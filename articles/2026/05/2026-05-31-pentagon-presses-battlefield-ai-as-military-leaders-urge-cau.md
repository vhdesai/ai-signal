# Pentagon presses battlefield AI as military leaders urge caution

As Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth pushes to accelerate military AI adoption, the head of U.S. Special Operations Command, Adm. Frank Bradley, cautioned that forces must ensure AI "delivers violence only where we intend it to be delivered." The remarks expose a split between leadership eager to field AI targeting tools and uniformed commanders wary of inadequate safeguards.
