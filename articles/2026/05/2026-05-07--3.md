The system frames all optimization problems as iteratively improving a text artifact evaluated by a scoring function, challenging the long-held assumption that domain-specific tools are necessary for specialized optimization. The system encodes online design intent and offline-generated components, supporting automated, human-guided, and hybrid optimization modes to make multi-agent development more reproducible and auditable. Tressoir addresses a growing need for structured governance of complex AI agent pipelines as enterprise adoption of multi-agent systems accelerates.
