# Google Confirms Gemini Spark MCP Rollout; Canva Magic Layers Goes Live in Beta

Four days after the Google I/O 2026 keynote, Google confirmed Gemini Spark — its 24/7 personal AI agent — will support Model Context Protocol (MCP) for third-party apps "within weeks," with Canva's Magic Layers integration already live in beta. Magic Layers converts previously-flat AI-generated images from Gemini's Nano Banana into editable design assets routed into the Canva Editor. The MCP commitment is notable: Google opting for the cross-vendor standard rather than a proprietary extension layer signals continued convergence on MCP as the agent-integration default. xAI
