# Nvidia Concedes China AI Chip Market to Huawei; China Races on Efficiency

Nvidia has "largely conceded" China's AI chip market to Huawei following export restrictions, according to CNBC reporting, a major shift from its prior dominance in the region. Meanwhile, Chinese AI firms are doubling down on cost efficiency as their competitive moat: SenseTime cofounder Lin Dahua told CNBC the company is betting that cheaper, good-enough models can win market share despite quality gaps with US frontier labs.
