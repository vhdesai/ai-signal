# Nvidia Tops $40B in AI Equity Bets — Invests $3.2B in Corning, $2.1B in IREN

Nvidia's aggressive strategy of financing the entire AI supply chain — taking equity stakes in companies that then buy its hardware — crossed $40 billion in 2026 commitments. This week alone: $3.2B invested in glass maker Corning and $2.1B in data center operator IREN. Nvidia's $5B bet on Intel is now worth over $25B. The company generated $97B in free cash flow last fiscal year and holds a ~$5.2 trillion market cap. Analysts at Wedbush note the approach "fits squarely into the circular investment theme" and some critics have raised dot-com bubble parallels. (Source: CNBC)
