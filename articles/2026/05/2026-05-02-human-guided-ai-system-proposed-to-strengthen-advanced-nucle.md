# Human-Guided AI System Proposed to Strengthen Advanced Nuclear Reactor Monitoring New

Researchers published work proposing a human-in-the-loop AI framework for monitoring and control of advanced nuclear reactors, positioning AI as a key enabler for next-generation clean energy infrastructure. The system is designed to augment human operator decision-making rather than replace it, addressing both reliability requirements and the regulatory need for human oversight in critical safety systems. The research reflects a growing body of work applying AI to clean-power generation challenges — a high-stakes domain where accuracy and explainability matter above speed. 🛡 6. AI Safety & Policy
