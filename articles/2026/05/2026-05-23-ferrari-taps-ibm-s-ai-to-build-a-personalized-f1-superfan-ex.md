# Ferrari Taps IBM's AI to Build a Personalized F1 "Superfan" Experience

Ferrari is deploying IBM's generative-AI stack to power personalized fan engagement around Formula 1 — surfacing tailored content, race insights, and merchandise recommendations for the scuderia's global supporter base. The deal continues IBM's enterprise-AI positioning around vertical, brand-bound deployments rather than competing head-on with frontier-lab consumer products.
