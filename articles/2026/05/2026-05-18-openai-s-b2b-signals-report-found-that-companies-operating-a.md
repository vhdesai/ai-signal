# OpenAI's B2B Signals Report found that companies operating at the AI frontier use 3.5x more AI tools per employee than…

OpenAI's B2B Signals Report found that companies operating at the AI frontier use 3.5x more AI tools per employee than the average enterprise — a productivity and talent density gap that is widening. The finding has implications for Corp Dev assessments: AI-native acquirees may systematically understate their operational leverage on traditional productivity metrics. The report also noted that adoption velocity is now a stronger predictor of enterprise AI ROI than any individual tool or model capability.
