# Blackstone and Google move AI infrastructure financing beyond data centers

PitchBook reported that Google and Blackstone formed a joint venture to offer AI data center capacity, networking and compute hardware as a compute-as-a-service product. Google will supply TPUs, hardware, software and services, while Blackstone gains exposure to the compute layer inside data centers. CIO Dive separately framed the move as a response to rising AI infrastructure spend and enterprise demand for more flexible AI workload capacity.
