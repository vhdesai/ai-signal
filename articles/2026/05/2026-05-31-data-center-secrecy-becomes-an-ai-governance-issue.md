# Data-center secrecy becomes an AI governance issue

TechCrunch reported that Erin Brockovich is targeting secrecy around data-center development. The issue connects AI infrastructure to local permitting, water and power use, environmental transparency, and community consent. As AI demand drives massive data-center buildouts, infrastructure governance is becoming part of AI policy rather than a separate real-estate concern.
