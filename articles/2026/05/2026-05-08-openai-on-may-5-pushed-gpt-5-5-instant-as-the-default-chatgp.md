# OpenAI on May 5 pushed GPT-5.5 Instant as the default ChatGPT experience, replacing GPT-5.5 for everyday conversations

OpenAI on May 5 pushed GPT-5.5 Instant as the default ChatGPT experience, replacing GPT-5.5 for everyday conversations. The update delivers notably smarter and more accurate responses in a warmer, more natural tone, with Anthropic-like personalization controls and measurable reductions in hallucinations on common knowledge tasks. The model is positioned as the everyday complement to GPT-5.5 Pro, which remains available for complex multi-step coding and research workflows. The cadence — GPT-5.5 launched April 23, GPT-5.5 Instant on May 5 — reflects OpenAI's accelerating release rhythm heading into mid-2026.
