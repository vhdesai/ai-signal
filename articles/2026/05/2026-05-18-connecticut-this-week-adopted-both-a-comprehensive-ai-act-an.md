# Connecticut this week adopted both a Comprehensive AI Act and an AI Responsibility and Transparency Act — among the…

Connecticut this week adopted both a Comprehensive AI Act and an AI Responsibility and Transparency Act — among the most detailed state-level AI governance frameworks enacted in the US to date. Maryland's Algorithmic Pricing Ban Act is awaiting entry into force. South Korea's AI Action Plan 2026–2028 is now in force, building on the country's proposal to introduce a "citizen dividend" funded by taxes on AI corporate profits. The US also updated California's Generative AI Procurement Order and AI Transparency Amendments. Globally, regulations.ai now tracks 970 AI laws across 82 countries and jurisdictions.
