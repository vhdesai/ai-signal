# Meta’s reported AI pendant extends the fight for ambient interfaces

Meta is reportedly developing an AI pendant, pushing the AI hardware race beyond phones and glasses into always-available wearable companions. The product question is whether dedicated AI devices can create daily habit formation without repeating the failures of earlier smart-assistant hardware. For platform companies, the prize is control of the interface layer through which AI observes, remembers, and acts.
