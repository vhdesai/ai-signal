# Yann LeCun on What Comes After LLMs: JEPA, Tapestry, and a Quiet Distancing from Llama

Meta's chief AI scientist lays out the JEPA-plus-Tapestry roadmap as his answer to autoregressive LLM limits, and notably states he had "zero technical influence" on Llama. The remarks land days before Meta's expected mid-year research disclosure and read as a public bid to redirect attention toward world-model architectures. Notable as a signal of internal Meta direction-setting tension.
