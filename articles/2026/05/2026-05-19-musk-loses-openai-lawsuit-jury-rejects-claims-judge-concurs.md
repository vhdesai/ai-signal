# Musk Loses OpenAI Lawsuit — Jury Rejects Claims, Judge Concurs

A nine-member jury rejected Elon Musk's claims against OpenAI after just two hours of deliberation, and Judge Yvonne Gonzalez Rogers agreed with the verdict. The decision turned on statute-of-limitations grounds — jurors found Musk waited too long to bring the suit after his 2020 X post calling OpenAI “essentially captured by Microsoft.” Musk has signaled he will appeal. The win is a major boost for Sam Altman, OpenAI, and indirectly Microsoft, which had been named in the aiding-and-abetting claims.
