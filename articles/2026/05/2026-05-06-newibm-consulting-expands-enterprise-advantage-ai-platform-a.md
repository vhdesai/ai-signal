# NewIBM Consulting Expands Enterprise Advantage AI Platform at IBM Think 2026

At IBM Think 2026 in Boston, IBM Consulting announced significant updates to its Enterprise Advantage platform, designed to accelerate enterprise AI transformation across hybrid and regulated environments. The announcements included next-generation agent orchestration, an agentic development suite for unified planning and governance, and the general availability of IBM Sovereign Core for digital sovereignty compliance. CEO Arvind Krishna framed the event around IBM's "AI Operating Model" blueprint, emphasizing that the AI divide between early movers and laggards is widening and that agentic deployments at scale require a new enterprise operating architecture. 4 · Industry News
