# Amazon's 2026 AI infrastructure capex pegged at ~$200B — highest of any hyperscaler

Amazon's 2026 AI infrastructure capex is now estimated at roughly $200B — the largest spend among Microsoft, Google, Meta, and Oracle. AWS is growing at its fastest pace in nearly four years, driven by AI training and inference workloads. Together the figure positions Amazon as the single biggest capex spender in the current buildout cycle.
