# Research Stanford HAI 2026 AI Index Report — US-China Gap Closes, Coding Benchmarks Near 100%

The landmark Stanford Human-Centered AI Index delivers nine key findings: AI capability is accelerating, not plateauing. SWE-bench Verified coding performance rose from 60% to near 100% in a single year. Organizational AI adoption reached 88%. The US–China model performance gap has effectively closed (Anthropic leads by just 2.7% as of March 2026). US private AI investment reached $285.9B in 2025 — 23× China's declared figure. The number of AI researchers relocating to the US has dropped 89% since 2017. Generative AI reached 53% global adoption within three years, faster than the PC or the internet. Estimated annual consumer value of GenAI tools in the US reached $172 billion.
