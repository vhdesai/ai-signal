# Spain's Economy Minister Carlos Cuerpo confirmed at a meeting of EU finance chiefs in Nicosia that talks with Anthropic…

Spain's Economy Minister Carlos Cuerpo confirmed at a meeting of EU finance chiefs in Nicosia that talks with Anthropic over its Mythos AI model have made "limited progress." The EU has been attempting to get European banks and companies tested for digital vulnerabilities that Mythos — currently in restricted preview with approximately 50 enterprise partners — is capable of identifying. Anthropic has warned Mythos can supercharge complex cyber attacks, a concern that also factored into the now-delayed White House executive order. EU finance ministers are treating this as a short-term systemic risk, not merely a long-term policy concern.
