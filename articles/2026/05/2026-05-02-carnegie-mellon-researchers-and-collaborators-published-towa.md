# Carnegie Mellon researchers and collaborators published "Toward a Science of Human-AI Teaming for Decision Making: A…

Carnegie Mellon researchers and collaborators published "Toward a Science of Human-AI Teaming for Decision Making: A Complementarity Framework" in PNAS Nexus, one of the field's leading interdisciplinary journals. The framework operationalizes how humans and AI systems can be paired to maximize complementary strengths rather than simply substituting one for the other in high-stakes decisions. The work draws on decision theory, cognitive science, and empirical AI performance data across medical, legal, and financial domains. The paper is expected to influence how organizations structure human oversight of AI-assisted workflows.
