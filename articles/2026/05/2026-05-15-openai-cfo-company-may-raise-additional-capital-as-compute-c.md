# OpenAI CFO: Company May Raise Additional Capital as Compute Crunch Deepens

OpenAI CFO Sarah Friar told Bloomberg that the company is actively evaluating additional capital raises as GPU demand continues to outstrip supply, even after the $40B SoftBank-led round closed earlier this year. Friar described the compute environment as a "structural crunch" that is forcing OpenAI to prioritize model serving over training experiments. The statement comes as OpenAI simultaneously announced multiple product launches (Codex mobile, Windows sandbox) and faces mounting legal and safety challenges. Analysts view further fundraising as likely given OpenAI's $5B+ monthly cash burn on infrastructure.
