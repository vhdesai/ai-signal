# TrendingOpenAI GPT-5.5 Instant Becomes ChatGPT's Universal Default

OpenAI replaced GPT-5.3 Instant with GPT-5.5 Instant as the default model for all ChatGPT users on May 5, touting 52.5% fewer hallucinated claims on high-stakes prompts (medicine, law, finance) and a 37.3% reduction in inaccurate claims on previously flagged conversations. The model introduces deep personalization by drawing on past conversations, uploaded files, and connected Gmail accounts. It scored 81.2 on AIME 2025 math vs. 65.4 for its predecessor, and 76 on MMMU-Pro multimodal reasoning vs. 69.2. This is GPT-5.5's first fully retrained architecture since GPT-4.5 — a ground-up rebuild, not an incremental update, co-designed with Nvidia's GB200/GB300 NVL72 rack systems.
