# Google Releases Gemini 3.5 Flash — Agent-Optimized Efficiency Model

Google launched Gemini 3.5 Flash this week, positioning it as a breakthrough in the efficiency-vs-capability tradeoff that has held back agentic AI at scale. Rolling out across Google's product suite — Search, Workspace, Gemini API — the model reportedly matches or exceeds last-generation Pro capability while delivering the latency and cost economics required for high-frequency agent tasks. Google product leadership described this release as the key enabler for complex multi-step agentic workflows becoming economically viable in production.
