# Cerebras Systems Prices Largest US IPO of 2026 at $56.4B Valuation

AI chip company Cerebras Systems priced its IPO at $56.4 billion, raising $5.55 billion in what analysts are calling the biggest US technology listing of 2026. The stock surged 108% on debut, reflecting investor appetite for alternatives to Nvidia's H100/H200 GPU dominance in AI training workloads. Cerebras's wafer-scale engine architecture offers up to 900,000 compute cores on a single die, enabling dramatically faster inference for large language models. The listing signals that purpose-built AI silicon is now a standalone investable category, distinct from general compute infrastructure.
