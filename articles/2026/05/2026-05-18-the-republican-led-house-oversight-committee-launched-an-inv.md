# The Republican-led House Oversight Committee launched an investigation into Sam Altman's business dealings, and six GOP…

The Republican-led House Oversight Committee launched an investigation into Sam Altman's business dealings, and six GOP state attorneys general called for SEC review, based on a WSJ investigative report. Former OpenAI Chief Scientist Ilya Sutskever — who disclosed a $7B stake in the company — reportedly testified in federal court that he spent a year documenting Sam Altman's "deceptive behavior." These governance concerns arrive at a sensitive moment as OpenAI prepares for what would be one of the largest-ever technology IPOs, following the company's $6.6B employee cash-out and DeployCo launch.
