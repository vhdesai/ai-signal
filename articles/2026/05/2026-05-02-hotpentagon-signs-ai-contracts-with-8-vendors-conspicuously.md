# HotPentagon Signs AI Contracts with 8 Vendors — Conspicuously Excluding Anthropic

The Department of Defense announced deployment agreements with AWS, Google, Microsoft, OpenAI, Nvidia, SpaceX, Reflection AI, and Oracle for classified Impact Level 6 and Level 7 network access on May 3. Anthropic was excluded after declining to lift its usage policy to allow autonomous weapons targeting and mass surveillance — a refusal that led the Pentagon to designate Anthropic a "supply chain risk." Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth publicly criticized Anthropic CEO Dario Amodei, comparing the company's policy to "Boeing telling us who we can shoot at." Litigation between Anthropic and the DoD is ongoing. The episode marks a new phase in AI governance where safety policies directly shape access to the largest government procurement contracts.
