# SpaceX IPO filing reveals Anthropic's $1.25B/month compute deal with SpaceX

SpaceX's IPO paperwork disclosed that Anthropic agreed to pay SpaceX $1.25 billion per month to rent capacity from SpaceX's compute facilities. The filing surfaces the extraordinary scale of Anthropic's multi-provider compute diversification strategy, spanning Google Cloud, Amazon, SpaceX, and now potentially Microsoft. Together with the Google I/O launches, this underscores that AI compute deal-making has become a primary competitive lever in frontier model development.
