# The Vatican announced Monday that Pope Leo XIV and Anthropic co-founder Christopher Olah will jointly launch the…

The Vatican announced Monday that Pope Leo XIV and Anthropic co-founder Christopher Olah will jointly launch the pontiff's first encyclical on May 25 — a formal papal document titled around the care of human dignity in the era of AI. The presence of an Anthropic executive at the Vatican is significant and suggests the U.S. pope's AI position may become a flashpoint with the Trump administration's deregulatory stance. It is the first time a major AI company's leadership has co-presented a papal document, marking a new frontier in AI's intersection with global ethics governance.
