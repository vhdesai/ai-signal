# Analytics Vidhya: Top 10 LLM Research Papers of 2026 — DeepMind, Hugging Face, and More

Analytics Vidhya published a curated roundup of the ten most impactful LLM research papers of 2026 so far, drawing from Hugging Face, Google DeepMind, and academic labs. Highlights include Google DeepMind's large-scale manipulation study (10,101 participants), the AI Co-Mathematician collaborative reasoning framework, Cola DLM (distillation for diffusion language models), SteerEval (a new controllability benchmark), FinRetrieval (financial domain RAG), and AdapTime (time-series adaptation). The compilation serves as a useful mid-year benchmark for tracking research trajectory heading into NeurIPS and ICML abstract deadlines.
