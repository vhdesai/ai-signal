# NaRA: Noise-Aware LoRA for parameter-efficient fine-tuning of diffusion LLMs

A new arXiv preprint introduces NaRA, a noise-aware Low-Rank Adaptation method tailored to diffusion-based language models. Early results suggest meaningful gains in adaptation efficiency for the emerging class of diffusion LLMs, a category gaining attention as alternatives to autoregressive architectures.
