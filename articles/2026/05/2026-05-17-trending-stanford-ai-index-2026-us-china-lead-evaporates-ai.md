# 💜 TRENDING Stanford AI Index 2026: US-China Lead Evaporates; AI Agents Reach 77% Real-World Task Success

Stanford's ninth annual AI Index, newly highlighted by IEEE Spectrum this morning, documents a field accelerating faster than governance can follow. As of March 2026, Anthropic's leading model holds only a 2.7 percentage point performance edge over the best Chinese model — a gap that could close in a single release cycle. AI agents' success rate on real-world tasks jumped from 20% in 2025 to 77.3% in 2026, while SWE-bench coding scores surged from 60% to near 100% in a single year. The report flags a structural concern: the number of AI researchers moving to the U.S. has dropped 89% since 2017, with an 80% decline in the last year alone.
