# Microsoft + EY Deepen $1B+ Alliance; Scaling Copilot to 400,000 Enterprise Users  New

Microsoft and EY deepened a $1 billion-plus joint initiative to move enterprises from AI pilots to full-scale transformation. EY reports 15% productivity gains, 94% monthly Copilot adoption, and 95% faster finance-operations lead times after deploying M365 Copilot to 150,000 employees — and is now expanding to 400,000+ staff on the new M365 E7 SKU. The post positions Copilot, Azure Foundry, Fabric, and Azure together as Microsoft's enterprise AI differentiation strategy.
