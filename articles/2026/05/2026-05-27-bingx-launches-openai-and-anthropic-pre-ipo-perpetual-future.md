# BingX Launches OpenAI and Anthropic Pre-IPO Perpetual Futures

Crypto exchange BingX added OpenAI and Anthropic to its Pre-IPO Trading Zone via USDT-margined perpetual futures, with reference prices of roughly $1,600 for OpenAI and $1,800 for Anthropic. The contracts reference an $852 billion OpenAI post-money valuation and Anthropic's $380 billion February round. BingX joins HTX, Binance, and OKX in a pre-IPO derivatives category that has become standard infrastructure for retail exposure to frontier AI equity.
