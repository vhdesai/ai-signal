# Canada dismisses tech firms' warnings of 'back doors' to devices

Canadian regulators dismissed tech-industry warnings that a new lawful-access framework would amount to mandated device "back doors," setting up a fresh transatlantic encryption fight. The decision matters for AI: as more inference and sensitive workloads move on-device (cf. Apple above), lawful-access rules at the OS and device layer become a key constraint on enterprise and consumer AI privacy postures.
