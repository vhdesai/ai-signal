# VeriTrace: evolving mental models for deep-research agents

Introduces an architecture letting long-running research agents maintain a verifiable, evidence-cited "mental model" of the task. Targets the core failure mode of current deep-research products: hallucinated synthesis in multi-hour runs. A direct attack on the reliability ceiling currently holding back enterprise deployment.
