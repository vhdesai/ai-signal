# BNP Paribas, Mistral expand partnership for cyber-focused AI defense

BNP Paribas CIO Marc Camus said the eurozone's largest bank is expanding its Mistral partnership to build defenses against cybersecurity-focused frontier AI such as Anthropic's restricted Mythos. Mistral is building a dedicated cyber-focused model for European banks locked out of Mythos. The deal extends Mistral embedment across BNP's retail, compliance, and investment-banking units.
