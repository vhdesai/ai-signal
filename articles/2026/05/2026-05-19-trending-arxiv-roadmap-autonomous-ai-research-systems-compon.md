# Trending arXiv Roadmap: Autonomous AI Research Systems — Components & User Guide

A large multi-author team (Kong, Sun, Chow, Li, Lin, Zhang, Wang, Liu, Chua, Ooi and others) published a comprehensive roadmap for autonomous AI research systems, covering literature ingestion, hypothesis generation, experiment scheduling, and paper-writing automation. The paper functions as both a survey of current state-of-the-art and a practical user guide for teams building agentic research tools, accompanied by a public GitHub repository. It is one of the most discussed preprints in the May 19 arXiv cs.AI batch; not yet peer-reviewed. 🛠️ Products & Tools
