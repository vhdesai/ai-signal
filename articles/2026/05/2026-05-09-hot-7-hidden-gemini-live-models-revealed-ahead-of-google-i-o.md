# Hot 7 Hidden Gemini Live Models Revealed Ahead of Google I/O 2026

A teardown of Google App v17.18.22 uncovered a hidden model selector for Gemini Live featuring seven previously undisclosed AI models, including the codenames "Capybara," "Nitrogen," and a dedicated "personalization" variant. Two near-production RC2 models were also found, suggesting Google is preparing to ship user-selectable voice conversation tiers — likely at Google I/O 2026. The discovery implies Google may move toward a tiered Gemini Live offering where users choose between speed and deliberative reasoning, directly competing with OpenAI's o-series and Anthropic's Thinking modes.
