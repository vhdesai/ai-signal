# Isomorphic Labs Closes $2.1B Series B to Accelerate AI Drug Discovery

Isomorphic Labs — the Google DeepMind spinout behind AlphaFold — closed a $2.1 billion Series B led by Thrive Capital. The company is applying AI protein-structure prediction to drug discovery pipelines for major pharmaceutical partners. The round makes Isomorphic one of the best-capitalized AI bio companies globally and signals continued institutional conviction in AI's role in accelerating clinical timelines.
