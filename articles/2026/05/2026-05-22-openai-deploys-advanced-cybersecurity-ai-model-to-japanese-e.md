# OpenAI Deploys Advanced Cybersecurity AI Model to Japanese Enterprises

OpenAI Chief Strategy Officer Jason Kwon confirmed in a Tokyo interview that the company plans to provide its latest AI model — featuring enhanced cybersecurity capabilities and performance described as comparable to Anthropic's Claude Mythos — to select Japanese enterprises. The deployment comes amid growing global concern following Mythos' release last month, which demonstrated an exceptional ability to identify and exploit system vulnerabilities. Kwon said the model is intended to expand defensive cybersecurity capabilities, though questions about potential misuse of such advanced models are intensifying across governments and enterprises worldwide.
