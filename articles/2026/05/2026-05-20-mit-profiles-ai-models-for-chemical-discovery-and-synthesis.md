# MIT profiles AI models for chemical discovery and synthesis planning

MIT News profiled Connor Coley’s work building machine-learning models that can evaluate large chemical search spaces, design compounds and predict synthetic reaction pathways. The research fits the broader AI-for-science wave highlighted by Google DeepMind’s Co-Scientist work. It also shows how AI value in life sciences may accrue through workflow integration, not only through headline model performance.
