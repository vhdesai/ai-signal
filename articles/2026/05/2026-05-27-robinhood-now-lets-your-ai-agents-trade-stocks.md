# Robinhood now lets your AI agents trade stocks

Robinhood launched agentic-trading support, allowing users to create a separate, balance-limited account that AI agents (Claude, Cursor, etc.) can use to place trades. Users receive notifications of every trade, can pre-approve certain orders, and Robinhood has layered in fraud detection. The company also previewed an agentic credit card.
