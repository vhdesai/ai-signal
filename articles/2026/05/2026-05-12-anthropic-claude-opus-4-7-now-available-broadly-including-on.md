# Anthropic Claude Opus 4.7 Now Available Broadly, Including on Microsoft 365 Copilot

Claude Opus 4.7, launched April 16, is now available on Microsoft 365 Copilot, Palantir AIP (including IL2/IL4 government enrollments), and broadly via API. The flagship model triples vision resolution to ~3.75 megapixels, scores 70% on CursorBench (vs. 58% for 4.6), achieves 90.9% on BigLaw Bench, and introduces a new "xhigh" reasoning effort tier. A new tokenizer increases token usage 1.0–1.35× with no price change.
