# Trump Postpones AI Executive Order: "I Didn't Like Certain Aspects"  Breaking

President Trump postponed the signing of a long-anticipated AI executive order that would have created a voluntary framework for government pre-release evaluation of frontier AI models, citing U.S. competitiveness concerns against China. Axios reported the delay followed lobbying from Elon Musk, Mark Zuckerberg, and ex-adviser David Sacks; Musk denied the characterization on X. The order was tied to concerns sparked by Anthropic's Mythos offensive-security model and would have given the National Cyber Director two months to define a frontier model-review framework.
