# Elon Musk's xAI is reportedly operating nearly 50 gas turbines without proper environmental permits at its Memphis,…

Elon Musk's xAI is reportedly operating nearly 50 gas turbines without proper environmental permits at its Memphis, Tennessee data center — a site now under regulatory scrutiny. Environmental groups and local officials have raised concerns about air quality impacts. The story adds to a broader pattern: as AI infrastructure energy demands accelerate, data center siting and power sourcing are becoming material ESG and regulatory risk factors. Stanford's 2026 AI Index separately confirmed that training a single frontier model can now generate upwards of 72,000–140,000 tons of CO₂ equivalent.
