# OpenAI Launches ChatGPT Personal Finance — Connect 12,000+ Banks via Plaid

OpenAI launched a personal finance experience within ChatGPT, allowing U.S. Plus and Pro subscribers to securely connect bank accounts and credit cards from over 12,000 financial institutions via Plaid. The feature enables users to ask natural-language questions about spending patterns, track budgets, and receive AI-generated financial insights without leaving the ChatGPT interface. OpenAI emphasized privacy-first design, with account data processed in-session and not used to train models. The rollout positions ChatGPT as a direct competitor to fintech AI tools like Cleo and Monarch Money. 📈 TRENDING 🆕
