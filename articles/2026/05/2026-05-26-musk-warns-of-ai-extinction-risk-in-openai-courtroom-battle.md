# Musk warns of AI extinction risk in OpenAI courtroom battle

From the Musk v. Altman post-verdict proceedings in Oakland, Musk used the courtroom platform to argue frontier AI poses an extinction-level risk and that OpenAI's for-profit conversion increases the danger. The remarks come days after the advisory jury ruled Musk waited too long to sue, a decision adopted by Judge Yvonne Gonzalez Rogers.
