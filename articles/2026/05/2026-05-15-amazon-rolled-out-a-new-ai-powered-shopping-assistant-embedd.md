# Amazon rolled out a new AI-powered shopping assistant embedded directly into its search bar, built on the Alexa+…

Amazon rolled out a new AI-powered shopping assistant embedded directly into its search bar, built on the Alexa+ platform. The assistant offers personalized product recommendations, cross-platform shopping automation, and contextual deal surfacing. The launch deepens Amazon's AI-first commerce strategy and positions Alexa+ as an ambient shopping layer — extending beyond voice into core e-commerce search, a meaningful threat to Google Shopping's AI ambitions ahead of I/O 2026.
