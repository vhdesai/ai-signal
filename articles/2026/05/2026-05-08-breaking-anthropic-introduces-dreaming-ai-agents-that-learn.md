# Breaking Anthropic Introduces "Dreaming" — AI Agents That Learn From Their Own Mistakes

Anthropic shipped three major upgrades to its Claude Managed Agents platform: "Dreaming," a self-correction mechanism enabling agents to learn from prior failures without human intervention; outcome-based evaluation; and multi-agent orchestration — both promoted from research preview to public beta. The release collapses previously separate infrastructure layers (memory, evals, and orchestration) into a single managed runtime. VentureBeat flagged that the consolidation play gives Anthropic deep structural leverage inside enterprise AI stacks, a pattern that should concern system integrators building on top of Claude.
