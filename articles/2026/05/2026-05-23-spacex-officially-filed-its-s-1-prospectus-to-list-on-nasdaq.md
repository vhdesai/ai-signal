# SpaceX officially filed its S-1 prospectus to list on Nasdaq under ticker SPCX, with Goldman Sachs as lead underwriter

SpaceX officially filed its S-1 prospectus to list on Nasdaq under ticker SPCX, with Goldman Sachs as lead underwriter. The company was valued at $1.25T after merging with Elon Musk's xAI earlier this year, and the expected IPO valuation is approximately $1.75T. The filing revealed that xAI burned $6.4B last year — a figure that will inform how investors assess the combined entity's AI spending trajectory. Meanwhile, xAI is simultaneously acquiring $2.8B in additional data center generator capacity, signaling major infrastructure expansion. Source: CNBC, TechCrunch (May 20–22, 2026)
