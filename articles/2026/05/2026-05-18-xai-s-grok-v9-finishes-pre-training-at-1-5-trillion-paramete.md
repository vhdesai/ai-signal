# xAI's Grok V9 Finishes Pre-Training at 1.5 Trillion Parameters

xAI confirmed V9 — at 1.5 trillion parameters, roughly triple Grok 4.3 — has completed pre-training. Elon Musk said a public release is 3–4 weeks out, pending supervised fine-tuning and reinforcement-learning phases that will incorporate Cursor coding data. The model is positioned to compete head-on with OpenAI's GPT-5.5 and Google's Gemini 4.0 on agentic coding benchmarks.
