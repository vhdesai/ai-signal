# OpenRouter raises $113M Series B at $1.3B valuation; Modal raises $355M at $4.65B

OpenRouter's Series B was led by CapitalG, with valuation more than doubling as usage grew 5× in six months to 25T tokens/week. Modal raised $355M at $4.65B to build out a production cloud for inference, agent runtimes, RL, and sandboxed environments. Both rounds underline persistent investor appetite for the AI-infra middle layer.
