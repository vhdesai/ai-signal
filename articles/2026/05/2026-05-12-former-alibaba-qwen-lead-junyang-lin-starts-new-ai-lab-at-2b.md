# Former Alibaba Qwen Lead Junyang Lin Starts New AI Lab at ~$2B Valuation

Junyang Lin, former lead researcher of Alibaba's Qwen models, is seeking several hundred million dollars at roughly $2B valuation for his new AI lab. Chinese VC firms Gaorong Ventures and HongShan are in talks to fund the round — another sign of accelerating talent diffusion across China's frontier AI ecosystem.
