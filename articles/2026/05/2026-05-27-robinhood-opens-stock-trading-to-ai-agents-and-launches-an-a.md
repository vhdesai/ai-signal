# Robinhood opens stock trading to AI agents and launches an agentic credit card — TechCrunch, May 27, 2026 Robinhood…

Robinhood opens stock trading to AI agents and launches an agentic credit card — TechCrunch, May 27, 2026 Robinhood users can now create a dedicated account for an AI agent, fund it with a pre-loaded wallet, and let the agent read and analyze the portfolio to propose and execute trades within the wallet's balance. Users receive notifications on every trade, can require pre-execution approval on selected orders, and Robinhood has stood up a fraud-detection team specifically for the agent surface. A new agentic credit card launches alongside it — one of the first regulated-finance environments to formally permit agent-initiated, principal-funded trades.
