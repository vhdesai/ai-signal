# Khosla Ventures Bets $10M on Synthetic AI's Autonomous Bookkeeping Platform

Khosla Ventures led a $10M seed round in Synthetic AI, co-founded by Ian Crosby (former Bench.co CEO), which is building an agentic AI system that autonomously performs end-to-end bookkeeping for SMBs. The system ingests bank feeds, invoices, and receipts, then applies LLM reasoning to classify transactions, flag anomalies, and generate financial statements with minimal human review. Crosby's prior venture, Bench, was a high-profile AI bookkeeping failure that shut down in 2024 — making this a notable second-attempt by the same founder with an improved agentic architecture. The investment reflects continued VC conviction that financial operations (FinOps) is one of the highest-ROI applications of enterprise AI agents. 🛠️ Products & Tools
