# Amazon Alexa+ Now Generates AI Podcasts on Any Topic

Amazon launched AI-generated podcasts within its Alexa+ assistant, allowing users to request a full conversational audio episode on any topic — featuring two AI co-hosts and sourced from licensed media content. The feature competes directly with Google NotebookLM's "Deep Dive" format, which went viral in 2025. Alexa+ runs Claude under the hood, and the podcast feature represents Amazon's clearest play to recapture consumer time-on-device lost to ChatGPT voice and Gemini Live over the past 18 months.
