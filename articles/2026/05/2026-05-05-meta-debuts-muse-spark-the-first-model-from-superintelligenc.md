# Meta debuts Muse Spark, the first model from Superintelligence Labs

Meta released Muse Spark, marking its "first step" in the AI overhaul Mark Zuckerberg launched after acquiring a stake in Scale AI and installing Alexandr Wang as Chief AI Officer. The mid-size model reportedly matches reasoning quality with over an order of magnitude less compute than Llama 4 Maverick, signaling Meta is prioritizing efficiency over raw scale. It is the inaugural deliverable from a stack rebuilt almost from scratch in nine months at a reported cost of $14.3B.
