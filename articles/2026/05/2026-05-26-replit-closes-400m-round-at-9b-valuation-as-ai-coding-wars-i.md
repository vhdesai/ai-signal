# Replit Closes $400M Round at $9B Valuation as AI Coding Wars Intensify

Replit tripled its valuation from $3B to $9B in a Georgian-led Series D, expanding its "vibe-coding" platform and Agent 3 capabilities into mobile app generation. The round arrives alongside reports that Cursor (Anysphere) is now in talks at a $50B valuation off a $2B ARR run-rate, underscoring that AI-native coding tools are now the most heavily funded application category in enterprise software.
