# Anthropic raises $65B Series H at $965B valuation, overtaking OpenAI

Anthropic announced a $65B Series H at a $965B post-money valuation, vaulting past OpenAI's $852B March mark and making it the most valuable AI company in the world. The round was led by Altimeter, Dragoneer, Greenoaks, and Sequoia, with $15B in previously committed hyperscaler capital plus first-time strategic participation from Micron, Samsung, and SK Hynix. Anthropic disclosed a $47B revenue run rate and confirmed five-gigawatt compute deals with Amazon, Google/Broadcom, and SpaceX.
