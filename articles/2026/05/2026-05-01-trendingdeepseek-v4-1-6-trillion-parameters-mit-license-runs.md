# TrendingDeepSeek V4: 1.6 Trillion Parameters, MIT License, Runs on Huawei Ascend — Reshaping China's AI Stack

DeepSeek's V4 model—featuring 1.6 trillion parameters (V4-Pro) and a 1 million-token context window—was released under an MIT open-source license and optimized for Huawei's Ascend 950PR processors. The launch triggered a surge in Ascend chip orders from ByteDance ($5.6B committed alone), Alibaba, and Tencent, propelling Huawei to project $12 billion in AI chip revenue in 2026—a 60% jump. The model is widely viewed as the most strategically significant Chinese AI release since R1, cementing a bifurcated global AI chip market: one built on Nvidia silicon, the other on Huawei Ascend. DeepSeek is simultaneously targeting a $20 billion first external funding round. 5 · Academic Research
