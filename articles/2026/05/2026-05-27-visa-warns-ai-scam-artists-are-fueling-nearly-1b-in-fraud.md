# Visa warns AI scam artists are fueling nearly $1B in fraud

Visa warned that AI scam artists are helping fuel nearly $1 billion in scams. The report highlights how generative AI can scale fraud through more convincing phishing, impersonation, and social-engineering campaigns. Financial institutions and platforms will need stronger AI-assisted fraud detection and user education as scam quality rises.
