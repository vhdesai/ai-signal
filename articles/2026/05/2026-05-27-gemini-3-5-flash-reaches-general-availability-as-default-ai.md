# Gemini 3.5 Flash Reaches General Availability as Default AI Mode Search Model

Google's fastest frontier model is now generally available across Google Antigravity, the Gemini API, AI Studio, Android Studio, and the Gemini app, and has replaced the prior default in AI Mode Search, which has surpassed one billion monthly users. Flash reportedly processes roughly 280 tokens per second versus 60–70 for GPT-5.5 and Claude Opus 4.7, while pricing at less than half the cost of comparable frontier models. Pichai used Google I/O to argue that workloads moved to 3.5 Flash could save large enterprises over a billion dollars annually.
