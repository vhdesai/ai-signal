# TriSplat: simulation-ready feed-forward 3D scene reconstruction

A feed-forward reconstructor that turns sparse images into physics-compatible 3D scenes in a single pass, going beyond the visual-only Gaussian splats common today. Bridges photoreal reconstruction with robotics and AV simulators, eliminating a costly hand-tuning step. Directly applicable to humanoid-robot training pipelines and world-model research.
