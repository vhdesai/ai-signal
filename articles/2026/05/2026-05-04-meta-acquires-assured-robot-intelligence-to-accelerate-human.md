# Meta acquires Assured Robot Intelligence to accelerate humanoid AI ambitions

Meta has acquired Assured Robot Intelligence, a small San Diego-based humanoid robotics startup, to strengthen the AI models powering its embodied agents. The deal extends Meta's robotics push beyond research and signals continued willingness to buy talent and IP in the increasingly competitive humanoid stack. It follows similar moves from rivals consolidating around physical AI as the next platform.
