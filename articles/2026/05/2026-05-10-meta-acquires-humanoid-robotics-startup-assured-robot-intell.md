# Meta Acquires Humanoid Robotics Startup Assured Robot Intelligence

Meta acquired Assured Robot Intelligence, a humanoid robotics startup founded a year ago by Xiaolong Wang. The full team is joining Meta Superintelligence Labs to train physical AI agents that learn from human experience data — extending Meta's AI ambitions from language models into embodied intelligence. The acquisition accelerates Meta's physical-world AI roadmap alongside its established Llama model family and AI infrastructure build-out with Nvidia. (Source: The Neuron AI) 🖥️
