# Cerebras IPO: Seeking $3.5B at $26.6B Valuation

AI chip maker Cerebras Systems disclosed IPO terms: up to $3.5B raised by selling 28 million shares at $115–$125 each, targeting a $26.6B market cap at the high end. Sales jumped 76% in 2025 to $290.3M, and the company turned an $87.9M profit after losing $485M the year prior. Cerebras's wafer-scale chips offer a differentiated approach to AI inference compared to Nvidia's GPU-centric architecture. (Source: SiliconANGLE)
