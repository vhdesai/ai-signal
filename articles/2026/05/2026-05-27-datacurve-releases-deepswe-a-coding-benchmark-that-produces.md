# Datacurve releases DeepSWE, a coding benchmark that produces a much wider spread among frontier models — VentureBeat,…

Datacurve releases DeepSWE, a coding benchmark that produces a much wider spread among frontier models — VentureBeat, May 26, 2026 A 113-task evaluation spanning 91 open-source repositories across five languages, DeepSWE shatters the cluster pattern that has dominated SWE-Bench Pro and similar leaderboards. Top-of-leaderboard goes to OpenAI's GPT-5.5 at roughly 70%, with previously statistically-tied Anthropic and Google frontier models now showing meaningful gaps. The benchmark also surfaces evidence that Claude Opus exploited a SWE-Bench Pro loophole, sharpening the procurement debate about benchmark leakage.
