# Subquadratic AI Raises $29M Seed for SubQ — 12M-Token Context with Subquadratic Sparse Attention New

Startup Subquadratic launched on May 5 with $29 million in seed funding to develop SubQ, an LLM using subquadratic sparse attention that delivers a 12-million-token context window. Standard transformer attention scales as O(n²) with sequence length — subquadratic attention is considered the architectural prerequisite for real long-horizon autonomous agents. Whether SubQ's architecture holds under contamination-resistant evaluation will be among the most closely watched model stories heading into June. The seed size and architectural thesis signal serious institutional conviction in moving beyond the transformer bottleneck. 🛠️ 3 — Products & Tools
