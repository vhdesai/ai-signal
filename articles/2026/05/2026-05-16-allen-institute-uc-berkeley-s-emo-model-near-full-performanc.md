# Allen Institute + UC Berkeley's EMO Model: Near-Full Performance Using 12.5% of Experts

Researchers from the Allen Institute for AI and UC Berkeley published results on EMO (Expert Mixture Optimization), a Mixture-of-Experts model whose experts specialize in content domains rather than token types. The architecture allows three-quarters of expert parameters to be stripped out while retaining near-full benchmark performance — a potential step-change in inference cost for deployed MoE systems. The result has immediate implications for on-device and edge AI deployments.
