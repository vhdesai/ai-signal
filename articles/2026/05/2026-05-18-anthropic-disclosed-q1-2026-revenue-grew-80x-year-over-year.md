# Anthropic disclosed Q1 2026 revenue grew 80x year-over-year, pushing ARR above $44B in what observers called "AI's…

Anthropic disclosed Q1 2026 revenue grew 80x year-over-year, pushing ARR above $44B in what observers called "AI's biggest single week of 2026" (May 6–7). The figures were announced alongside a $200 billion Google Cloud contract and a landmark compute deal giving Anthropic exclusive access to SpaceX's entire Colossus 1 supercomputer (220,000+ NVIDIA GPUs, 300MW). The company also doubled Claude Code rate limits for all paid plans overnight. These numbers cement Anthropic as one of the fastest-growing enterprise software companies in history.
