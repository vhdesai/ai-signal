# Cursor 2.5 and Replit Agent Push Coding Tools Past $2B ARR

Cursor shipped Composer 2.5 on May 18 and is reportedly in talks for a fresh round at a ~$50B valuation, after hitting $2B ARR in Q1. Replit tripled its valuation to $9B with a $400M Series D and is publicly targeting $1B ARR by year-end. The AI coding-tool race has bifurcated: desktop-first IDE (Cursor) versus browser-native agent platform (Replit).
