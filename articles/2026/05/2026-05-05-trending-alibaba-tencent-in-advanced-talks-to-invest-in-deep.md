# 💜 TRENDING Alibaba & Tencent in Advanced Talks to Invest in DeepSeek at $20B Valuation

Alibaba and Tencent are in advanced discussions to invest in DeepSeek at a valuation of $20 billion — double the $10B figure circulated earlier in Q1. The deal would be DeepSeek's first acceptance of major external funding and coincides with preparations for a V4 model launch. DeepSeek V4 (1.6T parameters, 1M-token context, MIT license) has already triggered a scramble by ByteDance, Tencent, and Alibaba for Huawei's Ascend 950 chips, with V4 specifically optimized to run on domestic Chinese hardware — a direct signal of China's accelerating AI hardware sovereignty strategy.
