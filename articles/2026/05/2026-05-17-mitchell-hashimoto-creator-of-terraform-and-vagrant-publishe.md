# Mitchell Hashimoto (creator of Terraform and Vagrant) published widely-shared commentary arguing that many companies…

Mitchell Hashimoto (creator of Terraform and Vagrant) published widely-shared commentary arguing that many companies are currently in a state of "AI psychosis" — building meaningless AI workflows without clear purpose. His key thesis: "AI will only amplify the gap between those who know what they want and those who don't." The post drew 1,574 HN points and 811 comments, making it one of the most discussed AI pieces this week and reflecting growing pushback against hollow productivity theater in enterprise AI adoption.
