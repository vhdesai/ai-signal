# DeepMind's AlphaProof Nexus solves nine open Erdős problems

DeepMind's AlphaProof Nexus autonomously produced Lean-verified proofs for nine longstanding Erdős problems — some unsolved for 56 years — alongside 44 open OEIS conjectures and a 15-year-old algebraic-geometry problem. The system used only a few hundred dollars of compute and discovered a novel algorithmic parameter in optimization theory that no human had previously found. A Breaking Research
