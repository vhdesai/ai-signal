# Alibaba and Tencent pivot from chatbots to embodied AI and robotics

South China Morning Post reported that Alibaba and Tencent are leading a pivot from chatbots toward embodied AI for robotics. The shift shows China's major platforms pushing AI from software assistants into physical-world automation. Robotics and embodied agents could become a key differentiator for companies with cloud, hardware, data, and industrial ecosystems.
