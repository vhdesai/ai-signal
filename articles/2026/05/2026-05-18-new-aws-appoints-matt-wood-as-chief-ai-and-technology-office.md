# New AWS Appoints Matt Wood as Chief AI and Technology Officer

Matt Wood — a 14-year AWS AI/ML veteran who left in 2024 for PwC's CTIO role — is returning to Amazon Web Services in a newly created Chief AI and Technology Officer position, reporting to CMO Julia White. The hire is framed internally as both customer-facing and tied to AWS's AI-services innovation roadmap. The move signals Amazon doubling down on generative-AI customer engagement as it competes with Azure's OpenAI partnership and Google Cloud's agentic services portfolio.
