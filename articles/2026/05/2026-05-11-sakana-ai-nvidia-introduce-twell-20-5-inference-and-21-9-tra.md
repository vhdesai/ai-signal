# Sakana AI & NVIDIA Introduce TwELL: 20.5% Inference and 21.9% Training Speedup in LLMs

Sakana AI and NVIDIA jointly published research on TwELL, a technique that exploits activation sparsity in transformer models via custom sparse-CUDA kernels, achieving 20.5% faster inference and 21.9% faster training while retaining ~99.5% activation sparsity at near-zero quality loss. The approach is hardware-efficient and designed to run on existing NVIDIA GPU infrastructure without retraining from scratch. If the results hold up at scale, TwELL could meaningfully reduce inference costs across the industry. This is Sakana AI's highest-profile collaboration with NVIDIA to date. ________________________________
