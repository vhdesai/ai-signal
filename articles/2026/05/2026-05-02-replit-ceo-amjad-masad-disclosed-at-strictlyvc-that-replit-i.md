# Replit CEO Amjad Masad disclosed at StrictlyVC that Replit is running at a $1 billion annual revenue run-rate and has…

Replit CEO Amjad Masad disclosed at StrictlyVC that Replit is running at a $1 billion annual revenue run-rate and has declined acquisition offers, preferring to remain independent. Masad commented on Cursor's reported discussions about a $60 billion acquisition by SpaceX, framing the AI coding assistant landscape as increasingly strategic for major players. Replit is also engaged in a broader dispute with Apple over App Store policies affecting its mobile coding tools. The comments underscore the intensifying competition and valuation escalation in AI developer tooling.
