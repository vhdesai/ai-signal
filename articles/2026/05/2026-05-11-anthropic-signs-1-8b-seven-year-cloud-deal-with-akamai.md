# Anthropic Signs $1.8B Seven-Year Cloud Deal With Akamai

Anthropic has signed a seven-year, $1.8 billion cloud infrastructure agreement with Akamai Technologies, Bloomberg and Reuters reported on May 11. The deal represents one of the largest AI infrastructure commitments of 2026 and gives Anthropic dedicated edge-computing capacity through Akamai's global network of over 4,000 points of presence. The partnership is likely designed to reduce Anthropic's dependence on hyperscalers (AWS, Google Cloud) and improve latency for enterprise deployments of Claude. Combined with NVIDIA's equity stake and yesterday's Colossus compute arrangement with xAI, Anthropic is rapidly diversifying its infrastructure stack.
