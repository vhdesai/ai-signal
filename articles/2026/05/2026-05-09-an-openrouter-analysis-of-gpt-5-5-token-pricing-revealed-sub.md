# An OpenRouter analysis of GPT-5.5 token pricing revealed substantial cost increases compared to GPT-5, sparking…

An OpenRouter analysis of GPT-5.5 token pricing revealed substantial cost increases compared to GPT-5, sparking developer debate about the economics of frontier model adoption. The post garnered 134 points on Hacker News, with developers highlighting the challenge of building cost-efficient products on top of OpenAI's latest tier. The pricing tension is arriving just as OpenAI faces internal revenue-miss pressure: CFO Sarah Friar recently warned leadership that revenue growth may not cover future compute contracts, even as the company projects $14B in losses in 2026 alone.
