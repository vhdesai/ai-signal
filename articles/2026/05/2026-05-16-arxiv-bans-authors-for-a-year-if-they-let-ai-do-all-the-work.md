# ArXiv Bans Authors for a Year If They Let AI Do All the Work

Academic preprint repository ArXiv has announced a new policy banning authors for one year if they are found to have used AI to generate the entirety of a submitted paper without meaningful intellectual contribution. The policy draws a clear line between acceptable AI-assisted writing — grammar corrections, formatting, literature queries — and wholesale AI authorship. The move reflects growing anxiety among research institutions about the integrity of the scientific record as LLMs become capable of generating superficially plausible but technically shallow papers at scale.
