# New Perplexity Personal Computer Now Generally Available for All Mac Users

Perplexity opened its Personal Computer product — an OS-level AI assistant for macOS — to all users after a restricted beta period. The product integrates AI-assisted search, document summarization, and task completion directly into macOS workflows, competing with Apple Intelligence and Microsoft Copilot for macOS. The general availability launch comes amid Snap's reported exit from its $400M Perplexity partnership deal and signals Perplexity's push to capture the desktop AI assistant market before Apple fully deploys its iOS 27 Extensions framework.
