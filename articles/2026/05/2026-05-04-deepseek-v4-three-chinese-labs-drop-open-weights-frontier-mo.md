# DeepSeek V4 + Three Chinese Labs Drop Open-Weights Frontier Models in 12 Days

Four Chinese labs — Z.ai (GLM-5.1), MiniMax (M2.7), Kimi (K2.6), and DeepSeek (V4) — released open-weights coding models in a 12-day sprint, each matching Western frontier capabilities at under one-third the price of Claude Opus 4.7. The wave underscores China's accelerating open-source AI strategy and intensifying pressure on US labs to justify premium pricing. These models run on consumer hardware, directly competing with Cursor, GitHub Copilot, and Claude Code for developer mindshare. 🛠️ Products & Tools
