# Tencent launches Marvis — an OS-level AI assistant with cross-device control and local privacy mode

AIbase reports that Tencent launched Marvis, an AI assistant operating at the OS level with support for cross-device control and a local-privacy execution mode designed for sensitive enterprise contexts. Zhipu AI also officially launched its AutoClaw mobile app with cloud-and-local dual-mode AI execution on the same day.
