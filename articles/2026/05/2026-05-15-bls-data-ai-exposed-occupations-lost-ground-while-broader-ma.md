# BLS Data: AI-Exposed Occupations Lost Ground While Broader Market Grew

U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics data show employment in 18 AI-exposed occupations fell 0.2% between May 2024 and May 2025, while the broader U.S. labor market grew 0.8% over the same period — the clearest signal yet of AI-driven labor displacement in specific job categories. The data land as Big Tech reported Q1 2026 layoffs of 81,747 workers (likely undercounting by at least 50%), with AI cited as the top reason for cuts for the second consecutive month, per tracking firm Challenger, Gray & Christmas.
