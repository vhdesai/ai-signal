# Missouri AI Safeguards Bill Killed 11-0 in Committee, Same Day Iowa Enacts AI Medical Law

A Missouri House committee voted 11-0 to kill State Sen. Joe Nicola's AI liability bill — even after the sponsor removed chatbot restrictions and developer liability provisions in response to White House feedback. The unanimous rejection on enforcement and drafting grounds, on the same day Iowa enacted a meaningful AI restriction, illustrates the fragmented state-by-state AI governance landscape created by the Trump administration's December 2025 executive order preempting state AI rules.
