# Trump and Xi Discuss AI Guardrails — Nvidia H200 Chip Exports to China Remain Unresolved

President Trump discussed potential AI safety guardrails with Chinese President Xi Jinping during their Beijing summit, signaling a rare area of bilateral cooperation amid broader tech competition. However, the meeting ended without a signed chip deal — Nvidia H200 deliveries to approximately 10 cleared Chinese firms (including Alibaba and Tencent) remain stalled pending final export license approvals. The shift in rhetoric from the Trump administration, which initially prioritized innovation over any AI regulation, marks a notable evolution in posture as the US-China AI rivalry intensifies.
