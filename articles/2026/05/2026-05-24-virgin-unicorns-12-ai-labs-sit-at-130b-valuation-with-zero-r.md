# "Virgin Unicorns": 12 AI Labs Sit at ~$130B Valuation With Zero Revenue

AI economist Oren Etzioni's analysis catalogs 12 AI labs that have collectively raised more than $29 billion at a combined valuation approaching $130 billion — without shipping a single customer-purchasable product. Top of the list: Project Prometheus ($38B, Bezos/Bajaj), Safe Superintelligence ($32B, Sutskever), Thinking Machines Lab ($12B, Murati), and Reflection AI ($8B). The piece raises hard questions about whether the "research-lab-as-pre-company" thesis can repeat OpenAI's trajectory at scale.
