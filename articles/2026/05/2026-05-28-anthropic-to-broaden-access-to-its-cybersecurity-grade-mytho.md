# Anthropic to broaden access to its cybersecurity-grade Mythos model in coming weeks

Anthropic confirmed it will expand access to Claude Mythos — its market-moving cybersecurity-capable model — to all customers in the coming weeks. Mythos has so far been restricted to Project Glasswing partners (AWS, Apple, Broadcom, Cisco, CrowdStrike, Google, JPMorganChase, Linux Foundation, Microsoft, NVIDIA, Palo Alto Networks), where it has surfaced more than 10,000 vulnerabilities in its first month. The widened release raises new dual-use questions for regulators.
