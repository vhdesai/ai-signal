# Colorado, Connecticut & California Advance Distinct AI Governance Frameworks

Three major U.S. states are advancing distinct AI regulatory frameworks, creating a complex compliance landscape for enterprise AI deployments. Colorado, Connecticut, and California are each pushing different governance approaches — ranging from risk-based disclosure requirements to algorithmic impact assessments. Compliance professionals are navigating a patchwork that has no clear federal unifying standard, with some obligations paused and others deepening. Companies operating nationally should review state-specific AI risk obligations before Q3.
