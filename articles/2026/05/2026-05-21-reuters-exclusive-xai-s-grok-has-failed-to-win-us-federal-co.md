# Reuters exclusive: xAI's Grok has failed to win US federal contracts, undercutting SpaceX IPO pitch

Reuters, citing seven federal employees, three contracting experts, and a review of agency AI inventory records, reports that Grok has been largely absent from US government AI deployments. Of more than 400 publicly identified federal AI uses naming a specific vendor, only a handful name xAI. The finding directly undercuts the SpaceX IPO narrative framing xAI as a multi-trillion-dollar government AI services growth lever.
