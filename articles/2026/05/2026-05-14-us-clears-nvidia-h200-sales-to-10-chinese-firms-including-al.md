# US Clears Nvidia H200 Sales to 10 Chinese Firms Including Alibaba and Tencent

The US government cleared Nvidia's H200 AI chip for sale to 10 Chinese technology companies including Alibaba and Tencent, but deliveries remain on hold pending guidance from Beijing. The clearance reflects the ongoing tension in the US-China chip policy: the administration wants to use export access as geopolitical leverage while the technology industry presses for revenue access to the Chinese market. Nvidia has separately said it has "largely conceded" China's AI chip market to Huawei in the high-performance tier.
