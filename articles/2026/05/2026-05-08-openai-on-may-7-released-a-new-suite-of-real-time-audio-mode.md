# OpenAI on May 7 released a new suite of real-time audio models for developers: GPT-Realtime-2 (the first voice model…

OpenAI on May 7 released a new suite of real-time audio models for developers: GPT-Realtime-2 (the first voice model with GPT-5-class reasoning, featuring a 128K context window and parallel tool calls); GPT-Realtime-Translate (live speech translation across 70+ input languages into 13 output languages); and GPT-Realtime-Whisper (streaming speech-to-text that transcribes live as the speaker talks). Production partners already deploying the suite include Zillow (voice home-search), Priceline (full trip management by voice), and Deutsche Telekom (multilingual customer support). The models represent a significant shift from call-and-response voice to voice agents that can reason, take action, and carry conversations across languages in real time.
