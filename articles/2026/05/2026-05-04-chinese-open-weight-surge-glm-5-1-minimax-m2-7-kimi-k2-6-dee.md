# Chinese Open-Weight Surge: GLM-5.1, MiniMax M2.7, Kimi K2.6, DeepSeek V4

Four Chinese labs released open-weights coding models within a 12-day window — Z.ai's GLM-5.1, MiniMax M2.7, Moonshot's Kimi K2.6, and DeepSeek V4 (with 1M context) — each matching Western frontier capability on agentic engineering benchmarks at a fraction of the inference cost. DeepSeek V4, available via V4-Pro and V4-Flash, additionally supports open weights, raising significant competitive pressure on pricing. DeepSeek is separately reported to be in talks to raise capital at a $45B valuation.
