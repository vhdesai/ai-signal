# NextEra's $67 Billion Dominion Merger — AI Power Demand Drives Largest Utility Deal in US History

NextEra Energy announced a $67 billion acquisition of Dominion Energy — the largest utility merger in US history — with AI-driven power demand explicitly cited as the primary strategic rationale. Data center electricity consumption, overwhelmingly concentrated in AI training and inference workloads, has forced a fundamental restructuring of the US power grid. The deal signals that energy infrastructure is now as strategically contested as semiconductor supply chains: whoever controls reliable power delivery at hyperscale effectively controls the ceiling on AI capacity. 🎓
