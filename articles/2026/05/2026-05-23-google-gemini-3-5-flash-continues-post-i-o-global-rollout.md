# Google Gemini 3.5 Flash continues post-I/O global rollout

Gemini 3.5 Flash, announced at I/O on May 19, has continued its rollout through this weekend across Search, the Gemini app, Antigravity, the API, Android Studio, and Workspace. Benchmark scores cited by Google — Terminal-Bench 2.1 at 76.2%, GDPval-AA at 1656 Elo, MCP Atlas at 83.6% — reportedly outperform Gemini 3.1 Pro at roughly 4x the output speed of frontier competitors. 2.
