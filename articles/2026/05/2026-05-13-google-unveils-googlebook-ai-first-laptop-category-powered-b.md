# Google Unveils "Googlebook" — AI-First Laptop Category Powered by Gemini

At the Android Show, Google announced Googlebook, a new laptop form-factor "designed for Gemini Intelligence," shipping Fall 2026. Headline features include Magic Pointer (point at anything to invoke Gemini), Create My Widget (build on-screen widgets via natural language), and Cast My Apps (run phone apps on laptop with no installs). The thread drew 862 HN points and 1,400+ comments — many debating whether this signals "the end of apps as a concept."
