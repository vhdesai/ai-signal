# Elon Musk Loses OpenAI Lawsuit — Unanimous Jury Verdict in Under Two Hours

A California federal jury in Oakland unanimously rejected all of Elon Musk's claims against OpenAI and CEO Sam Altman after less than two hours of deliberation on May 19. The jury found that Musk had waited too long to file — all claims were barred by the statute of limitations. Musk had accused Altman of converting OpenAI from a nonprofit to a for-profit structure in breach of founding commitments after Musk donated $38 million early in the company's history. The verdict closes a three-week trial that included testimony from Musk, Altman, and Microsoft CEO Satya Nadella. Sam Altman's response on X: "Thank you." With xAI now formally merged into SpaceX as the SpaceXAI division, the Musk vs. OpenAI narrative is effectively closed.
