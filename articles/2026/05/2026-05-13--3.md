Alphabet, Amazon, Meta, and Microsoft are on track to spend a combined $650 billion on AI and data center infrastructure in 2026—with Amazon leading at up to $200B and Alphabet at up to $185B. The buildout is driven by hyperscaler competition for compute dominance to power frontier model training and inference.
