# HotGoogle Reveals 7 Hidden Gemini Live Models Ahead of I/O 2026

A hidden model-selector menu discovered inside Google App v17.18.22 exposes seven previously undisclosed Gemini Live voice AI variants — including codenames "Capybara," "Nitrogen," and a dedicated personalization model. Testing confirms they produce measurably different outputs: four can access the user's live location for weather; one identifies itself as "Gemini 3.1 Pro" rather than the expected Flash Live. The infrastructure for switchable voice AI is already deployed; Google I/O 2026 (May 19) is widely expected to formalize the announcement alongside a broader Gemini 4.0 reveal.
