# Musk vs. Altman Trial: What the Jury Will Decide — A Plain-Language Explainer

With the Musk v. Altman civil trial entering its evidence phase, TechCrunch published a comprehensive explainer on the three core legal questions the jury will decide: (1) whether Altman breached fiduciary duties to Musk as a co-founder during OpenAI's 2023 restructuring; (2) whether OpenAI's conversion from nonprofit to capped-profit violated Musk's original donation agreements; and (3) whether xAI's access to certain OpenAI IP constitutes misappropriation. Legal experts quoted in the piece assess OpenAI as having the stronger position on questions 1 and 3, but note Musk's breach-of-contract claim on question 2 as potentially viable. The trial is expected to run through late June.
