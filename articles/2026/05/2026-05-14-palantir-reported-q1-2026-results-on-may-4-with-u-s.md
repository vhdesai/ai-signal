# Palantir reported Q1 2026 results on May 4 with U.S

Palantir reported Q1 2026 results on May 4 with U.S. revenue growing 104% year-over-year and total revenue up 85%. The company raised full-year 2026 revenue guidance to 71% growth and U.S. commercial revenue guidance to 120% growth — crushing analyst consensus. Palantir's AIP platform continues to win new enterprise and defense deployments; the company also joined a U.S. Army "Right to Integrate" hackathon for defense-wide interoperability on May 6.
