# Anthropic Introduces "Dreaming" — AI Agents That Learn From Their Own Mistakes

Anthropic updated its Claude Managed Agents platform with three new capabilities — "dreaming" (a self-correction mechanism that lets agents learn from failures), outcomes tracking, and multi-agent orchestration — moving the latter two from research preview to public beta. The features address what Anthropic calls the hardest problems in production-grade agents: accuracy, learning, and parallelism. The update comes as Anthropic also reported Claude for Legal launched with 12 practice-area plugins and 20+ MCP connectors. 🛡 AI Safety & Policy
