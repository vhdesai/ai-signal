# The International Monetary Fund issued a formal warning on May 7 that advanced AI models with autonomous cybersecurity…

The International Monetary Fund issued a formal warning on May 7 that advanced AI models with autonomous cybersecurity capabilities — such as GPT-5.5-Cyber and Claude Mythos — have the potential to "destabilize" the world economy if exploited at scale. The warning landed on the same day OpenAI unveiled GPT-5.5-Cyber, amplifying calls from security researchers for mandatory government evaluation frameworks for this class of model. The IMF's intervention marks the first time a major multilateral economic institution has directly connected frontier AI capability to macroeconomic stability risk, raising the profile of pending executive action at the White House.
