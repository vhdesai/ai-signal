# BREAKINGOpenAI and Anthropic both launch multibillion-dollar enterprise services ventures

In a same-day announcement, both labs unveiled joint ventures aimed at enterprise AI services. OpenAI's "The Development Company" pairs OpenAI with TPG, Brookfield, Advent, and Bain in a roughly $10B vehicle. Anthropic's $1.5B services venture pairs it with Goldman Sachs, Blackstone, and Hellman & Friedman. Both signal that the labs see professional services and deployment — not just model API revenue — as the next frontier.
