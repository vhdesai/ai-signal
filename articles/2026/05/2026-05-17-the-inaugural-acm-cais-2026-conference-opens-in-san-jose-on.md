# The inaugural ACM CAIS 2026 conference opens in San Jose on May 26 with 61 peer-reviewed research papers and 45 system…

The inaugural ACM CAIS 2026 conference opens in San Jose on May 26 with 61 peer-reviewed research papers and 45 system demos from 115+ institutions including Microsoft, Google, Meta, OpenAI, Stanford, MIT, and CMU. Keynotes include Percy Liang (Stanford / Together AI) and a member of the Anthropic Claude Code team. The conference has partnered with the AI Engineer World's Fair (June 29–July 2, San Francisco). Cornell Tech's inaugural Frontiers of AI Summit runs on May 27 in New York.
