# WSJ Wealth Adviser: AI-chip concentration as a portfolio risk

WSJ's wealth-advisory desk flagged the degree to which "AI" exposure in most diversified portfolios is functionally a concentrated bet on a handful of semiconductor and hyperscaler names. The piece reinforces a growing private-banking conversation about constructing AI exposure with explicit infrastructure, application, and labor-displacement legs. ________________________________ Sources: Business Insider, The Wall Street Journal, WSJ Wealth Adviser, PitchBook News, CIO Dive, The Information, The Information AM, RealClearPolitics, OfficeChai, AOL, NBC News, Florida Today.
