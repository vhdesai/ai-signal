# Pope Leo XIV announced his first papal encyclical, Magnifica Humanitas, will address artificial intelligence, human…

Pope Leo XIV announced his first papal encyclical, Magnifica Humanitas, will address artificial intelligence, human dignity, workers' rights, AI in warfare, and Vatican AI policy — making it the first major religious doctrinal statement on AI governance. The encyclical is expected to carry influence with Catholic-majority governments and international ethics bodies. Source: The AI Track (May 18, 2026)
