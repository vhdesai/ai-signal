# China Formally Requires Government Sign-off Before Top Alibaba/DeepSeek AI Staff Can Travel Abroad

Beijing has codified a previously informal practice into a formal travel-approval requirement for senior AI researchers at Alibaba's Qwen team and DeepSeek. The move structurally raises the cost of US-China research collaboration and effectively turns Chinese frontier-lab talent into a constrained strategic asset. Significant signal for any cross-border M&A or research-partnership planning involving Chinese AI talent.
