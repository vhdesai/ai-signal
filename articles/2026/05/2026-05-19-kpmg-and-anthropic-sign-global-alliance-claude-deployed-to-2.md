# KPMG and Anthropic Sign Global Alliance — Claude Deployed to 276,000 Employees Worldwide

In one of the largest enterprise AI deployments in professional services to date, KPMG and Anthropic today announced the KPMG Digital Gateway Powered by Claude — embedding Claude directly into KPMG's global client delivery platform, starting with Tax & Legal services and private equity. All 276,000+ KPMG employees globally will gain access to Claude. Anthropic named KPMG a preferred consultant for PE deployments. KPMG Blaze, a new offering, embeds Claude Code to accelerate IT modernization for PE portfolio companies. Full implementation on Microsoft Azure is targeted by September 2026. The deal follows similar alliances Anthropic has struck with Deloitte and PwC, accelerating the Big Four AI arms race. WSJ coverage noted KPMG's tax and legal services unit grew ~8% YoY to $9.3B in 2025.
