# A peer-reviewed open-access study published today in Software Quality Journal assessed the readiness of generative AI…

A peer-reviewed open-access study published today in Software Quality Journal assessed the readiness of generative AI tools for industrial software quality tasks including test generation, code review, defect prediction, and documentation. The authors conclude that leading models have crossed a practical threshold for adoption in several quality-assurance workflows, while flagging persistent gaps in handling legacy codebases and security-critical logic. The study surveyed real practitioners across 14 companies, giving the findings unusual grounding compared with benchmark-only evaluations.
