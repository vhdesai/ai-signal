# StartupHub.ai's 2026 ranking of the top 20 coding agents confirms Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Replit, and Codeium at the…

StartupHub.ai's 2026 ranking of the top 20 coding agents confirms Cursor, GitHub Copilot, Replit, and Codeium at the top, driven primarily by distribution advantage rather than raw model quality. Cursor (an agentic VS Code fork) achieved the category's fastest revenue ramp, while Copilot holds position through Microsoft's enterprise bundle. Replit CEO Amjad Masad confirmed the platform is tracking toward $1B ARR, up from $2.8M in all of 2024, and intends to remain independent. Cursor is separately reported to be in acquisition talks with SpaceX at an estimated $60B valuation. The market is consolidating around tools that offer full-codebase comprehension and compliance, rather than simple autocomplete.
