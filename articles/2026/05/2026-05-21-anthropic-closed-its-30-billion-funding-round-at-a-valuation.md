# Anthropic closed its $30 billion funding round at a valuation above $900 billion, led by Sequoia Capital, Dragoneer, Greenoaks Capital, and Altimeter Capital…

Anthropic closed its $30 billion funding round at a valuation above $900 billion, led by Sequoia Capital, Dragoneer, Greenoaks Capital, and Altimeter Capital — nearly tripling its $380B February valuation. The company shared investor projections showing $10.9 billion in Q2 2026 revenue (up 130% QoQ from $4.8B in Q1) and an estimated $559M operating profit, its first-ever quarterly operating income. The revenue acceleration is driven by Claude Code's enterprise dominance, compute efficiency gains, and a doubling of $1M+ enterprise accounts to over 1,000.
