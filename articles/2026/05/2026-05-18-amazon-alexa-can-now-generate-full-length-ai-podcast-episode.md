# Amazon Alexa+ Can Now Generate Full-Length AI Podcast Episodes

Amazon's Alexa+ now includes a feature that generates full-length, conversational podcast episodes from user prompts, powered by Amazon's AI infrastructure. The addition expands Alexa+'s agentic media creation capabilities and positions it as a consumer AI content tool alongside ChatGPT's personal finance features and Google's Gmail Live. Separately, Amazon also launched conversational AI shopping agents across millions of product pages.
