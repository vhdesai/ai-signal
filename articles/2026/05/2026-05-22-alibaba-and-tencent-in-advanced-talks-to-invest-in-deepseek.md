# Alibaba and Tencent in Advanced Talks to Invest in DeepSeek at $20B Valuation

Alibaba and Tencent are in advanced discussions to co-invest in DeepSeek at a valuation reaching $20 billion — double the $10 billion figure that had been circulating earlier in Q1. DeepSeek's V3.2 model has demonstrated a compelling inference cost advantage over flagship Western models at production scale, fueling significant enterprise and investor interest. If completed, this would mark DeepSeek's first acceptance of major external funding after months of declining offers, fundamentally reshaping China's open-source AI ecosystem with well-capitalized incumbents now backing the country's most technically competitive lab.
