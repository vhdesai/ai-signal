# SpaceX Files Plans for $55B "Terafab" Chip Factory in Texas

SpaceX has filed plans for a $55B semiconductor fabrication facility in Texas dubbed "Terafab," positioning the company as a domestic chip manufacturing play alongside its Colossus AI supercomputer. The filing comes days after Anthropic secured the entire Colossus 1 cluster (220,000+ NVIDIA GPUs, 300MW) under a long-term compute contract. If built, Terafab would be one of the largest private semiconductor investments in U.S. history and would directly address America's dependency on TSMC for advanced node production. 🎓 Academic Research
