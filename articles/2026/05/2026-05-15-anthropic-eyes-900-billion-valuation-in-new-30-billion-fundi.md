# Anthropic Eyes $900 Billion Valuation in New $30 Billion Funding Round

Reports indicate Anthropic is in the final stages of raising approximately $30 billion in fresh equity, which would nearly triple its February 2026 valuation and place the company at a $900 billion mark — approaching parity with OpenAI's $852 billion post-money valuation. The round follows Anthropic's Q1 disclosure of 80x year-over-year revenue growth with ARR now above $44 billion, its $200 billion Google Cloud contract, and the Colossus 1 compute deal with SpaceX secured earlier in May.
