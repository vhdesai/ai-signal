# Tencent commercializes Hy3 and DeepSeek-V4-Pro; shares jump 4.4%

Tencent Cloud announced paid commercial services for its Hy3 Preview and DeepSeek-V4-Pro models begin May 27, ending the free beta with usage-based pricing on model invocation volume. Hong Kong-listed shares climbed as much as 4.4% to HK$468.80, signaling investor confidence in Chinese AI monetization momentum. The transition marks one of the first paid commercial deployments of a DeepSeek model under a major cloud provider's commercial terms. Single-outlet at time of compilation.
