# "This is fine" creator accuses Artisan of stealing his art for AI-BDR ad

Cartoonist KC Green says AI startup Artisan repurposed his iconic "This is fine" comic in a subway ad campaign for an AI BDR product without permission. Green is reportedly seeking legal representation; Artisan said it has "a lot of respect" for Green and is reaching out directly. Another data point in the growing IP-and-training-data legal front.
