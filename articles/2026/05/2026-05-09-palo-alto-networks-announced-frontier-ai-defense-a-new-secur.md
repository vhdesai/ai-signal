# Palo Alto Networks announced Frontier AI Defense, a new security initiative combining its AI-native platforms, Unit 42…

Palo Alto Networks announced Frontier AI Defense, a new security initiative combining its AI-native platforms, Unit 42 consulting, and strategic partners to deliver AI-assisted vulnerability analysis at scale. A headline data point from internal testing: three weeks of AI-powered analysis matched the breadth and coverage of a full year of traditional manual penetration testing. The initiative signals a shift in enterprise security from human-paced analysis to continuous, automated threat modeling. For CISOs, the implication is a meaningful reduction in the window between vulnerability emergence and detection.
