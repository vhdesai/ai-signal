# Microsoft's Agent 365 — a platform for discovering, governing, and securing AI agents across Microsoft, SaaS, cloud,…

Microsoft's Agent 365 — a platform for discovering, governing, and securing AI agents across Microsoft, SaaS, cloud, and local environments — became generally available for commercial customers on May 1. Simultaneously, Google announced a new AI Control Center for Workspace, giving admins centralized visibility over AI usage, data protection, and privacy settings. Both moves reflect enterprise IT's push to govern agents that increasingly operate across business applications autonomously. Microsoft's accompanying blog post framed the organizational shift through four human-agent collaboration patterns: Author → Editor → Director → Orchestrator.
