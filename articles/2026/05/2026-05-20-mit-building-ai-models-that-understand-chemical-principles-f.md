# MIT: Building AI models that understand chemical principles for drug discovery

MIT profiles researcher Connor Coley, who works at the intersection of chemistry and machine learning to discover and design new drug compounds. The focus is on building ML models that respect underlying chemical principles — incorporating physical and chemical inductive biases — rather than treating molecules as black-box inputs. This approach represents an emerging direction in scientific AI that prioritizes domain-grounded model design over general pattern matching.
