# The Trump administration is reportedly considering an executive order that would establish a formal government review…

The Trump administration is reportedly considering an executive order that would establish a formal government review process for new AI models before public release — a significant reversal from earlier deregulatory signals. The proposed order would create a working group including tech executives and government officials, modeled on a similar framework under development in the UK. The shift follows a reported falling-out between the Trump administration and Anthropic. If enacted, the order would mark the most substantial US federal oversight intervention in frontier AI development to date.
