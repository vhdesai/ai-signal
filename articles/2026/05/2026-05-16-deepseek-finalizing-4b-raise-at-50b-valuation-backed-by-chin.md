# DeepSeek Finalizing $4B Raise at $50B Valuation, Backed by China's State AI Fund

DeepSeek, the Chinese AI lab best known for its efficiency-first R-series reasoning models, is finalizing a $4 billion funding round that would value the company at $50 billion. Notably, China's national state AI investment fund is participating — a signal of strategic government backing for the lab that rattled U.S. AI equities with its low-cost model releases earlier this year. The capital is expected to accelerate DeepSeek's next-generation model training and reduce dependence on Nvidia hardware through domestic chip partnerships. The deal would represent one of the largest Chinese AI private financings on record. 📈
