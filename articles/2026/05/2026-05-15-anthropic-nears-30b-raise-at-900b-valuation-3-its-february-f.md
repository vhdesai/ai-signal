# Anthropic Nears $30B Raise at $900B Valuation — 3× Its February Figure

Anthropic is reportedly in advanced talks for a funding round of approximately $30 billion — a figure that would value the Claude maker at $900 billion, roughly three times its $300 billion February valuation. The round would represent one of the largest private capital raises in tech history and would cement Anthropic's position as the highest-valued AI safety-focused lab globally. Amazon and Google are expected to participate alongside new strategic investors. The deal is expected to close in the coming weeks, subject to final terms. ⚡
