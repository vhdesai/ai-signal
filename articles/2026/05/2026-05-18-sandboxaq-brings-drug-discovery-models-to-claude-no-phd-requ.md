# SandboxAQ Brings Drug Discovery Models to Claude — No PhD Required

SandboxAQ, the quantum and AI spinout from Alphabet, announced integration of its pharmaceutical drug discovery models with Anthropic's Claude API, making advanced molecular simulation accessible to researchers without deep computational expertise. The integration allows scientists to query complex molecular interaction models through natural language, dramatically lowering the barrier to AI-assisted drug discovery workflows. The partnership underscores a broader trend of domain-specific AI models being surfaced via general-purpose LLM interfaces.
