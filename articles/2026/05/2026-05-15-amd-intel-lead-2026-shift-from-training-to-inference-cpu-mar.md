# AMD & Intel Lead 2026 Shift from Training to Inference; CPU Market Booming

The AI hardware spotlight has shifted from GPU-heavy training to CPU-driven inference as agentic AI workloads transform data center architecture. AMD CEO Lisa Su projects the server CPU market will exceed $120B annually by 2030 (35%+ CAGR), a forecast she says has doubled in six months. AMD's Q1 revenue rose 38% year-over-year; Intel's stock has gained 240% year-to-date on Xeon demand. The CPU-to-GPU ratio is moving toward parity for inference workloads, reshaping procurement priorities for large enterprises.
