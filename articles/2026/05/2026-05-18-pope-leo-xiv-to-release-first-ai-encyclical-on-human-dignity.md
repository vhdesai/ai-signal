# Pope Leo XIV to Release First AI Encyclical on Human Dignity

Pope Leo XIV's first encyclical, Magnifica Humanitas, will address artificial intelligence, human dignity, workers' rights, warfare, and Vatican AI policy. The document is the first formal Vatican position paper on AI and is expected to influence Catholic-aligned regulatory voices in Europe and Latin America. Publication date has not been confirmed.
