# CEO Satya Nadella is reshaping Microsoft's upper echelons for the AI era, dismantling the existing senior leadership team structure.

CEO Satya Nadella is reshaping Microsoft's upper echelons for the AI era, dismantling the existing senior leadership team structure. The company is also preparing for the departure of Yusuf Mehdi, a 35-year veteran and commercial chief marketing officer. The restructuring is designed to position Microsoft to compete more effectively as AI transforms every layer of the enterprise software stack.
