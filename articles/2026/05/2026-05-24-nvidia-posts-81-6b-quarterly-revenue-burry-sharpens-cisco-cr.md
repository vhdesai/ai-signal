# Nvidia posts $81.6B quarterly revenue; Burry sharpens "Cisco" critique

Nvidia reported $81.6B in quarterly revenue (up 85% YoY), with the data center segment alone at $75.2B (up 92%), and disclosed $43B in startup holdings. The print was strong enough for Jensen Huang to claim a "brand new" $200B market for Nvidia, but Michael Burry doubled down on his Substack call comparing Nvidia to Cisco circa 1999 — prompting Nvidia to send sell-side analysts a rebuttal memo, an unusual move.
