# European banks back Mistral for a sovereign Mythos alternative

BNP Paribas is working with Mistral AI on a cyber-focused model intended to give European banks a defensive counterpart to Anthropic’s restricted Mythos. The Next Web, citing Bloomberg, reports that European supervisors worry defenders without Mythos-class tools could be structurally behind. The story reinforces a broader shift toward sovereign AI capacity in regulated industries.
