# OpenAI Launches "Daybreak" Cybersecurity Initiative Powered by GPT-5.5

OpenAI introduced Daybreak, a cybersecurity-focused product line built on GPT-5.5-cyber, opening a direct competitive front with Anthropic's Mythos and Glasswing. Distribution is currently limited to a select set of enterprise customers and government agencies for vulnerability discovery and patching.
