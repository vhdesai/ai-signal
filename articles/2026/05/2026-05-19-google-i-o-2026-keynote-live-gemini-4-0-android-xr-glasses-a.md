# Google I/O 2026 Keynote Live — Gemini 4.0, Android XR Glasses & Aluminium OS

Google's annual developer conference kicked off today at 10am PT, with CEO Sundar Pichai and DeepMind's Demis Hassabis headlining. The keynote is expected to unveil Gemini 4.0, Android XR smart glasses, and "Aluminium OS" — a merged Android-ChromeOS platform powering a new Googlebook laptop category. Also on deck: Gemini Nano 4 (3× speed gains over prior Nano models), Veo 4 30-second video generation, and a major agentic coding developer session at 1:30pm PT. Google is under significant competitive pressure to match OpenAI's monthly model cadence and China's open-weights efficiency gains.
