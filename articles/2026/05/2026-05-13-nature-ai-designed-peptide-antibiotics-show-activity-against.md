# Nature: AI-Designed Peptide Antibiotics Show Activity Against Multi-Drug Resistant Pathogens

A fresh Nature paper details AI-designed peptide antibiotics with measurable activity against multi-drug resistant clinical isolates. The work uses generative protein models to propose novel sequences that bypass known resistance mechanisms — a meaningful proof point for AI-led discovery in biomedicine and another data point in the rising thesis that frontier models are now compressing R&D cycles in life sciences. 3 · Products & Tools
