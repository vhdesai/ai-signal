# Meta Cuts 8,000 Jobs, Tells Employees AI Agents Will Primarily Do the Work Now

Meta finalized layoffs of roughly 10% of its workforce — about 8,000 employees — to redirect spend toward AI, telling staff that AI agents will increasingly handle the work going forward. The cuts land on the same day SpaceX's S-1 filing arrived and OpenAI was reportedly racing to confidentially file by Friday for a September listing, capping a watershed day in tech.
