# HotOpenAI and Anthropic Each Launch Billion-Dollar Enterprise AI Joint Ventures

On the same day in early May, both frontier labs revealed competing enterprise deployment vehicles. Anthropic launched a $1.5 billion venture with Blackstone, Goldman Sachs, and Hellman & Friedman to embed AI engineers inside mid-sized portfolio companies and redesign workflows around Claude agents — modeled on Palantir's forward-deployed engineer model. Hours later, OpenAI announced The Development Company, a larger $4 billion vehicle backed by TPG, Brookfield, Bain, and Advent, structured to scale enterprise deployment across 19 investor-aligned organizations. Both ventures target the same bottleneck: the scarcity of talent capable of implementing AI inside real business operations.
