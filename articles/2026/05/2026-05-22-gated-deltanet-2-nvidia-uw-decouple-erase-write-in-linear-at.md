# Gated DeltaNet-2: NVIDIA & UW Decouple Erase/Write in Linear Attention  New

NVIDIA Research and University of Washington's Yejin Choi introduce Gated DeltaNet-2, a new linear-attention architecture that decouples the erase and write operations within gated DeltaNet recurrences. The approach targets sub-quadratic attention for long-context training and inference efficiency — an active research frontier aimed at reducing the cost of scaling context windows. The collaboration reinforces both organizations' investment in efficient transformer alternatives.
