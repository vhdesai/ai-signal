# CMU and UT Austin Detail New Methods for Long-Context Retrieval

Researchers at Carnegie Mellon and UT Austin released a paper on hierarchical retrieval that closes the gap between vector-DB RAG and full long-context attention at significantly lower inference cost. The work is framed as practical for enterprise deployments that must reason across millions of tokens of internal documents — an area of high relevance for Microsoft 365 Copilot–style products. 6.
