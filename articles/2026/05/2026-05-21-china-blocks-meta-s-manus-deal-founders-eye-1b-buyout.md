# China Blocks Meta's Manus Deal; Founders Eye $1B Buyout

Manus co-founders Xiao Hong, Ji Yichao, and Zhang Tao are exploring options to fulfill Beijing's regulatory order to unwind their multi-billion-dollar acquisition by Meta, including raising an estimated $1 billion from external investors to buy back the operation. Beijing's national-security review of cross-border AI-agent data flows triggered the unwind, mirroring Washington's parallel reviews of Chinese AI investments and underscoring the bifurcation of the global agent market along geopolitical lines.
