# Hassabis says humanity is "in the foothills of the singularity"; LeCun disagrees AI is intelligent

Within hours of each other, Google DeepMind CEO Demis Hassabis described current progress as the beginning of the singularity, while Meta's Yann LeCun argued today's systems are not genuinely intelligent. Gemini co-lead Oriol Vinyals split the difference. The exchange has become the weekend's dominant frame for how senior lab leaders disagree on what current capabilities actually represent.
