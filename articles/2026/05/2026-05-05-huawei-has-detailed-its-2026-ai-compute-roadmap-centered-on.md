# Huawei has detailed its 2026 AI compute roadmap, centered on the Ascend 950 chip (1 petaflop FP8, 128–144GB HBM) and…

Huawei has detailed its 2026 AI compute roadmap, centered on the Ascend 950 chip (1 petaflop FP8, 128–144GB HBM) and the Atlas 950 SuperPoD — a cluster linking 8,192 Ascend chips to deliver 8 exaflops, backed by 1,152 TB of memory and a footprint spanning two basketball courts. Huawei is projected to capture roughly 50% of China's AI chip market by end of 2026, fueled by Chinese government mandates and Nvidia export restrictions. Analysts describe a "two-track" global AI infrastructure now taking shape: Nvidia dominates everywhere except China, where Huawei's full-stack hardware and CANN software ecosystem is becoming the incumbent.
