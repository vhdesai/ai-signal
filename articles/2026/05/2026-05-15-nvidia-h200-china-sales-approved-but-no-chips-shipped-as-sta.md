# Nvidia H200 China Sales Approved — But No Chips Shipped as Standoff Continues

The US approved export licenses for roughly 10 Chinese firms — including Alibaba, Tencent, ByteDance, and JD.com — to purchase Nvidia's H200 AI chips. Despite the approvals, not a single chip has shipped, with Beijing's security concerns blocking deliveries. Nvidia CEO Jensen Huang joined President Trump on his Beijing trip to advance the deal, but no resolution was reached. The impasse leaves one of the biggest AI hardware trade deals in limbo and highlights the persistent geopolitical tension underpinning the global AI compute race.
