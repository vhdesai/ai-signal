# Jensen Huang delivers Carnegie Mellon commencement: "Shape what comes next"

NVIDIA founder Jensen Huang received an honorary Doctor of Science and Technology and delivered the keynote at CMU's 128th Commencement, charging 5,800+ new graduates to lead the next phase of the AI era. The address reinforced CMU's position as a critical pipeline for the U.S. AI talent stack alongside Stanford, MIT, and Berkeley.
