# Trump Administration Reverses Course — Signs Pre-Deployment AI Evaluation Agreements

In a notable policy reversal, the Trump administration signed pre-deployment AI evaluation agreements with Google DeepMind, Microsoft, and xAI through CAISI (the renamed US AI Safety Institute). The agreements allow federal security evaluation of frontier AI models before release. National Economic Council Director Kevin Hassett confirmed on Fox Business that Trump may issue an executive order mandating "FDA-style" government testing of advanced AI systems. The catalyst was Anthropic's Mythos — a model deemed too dangerous to release publicly. CAISI has now completed ~40 evaluations, including unreleased frontier models. (Sources: Tech Xplore, Ars Technica, POLITICO)
