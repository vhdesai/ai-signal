# Hot OpenAI and Dell Partner to Deploy Codex in Enterprise On-Premises Environments

OpenAI announced an enterprise-focused partnership with Dell Technologies to bring Codex — OpenAI's agentic coding system — into hybrid and on-premises customer environments. The deal targets large enterprises with data-residency compliance requirements that cannot use cloud-only AI services. The partnership positions Codex as an enterprise developer-productivity tool and extends OpenAI's reach into the Dell customer base, which skews heavily toward regulated industries including financial services, healthcare, and government. 🔬 Research Breakthroughs aX
