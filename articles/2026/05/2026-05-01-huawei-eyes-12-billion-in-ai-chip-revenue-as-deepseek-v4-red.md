# Huawei Eyes $12 Billion in AI Chip Revenue as DeepSeek V4 Redirects Chinese Demand From Nvidia Breaking

Huawei is projecting a 60% year-over-year surge in AI chip revenue to approximately $12 billion in 2026, driven by large orders from Chinese technology giants for its Ascend 950PR processors. The acceleration followed the DeepSeek V4 launch, which was optimized for Huawei hardware, triggering a wave of procurement decisions that bypassed Nvidia altogether. U.S. export restrictions have effectively catalyzed demand for domestic Chinese AI silicon, and Huawei's Ascend line is emerging as the de-facto domestic alternative, with significant implications for Nvidia's Chinese market share.
