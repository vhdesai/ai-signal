# Alibaba unveils new AI chip and Qwen model as China pushes domestic AI stack

The Information reported that Alibaba’s T-Head unit unveiled the Zhenwu M890 chip for training and running AI models, claiming three times the performance of its predecessor. Alibaba also launched Qwen3.7-Max, emphasizing coding and complex multi-step tasks. The announcement reflects China’s continued push for domestic AI chips and full-stack cloud-model capability amid constraints on access to Nvidia hardware.
