# Nvidia Vera Rubin Coverage Continues: $1T Demand Through 2027, Hyperscaler Lock-In

Ongoing analyses of Nvidia's GTC 2026 announcements confirm the Vera Rubin platform — Rubin GPUs, Vera CPU, NVLink 6, Groq 3 LPX — delivers up to 10× more inference throughput per watt and one-tenth the cost-per-token vs. Blackwell. AWS has committed to deploying 1M+ Nvidia GPUs alongside Groq LPUs; Azure, Google Cloud, and Oracle are all on board. Jensen Huang now sees at least $1T in AI-infrastructure demand through 2027.
