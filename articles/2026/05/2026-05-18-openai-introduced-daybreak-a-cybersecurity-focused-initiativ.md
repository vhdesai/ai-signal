# OpenAI introduced Daybreak, a cybersecurity-focused initiative powered by GPT-5.5, available to authorized developers,…

OpenAI introduced Daybreak, a cybersecurity-focused initiative powered by GPT-5.5, available to authorized developers, security teams, industry partners, and government agencies. Capabilities include secure code review, threat modeling, vulnerability triage, patch validation, and controlled red-team workflows. The platform is positioned as a direct competitor to Anthropic's enterprise security offerings and signals OpenAI's intent to compete in the government/defense AI market.
