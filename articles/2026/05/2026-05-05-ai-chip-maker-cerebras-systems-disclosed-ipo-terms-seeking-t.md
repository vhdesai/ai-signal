# AI chip maker Cerebras Systems disclosed IPO terms seeking to raise up to $3.5B by selling 28 million shares at…

AI chip maker Cerebras Systems disclosed IPO terms seeking to raise up to $3.5B by selling 28 million shares at $115–$125 per share, implying a $26.6B market cap. The company's revenue jumped 76% in 2025 to $290.3M, and it swung to a $87.9M profit after a $485M loss the year prior. Underwriters hold an option to purchase an additional 4 million shares, and strong investor demand could push pricing higher. Cerebras's wafer-scale chips — far larger than conventional GPUs — target inference workloads where latency is paramount.
