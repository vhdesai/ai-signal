# Google’s always-on Gemini Spark shows where assistants are headed

A TechCrunch test drive of Google’s 24/7 Gemini Spark assistant found the product direction increasingly practical for lightweight task monitoring, synthesis, and personal workflow support. The broader signal is that AI platforms are moving from reactive chat toward persistent, ambient assistance. That will raise the bar on privacy, memory, interruptibility, and user trust.
