# The US Center for AI Standards and Innovation (CAISI, part of the Commerce Department) confirmed vetting agreements…

The US Center for AI Standards and Innovation (CAISI, part of the Commerce Department) confirmed vetting agreements requiring Google DeepMind, Microsoft, and xAI to share unreleased frontier models for pre-release national security testing — focusing on cybersecurity, biosecurity, and chemical weapons risk. The agreements were catalyzed partly by concerns around Anthropic's Claude Mythos capabilities. Notably, Anthropic is not among the signatories, adding a layer of competitive and political complexity to the lab's relationship with US defense agencies.
