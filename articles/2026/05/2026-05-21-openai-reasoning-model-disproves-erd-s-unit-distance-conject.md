# OpenAI Reasoning Model Disproves Erdős Unit-Distance Conjecture

An OpenAI reasoning model produced a counterexample disproving a long-standing form of the Erdős unit-distance conjecture, with the result independently verified by working combinatorialists. The episode is being treated as a credible data point that frontier reasoning systems can now contribute novel results at the frontier of pure mathematics — not just reproduce known proofs — and is reframing the conversation about AI's role in fundamental research.
