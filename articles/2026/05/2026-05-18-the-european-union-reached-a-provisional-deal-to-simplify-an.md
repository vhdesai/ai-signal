# The European Union reached a provisional deal to simplify and partially delay the AI Act's high-risk AI obligations — a…

The European Union reached a provisional deal to simplify and partially delay the AI Act's high-risk AI obligations — a concession to European tech companies and startups who argued the compliance burden was too heavy relative to U.S. and Chinese competitors. At the same time, the deal includes a new ban on non-consensual explicit AI-generated content ("nudification apps"), which had been a significant policy pressure point in multiple EU member states. Enterprises with AI deployments in Europe should review updated compliance timelines with their legal teams.
