# Devendra Chaplot, a founding member of Mistral AI and one of xAI's highest-profile hires when he joined in March, has…

Devendra Chaplot, a founding member of Mistral AI and one of xAI's highest-profile hires when he joined in March, has departed xAI after roughly one month, according to The Information. Chaplot was considered a marquee addition to Elon Musk's AI lab given his credentials across large-scale language and multimodal research. The rapid exit raises questions about xAI's internal culture and talent retention as it competes for top researchers against better-funded and more established labs. His departure follows the company's release of Grok 4.3 last week, which added voice cloning capabilities.
