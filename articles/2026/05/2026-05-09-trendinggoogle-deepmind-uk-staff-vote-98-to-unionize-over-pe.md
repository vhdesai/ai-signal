# TrendingGoogle DeepMind UK Staff Vote 98% to Unionize Over Pentagon AI Contracts

In a landmark labor development at an AI lab, Google DeepMind's UK staff voted 98% in favor of unionization on May 9 — the first union at any top-tier AI organization worldwide. The primary catalyst was employee opposition to classified AI contracts with the U.S. Department of Defense. The vote raises questions about how frontier AI labs — which depend heavily on mission-driven engineering talent — will navigate the growing tension between commercial defense contracts and employee values as AI capabilities become increasingly dual-use. 6 · AI Safety & Policy
