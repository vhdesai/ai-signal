# Gemini 3.5 Flash and Gemini Omni Roll Out Globally as Google's New Defaults

Gemini 3.5 Flash — clocked at 289 tokens/second, which Google claims is 4× competitor frontier speed — is now the default in the Gemini app and AI Mode in Search globally, with continued rollout this week. Gemini Omni Flash, the multimodal video-generation model, is shipping to Google AI subscribers and YouTube Shorts.
