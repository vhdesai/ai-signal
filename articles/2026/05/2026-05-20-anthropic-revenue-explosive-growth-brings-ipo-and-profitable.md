# Anthropic Revenue Explosive Growth Brings IPO and Profitable Quarter Into View

Anthropic projects turning an operating profit for the first time in Q2, with revenue more than doubling sequentially to $10.9 billion as enterprise Claude adoption accelerates. The disclosure lands as the company eyes an October IPO and locks in a $1.25B/month compute deal with SpaceX's Colossus data centers. The milestone marks Anthropic overtaking OpenAI in enterprise model spending.
