# Pope Leo XIV releases the Vatican's first AI encyclical, Magnifica Humanitas

Pope Leo XIV released "Magnifica Humanitas," the Vatican's first encyclical on AI, framing the technology as the Industrial Revolution of our era and centering it on "the protection of the human person." It was co-presented by Anthropic co-founder Christopher Olah — a deliberate signal that the Church views Anthropic as a credible safety partner over OpenAI or Google. F I
