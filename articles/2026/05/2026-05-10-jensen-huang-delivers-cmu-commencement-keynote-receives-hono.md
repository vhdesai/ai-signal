# Jensen Huang Delivers CMU Commencement Keynote, Receives Honorary Doctorate — Rejects AI Job Doom Narrative

Jensen Huang delivered the keynote at CMU's 128th commencement on May 10 and received an honorary doctorate. He encouraged graduates to view the current moment as an optimal entry point into the workforce, pushing back against widespread AI job-displacement anxiety. Huang framed AI as an "amplifier" for human capability rather than a replacement, and outlined his vision for a physical-AI convergence where robotics and language models intersect. The speech received significant media pickup and provided a rare high-profile counter-narrative to the 92,000+ layoff headlines published the same day.
