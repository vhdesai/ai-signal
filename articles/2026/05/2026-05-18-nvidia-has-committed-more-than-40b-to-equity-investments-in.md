# Nvidia has committed more than $40B to equity investments in AI companies in 2026 alone — led by a $30B investment in…

Nvidia has committed more than $40B to equity investments in AI companies in 2026 alone — led by a $30B investment in OpenAI, plus $3.2B in Corning and $2.1B in data center operator IREN — and participated in roughly two dozen private startup rounds. Separately, AI startups captured $25B across 37 deals in May (45% of all venture activity), with notable rounds including Lambda ($1B for AI compute infrastructure) and ROBOTERA ($200M for humanoid robots). Morgan Stanley projects $2.9T in global data center capex through 2028, with 80% still ahead.
