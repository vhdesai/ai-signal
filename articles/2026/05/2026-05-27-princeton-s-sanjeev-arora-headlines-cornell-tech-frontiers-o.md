# Princeton's Sanjeev Arora Headlines Cornell Tech Frontiers of AI Summit

Cornell Tech is hosting its Frontiers of AI Summit at the Roosevelt Island campus today, with Princeton's Sanjeev Arora delivering a keynote on theoretical foundations of large language models. Cornell President Kavita Bala and Cursor's chief research scientist Sasha Rush — a Cornell faculty member — are among the speakers, with sessions focused on agent reasoning, alignment, and the academic-industry research interface.
