# Meta to Cut ~8,000 Jobs as Capex Hits $145B AI Push

Meta is preparing to eliminate roughly 8,000 roles — about 10% of headcount — while raising its 2026 capital expenditure to as much as $145B, largely earmarked for AI infrastructure. The cuts began May 20 and leave 6,000 open roles unfilled. The story underscores a Big Tech pattern: trimming people while doubling down on compute.
