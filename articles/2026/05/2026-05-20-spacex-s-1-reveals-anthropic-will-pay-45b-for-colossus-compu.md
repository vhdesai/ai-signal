# SpaceX S-1 Reveals Anthropic Will Pay $45B for Colossus Compute Through 2029

SpaceX's IPO filing disclosed the structure of its Anthropic compute deal: $1.25B per month through May 2029, totaling $45B — roughly 3–5× what analysts had estimated. xAI burned $6.4B last year; the Anthropic contract alone now generates more annual revenue for SpaceX than its entire standalone 2025 business. The disclosure resets the market's understanding of frontier compute economics.
