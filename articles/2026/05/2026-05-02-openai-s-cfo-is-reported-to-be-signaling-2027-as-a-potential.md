# OpenAI's CFO is reported to be signaling 2027 as a potential IPO window while the company prepares retail investor…

OpenAI's CFO is reported to be signaling 2027 as a potential IPO window while the company prepares retail investor share allocation mechanisms, according to aggregated coverage of a WSJ profile. The disclosure suggests OpenAI is seriously advancing its public market preparations after completing its for-profit restructuring. A public offering would unlock liquidity for employees and early investors and provide a public currency for future M&A. The timing aligns with the company's expected revenue trajectory and continued subscriber growth for ChatGPT.
