# OpenAI's frontier system autonomously cracks an 80-year-old geometry problem

An OpenAI model is reported to have produced an autonomous solution to a long-open problem in classical geometry that had resisted human proof for roughly eight decades. Coverage continued to circulate this weekend; specifics of the proof and peer review status have not yet been publicly detailed, but the result is being cited alongside Isomorphic Labs and DeepMind work as evidence of accelerating AI-led mathematical discovery.
