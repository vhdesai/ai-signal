# TrendingEU AI Act High-Risk Compliance Deadline Approaching — August 2, 2026

The EU AI Act's next major enforcement milestone — compliance requirements for high-risk AI systems — is 83 days away as of today (August 2, 2026). Companies must now implement risk management processes, high-quality training datasets, detailed technical documentation, and post-market monitoring or face fines of up to 7% of global annual revenue. U.S.-based enterprises selling or deploying AI systems that affect EU residents are squarely in scope. Simultaneously, state-level AI legislation in 45 U.S. states (1,561 bills introduced as of March 2026) is creating a fragmented domestic compliance landscape, with California and Colorado leading with the most binding frameworks.
