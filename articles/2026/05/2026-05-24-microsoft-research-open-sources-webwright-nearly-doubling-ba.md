# Microsoft Research open-sources Webwright, nearly doubling baseline performance on long-horizon web tasks

Microsoft Research released Webwright, a terminal-native web-agent framework, scoring 60.1% on the Odysseys long-horizon benchmark versus 33.5% for base GPT-5.4. The release is one of the strongest open-sourced web-agent stacks to date and signals continued Microsoft investment in agent infrastructure alongside its model partnerships. Products & Tools
