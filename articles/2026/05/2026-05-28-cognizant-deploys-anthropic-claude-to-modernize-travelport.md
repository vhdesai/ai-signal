# Cognizant Deploys Anthropic Claude to Modernize Travelport

Cognizant signed a deal to deploy Anthropic's Claude across Travelport's booking and servicing platform — covering bookings, exchanges, refunds, and disruption management. Cognizant will use Claude for code development, testing, and analysis of Travelport's legacy codebase. The contract is notable as a global-systems-integrator commitment that defaults to Claude for a complex, mission-critical migration, signaling Anthropic's continued enterprise-channel momentum in regulated travel verticals.
