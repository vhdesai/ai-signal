# Fresh off its $5.55B IPO at a $95B market cap, Cerebras Systems announced it is serving Moonshot AI's Kimi K2.6 (a trillion-parameter open-weight model) at 981 output tokens per second — independently verified by Artificial Analysis as 6.7x faster than the best GPU-based provider and 23x faster than the median. The benchmark underscores the growing competitive challenge to Nvidia's dominance in AI inference hardware.
