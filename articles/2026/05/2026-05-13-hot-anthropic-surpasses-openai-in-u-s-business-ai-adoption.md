# 🔥 HOT Anthropic Surpasses OpenAI in U.S. Business AI Adoption

For the first time, more U.S. businesses are paying for Anthropic's Claude than OpenAI's ChatGPT: Anthropic reached 34.4% adoption vs. OpenAI's 32.3% in April, per the Ramp AI Index tracking 50,000+ businesses. Anthropic has quadrupled its business adoption in a year — driven primarily by Claude Code — while overall business AI adoption crossed 50% for the first time. The report notes Anthropic's lead may be fragile, threatened by escalating compute costs and token-based pricing exposure.
