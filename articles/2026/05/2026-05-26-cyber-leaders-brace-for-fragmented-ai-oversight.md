# Cyber leaders brace for fragmented AI oversight

WSJ Pro CyberSecurity reports that enterprise security leaders are preparing for a looser U.S. AI oversight regime and a fragmented compliance landscape. As states, China, and the European Union move forward with their own AI governance efforts, CISOs are building internal evaluation frameworks for agentic systems. The practical takeaway is that enterprises should assume regulatory clarity will lag production deployment.
