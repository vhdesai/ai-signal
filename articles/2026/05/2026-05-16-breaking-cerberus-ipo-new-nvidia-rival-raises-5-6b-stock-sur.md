# 🔴 BREAKING Cerberus IPO: New Nvidia Rival Raises $5.6B, Stock Surges 68% on Debut

AI chipmaker Cerberus (CBRS) priced its IPO at $185/share on Wednesday in what became 2026's largest public offering to date, raising an upsized $5.6 billion. The stock surged 68% on its first day of trading before pulling back 10% on Friday, reflecting both intense investor demand for AI chip exposure and volatility in the sector. The offering underscores the appetite for Nvidia alternatives as the AI data center TAM is now estimated by Bank of America to reach $1.7 trillion annually by 2030.
