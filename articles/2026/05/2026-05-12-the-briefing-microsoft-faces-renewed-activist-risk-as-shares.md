# The Briefing: Microsoft Faces Renewed Activist Risk as Shares Lag

Microsoft shares are down nearly 16% YTD, the worst performer of big tech. British hedge fund TCI sold "almost all" its stake, citing uncertainty about how AI could undermine Office productivity. With SpaceX's IPO weeks away likely to drain capital from incumbents, pressure on Microsoft shares could intensify, raising the possibility of another activist run at the company.
