# Sources cited by WinBuzzer claim Anthropic is exploring a new funding round of approximately $50 billion that would…

Sources cited by WinBuzzer claim Anthropic is exploring a new funding round of approximately $50 billion that would value the company at $900 billion, a dramatic step up from its previous $61 billion valuation. Anthropic declined to comment on the report. The figure would place Anthropic's valuation above many established tech giants and reflects ongoing investor enthusiasm for frontier AI safety-focused labs. Note: Single-source reporting — treat with caution pending confirmation.
