# ClickUp mass layoff signals the white-collar AI restructuring is mainstream

ClickUp's mass layoff this week joins Intuit (17%), Salesforce, Microsoft, Google, and Meta (8,000 jobs) in a pattern too consistent to ignore: companies aren't laying off because AI replaced workers directly — they're laying off because AI enabled them to ship the same roadmap with smaller teams.
