# Intel CEO Lip-Bu Tan publicly confirmed ongoing collaboration with Nvidia following their historic partnership…

Intel CEO Lip-Bu Tan publicly confirmed ongoing collaboration with Nvidia following their historic partnership announced eight months ago. The work involves custom x86 CPUs integrated with Nvidia RTX GPU chiplets — one variant for Nvidia's AI infrastructure buildout, another as a consumer SoC for PCs. Products are expected by 2026–2027. The partnership supports Intel's foundry ambitions and signals deeper vertical integration in the AI hardware stack.
