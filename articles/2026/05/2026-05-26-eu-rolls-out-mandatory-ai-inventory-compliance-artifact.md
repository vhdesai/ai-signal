# EU Rolls Out Mandatory "AI Inventory" Compliance Artifact

The European Commission published the specification for the mandatory "AI Inventory" — a registered artifact every covered organization must maintain listing each AI system in use, its risk classification, training data lineage, and human-oversight controls. The Inventory is the operational backbone of the Omnibus-amended AI Act and is the single artifact EU regulators will request first in any high-risk audit. CIOs and CISOs should treat May 26 as the start of the compliance clock.
