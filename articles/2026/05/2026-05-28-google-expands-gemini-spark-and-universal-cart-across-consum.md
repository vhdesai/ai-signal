# Google Expands Gemini Spark and Universal Cart Across Consumer Surfaces

Google's follow-on I/O coverage detailed broader rollout of Gemini Spark and Daily Brief in the Gemini app, Universal Cart for agentic shopping, and deeper integration into Google Pics, intelligent eyewear, and Ask YouTube. The strategy is to put a Gemini agent inside every existing distribution surface rather than competing for a standalone chatbot relationship — a meaningfully different bet from OpenAI and Anthropic's API-first posture. 4.
