# Google DeepMind Publishes Co-Scientist: Multi-Agent AI for Scientific Discovery New

Google DeepMind published details on Co-Scientist, a multi-agent system designed to act as a research partner across scientific domains including life sciences, materials, and drug discovery. The announcement was accompanied by updates on AlphaEvolve — a Gemini-powered coding agent scaling impact across engineering and science — and a cluster of science-focused posts covering liver fibrosis, ALS, cellular aging, and infectious disease. DeepMind's May publishing cadence is the most science-dense it has released this year, positioning Gemini-family models as core infrastructure for biomedical R&D, not just text generation. 3 · Products & Tools
