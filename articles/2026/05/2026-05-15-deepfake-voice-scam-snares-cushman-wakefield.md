# Deepfake Voice Scam Snares Cushman & Wakefield

A deepfake voice-cloning attack successfully targeted real-estate giant Cushman & Wakefield, the latest enterprise to fall to AI-generated audio impersonation of executives. The incident adds to a growing pattern of high-profile voice-clone fraud and reinforces the urgency of out-of-band authentication for any high-value workflow.
