# EU AI Act High-Risk Enforcement Now in Effect; Global Compliance Complexity Rises

The EU AI Act entered active enforcement in early 2026, requiring all high-risk AI systems to comply with risk management, data governance, transparency, and human oversight requirements. Simultaneously, U.S. government AI vetting agreements were confirmed with Google DeepMind, Microsoft, and xAI for model evaluation before classified deployment.
