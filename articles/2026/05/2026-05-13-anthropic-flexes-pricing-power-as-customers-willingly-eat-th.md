# Anthropic Flexes Pricing Power as Customers Willingly Eat the Cost

Anthropic's revenue growth is unprecedented, and enterprise buyers like PagerDuty say they are bracing for volatile costs as employees ramp on Claude-based coding tools. CIO Eric Johnson said his 1,200-person org will absorb the higher spend in exchange for productivity gains, even as forecasting becomes harder. The dynamic underscores Anthropic's pricing leverage — and the broader trend of enterprise budgets bending around AI spend.
