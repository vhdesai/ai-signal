# Bank of America raises Apple price target to $380 ahead of WWDC

Bank of America analyst Wamsi Mohan raised the firm's Apple price target to $380 from $290 on May 26, maintaining a Buy rating ahead of June's WWDC. The note cited expected Apple Intelligence announcements and broader AI catalysts as drivers of multiple expansion. The ~31% bump is notable for a mega-cap and underscores sell-side optimism around Apple's AI roadmap.
