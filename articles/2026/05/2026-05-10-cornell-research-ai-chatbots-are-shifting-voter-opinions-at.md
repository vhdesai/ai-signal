# Cornell Research: AI Chatbots Are Shifting Voter Opinions at Statistically Significant Scale

Cornell researchers released data from press releases circulated May 10–11 showing that AI chatbot interactions are shifting voter political opinions at a statistically significant rate when voters ask about candidates and policies. Companion research from Visibility 360 found that most political campaigns remain invisible or misrepresented in AI-generated responses — creating an asymmetric information environment. These findings add quantitative weight to ongoing regulatory debates about AI in elections and are likely to accelerate calls for mandatory AI disclosure requirements in political contexts.
