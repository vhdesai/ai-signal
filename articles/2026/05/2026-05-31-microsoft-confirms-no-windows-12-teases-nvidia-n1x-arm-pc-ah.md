# Microsoft confirms no "Windows 12," teases NVIDIA N1X ARM PC ahead of a major announcement

Microsoft clarified it is not launching a "Windows 12" branded release, while teasing a significant upcoming reveal tied to an NVIDIA N1X ARM-based PC. The framing points to a Windows-on-ARM push positioned against Apple silicon and timed to the Build/Computex window. Specifics on silicon, OEMs, and timing remain pre-announcement.
