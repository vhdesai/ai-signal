# Microsoft in Talks to Supply Maia 200 Chips to Anthropic Following $5B Investment  Hot

Microsoft is in early-stage talks to supply its Maia 200 custom AI chip to Anthropic — a deal that would mark the first major external customer for Maia 200 and a strategic diversification from Anthropic's current Nvidia/AWS Trainium/Google TPU mix. Satya Nadella has said Maia 200 offers 30%+ better tokens-per-dollar than other silicon in Microsoft's fleet. The talks come as Anthropic disclosed $1.25B/month payments to SpaceX's Colossus and over $100B in AWS compute commitments — a signal of unprecedented frontier-lab compute demand.
