# Thinking Machines Lab Debuts TML-Interaction-Small: Full-Duplex AI That Listens While It Speaks

Mira Murati's Thinking Machines Lab released a closed research preview of TML-Interaction-Small, a 276B-parameter mixture-of-experts model with 12B active parameters that processes audio, video, and text in 200-millisecond simultaneous micro-turns. The lab's FD-bench V1 benchmarks show 0.40-second turn-taking latency versus 1.18 seconds for GPT-Realtime-2.0, and a live demo showcased simultaneous multilingual translation and chart generation across three speakers. The architecture directly challenges the turn-based assumptions behind GPT Realtime 2 and Gemini Live.
