# Itron hack reaches more downstream companies than initially disclosed

WSJ Pro reports the Itron utility-metering breach affected more downstream customers than initially disclosed, expanding the blast radius across power and water utilities relying on Itron's data platform. AI-driven anomaly-detection vendors integrated with Itron telemetry are among the systems being audited as part of the response.
