# Trending Mistral expands into legal sector via enlarged Harvey AI partnership

Mistral and Harvey AI announced an expanded partnership that integrates Mistral models into Harvey's multi-model legal platform, joining OpenAI, Anthropic and xAI in Harvey's routing pool. Early-access opt-in begins with EU customers; U.S. and Australia general availability follows in the coming months. The deal gives Mistral access to Harvey's 1,500+ customers across 60+ countries and intensifies competition with Anthropic, which has been aggressively building legal-specific Claude plug-ins.
