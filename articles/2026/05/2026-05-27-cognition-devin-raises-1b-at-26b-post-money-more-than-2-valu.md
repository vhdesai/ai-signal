# Cognition (Devin) raises $1B+ at $26B post-money — more than 2× valuation in eight months

Cognition, the maker of autonomous AI software engineer Devin, raised over $1 billion at a $25B pre-money / $26B post-money valuation — up from $10.2B in September. Lux Capital, General Catalyst, and 8VC led, with Ribbit, Atreides, Founders Fund, and Elad Gil participating. Cognition disclosed $492M ARR with enterprise usage growing 50% month-over-month for six months and named Mercedes-Benz, NASA, Goldman Sachs, and Santander as customers. The round is a strong vote that independent coding-agent startups can survive alongside Anthropic's Claude Code and OpenAI's Codex.
