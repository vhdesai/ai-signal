# NewHuawei Eyes $12B in AI Chip Revenue as DeepSeek V4 Drives Domestic Chip Shift

Huawei projects its AI chip revenue will climb 60% to approximately $12 billion in 2026, driven by massive orders for its Ascend 950PR processor from ByteDance ($5.6B+), Alibaba, and Tencent — who had previously sourced primarily from Nvidia. The surge was catalyzed by DeepSeek V4's launch (a 1.6-trillion parameter open-source model co-designed with Huawei hardware), which Alibaba Cloud and Tencent Cloud deployed simultaneously. U.S. export controls that banned the H100 and restricted the H20 have effectively cleared the field, splitting the global AI chip market into two ecosystems: Nvidia silicon and Huawei Ascend. Chip prices have already risen ~20% as supply lags demand.
