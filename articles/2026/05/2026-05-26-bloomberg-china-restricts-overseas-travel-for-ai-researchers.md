# Bloomberg: China Restricts Overseas Travel for AI Researchers at Alibaba and DeepSeek

Chinese government agencies have begun requiring prior approval before top AI researchers, founders, and senior executives at Alibaba and DeepSeek can travel abroad — a sharp escalation from the prior reporting-only regime. Beijing now appears to be treating private-sector frontier AI work with the same national-security posture historically reserved for nuclear scientists and defense researchers. Analysts flag risk of accelerated brain drain from the most restricted firms.
