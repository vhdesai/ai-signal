# Anthropic raises $65B at $965B valuation, surpassing OpenAI as world's most valuable AI company

Anthropic closed a $65 billion Series H at a $965 billion post-money valuation, leapfrogging OpenAI's $852 billion mark from March. The round was led by Altimeter, Dragoneer, Greenoaks, and Sequoia, with $15 billion in previously committed cloud-partner capital including $5 billion from Amazon. Micron, Samsung, and SK Hynix joined as strategic infrastructure partners. Anthropic reported a $47 billion revenue run rate and confirmed Claude is now the first frontier model live across AWS, Google Cloud, and Microsoft Azure — setting the stage for a potential IPO race against OpenAI later this year.
