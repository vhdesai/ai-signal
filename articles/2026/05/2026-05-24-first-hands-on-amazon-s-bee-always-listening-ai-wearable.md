# First hands-on: Amazon's Bee always-listening AI wearable

TechCrunch's hands-on with Amazon's Bee — an always-listening ambient-AI wearable — concludes it is "intriguing and slightly creeped out." The review highlights the always-on microphone trade-off central to the entire ambient-AI device category, and is one of the first independent assessments since the device entered limited release.
