# White House Mulls Executive Order Banning Private Sector "Interference" with Government AI Use

The Trump administration is reportedly circulating a 16-page draft executive order that would prohibit the private sector from "interfering" with the government's use of AI models — driven directly by the Pentagon-Anthropic standoff. The order would also create more aggressive contracting and termination standards for federal AI vendors. Industry groups warn this could stifle innovation; the Information Technology and Innovation Foundation cautioned: "Nobody wants to see a world where you have to get permission from the government to release the next version of an AI model." (Source: POLITICO)
