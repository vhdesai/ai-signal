# University of Vaasa research argues AI adoption risk is a skills-management problem

University of Vaasa research suggests generative AI can make employees more engaged and adaptable when they treat the technology as a collaborator rather than a threat. The work also warns that both over-trust and under-trust can harm outcomes: uncritical reliance increases error risk, while blanket distrust leaves productivity gains untapped. The study is a useful counterpoint to displacement narratives because it reframes workforce AI strategy around skill development, judgment, and trust calibration.
