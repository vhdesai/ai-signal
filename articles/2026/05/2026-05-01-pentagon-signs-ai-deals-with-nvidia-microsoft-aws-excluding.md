# Pentagon Signs AI Deals with Nvidia, Microsoft, AWS — Excluding Anthropic

The U.S. Department of Defense signed AI deployment agreements with Nvidia, Microsoft, AWS, and Reflection AI for classified IL6/IL7 networks — notably excluding Anthropic, with which it is currently in active litigation. Anthropic had won an injunction in March against the DOD's attempt to brand it a "supply-chain risk" after rejecting the Pentagon's request for unrestricted use of its models for autonomous weapons and mass surveillance. More than 1.3 million DOD personnel now use the secure GenAI.mil platform.
