# NVIDIA Research advances robotics from simulation to the real world at ICRA

At the International Conference on Robotics and Automation, NVIDIA Research presented eight papers pushing past scripted automation toward generalizable embodied autonomy. The work spans sim-to-real transfer, dexterous manipulation, and reliable real-world deployment — signalling that physical AI is approaching the same inflection that language models reached two years ago.
