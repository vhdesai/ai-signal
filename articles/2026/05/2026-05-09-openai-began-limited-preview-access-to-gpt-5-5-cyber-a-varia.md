# OpenAI began limited preview access to GPT-5.5-Cyber, a variant of GPT-5.5 purpose-built for cybersecurity teams and…

OpenAI began limited preview access to GPT-5.5-Cyber, a variant of GPT-5.5 purpose-built for cybersecurity teams and trained to be more permissive on security-related tasks including vulnerability research and offensive emulation. The rollout is restricted to vetted organizations, mirroring the gated release Anthropic used for Claude Mythos Preview last month. OpenAI framed this as complementary to — not competing with — its existing safety policies, though the precedent of domain-specific permissive models is drawing scrutiny from policy observers. Notably, the White House has separately opposed broader release of Mythos' cyber capabilities even as NSA tests it internally.
