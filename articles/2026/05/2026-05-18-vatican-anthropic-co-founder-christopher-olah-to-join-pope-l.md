# Vatican: Anthropic Co-Founder Christopher Olah to Join Pope Leo XI for Historic AI Encyclical Hot

The Vatican confirmed that Christopher Olah, an Anthropic co-founder and the field's leading researcher in neural network interpretability, will stand alongside Pope Leo XI on May 25 for the public launch of the Pope's first encyclical—a formal doctrinal document outlining the Catholic Church's official theological and ethical position on artificial intelligence. The encyclical is expected to address AI's implications for human dignity, labor displacement, consciousness, and moral responsibility. The event marks the most prominent engagement between a frontier AI researcher and a major world religious institution in the technology's history.
