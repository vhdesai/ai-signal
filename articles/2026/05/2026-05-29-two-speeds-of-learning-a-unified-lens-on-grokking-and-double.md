# "Two Speeds of Learning": a unified lens on grokking and double descent

Researchers propose a representation-versus-readout decomposition that separates representation learning dynamics from output-head dynamics to jointly explain both grokking and double descent. The framework offers a unified theoretical lens on two of the most studied generalization phenomena in deep learning.
