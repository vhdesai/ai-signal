# New Cornell AI Initiative Opens "Community-Centered AI" Three-Day Convening

A three-day Cornell convening began May 18, bringing researchers, practitioners, and community members together to address AI's carbon footprint, displacement of local expertise, and violations of community consent. Format includes participatory algorithm-auditing workshops and solution-generating discussions. The event reflects Cornell's continued investment in responsible-AI research and signals growing institutional attention to community-level AI impacts beyond benchmark performance metrics.
