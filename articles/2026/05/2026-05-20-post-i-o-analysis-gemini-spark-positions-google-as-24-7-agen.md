# Post-I/O Analysis: Gemini Spark Positions Google as 24/7 Agentic Platform Trending

Post-keynote analysis on May 20–21 highlighted Gemini Spark — Google's new always-on AI agent — as the strategic centerpiece of I/O. Analysts described Google treating Gemini as an OS-level layer rather than a standalone product. Separately, Google redesigned its Search box for the first time in 25 years, now accepting images, files, videos, and Chrome tabs as input with AI-powered, context-aware suggestions beyond autocomplete. The cumulative picture: Google is embedding Gemini into every surface it owns, aiming for ubiquity over exclusivity. 2 · Research Breakthroughs
