# SoftBank to Invest up to €75B in French Data Centers

SoftBank said it will invest up to €75 billion (~$87B) to build data centers in France, targeting up to 5 GW of capacity. The move extends the sovereign-AI infrastructure buildout into Western Europe and reinforces that compute capacity — not model architecture — remains the binding constraint and the largest capital sink in the current cycle.
