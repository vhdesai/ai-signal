# Hot Gemini Spark: Google's 24/7 Personal AI Agent — Launching Next Week for Ultra Subscribers

Google's most ambitious product announcement at I/O is Gemini Spark — a persistent background AI agent running on Google Cloud VMs even when your device is off. It works autonomously across Gmail, Docs, Sheets, Slides, third-party apps, and the web, surfacing progress updates via Android's new Halo notification layer and queuing high-stakes actions for user approval before executing. Spark launches next week for Google AI Ultra subscribers (price slashed from $250 to $100/month); Chrome integration and MCP support for apps like Canva and OpenTable follow this summer. CEO Sundar Pichai noted the Gemini app now has 900 million monthly active users — 2× year-over-year growth — and Google processes 9.7 trillion tokens per month.
