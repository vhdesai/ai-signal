# Cyber leaders brace for fragmented AI oversight in the U.S.

WSJ Pro CyberSecurity reports that security leaders are preparing for a more fragmented AI compliance landscape after the Trump administration stepped back from a federal AI oversight order. The newsletter notes that states, the European Union, and China continue to move ahead on AI governance, leaving CISOs to manage overlapping requirements while AI agents and vulnerability-discovery models expand the attack surface. For enterprises, the practical implication is that governance cannot wait for a single federal standard.
