# Google launches Pics — AI-native design app embedded in Google Workspace

Google announced Pics, a new AI design app powered by the Nano Banana 2 image model and embedded natively in Google Workspace, targeting Canva and Anthropic's Claude Design. Users can click any element of a generated image and leave a comment or edit directly — mirroring Google Docs review mode. Available to I/O testers now, rolling out to Google AI Ultra subscribers this summer.
