# Gartner projects global AI spending will reach $2.59 trillion in 2026

CIO Dive reported that global AI spending is expected to reach $2.59 trillion in 2026, with enterprises accounting for a significant share as generative and agentic AI adoption accelerates. The estimate helps explain the intensity of infrastructure partnerships and the urgency behind enterprise platform consolidation. It also suggests CIO scrutiny will increasingly shift from experimentation to cost, integration and measurable return.
