# Google DeepMind unveils Gemini Omni — a natively multimodal world model

DeepMind introduced Gemini Omni, a natively multimodal model that processes video, audio, images, and text from a single architecture — rather than converting each modality to text tokens first. The first variant, Gemini Omni Flash, ships immediately in the Gemini app, Google Flow, and YouTube Shorts, and supports multi-turn conversational video editing with character continuity and physics-aware scene consistency. DeepMind framed Omni as a step toward "creating anything from any input, starting with video."
