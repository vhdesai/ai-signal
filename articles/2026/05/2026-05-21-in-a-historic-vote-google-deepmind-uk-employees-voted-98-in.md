# In a historic vote, Google DeepMind UK employees voted 98% in favor of unionization — becoming the first union at any top-tier AI research lab globally.

In a historic vote, Google DeepMind UK employees voted 98% in favor of unionization — becoming the first union at any top-tier AI research lab globally. The vote was triggered primarily by DeepMind's undisclosed participation in a classified Pentagon AI contract, which employees argue they had no opportunity to evaluate or consent to. The union's formation is expected to pressure other major AI labs on governance, disclosure, and employee consent for defense-related work.
