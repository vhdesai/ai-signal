# UMG and TikTok renew global licensing with AI governance at the center — TechCrunch / The AI Insider / Music Ally, May…

UMG and TikTok renew global licensing with AI governance at the center — TechCrunch / The AI Insider / Music Ally, May 26-27, 2026 Universal Music Group and TikTok announced a multi-year renewal of their global licensing agreement with explicit commitments to remove unauthorized AI-generated music and tighten songwriter and artist attribution. The deal repairs the 2024 rupture over AI content concerns and is being treated by industry analysts as an emerging template for how platforms negotiate AI accountability with rights holders — a structure EU AI Act implementing measures and U.S. state regimes are likely to pressure other platforms to adopt.
