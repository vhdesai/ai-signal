# Google unveils Coral Board — a tiny on-device AI computer running Gemma 3 locally

Google introduced the Coral Board, a compact single-board computer built around the open-source Coral NPU on RISC-V. Powered by a Synaptics Astra chip with 2 GB RAM and 1 TOPS of compute, it runs Gemma 3 270M entirely on-device — targeting headphones, AR glasses, and smartwatches. Demos at I/O included real-time translation and voice-controlled hardware. Ships this summer.
