# UC Berkeley School of Law announced it will prohibit AI use in almost all graded assignments — including outlining,…

UC Berkeley School of Law announced it will prohibit AI use in almost all graded assignments — including outlining, drafting, and proofreading — starting summer 2026. Only research use remains permitted. The school's rationale: future lawyers must demonstrate core legal reasoning skills without AI assistance, and the bar exam does not permit AI. This is one of the most aggressive academic AI restriction policies implemented at a top-tier US institution. Source: The Decoder (May 23, 2026)
