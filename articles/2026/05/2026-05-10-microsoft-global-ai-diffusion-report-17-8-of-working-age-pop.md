# Microsoft Global AI Diffusion Report — 17.8% of Working-Age Population Now Using AI

Microsoft's Q1 2026 Global AI Diffusion Report found 17.8% of the global working-age population used generative AI — up 1.5 percentage points from Q4 2025. The UAE leads at 70.1%; the US ranks 21st at 31.3%. Git pushes grew 78% YoY globally as AI coding tools drove a developer productivity surge — and paradoxically, US software developer employment reached a record 2.2 million in 2025 (+8.5%), suggesting AI may be growing software demand rather than displacing jobs. (Source: Microsoft On the Issues)
