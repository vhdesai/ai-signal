# Mistral CEO confirms exploration of custom AI chip design

France's Mistral confirmed it is exploring designing its own silicon as it builds out infrastructure capacity. The move would put Mistral on a path similar to OpenAI's and Anthropic's vertical-integration plays and would mark the most concrete European response yet to dependence on NVIDIA accelerators. Mistral's Le Chat Work Mode and Medium 3.5 model continue to anchor enterprise traction.
