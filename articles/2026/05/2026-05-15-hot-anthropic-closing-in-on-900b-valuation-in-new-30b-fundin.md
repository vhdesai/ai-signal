# 🔥 HOT Anthropic Closing In on $900B Valuation in New $30B Funding Round

Anthropic is in the final stages of a funding round expected to raise roughly $30 billion, which would value the Claude maker at approximately $900 billion — nearly tripling its February 2026 valuation. This comes just weeks after the company disclosed Q1 revenue grew 80x year-over-year, pushing annualized recurring revenue above $44B. The round would position Anthropic as one of the most valuable private companies in history, ahead of a widely anticipated IPO later in 2026.
