# Trending Huawei Eyes $12B in AI Chip Revenue as DeepSeek V4 Redirects Chinese Orders Away from Nvidia

Huawei projects AI chip revenue will climb roughly 60% to $12 billion in 2026, driven by a flood of orders from Chinese tech giants following DeepSeek V4's release — a model specifically optimized for Huawei's Ascend 950PR processors. U.S. export restrictions have accelerated the domestic pivot, and Huawei is the principal beneficiary. The projection, based on current order volume, underscores that the China AI infrastructure market is rapidly decoupling from Nvidia, a structural shift that pressures Nvidia's long-term revenue outlook beyond current export ban impacts.
