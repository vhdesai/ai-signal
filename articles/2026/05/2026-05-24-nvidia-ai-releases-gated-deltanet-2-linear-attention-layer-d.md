# NVIDIA AI releases Gated DeltaNet-2 — linear-attention layer decoupling erase/write

NVIDIA AI released Gated DeltaNet-2, a linear attention layer that decouples the erase and write operations in the delta rule — a structural change relevant for long-context efficiency and agentic-loop stability. Positioned in NVIDIA's continuing push to make linear-attention architectures competitive with quadratic attention for production inference.
