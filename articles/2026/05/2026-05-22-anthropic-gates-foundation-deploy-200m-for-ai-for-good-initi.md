# Anthropic + Gates Foundation deploy $200M for "AI-for-good" initiatives

Anthropic and the Gates Foundation announced a $200 million partnership focused on vaccine discovery, education, and economic mobility. Anthropic also rolled out new Claude products tailored for small businesses (financial and operational workflows) and creative professionals (content production), broadening its enterprise surface area beyond developer use cases.
