# CATL (Contemporary Amperex Technology) is planning to participate in DeepSeek's first-ever funding round, which targets ~50 billion yuan ($7.35B) and could close as early as June. DeepSeek's valuation could exceed 350 billion yuan ($51.4B) upon completion. JD.com and NetEase are also in discussions. The investment reflects CATL's aggressive push into AI data center power infrastructure, where the battery giant is seeking to sell power equipment as compute demand surges.
