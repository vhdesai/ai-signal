# Meta has acquired Assured Robot Intelligence (ARI), a humanoid robotics startup, in a move to accelerate its physical…

Meta has acquired Assured Robot Intelligence (ARI), a humanoid robotics startup, in a move to accelerate its physical AI ambitions alongside its existing software and foundation model investments. The acquisition signals Meta's intent to compete in the embodied AI space against Tesla's Optimus, Figure, and 1X Technologies. ARI brings specialized expertise in robot motor control, perception systems, and real-world manipulation tasks. The deal follows Meta's broader AGI-oriented strategic shift announced by Zuckerberg earlier this year.
