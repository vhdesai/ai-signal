# Illinois passes nation's toughest AI safety law as federal authority recedes

Days after President Trump canceled a plan giving the federal government authority to vet frontier AI models, the Illinois legislature passed SB 315 — the strongest state-level AI safety law in the U.S. If signed by Governor Pritzker (who has signaled he will sign), the largest AI firms must publish safety plans, file annual third-party safety-test summaries, and report critical incidents within 72 hours (24 if imminent harm is suspected). Both Anthropic and OpenAI publicly endorsed the bill.
