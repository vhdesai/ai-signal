# Google DeepMind Turns the Mouse Cursor Into a Gemini AI Interface

DeepMind is treating cursor position as a real-time signal for Gemini, letting the model infer user intent from what the cursor hovers over rather than requiring explicit text prompts. The "AI Pointer" prototype lets users hover over a data table, say "make a pie chart," and receive an inline visualization. The pattern hints at a broader shift toward ambient agentic UX inside everyday productivity surfaces.
