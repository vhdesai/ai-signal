# Google Research: Catalyzing Scientific Impact Through Global AI Partnerships New

Google Research published a new piece highlighting its strategy for catalyzing scientific impact through open resources and global academic partnerships, spanning data mining, health and bioscience, and open-source model initiatives. The post coincides with Google's AI Impact Summit in India where the company announced new global AI funding and partnership programs. It reflects Google's continued push to frame AI research as a collaborative, global scientific endeavor rather than purely a commercial race. 🛠 3. Products & Tools
