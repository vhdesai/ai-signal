# Alibaba Qwen3.7-Max: 35 Hours of Autonomous Execution, 1M-Token Context  Hot

Alibaba launched Qwen3.7-Max, a proprietary (no longer open-source) agentic model with a 1M-token context window, demonstrating 35 hours of autonomous execution on a kernel-optimization task involving 1,158 tool calls. The model supports cross-harness generalization including third-party scaffolds such as Claude Code, and reportedly beats GLM-5.1 and Kimi K2.6 on long-horizon tasks. Access is currently limited to Chinese-based endpoints, raising data-sovereignty questions for Western enterprises.
