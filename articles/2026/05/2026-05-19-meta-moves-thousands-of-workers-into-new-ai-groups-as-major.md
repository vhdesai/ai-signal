# Meta Moves Thousands of Workers Into New AI Groups as Major Layoffs Loom

Meta is shifting thousands of employees into newly formed AI units ahead of a broader workforce reduction expected this week. The reorganization is paired with a $145B AI infrastructure raise — making explicit Meta's pivot from headcount-led growth to compute-led growth. Roughly 20% of the workforce is being moved into AI-aligned roles.
