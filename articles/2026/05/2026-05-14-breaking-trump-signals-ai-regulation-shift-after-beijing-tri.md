# 🔴 BREAKING Trump Signals AI Regulation Shift After Beijing Trip; Xi Guardrails Dialogue Opens

President Trump indicated he discussed possible AI guardrails with Xi Jinping during his Beijing visit this week — a notable rhetorical shift from an administration that has prioritized AI innovation over safety frameworks since January 2025. U.S. officials are simultaneously weighing AI safety risks, US-China competition dynamics, and the fate of Nvidia chip exports to China. While the Trump administration previously dismissed European-style regulation, aides suggest the competitive pressure from Chinese AI models is creating new political appetite for some form of bilateral AI governance dialogue.
