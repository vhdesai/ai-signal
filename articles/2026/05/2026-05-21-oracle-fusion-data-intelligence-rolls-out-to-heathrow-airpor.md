# Oracle Fusion Data Intelligence rolls out to Heathrow Airport, Kent County Council, MTN

Oracle announced that Heathrow Airport, Kent (UK county council), and MTN are live on Oracle Fusion Data Intelligence, extending AI-driven analytics to operations, finance, and HR. The rollout marks the product's first major wave of enterprise go-lives and positions Oracle's ERP-native AI capabilities against Microsoft Fabric and Databricks for enterprise intelligence workloads.
