# China expands travel curbs to top AI talent at private firms

Chinese authorities are extending exit-ban-style travel restrictions previously reserved for SOE scientists to senior AI researchers at private firms including ByteDance, Alibaba, and DeepSeek. The measure is framed as protecting national strategic AI talent from US recruiting and follows a wave of high-profile defections.
