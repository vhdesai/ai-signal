# Bloomberg reports Anthropic's latest funding round — at least $30 billion — is expected to close by end of May 2026 at…

Bloomberg reports Anthropic's latest funding round — at least $30 billion — is expected to close by end of May 2026 at a valuation exceeding $900 billion. Co-led by Sequoia, Dragoneer, Greenoaks, and Altimeter, the round would make Anthropic more valuable than OpenAI (valued at $852B in March 2026) for the first time — a remarkable reversal from Anthropic's $380B valuation just three months ago in February. CEO Dario Amodei has indicated the capital is targeted at compute infrastructure buildout, primarily AWS and Google Cloud commitments through 2027. This is infrastructure-scale capital — not growth-stage fundraising — reflecting the race to control compute capacity as the primary competitive moat.
