# Google Gemini 3.5 Flash & Gemini Omni Debut at I/O 2026 Hot

Google unveiled Gemini 3.5 Flash at its I/O 2026 keynote — engineered for complex, long-horizon agentic and coding tasks, it is now the default model powering AI Mode in Search. Alongside it, Google launched Gemini Omni Flash, the first model in a new multimodal family that accepts any combination of text, images, audio, and video as input and generates or edits video through conversational back-and-forth. Day 2 on May 20 brought developer deep-dives, Gemma model updates, and Google Marketing Live integrations. Gemini 3.5 Pro remains in internal testing with a broader rollout planned, while Gemini 3.5 Flash is already live across Search, Chrome, and Workspace.
