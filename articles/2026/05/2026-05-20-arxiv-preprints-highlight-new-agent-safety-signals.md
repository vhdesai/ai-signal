# arXiv Preprints Highlight New Agent-Safety Signals

A wave of new arXiv preprints converged on agent reliability: papers detailed jailbreak transfer across model families, prompt-injection in retrieval pipelines, and a benchmark for measuring agent behavior under adversarial tool use. The collective finding — that agentic systems remain materially less robust than chat-style deployments — is feeding into both policy debate and enterprise procurement criteria.
