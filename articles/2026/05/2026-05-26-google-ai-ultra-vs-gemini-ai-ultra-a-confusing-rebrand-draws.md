# Google AI Ultra vs. Gemini AI Ultra: a confusing rebrand draws backlash

Google's consumer "Google AI Ultra" subscription and Workspace "Gemini AI Ultra" tier share nearly identical names but differ in feature set, model access, and price. Clarifying guidance was issued Tuesday after user complaints. The muddled naming risks blunting the rollout of Gemini Spark, the personal-agent tier launched at I/O.
