# Trending CMU PolyPulse: contactless mmWave-radar AI tracks blood flow, estimates blood pressure

PolyPulse is an AI-powered millimeter-wave radar platform that emits radar chirps at a seated person and uses beamforming plus neural networks to capture microscopic cardiac-driven motion simultaneously at the heart apex, carotid, radial artery, and mastoid. From those signals it reconstructs pulse-transit times along multiple vascular pathways, yielding remote estimates of arterial stiffness and diastolic blood pressure — a marker linked to hypertension, stroke, coronary disease, and Alzheimer's risk. Unlike prior contactless monitors that track a single pathway, PolyPulse covers several at once, enabling continuous at-home cardiovascular screening from a smart-speaker-sized device.
