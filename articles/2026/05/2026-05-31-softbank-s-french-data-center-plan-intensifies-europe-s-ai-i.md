# SoftBank’s French data-center plan intensifies Europe’s AI infrastructure race

SoftBank’s plan to invest tens of billions in French data centers adds another major capital commitment to the AI infrastructure race. The strategic signal is that sovereign compute, power access, land, and network capacity are becoming national competitiveness levers. For technology leaders, this is a reminder that AI roadmaps increasingly depend on real assets and permitting as much as model roadmaps.
