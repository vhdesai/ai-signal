# Springer Nature: Cluster of Applied AI Papers in Energy, Cybersecurity, Governance

Springer's AI feed published several peer-reviewed papers, including "Explainable AI-driven prognostics for battery health in sustainable energy systems" (Neural Computing and Applications), "Spacnet: spectral-aware dual-path CNN-transformer for encrypted traffic classification in ICVs" (Cybersecurity), "AI in urban governance: impact on citizen participation" (SN Social Sciences), and "TA-FRCNet for attack detection in blockchain network layer." None is paradigm-shifting individually, but the cluster underscores how applied-AI research is fanning out into vertical infrastructure rather than just frontier-model architecture.
