# Researchers from UC Berkeley, Stanford, CMU, Databricks, and Google announced the ACM CAIS 2026 workshop "AI Agents for…

Researchers from UC Berkeley, Stanford, CMU, Databricks, and Google announced the ACM CAIS 2026 workshop "AI Agents for Discovery in the Wild," with a submission deadline extended to May 6 to accommodate NeurIPS '26 submissions. The workshop focuses on autonomous AI systems for search, optimization, and scientific discovery with invited speakers including Ion Stoica (UC Berkeley), Graham Neubig (CMU/OpenHands), Azalia Mirhoseini (Stanford/Ricursive Intelligence), and James Zou (Stanford). The workshop reflects growing academic focus on closing the gap between benchmark performance and real-world deployment reliability.
