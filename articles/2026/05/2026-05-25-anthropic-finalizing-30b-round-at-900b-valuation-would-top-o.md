# Anthropic finalizing $30B round at >$900B valuation — would top OpenAI's March mark

Anthropic is reportedly closing a $30B funding round co-led by Sequoia, Dragoneer, Greenoaks, and Altimeter. If priced at the top of the range, the >$900B valuation would surpass OpenAI's $852B March mark and make Anthropic the highest-valued private AI lab to date. The story significantly shifts the competitive financing picture across the frontier-lab tier.
