# DeepSeek closes $4B round, intensifying the open-weights competition

China's DeepSeek closed a $4 billion funding round that values the lab among the top-tier global frontier players. The raise will fund a multi-cluster training campaign and is expected to accelerate the next open-weights release — a meaningful counterweight to the closed-model momentum at OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google.
