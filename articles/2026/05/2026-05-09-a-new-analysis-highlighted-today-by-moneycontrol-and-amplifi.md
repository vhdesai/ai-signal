# A new analysis highlighted today by Moneycontrol and amplified across tech media argues that OpenAI, Anthropic, and…

A new analysis highlighted today by Moneycontrol and amplified across tech media argues that OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google's deepening enterprise partnerships with private equity firms in India represent an emerging competitive threat to the country's $250B+ IT services industry — as traditionally labor-intensive services become increasingly automatable. The analysis notes that major Indian IT firms (Infosys, TCS, Wipro) are simultaneously investing heavily in AI partnerships to adapt, but the pace of automation could outstrip workforce reskilling timelines. This story has immediate relevance for enterprise deal dynamics and the M&A landscape in Indian IT.
