# NTSB Blocks Public Docket Access After Researchers Used AI to Reconstruct Deceased Pilots' Voices

The National Transportation Safety Board temporarily suspended public access to its docket system after researchers used AI on spectrogram images of cockpit voice recordings to reconstruct deceased pilots' voices. The action highlights a new category of risk involving AI-generated content built from public-record audio data — sitting in a regulatory grey zone between public-interest research and posthumous-likeness ethics. Expect this to influence future open-records frameworks across federal agencies.
