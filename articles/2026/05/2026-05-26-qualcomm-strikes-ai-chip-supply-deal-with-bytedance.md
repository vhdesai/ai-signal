# Qualcomm strikes AI chip supply deal with ByteDance

ByteDance has agreed to procure millions of Qualcomm AI-focused ASICs to power its AI-agent software, making ByteDance one of Qualcomm's first major data-center ASIC customers. Qualcomm shares rose nearly 5% on the news. The deal is a meaningful step in Qualcomm's pivot from a mobile-SoC story into a credible data-center accelerator vendor.
