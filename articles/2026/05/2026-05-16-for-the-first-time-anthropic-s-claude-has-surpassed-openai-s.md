# For the first time, Anthropic's Claude has surpassed OpenAI's ChatGPT in U.S

For the first time, Anthropic's Claude has surpassed OpenAI's ChatGPT in U.S. enterprise AI adoption, per the May 2026 Ramp AI Index (tracking 50,000+ businesses). Claude adoption rose 3.8% to 34.4%; OpenAI fell 2.9% to 32.3%. Overall AI business adoption crossed 50.6%. Anthropic quadrupled its enterprise adoption over the past year vs. OpenAI's 0.3% growth. VentureBeat notes three structural threats that could reverse the lead: compute access, safety perception, and enterprise trust at scale.
