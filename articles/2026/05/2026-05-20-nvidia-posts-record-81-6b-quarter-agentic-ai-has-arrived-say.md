# Nvidia Posts Record $81.6B Quarter — "Agentic AI Has Arrived," Says Jensen Huang

Nvidia reported Q1 FY2027 revenue of $81.6 billion, up 85% year-over-year and beating the $78.9B consensus. Data center revenue hit a record $75.2 billion (+92% YoY), with the Blackwell architecture driving demand across hyperscalers, AI-native clouds, and sovereign customers in nearly 40 countries. The board authorized an additional $80B in buybacks and raised the dividend 25-fold to $0.25/share; Q2 guidance of ~$91B again topped estimates. CEO Jensen Huang declared "demand has gone parabolic" and flagged the new Vera CPU as a potential $200B opportunity.
