# CausaLab: scalable environment for interactive causal discovery

A scalable interactive sandbox lets LLM agents perform causal discovery on synthetic and real systems with controllable ground truth. The authors position it as the first benchmark combining causal interventions with agent-style behavior at scale. Directly relevant to the autonomous-research-agent thesis already being commercialized by DeepMind's Co-Scientist and Lila Sciences.
