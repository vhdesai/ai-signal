# NVIDIA released SANA-WM, a 2.6 billion parameter world model capable of generating 1-minute 720p video from text prompts

NVIDIA released SANA-WM, a 2.6 billion parameter world model capable of generating 1-minute 720p video from text prompts. The release is notable for its compact size relative to its output quality and marks a meaningful advance in text-to-video generation. Early HN discussion (92 points) flagged it as a meaningful step for physical AI and simulation pipelines.
