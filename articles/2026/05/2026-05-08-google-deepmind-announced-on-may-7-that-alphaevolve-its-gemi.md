# Google DeepMind announced on May 7 that AlphaEvolve — its Gemini-powered coding agent that autonomously discovers and…

Google DeepMind announced on May 7 that AlphaEvolve — its Gemini-powered coding agent that autonomously discovers and optimizes algorithms — has transitioned from a research tool into core Google infrastructure and is being deployed to external enterprise partners. Quantified production results include a 10x reduction in quantum circuit errors on the Willow processor, a 30% reduction in DNA sequencing variant-detection errors in genomics workflows, and measurable improvements in TPU chip design and large-scale database systems. AlphaEvolve is now also active in commercial logistics optimization. The transition from research preview to multi-domain production system within roughly 12 months is a significant milestone for agentic AI delivering measurable enterprise value.
