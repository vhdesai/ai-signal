# Pentagon deploys Anthropic's Mythos to patch cyber gaps — while racing to off-board Anthropic

DOD CTO Emil Michael disclosed the Pentagon is actively using Anthropic's Mythos cybersecurity model (under "Project Glasswing") to find and patch software vulnerabilities across US government systems — even as the DoD attempts to off-board Anthropic after declaring it a supply-chain risk. Anthropic sued the Trump administration in March to reverse the blacklisting. Michael called it "a national security moment," framing Mythos as too critical to pause regardless of procurement concerns. The situation underscores how frontier AI has become genuine strategic infrastructure.
