# AWS Veteran Matt Wood Returns as Chief AI and Technology Officer

Amazon Web Services veteran Matt Wood is returning to AWS in a newly created role as Chief AI and Technology Officer, reporting to AWS CMO Julia White. Wood spent over 14 years building AWS's AI and ML product portfolio before departing in 2024 to lead AI strategy at PwC. His return signals AWS's intent to deepen customer-facing AI engagement as it competes with Azure and Google Cloud for enterprise AI platform dominance.
