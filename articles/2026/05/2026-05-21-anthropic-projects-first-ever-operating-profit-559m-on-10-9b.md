# Anthropic Projects First-Ever Operating Profit: $559M on $10.9B Q2 Revenue  Hot

Anthropic projects a Q2 2026 operating profit of $559 million on $10.9 billion in revenue — up 130% from Q1's $4.8 billion — making it the first frontier AI lab projected to reach operating profitability. Coding tools and long-horizon agentic Claude usage are the primary revenue drivers, while compute cost per revenue dollar has fallen from 71¢ to a projected 56¢. The milestone arrives roughly two years ahead of Anthropic's own internal target and lands the same week as the OpenAI IPO filing.
