# Microsoft's 2026 Global AI Diffusion Report documented that 17.8% of the global working-age population now uses AI…

Microsoft's 2026 Global AI Diffusion Report documented that 17.8% of the global working-age population now uses AI tools regularly — with the UAE leading adoption at 70.1%, followed by Singapore and South Korea. US software developer employment is at a record high despite widespread AI-driven layoff headlines, reinforcing the thesis that AI is currently augmenting developer productivity rather than replacing developers in aggregate. The report also found that software developer employment reached historic highs even as the IT sector shed 13,000 jobs in April 2026.
