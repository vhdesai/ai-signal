# Before the cancellation, the White House's Office of the National Cyber Director hosted a briefing for OpenAI, Anthropic, Reflection AI, cloud providers, semiconductor companies, and banks on the executive order. The proposed voluntary framework would have had AI labs inform the government about planned releases and share models up to 90 days in advance. The push followed growing concern from the Treasury Department and Federal Reserve about cybersecurity risks posed by advanced AI models, particularly Anthropic's Claude Mythos.
