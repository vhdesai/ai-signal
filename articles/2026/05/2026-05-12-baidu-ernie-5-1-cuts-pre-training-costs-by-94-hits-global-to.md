# Baidu ERNIE 5.1 Cuts Pre-Training Costs by 94%, Hits Global Top-5

Baidu officially released ERNIE 5.1 with a striking efficiency claim: roughly 94% lower training cost than comparable frontier-class systems, achieved through a "parameter efficiency" leap. The model ranks fourth on LMArena and tops Chinese AI leaderboards. The release reinforces a broader trend of Chinese labs prioritizing cost-per-FLOP as a competitive lever against scale-led Western labs.
