# Microsoft 365 E7 & Agent 365 — Generally Available

Microsoft's first new enterprise license tier in a decade — M365 E7 (the "Frontier Suite") — became generally available May 1 at $99/user/month, bundling E5, M365 Copilot, Agent 365, and the Entra Suite. Agent 365 became generally available May 2, providing a governance and identity control plane for AI agents across enterprise environments. Microsoft reported M365 Copilot has crossed 15 million paid seats with daily active usage up 10x YoY. (Sources: CRN, Microsoft Learn)
