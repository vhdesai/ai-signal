# Weave launches AI receptionist on Google Gemini Enterprise Agent Platform

Weave unveiled an enterprise-grade omnichannel AI receptionist for healthcare front offices built on Google Cloud's Gemini Enterprise Agent Platform. The product targets a high-friction operational workflow — intake, scheduling, communications, and front-desk coordination. Healthcare front-office automation is a practical example of vertical AI agents moving into production business processes.
