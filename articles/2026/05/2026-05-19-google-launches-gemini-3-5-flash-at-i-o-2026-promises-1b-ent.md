# Google launches Gemini 3.5 Flash at I/O 2026, promises $1B+ enterprise savings

At I/O 2026, Sundar Pichai unveiled Gemini 3.5 Flash, positioned as faster, cheaper, and more capable than its predecessor, with Google claiming customers running ~1 trillion tokens/day on Google Cloud could save over $1 billion annually. The model anchors a sweeping agent-stack rollout alongside Gemini Omni and Gemini Spark, with 3.5 Pro listed as coming soon in the same family. The 3.5 series is framed as "frontier intelligence with action" and is specifically tuned for agentic and coding workloads.
