# OpenAI GPT-5.5 Pricing Controversy: Users Report 40% Bill Increases Despite Efficiency Gains

OpenAI shipped GPT-5.5 on April 23 with standout benchmarks — 82.7% on Terminal-Bench 2.0 and 58.6% on SWE-Bench Pro — making it the strongest agentic coding model in OpenAI's lineup. However, May 2026 price increases have enterprise users reporting approximately 40% higher bills despite the model using fewer tokens per task. The efficiency paradox — where technical improvements are captured as company margin rather than passed to customers — is now reshaping enterprise AI procurement strategies. OpenAI noted that 92% of Fortune 500 companies now use its products. 🔬 Research Breakthroughs
