# Andrej Karpathy joins Anthropic's pre-training team

Andrej Karpathy — founding member of OpenAI, former director of AI at Tesla, and creator of Stanford's first deep-learning course — announced on X that he is joining Anthropic. Karpathy said "the next few years at the frontier of LLMs will be especially formative," and signaled he will continue side work on AI-native education via Eureka Labs. The hire is a coup for Anthropic as researchers increasingly leave frontier labs for "neolab" startups.
