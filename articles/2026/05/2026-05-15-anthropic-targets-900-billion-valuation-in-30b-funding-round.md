# Anthropic Targets $900 Billion Valuation in ~$30B Funding Round — PwC and Gates Foundation Among New Backers

Anthropic is in advanced discussions to raise approximately $30 billion at a valuation approaching $900 billion — nearly tripling its February 2026 valuation and surpassing OpenAI's most recent private-market mark. PwC is reportedly going "all-in" on Claude with a firm-wide enterprise agreement, while a deal with the Gates Foundation for AI-assisted global health initiatives has been confirmed. The round comes on the heels of Anthropic disclosing Q1 2026 revenue of 80× year-over-year, with ARR exceeding $44 billion, driven by Claude Code, enterprise agents, and its JPMorgan financial services partnership.
