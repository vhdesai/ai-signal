# HOTReplit Tracking Toward $1B ARR; Cursor in Talks with SpaceX at $60B

Replit CEO Amjad Masad disclosed at TechCrunch's StrictlyVC event that the company is tracking toward a billion-dollar annual run rate — up from $2.8M in all of 2024 — with net revenue retention up to 300%. Meanwhile, rival Cursor is reportedly in talks for a $60B acquisition by SpaceX (structured as a $10B Colossus collaboration with an option to acquire). The AI coding category continues its astonishing capital concentration.
