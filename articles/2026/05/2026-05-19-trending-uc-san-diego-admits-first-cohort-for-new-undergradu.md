# Trending UC San Diego Admits First Cohort for New Undergraduate AI Major

UC San Diego's new undergraduate AI major admitted its first cohort of approximately 125 freshmen for fall 2025, with USC and CSU San Diego State launching comparable programs in fall 2026. CSE chair Steven Swanson cites BLS projections of 7.5% growth in AI-system roles as the curriculum rationale. The piece also surfaces campus social dynamics — reported tension between traditional CS students and the new AI-major cohort — as institutions grapple with differentiated AI-focused degree tracks.
