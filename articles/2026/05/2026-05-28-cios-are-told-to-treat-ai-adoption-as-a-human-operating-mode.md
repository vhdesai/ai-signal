# CIOs are told to treat AI adoption as a human operating-model problem

CIO Dive’s enterprise adoption coverage argued that AI rollouts often stall because organizations underinvest in user readiness, process redesign, and risk management. Forrester’s J. P. Gownder framed AI launches as “a very human exercise,” which is a useful reminder that enterprise AI value will depend on workforce design as much as model capability.
