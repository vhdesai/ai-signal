# NIST to evaluate upcoming frontier models before public release

A new multi-agency task force coordinated by NIST will assess national-security risks of cutting-edge models prior to deployment, with leading U.S. AI companies agreeing to submit models for evaluation. The framework focuses on demonstrable risks in cybersecurity, biosecurity, and chemical weapons — a sharp reversal from the White House's earlier hands-off posture. Academic & Industry-Academic
