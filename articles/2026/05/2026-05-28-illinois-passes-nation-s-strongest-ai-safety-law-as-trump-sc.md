# Illinois passes nation's strongest AI safety law as Trump scraps federal AI plan

The Illinois legislature passed SB 315, the strongest U.S. state-level AI safety law to date. If signed by Governor Pritzker, the largest AI firms must publish safety plans, submit annual third-party safety testing summaries, and report critical incidents within 72 hours (24 hours when there is imminent risk). Both Anthropic and OpenAI publicly support the bill — and the law arrives just days after President Trump canceled a federal frontier-model vetting plan, accelerating state-level regulatory leadership.
