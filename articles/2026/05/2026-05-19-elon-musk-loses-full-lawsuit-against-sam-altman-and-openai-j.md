# Elon Musk Loses Full Lawsuit Against Sam Altman and OpenAI — Jury Unanimous

A California jury deliberated for less than two hours before unanimously rejecting Elon Musk's entire lawsuit against Sam Altman and OpenAI on May 19. TechCrunch reported the trial had exposed that Musk himself held similar aims for OpenAI during its early days, undermining the core nonprofit-mission arguments. The verdict removes a major legal overhang from OpenAI as it completes its for-profit restructuring and advances its commercial partnership with Oracle, Nvidia, and Microsoft.
