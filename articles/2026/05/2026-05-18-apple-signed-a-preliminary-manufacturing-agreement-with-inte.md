# Apple signed a preliminary manufacturing agreement with Intel for US-based chip production, responding to White House…

Apple signed a preliminary manufacturing agreement with Intel for US-based chip production, responding to White House pressure to reduce dependency on TSMC amid geopolitical risk. Intel's stock has risen 240% year-to-date on surging Xeon CPU demand for AI inference workloads, as the hardware focus shifts from GPU training to CPU-driven inference at scale. Separately, top motherboard manufacturers slashed 2026 shipment projections by 30% due to an AI-driven DDR5 memory shortage; AMD CEO Lisa Su projects the server CPU market will exceed $120B by 2030.
