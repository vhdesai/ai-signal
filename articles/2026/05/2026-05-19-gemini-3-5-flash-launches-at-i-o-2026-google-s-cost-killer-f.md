# Gemini 3.5 Flash Launches at I/O 2026 — Google's "Cost-Killer" Frontier Model

Google launched Gemini 3.5 Flash at its I/O 2026 keynote on May 19, positioning it as the model that "shatters the iron law" that smarter AI must be slower and more expensive. VentureBeat reported the model could cut enterprise AI costs by more than $1 billion annually at scale. It powers Gemini Spark and forms the backbone of Google's agentic product suite. It is available today across Google AI Plus, Pro, and Ultra tiers.
