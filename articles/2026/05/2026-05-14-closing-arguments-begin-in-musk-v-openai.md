# Closing Arguments Begin in Musk v. OpenAI

Closing arguments have begun in the long-running Musk v. OpenAI litigation, with the court set to rule on whether OpenAI's pivot away from its original non-profit charter breached founding commitments. A ruling could materially affect OpenAI's corporate structure, Microsoft's contractual rights, and the governance template the rest of the industry has copied.
