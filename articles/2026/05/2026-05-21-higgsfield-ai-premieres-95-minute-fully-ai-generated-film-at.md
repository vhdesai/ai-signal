# Higgsfield AI Premieres 95-Minute Fully AI-Generated Film at Cannes Trending

Higgsfield AI premiered Hell Grind — a 95-minute feature film generated entirely by AI — at the Cannes Film Festival. Total production cost was $500,000 (of which $400,000 went to AI compute) and the film was completed in two weeks. The premiere marks a symbolic milestone for generative video: Hollywood-length narrative content created without a human cast, crew, or traditional production pipeline. Guild and studio reactions at Cannes ranged from fascination to alarm, with intellectual property and labor implications expected to prompt legislative attention in the U.S. and EU before year-end. 4 · Industry News
