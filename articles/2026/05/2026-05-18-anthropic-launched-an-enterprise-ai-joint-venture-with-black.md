# Anthropic launched an enterprise AI joint venture with Blackstone, Hellman & Friedman, and Goldman Sachs as founding…

Anthropic launched an enterprise AI joint venture with Blackstone, Hellman & Friedman, and Goldman Sachs as founding partners — valued at $1.5B with $300M committed by each. Hours earlier, Bloomberg reported OpenAI was fundraising for "The Development Company," a similar vehicle targeting $4B from 19 investors. Both structures signal a new go-to-market strategy: institutional capital co-investing in enterprise deployment alongside the AI labs themselves, rather than traditional SaaS licensing.
