# Altman Tries to Turn the Tables on Musk in Contentious Trial Testimony

Sam Altman took the stand in the Musk v. OpenAI trial, testifying that Musk abandoned the group they co-founded and worked to undermine it. Musk's lawyers attacked Altman's honesty during cross-examination; Altman defended his for-profit restructuring as advancing the original charitable mission. The appearance was the highest-stakes moment of the trial to date.
