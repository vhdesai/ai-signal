# Virginia's Joint Legislative Audit and Review Commission (JLARC) began a year-long study on AI's use in state…

Virginia's Joint Legislative Audit and Review Commission (JLARC) began a year-long study on AI's use in state government and the private sector, examining risks, rewards, and potential regulatory approaches. Lawmakers highlighted AI's reach across education, transportation, and public services. The study will include expert interviews and benchmarking of other states' AI policy approaches — making Virginia one of a growing number of states filling the regulatory void at the federal level.
