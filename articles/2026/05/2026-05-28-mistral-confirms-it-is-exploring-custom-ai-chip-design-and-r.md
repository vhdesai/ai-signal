# Mistral confirms it is exploring custom AI chip design and rebrands Le Chat as "Vibe"

Mistral CEO Arthur Mensch confirmed the French lab is exploring designing its own silicon as it ramps up infrastructure capacity, joining OpenAI, Google, Amazon, and Meta in the in-house-ASIC race. Mistral also rebranded its consumer chatbot Le Chat as "Vibe," repositioning it from a Q&A assistant into a full agentic workspace and signaling the strongest European response yet to NVIDIA dependence.
