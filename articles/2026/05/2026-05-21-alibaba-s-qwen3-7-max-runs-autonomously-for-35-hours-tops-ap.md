# Alibaba's Qwen3.7-Max Runs Autonomously for 35 Hours, Tops Apex Math Benchmark

Alibaba's proprietary Qwen3.7-Max can operate autonomously for 35 hours and is compatible with external harnesses including Anthropic's Claude Code. On the Apex Math Reasoning benchmark, Qwen3.7-Max scored 44.5, eclipsing Claude Opus-4.6 Max (34.5) and DeepSeek V4-Pro Max (38.3). The release sharpens Chinese labs' positioning on agentic endurance and reasoning, a category previously dominated by U.S. frontier vendors.
