# Salvatore Sanfilippo (creator of Redis) published a nuanced analysis of DeepSeek V4, concluding the model is "almost on…

Salvatore Sanfilippo (creator of Redis) published a nuanced analysis of DeepSeek V4, concluding the model is "almost on the frontier" but still trails the very top tier in key reasoning tasks. The post generated 377 upvotes and 155 comments on Hacker News, making it one of the most-discussed AI pieces of the day. The 1.6-trillion-parameter Pro edition and the quantized Flash edition (145 GB, ~22 tokens/sec) serve distinct use cases, with developers trending toward Flash for local deployments. DeepSeek's pricing remains 5–35× cheaper than OpenAI equivalents.
