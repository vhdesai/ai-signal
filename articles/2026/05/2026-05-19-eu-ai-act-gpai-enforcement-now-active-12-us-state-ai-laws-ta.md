# EU AI Act GPAI Enforcement Now Active; 12+ US State AI Laws Taking Effect in 2026

The EU AI Act's General Purpose AI (GPAI) rules entered their operational enforcement phase in 2026, with the European Commission now issuing fines, audit letters, and procurement compliance checklists. Obligations include transparency requirements (Article 50), systematic risk assessments for frontier models, and mandatory incident reporting. Simultaneously, more than a dozen US state AI laws are taking effect or advancing through legislatures — from Colorado's early mover framework to California's expanding regulations — creating a complex, multi-jurisdictional compliance landscape for any company deploying public-facing AI. Finance teams at major enterprises are already modeling compliance costs as a line item.
