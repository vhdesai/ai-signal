# Google DeepMind reimagines the mouse pointer as a Gemini AI agent

Google DeepMind researchers Adrien Baranes and Rob Marchant published a landmark HCI x foundation-model paper reimagining the 50-year-old desktop cursor as a context-aware Gemini agent. The system — dubbed Magic Pointer — identifies on-screen text, images, objects, and locations in real time, allowing users to simply point at a building and say "show me directions" without typing. The feature will ship in Google's new Googlebook premium laptops launching fall 2026, and secondary coverage confirms it is driven by the same Gemini models powering the broader Android ecosystem. ◆ Products & Tools
