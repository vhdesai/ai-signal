# DeepSeek's upcoming V4 model — widely anticipated as a follow-on to the market-rattling V3 and R1 — is being optimized…

DeepSeek's upcoming V4 model — widely anticipated as a follow-on to the market-rattling V3 and R1 — is being optimized to run on Huawei's next-generation Ascend chips rather than Nvidia hardware. In preparation, Chinese tech giants Alibaba, ByteDance, and Tencent have placed bulk orders totaling hundreds of thousands of Huawei chip units. The shift signals a structural move toward a fully indigenous Chinese AI stack. If V4 achieves frontier-level performance on domestic silicon, it would substantially blunt the effectiveness of US export controls and accelerate a "two-track" global AI infrastructure — Nvidia outside China, Huawei inside.
