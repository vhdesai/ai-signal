# Latest Anthropic Mythos AI is "Even Better at Hacking," UK AISI Says

The U.K. AI Security Institute reported "notable capability jumps" in Anthropic's latest Mythos at finding and exploiting undiscovered software vulnerabilities. Anthropic has not released Mythos widely; access is gated to a small set of enterprises and government agencies. Palo Alto Networks and CrowdStrike shares are up roughly 20% YTD partly on the resulting "AI-cyber tailwind" thesis.
