# The Pope's AI encyclical isn't really about AI

Rebecca Bellan reads the 200-page encyclical as a critique of power concentration, citing Leo's line that "when such power is concentrated in the hands of a few, it tends to become opaque and evade public oversight," and his call to end the AI arms race. TechCrunch ties the timing to President Trump's recent delay of an AI executive order on the urging of VC David Sacks.
