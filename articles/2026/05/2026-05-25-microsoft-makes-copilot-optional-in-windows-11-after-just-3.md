# Microsoft Makes Copilot Optional in Windows 11 After Just 3.3% of Eligible Users Pay

The Windows 11 April 2026 update adds an official uninstall path for Copilot through Settings, plus a new Group Policy ("Remove Microsoft Copilot app") for enterprise administrators. Microsoft cited weak consumer monetization — only 3.3% of eligible users converted to paid Copilot — and persistent IT-admin pressure. The move is a notable concession that bundling AI alone has not driven willingness-to-pay among consumer users.
