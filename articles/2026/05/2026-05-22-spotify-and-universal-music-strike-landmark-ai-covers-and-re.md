# Spotify and Universal Music Strike Landmark AI Covers and Remixes Deal

Spotify and Universal Music Group have signed a first-of-its-kind agreement allowing fan-made AI covers and remixes of UMG catalog songs within sanctioned, royalty-bearing frameworks. The deal is being read as a template for how major rights holders will commercialize — rather than litigate — generative-AI use of their catalogs. Exa Labs · Parallel Web Systems · Tavily
