# Google's Threat Intelligence Group disclosed it successfully disrupted a planned mass exploitation attempt centered on an AI-assisted zero-day vulnerability…

Google's Threat Intelligence Group disclosed it successfully disrupted a planned mass exploitation attempt centered on an AI-assisted zero-day vulnerability targeting an unnamed open-source web-based system administration tool. The incident marks one of the first publicly confirmed cases of an AI-generated exploit being developed and operationalized for a mass attack — and equally, one of the first confirmed AI-assisted defensive interdictions at scale. The event is expected to accelerate regulatory pressure for mandatory AI exploit disclosure frameworks. This digest is prepared for executive situational awareness.
