# NewGemini 3.2 Flash — What We Know Before Google I/O 2026

Ahead of Google I/O, analysis of Gemini 3.2 Flash has surfaced indicating strong gains in price-performance efficiency. The Flash model family has become a benchmark in the market for fast, cost-effective inference—Replit CEO Amjad Masad publicly ranked Google's Flash models as the best for price-performance, calling them capable of beating open-source alternatives on speed and cost. Google I/O is expected to be the formal launch venue for the full 3.2 family.
