# Tilde Research introduces Aurora: leverage-aware optimizer fixing Muon neuron-death

Tilde Research released Aurora, a new neural network training optimizer targeting a structural flaw in the widely-used Muon optimizer that quietly kills off a significant fraction of MLP neurons during training. Aurora's leverage-aware design corrects this failure mode with no additional compute overhead, positioning it as a drop-in improvement for large-model pretraining. Peer evaluation is ongoing.
