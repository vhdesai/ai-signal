# EU-Anthropic Talks on Mythos Offensive-Security Model Stall — Spain Raises Alarm  Trending

Spanish economy minister Carlos Cuerpo said EU talks aimed at stress-testing European banks and critical infrastructure against Anthropic's Mythos AI model have made only limited progress. He indicated the issue would be raised again at the Nicosia meeting of EU finance ministers. The dispute represents one of the first concrete regulatory frictions around a restricted-preview offensive-security AI model and signals widening EU concern about asymmetric access to AI adversarial testing capabilities.
