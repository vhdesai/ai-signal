# 2026 Endpoint Ecosystem Study: Only 29% of Employees Report Meaningful Value from AI Tools

A global study of 2,500+ employees across the US, UK, Australia, and New Zealand found that only 29% report AI tools provide "regular or essential value" in their work, while 38% report little or no AI value. The gap between leadership and frontline workers is striking: 38% of frontline employees report not using AI at all, versus 11% of managers and executives. The study found employees receiving role-specific AI training were more than three times as likely to report meaningful value — surfacing a training gap as the primary bottleneck to enterprise AI ROI. Shadow AI risk is highest at the leadership level, with senior leaders more than twice as likely to use unapproved AI tools. ⚖️ 6 · AI Safety & Policy
