# Both Anthropic and OpenAI announced parallel enterprise joint ventures in early May

Both Anthropic and OpenAI announced parallel enterprise joint ventures in early May. Anthropic's venture — valued at $1.5B and backed by Blackstone, Hellman & Friedman, Goldman Sachs, Apollo, General Atlantic, and Sequoia — focuses on deploying enterprise AI services with each founding partner committing $300M. OpenAI's equivalent, "The Development Company," is raising $4B from 19 investors at a larger scale. The near-simultaneous announcements reflect a shared strategic bet: that the next phase of AI monetization requires financial-services distribution muscle, not just API sales, to reach the enterprise segment at the speed both companies need to justify their valuations (Anthropic disclosed $30B annualized run rate; OpenAI closed a $122B round at an $852B valuation).
