# Startup Adaption launched AutoScientist, a tool that automates the process of identifying and running experiments to…

Startup Adaption launched AutoScientist, a tool that automates the process of identifying and running experiments to improve AI models — essentially having AI iterate on its own training regimes. The system generates hypotheses about what is causing model weaknesses, designs targeted fine-tuning experiments, evaluates outcomes, and loops back, requiring minimal human intervention between cycles. Adaption positions AutoScientist as infrastructure for organizations that want continuous model improvement without the cost of a dedicated ML research team, targeting enterprise AI teams running custom deployments.
