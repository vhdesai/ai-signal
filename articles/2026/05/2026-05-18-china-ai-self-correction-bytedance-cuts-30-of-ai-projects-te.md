# China AI Self-Correction: ByteDance Cuts 30% of AI Projects, Tencent Pivots Strategy

A widely circulated internal update from ByteDance revealed the company has cut roughly 30% of its AI application projects and abandoned its prior "spray-and-pray" product strategy in favor of concentrated bets on a smaller number of high-conviction products. Tencent is simultaneously pivoting its AI organizational structure. Analysts describe China's AI application layer as entering a "structural reset" — a self-correction cycle driven by the recognition that broad product proliferation has not translated into user retention or monetization at scale. The retrenchment contrasts sharply with the continued model-layer investment from DeepSeek, Moonshot, and Alibaba's Qwen team.
