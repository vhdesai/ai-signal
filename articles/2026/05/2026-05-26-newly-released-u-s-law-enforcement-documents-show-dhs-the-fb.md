# Newly released U.S. law-enforcement documents show DHS, the FBI, and other federal agencies have formally introduced a…

Newly released U.S. law-enforcement documents show DHS, the FBI, and other federal agencies have formally introduced a domestic threat category called "anti-tech violent extremism," tied to backlash against the AI boom. The classification will affect how federal agencies prioritize investigations of attacks on data centers, AI labs, and tech executives—a category to monitor as Microsoft expands its U.S. AI infrastructure footprint.
