# OpenAI reasoning model disproves an 80-year-old Erdős conjecture

OpenAI's internal reasoning model produced a counterexample to Paul Erdős's 1946 conjecture on the unit-distance problem in combinatorial geometry — a result mathematicians had treated as settled for nearly eight decades. The finding continues to circulate this week as researchers validate the proof. It is the highest-profile AI-assisted mathematics result to date and a meaningful marker for autonomous scientific discovery.
