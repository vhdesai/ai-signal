# OpenAI Files Confidential IPO Targeting $1 Trillion Valuation

OpenAI filed a confidential IPO prospectus with Goldman Sachs and Morgan Stanley advising, targeting a listing as early as September 2026 at a valuation above $1 trillion. The company reports $25B in ARR and 900M weekly active users but is operating at a loss — a stark contrast to rival Anthropic, which posted its first operating profit the same week.
