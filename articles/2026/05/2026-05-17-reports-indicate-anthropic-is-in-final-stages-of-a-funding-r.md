# Reports indicate Anthropic is in final stages of a funding round seeking approximately $30 billion, which would value…

Reports indicate Anthropic is in final stages of a funding round seeking approximately $30 billion, which would value the company at ~$900 billion — nearly tripling its February 2026 valuation and approaching the trillion-dollar mark. This follows a remarkable first quarter: Anthropic disclosed Q1 2026 revenue grew 80x year-over-year with ARR above $44 billion, making it one of the fastest-growing companies in enterprise software history. Anthropic also formed a $200 million partnership with the Gates Foundation (May 14) and launched Claude for Small Business (May 13).
