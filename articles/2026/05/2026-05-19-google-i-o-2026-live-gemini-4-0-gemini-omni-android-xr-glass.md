# Google I/O 2026 Live: Gemini 4.0, Gemini Omni, Android XR Glasses and Aluminium OS Headline Keynote

Sundar Pichai and DeepMind's Demis Hassabis opened the I/O developer conference at 10am PT, with the centerpiece a full Gemini 4.0 launch and Gemini Omni — a unified pipeline generating text, images, and video in a single model. Google also unveiled Android XR smart glasses with hardware partners Samsung (“Jinju”), XREAL (Project Aura), Warby Parker, and Gentle Monster — directly countering Meta's Ray-Ban line. Agentic features including Gemini Spark (a persistent AI agent) and Aluminium OS — merging Android and ChromeOS — rounded out the keynote.
