# Google Search gets its biggest AI makeover in 25 years

Google used I/O to push AI deeper into its core search experience, introducing AI-powered suggestions and new information-agent workflows. Business Insider characterized the update as the search box’s biggest change in a quarter century, while DealBook noted that Google is embedding AI more deeply into products including its all-important search box. The strategic implication is clear: Google is moving search from a link-retrieval product toward an answer-and-action interface.
