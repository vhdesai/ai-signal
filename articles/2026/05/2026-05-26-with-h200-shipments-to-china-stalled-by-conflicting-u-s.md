# With H200 shipments to China stalled by conflicting U.S

With H200 shipments to China stalled by conflicting U.S. and Beijing rules, Huawei's Ascend 950PR has become the procurement target for Alibaba, ByteDance, and Tencent—with ByteDance alone committing $5.6B. Huawei expects 2026 AI-chip revenue near $12B and could capture roughly 60% of the Chinese AI accelerator market by year-end. Jensen Huang told CNBC the U.S. chipmaker had "conceded" the Chinese market.
