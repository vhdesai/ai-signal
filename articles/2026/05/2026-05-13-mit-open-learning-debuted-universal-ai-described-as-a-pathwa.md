# MIT Open Learning debuted Universal AI, described as "a pathway to AI fluency that's accessible and approachable to…

MIT Open Learning debuted Universal AI, described as "a pathway to AI fluency that's accessible and approachable to anyone, anywhere." The initiative launches with an AI-personalized introductory course available globally at no cost, adapting its pace and depth to each learner's background. MIT faculty Dimitris Bertsimas and Megan Mitchell framed it as part of the broader Universal Learning initiative aimed at reaching the 1 billion+ people globally without formal access to quality AI education. The program is MIT's most ambitious open-access AI education effort to date and arrives as demand for AI literacy is surging across the global workforce.
