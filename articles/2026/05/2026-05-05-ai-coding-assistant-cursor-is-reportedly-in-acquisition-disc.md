# AI coding assistant Cursor is reportedly in acquisition discussions with SpaceX at a staggering $60B valuation,…

AI coding assistant Cursor is reportedly in acquisition discussions with SpaceX at a staggering $60B valuation, reflecting the intense strategic premium being placed on AI developer tooling. Meanwhile, rival Replit CEO Amjad Masad disclosed at TechCrunch's StrictlyVC event that the company has gone from $2.8M in 2024 annual revenue to tracking toward a $1B annual run rate — with net revenue retention hitting as high as 300%. Both data points signal that AI-native developer tools are now among the fastest-growing software categories on record.
