# Anthropic Refuses China Access to Mythos; Pentagon Already Deploying It for Cyber Defense

In what Politico described as a "China-summit flashpoint," representatives from China reportedly approached Anthropic at a Singapore meeting to request access to its newest Mythos model family — and were refused. Simultaneously, Reuters confirmed the Pentagon has been deploying Anthropic's Mythos cybersecurity model to find and patch vulnerabilities across US government systems. Anthropic also published an essay arguing democracies must preserve "a commanding AI lead over China" through compute controls and anti-distillation measures. Frontier AI model access has formally become a diplomatic and national-security issue.
