# Stanford HAI Consolidates AI & Data Science Programs Under Single Roof

Stanford merged the Stanford Data Science initiative with the Stanford Institute for Human-Centered AI (HAI) under the HAI banner, creating an integrated hub that combines large-scale data science, technical AI advances, ethics, policy, law, medicine, and societal-impact research. The consolidation mirrors moves at Harvard and signals academia's shift toward treating AI governance and technical capability as inseparable research problems. The combined institute will share faculty, compute, and educational programs starting summer 2026. 🛡️ 6 · AI Safety & Policy
