# Trump and Xi Discuss AI Guardrails; Nvidia Chip Export Status Remains Unclear

President Trump confirmed he discussed potential bilateral AI safety guardrails directly with Chinese President Xi Jinping, as the administration weighs AI safety risks against export-control policy and strategic competition. The status of Nvidia H100/H200/Blackwell chip exports to China remains formally unresolved, creating significant uncertainty for both companies planning China AI infrastructure investment and for the broader AI supply chain. The conversation marks the first confirmed heads-of-state AI safety discussion between the U.S. and China.
