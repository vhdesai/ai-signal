# Stanford HAI's 2026 AI Index report delivers a clear headline: AI capability is not leveling off — it is accelerating…

Stanford HAI's 2026 AI Index report delivers a clear headline: AI capability is not leveling off — it is accelerating and reaching more people than ever. Industry produced over 90% of notable frontier models in 2025. AI systems now meet or exceed human baselines on PhD-level science questions, multimodal reasoning, and competition mathematics. Most strikingly, performance on SWE-bench Verified (software engineering) rose from 60% to near 100% in a single year. Organizational AI adoption reached 88%, and four in five universities are incorporating AI into curricula.
