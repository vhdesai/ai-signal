# Google folds Display Ads into AI-first Demand Gen platform

Google announced that it is consolidating Display Ads into its AI-first Demand Gen platform — accelerating the shift from manual targeting to agentic, AI-driven creative and delivery across Google surfaces. The move is significant for any enterprise relying on Google Ads inventory, and likely a meaningful tailwind for Google's ad-tech margin trajectory.
