# Anthropic in Talks to Use Microsoft's Maia AI Chips

Anthropic is reportedly negotiating to rent servers powered by Microsoft's in-house Maia AI chips as it scrambles for compute capacity to meet Claude's surging enterprise demand. Winning Anthropic would be a major validation for Microsoft's custom-silicon program, which faced delays last year, and accelerates the broader shift among hyperscalers to build Nvidia alternatives. Microsoft has pitched Maia 200 as cheaper than Nvidia for some inference workloads.
