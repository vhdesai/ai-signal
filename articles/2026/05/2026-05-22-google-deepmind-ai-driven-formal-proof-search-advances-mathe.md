# Google DeepMind: AI-Driven Formal Proof Search Advances Mathematics Research  Hot

A 20-author Google DeepMind preprint introduces a system advancing mathematics research through AI-driven formal proof search, extending the AlphaProof lineage. Co-authors include Pushmeet Kohli, Thomas Hubert, Aja Huang, and UT Austin's Swarat Chaudhuri — signaling continued investment in autoformalization and theorem-proving pipelines. The paper aligns with the broader "AI co-scientist" trend that dominated tech media coverage this week.
