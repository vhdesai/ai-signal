# MIT disclosed a 20% decline in incoming graduate students — a significant signal for the long-term talent pipeline…

MIT disclosed a 20% decline in incoming graduate students — a significant signal for the long-term talent pipeline underpinning AI research. The drop is attributed to a combination of visa policy changes, competition from industry AI labs offering immediate compensation far exceeding academic stipends, and shifting perceptions about the value of a PhD in an era where AI tools accelerate individual productivity. Industry analysts view this as a structural risk to the next generation of foundational AI research.
