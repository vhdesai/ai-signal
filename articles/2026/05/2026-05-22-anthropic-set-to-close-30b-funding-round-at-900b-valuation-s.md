# Anthropic Set to Close $30B+ Funding Round at $900B+ Valuation — Surpassing OpenAI

Anthropic is expected to close a funding round exceeding $30 billion at a valuation above $900 billion — a figure that would surpass OpenAI's last known valuation and make it the most highly valued private AI company in the world. The round draws from a broad investor base spanning major tech strategics and institutional funds. The deal is expected to finalize within weeks and reflects the continued expectation among investors that frontier AI labs will capture outsized value in the AI transition.
