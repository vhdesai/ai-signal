# Palantir Price Target Raised to $225 — Rosenblatt Names Ontology the "Durable AI Competitive Advantage" New

Rosenblatt analyst John McPeake raised Palantir's (PLTR) price target to $225 from $200 with a Buy rating, citing strong Q1 2026 earnings beats and characterizing the Palantir Ontology as a competitive advantage that is structurally difficult for competitors to replicate. The Ontology functions as a semantic layer translating AI model outputs into enterprise operations data — the analyst argues it makes Palantir the most defensible pure-play enterprise AI company. The 112× forward P/E leaves little room for error, but institutional conviction in the enterprise AI stack play is growing. 🎓 5 — Academic Research
