# HOTAI Researcher Inflow to US Down 89% Since 2017

A finding from the Stanford AI Index continuing to drive policy discussion: the flow of AI scholars into the United States has dropped 89% since 2017, with an 80% decline in the last year alone. Stanford frames this as a structural vulnerability that capital alone cannot offset — directly relevant to corporate development strategy and talent planning. 6. AI Safety & Policy
