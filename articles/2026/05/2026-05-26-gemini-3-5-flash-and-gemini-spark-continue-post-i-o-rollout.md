# Gemini 3.5 Flash and Gemini Spark Continue Post-I/O Rollout Across Search, Android, and Workspace

Gemini 3.5 Flash continues rolling out across Search, the Gemini app, and the API, with Google citing 4x the output speed of frontier competitors. Gemini Spark, a 24/7 personal agent, is reaching AI Ultra subscribers this week, while Samsung XR glasses are slated for a fall launch. Google's framing positions Gemini as an agentic layer cutting across Search, Chrome, Android, Workspace, YouTube, and shopping — the most distribution-rich AI deployment to date.
