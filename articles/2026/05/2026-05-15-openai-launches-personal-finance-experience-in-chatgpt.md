# OpenAI Launches Personal Finance Experience in ChatGPT

OpenAI released a preview of a personal finance dashboard in ChatGPT for Pro users in the U.S., enabling secure connection of more than 12,000 financial institutions via Plaid. Users can view spending, portfolio performance, subscriptions, and upcoming payments alongside conversational AI guidance. The feature leverages GPT-5.5's enhanced reasoning capabilities and will roll out to Plus and Free tiers following the preview phase.
