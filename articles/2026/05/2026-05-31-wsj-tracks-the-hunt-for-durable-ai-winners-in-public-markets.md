# WSJ tracks the hunt for durable AI winners in public markets

WSJ highlighted investor efforts to identify the next long-duration winner in the AI market, while also featuring AI’s use inside hedge-fund workflows. The common thread is that AI has become both an investable theme and an operating tool for investors themselves. For executives, this reinforces that AI valuation narratives increasingly reward defensibility, infrastructure leverage, and measurable workflow substitution — not just growth stories.
