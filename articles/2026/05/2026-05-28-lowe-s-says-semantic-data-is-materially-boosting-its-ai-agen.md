# Lowe's says "semantic" data is materially boosting its AI agents

Lowe's executives say investments in a semantic data layer — a management tool that standardizes business definitions across systems — are translating into better agent performance. The disclosure crystallizes the ongoing fight among Microsoft, Databricks, SAP, and other AI software vendors over control of the enterprise semantic layer, the connective tissue agents need to make trustworthy decisions on company data.
