# Anthropic is expected to close its $30B funding round at a pre-money valuation above $900B as soon as this week, co-led…

Anthropic is expected to close its $30B funding round at a pre-money valuation above $900B as soon as this week, co-led by Sequoia, Dragoneer, Altimeter, and Greenoaks (~$2B each), with Founders Fund and General Catalyst also participating. The mark would surpass OpenAI's $852B March valuation for the first time. Anthropic disclosed Q2 revenue of $10.9B and its first-ever quarterly operating profit ($559M)—reaching profitability roughly two years ahead of its own 2028 target.
