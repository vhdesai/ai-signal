# OpenAI Wins Elon Musk Lawsuit — IPO Path Cleared, but Musk Calls Verdict a "Dangerous Precedent"

A jury rejected Elon Musk's $150 billion lawsuit against OpenAI and CEO Sam Altman on May 18, finding that Musk waited too long to sue over claims that OpenAI's leaders improperly converted a charity he co-founded into a for-profit business. The judge accepted the verdict and dismissed all claims. In his first post-verdict interview with Forbes, Musk called the outcome "a dangerous precedent." The ruling clears a significant overhang for OpenAI's anticipated IPO, though analysts note the company still faces rising competition and dozens of other outstanding lawsuits.
