# OpenAI published research showing that one of its AI models autonomously disproved a central conjecture in discrete…

OpenAI published research showing that one of its AI models autonomously disproved a central conjecture in discrete geometry that had remained unsolved for approximately 80 years. OpenAI describes the result as validated mathematical research — not a near-miss — representing a meaningful advance in AI-assisted mathematics. This follows prior work including AI systems achieving gold medals at the International Mathematical Olympiad. The result signals that AI is beginning to contribute novel, peer-reviewed scientific knowledge, not merely assist human researchers with known techniques.
