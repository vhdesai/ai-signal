# Marines mandate servicewide AI training by year's end — Marine Corps Times, May 13, 2026 A Marine Administrative…

Marines mandate servicewide AI training by year's end — Marine Corps Times, May 13, 2026 A Marine Administrative Message requires every active-duty, reserve, officer, and enlisted Marine to complete a foundational generative AI course by Dec. 31, 2026. The 45-minute module covers Gemini, ChatGPT, and Grok, all accessible through the DoD's GenAI.mil platform — one of the largest single AI literacy mandates in any institution to date.
