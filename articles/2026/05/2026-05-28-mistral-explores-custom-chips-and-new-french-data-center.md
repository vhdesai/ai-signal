# Mistral explores custom chips and new French data center

Mistral is exploring custom chip designs and announced a new data center in France as part of a broader infrastructure buildout. The move signals that leading AI labs increasingly see compute strategy as a competitive moat. For Europe, Mistral's infrastructure push also supports sovereignty goals by reducing dependence on U.S. cloud and chip ecosystems.
