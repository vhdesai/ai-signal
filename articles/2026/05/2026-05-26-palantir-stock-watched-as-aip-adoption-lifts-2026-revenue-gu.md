# Palantir Stock Watched as AIP Adoption Lifts 2026 Revenue Guide to $7.65B

Palantir traded at $136 on May 26 as analyst attention focused on the company's Artificial Intelligence Platform (AIP) momentum. Strong adoption among U.S. commercial clients and defense agencies drove a raised full-year 2026 revenue guide of approximately $7.65 billion, with some analysts modeling triple-digit growth in U.S. commercial revenue. The stock remains down roughly 18% year-to-date despite the AI-driven re-rating thesis.
