# SoftBank insiders question Masayoshi Son's $60B OpenAI bet

SoftBank insiders are reportedly raising concerns about whether Son's $60B OpenAI commitment can be justified given rising debt loads, asset sales and growing enterprise pressure from Anthropic. The story lands days after OpenAI's confidential IPO filing targeting a $1T valuation, sharpening focus on whether public-market numbers will live up to private-market enthusiasm. V A
