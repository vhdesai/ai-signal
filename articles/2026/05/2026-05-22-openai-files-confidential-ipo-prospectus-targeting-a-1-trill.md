# OpenAI files confidential IPO prospectus targeting a $1 trillion valuation

OpenAI filed a confidential S-1 with Goldman Sachs and Morgan Stanley advising, targeting a listing as early as September 2026 at a valuation above $1 trillion. The company is reported to be running at roughly $25 billion in ARR with about 900 million weekly active users while still operating at a loss – an unusual public-markets profile that will force the first transparent disclosure of frontier-AI economics.
