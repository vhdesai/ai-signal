# OpenAI to Spend $50 Billion on Infrastructure in 2026, Brockman Tells U.S. Senate Breaking

OpenAI President Greg Brockman testified before the U.S. Senate that the company expects to spend approximately $50 billion on AI infrastructure in 2026 — up from $30 million in 2017, representing a 1,667× increase in nine years. That figure exceeds the entire 2025 venture financing for AI infrastructure combined. The testimony signals OpenAI's strategic intent to own the compute layer rather than rely solely on Microsoft's infrastructure, and arrives amid the company's ongoing legal crossroads and the newly expanded government safety testing mandate.
