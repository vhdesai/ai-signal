# xAI / SpaceX Secures $60B Option to Acquire Cursor, Explores Three-Way Alliance with Mistral

SpaceX — which absorbed xAI in a $1.25 trillion merger in February — has secured the option to acquire AI coding startup Cursor (Anysphere) for $60 billion later in 2026, or invest $10 billion into a joint development partnership. xAI simultaneously explored a three-way alliance with Paris-based Mistral AI, combining Mistral's efficient open-source model architecture, Cursor's developer workflow tools, and xAI's Colossus supercomputing cluster. Cursor is already training its Composer 2.5 model on tens of thousands of xAI GPUs. The play is a direct challenge to the Anthropic-AWS and OpenAI-Microsoft developer AI ecosystems, though xAI's president has acknowledged the company's GPU training efficiency sits at a "embarrassingly low" 11%, well below the industry norm of 35–45%. 🎓 5 · Academic Research
