# Anthropic eyes Microsoft Maia 200 as 5th silicon partner

Anthropic is in talks to adopt Microsoft's custom Maia 200 AI chip for Claude models, making Microsoft the fifth silicon partner alongside NVIDIA, AWS Trainium, Google TPUs, and SpaceX compute. Most labs lock into one chip vendor; Anthropic is treating compute optionality as a competitive moat.
