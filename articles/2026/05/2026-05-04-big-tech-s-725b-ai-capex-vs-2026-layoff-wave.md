# Big Tech's $725B AI capex vs. 2026 layoff wave

Google, Amazon, Microsoft, and Meta now collectively guide to roughly $725B of 2026 capex, up 77% year-over-year and front-loaded into data centers and accelerators. Q1 2026 tech layoffs hit 81,747 — already 45–55% of all of 2025's cuts — while ~275,000 AI roles sit open, exposing a sharp skills mismatch. The piece reframes the labor story as a deliberate capital-reallocation transaction rather than a cyclical correction.
