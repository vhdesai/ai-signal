# Gemini Spark Agent Spotted Ahead of Google I/O 2026

Screenshots leaked on X reveal Gemini Spark, a proactive background agent that works continuously without user prompts, pulling data from Connected Apps, location, login credentials, and Personal Intelligence. Unlike standard Gemini, Spark can execute tasks — including purchases and data sharing — without per-action confirmation in some cases. The experimental feature is expected to be previewed at Google I/O on May 19–20, 2026, alongside broader Gemini announcements.
