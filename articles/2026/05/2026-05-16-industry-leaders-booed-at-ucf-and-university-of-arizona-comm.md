# Industry leaders booed at UCF and University of Arizona commencements over AI remarks

Eric Schmidt was audibly booed during the AI-focused portion of his University of Arizona commencement address on May 16, while at UCF on May 8, Tavistock Development's Gloria Caulfield drew sustained jeers for framing AI as "the next industrial revolution." The two incidents — at very different institutions within nine days — are an early data point on graduate-cohort sentiment toward executive AI messaging, and a signal that recruiting narratives that play well in earnings calls may not land with the workforce employers are trying to attract. (User note: original source mentioned ASU; verified incident was at the University of Arizona.)
