# Google folds Display Ads into AI-first Demand Gen campaigns

Google is consolidating its standalone Display Ads product into its AI-driven Demand Gen campaign type, signaling a near-complete migration to generative ad creation and audience targeting. Advertisers will need to adopt the AI-first workflow as the legacy product winds down. Industry & Business
