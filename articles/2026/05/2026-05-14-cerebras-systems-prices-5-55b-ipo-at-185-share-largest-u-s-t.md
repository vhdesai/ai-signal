# Cerebras Systems Prices $5.55B IPO at $185/Share — Largest U.S. Tech IPO Since Arm

Cerebras priced its Nasdaq debut above the $150–$160 marketed range, raising $5.55B at a fully diluted valuation north of $56B. Institutional orders oversubscribed the book more than 20-fold. Disclosed contracted backlog of $24.6B includes a reported $20B OpenAI commitment and a cloud partnership with AWS — underscoring the strategic depth behind the listing.
