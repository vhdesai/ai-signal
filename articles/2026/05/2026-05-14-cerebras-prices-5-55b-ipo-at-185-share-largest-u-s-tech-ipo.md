# Cerebras Prices $5.55B IPO at $185/Share — Largest U.S. Tech IPO Since Arm

Cerebras priced its Nasdaq debut above the $150–$160 marketed range at $185, raising $5.55B at a fully diluted $56B valuation. Institutional orders oversubscribed the book more than 20-fold. Disclosed contracted backlog reached $24.6B, including a reported $20B OpenAI commitment and a new AWS cloud partnership. Foundation Capital is sitting on a 76x return, Benchmark 12x, and OpenAI itself holds a unique $6B post-IPO stake.
