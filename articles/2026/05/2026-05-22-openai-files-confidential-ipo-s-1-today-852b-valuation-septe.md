# OpenAI Files Confidential IPO S-1 Today — $852B Valuation, September Target

OpenAI is preparing to file its confidential IPO prospectus with the SEC today, working with Goldman Sachs and Morgan Stanley on a planned September listing at a ~$852B private market valuation — which at $1T+ at IPO would be the largest technology public offering in history. The confidential filing triggers a ~2-month SEC review before a public S-1, pointing to a late July/August public filing and September pricing. Anthropic has also signaled openness to a public listing by October 2026. Whoever prices first sets the valuation comparable for the other — a strategic consideration with significant M&A implications.
