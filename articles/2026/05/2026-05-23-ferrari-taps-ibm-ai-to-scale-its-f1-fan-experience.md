# Ferrari taps IBM AI to scale its F1 fan experience

Ferrari is deploying IBM watsonx-powered services to turn casual viewers into "superfans," with personalized race insights, telemetry-aware content recommendations, and multilingual fan engagement. The partnership marks one of IBM's most consumer-visible AI launches of 2026 and signals a re-emergence of legacy enterprise vendors in front-of-house AI deployments.
