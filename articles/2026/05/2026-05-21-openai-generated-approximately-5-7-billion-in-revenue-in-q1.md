# OpenAI generated approximately $5.7 billion in revenue in Q1 2026, nearly $1 billion more than Anthropic's $4.8 billion in the same period.

OpenAI generated approximately $5.7 billion in revenue in Q1 2026, nearly $1 billion more than Anthropic's $4.8 billion in the same period. OpenAI's coding agent Codex, enterprise sales growth, and ad testing on ChatGPT drove the gains. However, Anthropic's annualized revenue recently neared $45 billion, surpassing OpenAI's $25 billion run rate — with Anthropic projecting Q2 revenue to double to ~$11 billion and anticipating a $600M operating profit.
