# Cerebras IPO Reportedly 20x Oversubscribed; Range Lifted to $125–$135

Investor commentary reports Cerebras Systems' IPO — pricing May 14 — is 20x oversubscribed, prompting Morgan Stanley to require institutional limit orders and pushing the indicative share range from $115–$125 to $125–$135, implying an ~$28B valuation. OpenAI's $20B compute commitment anchors the deal, and OpenAI warrants for 33.5M shares would be worth ~$4.2B at the top of the new range. Single-source analyst commentary; not yet confirmed by primary disclosure. 📈
