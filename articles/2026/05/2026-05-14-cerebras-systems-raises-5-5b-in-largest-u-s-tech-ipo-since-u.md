# Cerebras Systems Raises $5.5B in Largest U.S. Tech IPO Since Uber — Stock Opens +108%

Cerebras Systems (NASDAQ: CBRS) priced at $185 per share, raising $5.55 billion — 60% above its original target. Shares opened at $385 (+108%) and closed day one at $311, giving the AI chip maker a market cap of approximately $95 billion. Cerebras' flagship Wafer Scale Engine 3 is built from an entire silicon wafer rather than smaller chips, delivering up to 15× higher inference throughput than comparable GPU systems. The company counts OpenAI, AWS, and the Mohamed bin Zayed University of AI among its customers, and posted $510M revenue and $238M net income in 2025. The IPO marks a landmark moment for AI-native hardware and validates inference-specialized silicon as an investable category.
