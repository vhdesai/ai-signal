# President Trump cancelled a planned AI executive order hours before a scheduled signing ceremony. The order would have created a voluntary framework for AI labs to share frontier models with the government up to 90 days before release for vulnerability scanning. Elon Musk, Mark Zuckerberg, and former White House AI czar David Sacks called Trump directly, arguing the review process could slow AI development and give China an advantage. OpenAI had supported the order. The cancellation deepens the US regulatory vacuum at a critical moment for frontier AI capabilities.
