# Nvidia CEO Jensen Huang Rebukes Leaders Who Predict AI Will Eliminate Most Jobs Trending

Nvidia CEO Jensen Huang publicly criticized tech industry leaders for what he called a "god complex" — specifically, prominent figures who make sweeping predictions that AI will imminently eliminate most human jobs. Huang argued that such claims do real, measurable harm by discouraging young people from pursuing AI-adjacent careers and creating unnecessary societal panic. The remarks are notable coming from the CEO of the company whose GPUs are most directly enabling AI advancement, and signal a growing divide within the industry over responsible public framing of AI's near-term workforce impact.
