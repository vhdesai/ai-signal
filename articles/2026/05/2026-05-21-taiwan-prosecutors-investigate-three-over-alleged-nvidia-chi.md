# Taiwan Prosecutors Investigate Three Over Alleged Nvidia Chip Smuggling to China

Taiwan's Keelung District Prosecutors Office is investigating three individuals accused of using forged documents to smuggle high-performance AI servers — containing advanced Nvidia chips and manufactured by Super Micro Computer — to mainland China in violation of US export controls. The case is the highest-profile enforcement action since the latest restrictions and signals tightening cross-strait scrutiny of AI semiconductor flows.
