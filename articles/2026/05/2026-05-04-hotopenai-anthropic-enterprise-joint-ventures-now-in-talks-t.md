# HotOpenAI & Anthropic Enterprise Joint Ventures Now in Talks to Acquire AI Services Firms

Both OpenAI and Anthropic's newly announced enterprise joint ventures are actively pursuing acquisitions of AI services and consulting firms, according to Reuters. OpenAI's vehicle—called "The Deployment Company," backed by TPG, Brookfield, Bain Capital, and Advent at a $10 billion valuation—is reportedly in advanced stages on three deals. Anthropic's $1.5 billion JV (with Blackstone, Hellman & Friedman, and Goldman Sachs) is pursuing a parallel strategy. Both ventures aim to incorporate hundreds of forward-deployed engineers to help mid-market enterprises integrate AI agents into core workflows—a Palantir-style engagement model adapted at scale. Goldman's global head of asset management described the effort as addressing "one of the most significant bottlenecks to enterprise AI adoption."
