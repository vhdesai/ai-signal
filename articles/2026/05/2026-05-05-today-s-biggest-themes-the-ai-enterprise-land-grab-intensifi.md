# Today's biggest themes: The AI enterprise land-grab intensified dramatically — both Anthropic and OpenAI simultaneously…

Today's biggest themes: The AI enterprise land-grab intensified dramatically — both Anthropic and OpenAI simultaneously unveiled forward-deployed enterprise joint ventures backed by Wall Street's biggest names, signaling a new "Palantir-ization" of AI services. On the hardware front, Cerebras filed IPO terms at a $26.6B valuation while China's AI stack accelerated its decoupling from Nvidia as DeepSeek V4 readies on Huawei silicon. Governance moved to center stage as the White House weighed a pre-release AI review executive order — a sharp pivot from earlier deregulatory posture. Meanwhile, venture funding hit $56B in April — 100% above prior year — and the Stanford AI Index confirmed the US–China frontier gap has collapsed to a near-statistical-tie.
