# Cerebras Systems IPO Soars 68% on Debut — Raises $5.5B in 2026's Biggest Public Offering

Cerebras Systems, the AI chip startup challenging Nvidia's GPU dominance with wafer-scale architecture, began trading on May 14 in the largest IPO of 2026, raising $5.5B and surging 68% on its first day. The company's chips target AI inference at speeds that outpace Nvidia's standard GPU configurations for specific workload profiles. The IPO values Cerebras as a credible long-term challenger in AI hardware — though Nvidia, which has surged more than 1,500% over five years, retains commanding market leadership. The debut signals investor appetite for alternative AI compute supply chains.
