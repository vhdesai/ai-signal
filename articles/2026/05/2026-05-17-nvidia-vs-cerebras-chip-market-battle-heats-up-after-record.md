# Nvidia vs. Cerebras: Chip Market Battle Heats Up After Record-Breaking IPO Trending

Cerebras Systems went public on May 14 in the year's largest IPO, with shares surging 68% on debut and the company raising over $5.5 billion at a multi-billion-dollar market cap. Cerebras's wafer-scale chip eliminates traditional inter-chip interconnects, giving it significant latency and throughput advantages on large inference workloads—though production volumes remain far smaller than Nvidia's H100/H200 ecosystem. The public listing sets up a new competitive narrative in AI silicon, even as Nvidia maintains commanding market share and its own stock has risen over 1,500% over five years. 5 · Academic Research
