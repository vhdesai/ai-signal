# President Trump confirmed discussions with Chinese President Xi Jinping on potential bilateral AI safety guardrails,…

President Trump confirmed discussions with Chinese President Xi Jinping on potential bilateral AI safety guardrails, even as U.S. officials continue to debate the scope of Nvidia chip export restrictions. The timing is notable: the conversations come ahead of Google I/O tomorrow, which is expected to advance U.S. AI leadership, and amid Anthropic's massive valuation jump. U.S. policymakers are weighing AI safety risks, China competition, and the economic cost of chip export controls on American semiconductor companies.
