# Meta released Forum on iOS today, a standalone app that surfaces Facebook Group conversations in a Reddit-style feed…

Meta released Forum on iOS today, a standalone app that surfaces Facebook Group conversations in a Reddit-style feed format and includes an AI-powered "Ask" feature for querying group knowledge. The launch reflects Meta's continued investment in integrating AI across its consumer platforms, positioning Facebook Groups as an AI-enhanced community layer rather than just a social network feature.
