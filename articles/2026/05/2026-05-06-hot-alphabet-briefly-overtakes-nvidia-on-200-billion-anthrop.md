# Hot Alphabet Briefly Overtakes Nvidia on $200 Billion Anthropic–Google Cloud Deal

Alphabet's market cap briefly surpassed Nvidia's in after-hours trading after reports that Anthropic committed $200 billion to Google Cloud over five years — a contract that would represent more than 40% of Google's projected contracted cloud revenue. The mega-deal follows Google's announced plan to invest up to $40 billion in Anthropic, cementing a Google-Anthropic axis that is reshaping the competitive landscape for cloud AI. For Microsoft and Azure, the implication is clear: Google Cloud is becoming the infrastructure layer of choice for the AI labs most likely to define the next generation of enterprise AI products.
