# The Wall Street Journal profiled Anthropic CFO Krishna Rao, noting his deliberately conservative approach to revenue…

The Wall Street Journal profiled Anthropic CFO Krishna Rao, noting his deliberately conservative approach to revenue projections and his track record of raising less capital than was on offer — unusual for a company approaching a reported $900B+ valuation round. Rao's philosophy reflects a broader tension at Anthropic: the company is simultaneously the most safety-constrained frontier lab and one of the most capitalized, with April's $15B round the largest single VC investment in history. His cautious posture on revenue sets an interesting contrast with OpenAI's CFO Sarah Friar, who publicly flagged revenue miss concerns weeks ago.
