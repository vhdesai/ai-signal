# Cerebras Systems hit the Nasdaq on May 14 in the most closely watched tech IPO of 2026, raising $4.8 billion at an IPO…

Cerebras Systems hit the Nasdaq on May 14 in the most closely watched tech IPO of 2026, raising $4.8 billion at an IPO price range of $150–$160/share (increased from its original $115–$125 band). The stock surged 108% on its debut day, reflecting strong investor appetite for AI chip infrastructure plays. Cerebras makes the Wafer-Scale Engine — a single chip designed to handle LLM compute more efficiently than clusters of traditional GPUs, positioning it as a serious NVIDIA challenger for inference workloads.
