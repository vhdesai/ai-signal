# Connecticut becomes the latest U.S. state to enact an AI accountability law

Connecticut enacted an AI accountability statute requiring impact assessments for "high-risk" deployments in employment, credit, and government services, with disclosure obligations layered on top. The law adds further fragmentation to the U.S. compliance map and increases pressure for a federal framework before the 2027 enforcement cycle.
