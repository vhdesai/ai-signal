# Big Tech's $725B AI Capex Wave: Google, Amazon, Microsoft & Meta in 2026

Google, Amazon, Microsoft, and Meta are collectively projecting $725 billion in AI capital expenditure for 2026 — up 77% year over year. The spending surge coincides with the largest wave of tech layoffs in a decade; trackers logged over 150,000 tech jobs eliminated by mid-April 2026. The Microsoft Global AI Diffusion Report (May 10) found 17.8% of the world's working-age population now actively uses AI, with the UAE leading at 70.1% and U.S. software developer employment at a record high despite the headline layoff numbers.
