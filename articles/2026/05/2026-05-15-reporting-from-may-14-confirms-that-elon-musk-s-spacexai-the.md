The departures span research and engineering functions, raising questions about organizational cohesion post-merger. This comes as the Musk vs. Altman OpenAI trial continues in Oakland and xAI faces environmental pressure over ~50 gas turbines running unchecked at its Memphis, Tennessee data center.
