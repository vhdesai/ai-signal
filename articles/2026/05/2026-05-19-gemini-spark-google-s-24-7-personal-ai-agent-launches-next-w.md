# Gemini Spark: Google's 24/7 Personal AI Agent Launches Next Week for Ultra Subscribers

Gemini Spark is the most ambitious agentic product announced by any lab in 2026 — a 24/7 personal AI agent running on Google Cloud VMs even when devices are closed. It autonomously drafts emails, tracks RSVPs, creates Sheets trackers, monitors Gmail, and queues every action for user approval before executing via Android Halo notifications. It launches next week for US Google AI Ultra subscribers (now $100/mo, down from $250). MCP support for Canva, Instacart, and OpenTable follows this summer. Observers describe it as the most concrete response yet to OpenAI's Operator.
