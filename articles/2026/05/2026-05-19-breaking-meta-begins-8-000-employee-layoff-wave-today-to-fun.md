# Breaking Meta Begins 8,000-Employee Layoff Wave Today to Fund $145B AI Infrastructure Bet

Meta begins cutting approximately 8,000 employees this week — roughly 10% of its global workforce — while simultaneously canceling 6,000 open requisitions, pulling ~14,000 headcount slots off the board. Cuts span Reality Labs, the Facebook social org, recruiting, sales, and global operations. The wave arrives in the same quarter Meta posted $56.3B in revenue and $26.8B net income, while the company raises its 2026 AI capex guidance to $125–$145 billion. Zuckerberg is rerouting every freed dollar into AI compute and custom data centers. Additional rounds are expected in August and later this year. 腾
