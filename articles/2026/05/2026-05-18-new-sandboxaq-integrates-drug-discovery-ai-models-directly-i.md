# New SandboxAQ Integrates Drug-Discovery AI Models Directly into Anthropic's Claude

Alphabet spinout SandboxAQ — backed by Eric Schmidt — is embedding its scientific AI models for drug discovery and materials science directly into Claude, arguing that the bottleneck for non-specialist scientists is the conversational interface rather than raw model capability. The partnership puts SandboxAQ in direct competition with Chai Discovery and Isomorphic Labs (which raised $2.1B the prior week). The integration requires no scientific-computing expertise from the end user — a deliberate positioning choice aimed at broadening Claude's scientific user base.
