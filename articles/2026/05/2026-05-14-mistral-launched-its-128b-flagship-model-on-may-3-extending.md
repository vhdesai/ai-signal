# Mistral launched its 128B flagship model on May 3, extending its open-weight strategy to a frontier-class parameter…

Mistral launched its 128B flagship model on May 3, extending its open-weight strategy to a frontier-class parameter count. Separately, Anthropic launched ten production financial-services AI agents in partnership with JPMorgan Chase during its "Code with Claude" conference (May 2), covering workflows in risk, compliance, and client analytics. The JPMorgan deployment is the largest disclosed production rollout of Claude agents in a regulated financial institution to date.
