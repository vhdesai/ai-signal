# Anthropic in Advanced Talks to Acquire Stainless for $300M+

Anthropic is in advanced talks to acquire developer-tools startup Stainless for at least $300 million. Stainless sells software used by OpenAI, Google, and Anthropic themselves to expose AI models via fast, well-typed APIs — software whose demand has spiked alongside agentic tools like Claude Code and OpenClaw. Owning Stainless would give Anthropic control over a key piece of infrastructure used by its direct competitors.
