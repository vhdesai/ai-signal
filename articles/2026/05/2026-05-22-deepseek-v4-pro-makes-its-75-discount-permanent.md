# DeepSeek V4-Pro makes its 75% discount permanent

DeepSeek confirmed on May 22 that V4-Pro's promotional 75% discount is now its standing list price — $0.435 per million input tokens and $0.87 per million output, optimized to run on domestic Huawei Ascend 950 chips. The structural undercut against GPT-5.5 (priced up to $180 per million output) intensifies the inference-cost arbitrage conversation enterprises had largely deferred.
