# Breaking Andrej Karpathy Joins Anthropic's Pre-Training Team

OpenAI co-founder and AI luminary Andrej Karpathy has announced he is joining Anthropic's pre-training team. Karpathy previously co-founded OpenAI, led Tesla's Autopilot program, and is widely credited with coining the term "vibe coding." His move to Anthropic is seen as one of the most significant talent acquisitions in the current frontier AI research competition, and underscores Anthropic's growing momentum following its record Q1 revenue disclosures.
