# U.S.-China to Establish Bilateral AI Safety Protocol, Bessent Announces at Trump-Xi Summit

Speaking from the sidelines of the Trump-Xi summit in Beijing, Treasury Secretary Scott Bessent told CNBC the U.S. and China will "set up a protocol in terms of how do we go forward with best practices for AI to make sure non-state actors don't get a hold of these models." He framed the dialogue as possible because the U.S. is "in the lead" — positioning strength as the condition for engagement. If formalized, it would be the first government-to-government AI safety mechanism for frontier AI.
