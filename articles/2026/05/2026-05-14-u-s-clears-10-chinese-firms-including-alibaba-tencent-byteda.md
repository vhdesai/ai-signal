# U.S. Clears ~10 Chinese Firms — Including Alibaba, Tencent, ByteDance, JD — to Buy Nvidia H200

The U.S. Commerce Department approved approximately ten Chinese firms to purchase Nvidia's H200 chips. Three Reuters sources confirm not a single delivery has been made, leaving the deals in limbo as Jensen Huang travels with President Trump to unblock paused orders. The clearances mark a significant pivot in U.S. AI export-controls posture.
