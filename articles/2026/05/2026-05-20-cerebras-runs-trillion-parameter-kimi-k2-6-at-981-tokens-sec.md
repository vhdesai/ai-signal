# Cerebras Runs Trillion-Parameter Kimi K2.6 at 981 Tokens/Second — 6.7× Faster Than GPU Clouds

Fresh off the largest tech IPO of 2026 — raising $5.55 billion at a $95 billion market cap — Cerebras Systems announced it is now serving Moonshot AI's Kimi K2.6, a trillion-parameter open-weight model, for enterprise customers at 981 output tokens per second. Independently verified by Artificial Analysis, the speed is 6.7× faster than the next-best GPU-based provider and 23× faster than the median. For a standard agentic coding request, Cerebras completed the task in 5.6 seconds versus 163.7 seconds on the official Kimi endpoint. The announcement dispels lingering questions about whether Cerebras' wafer-scale chips can handle frontier-scale models, making Kimi K2.6 a viable open-weight alternative to Anthropic and OpenAI APIs — particularly for cost-sensitive or capacity-constrained enterprise buyers.
