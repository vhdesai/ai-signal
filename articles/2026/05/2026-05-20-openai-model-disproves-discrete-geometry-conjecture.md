# OpenAI Model Disproves Discrete-Geometry Conjecture

OpenAI reported that one of its frontier models produced a verified disproof of a long-standing conjecture in discrete geometry — a result independently checked by external mathematicians. It marks one of the first cases where an LLM-generated proof has cleared peer-level verification on an open problem, and is being read as a meaningful step beyond formula manipulation into genuine mathematical discovery.
