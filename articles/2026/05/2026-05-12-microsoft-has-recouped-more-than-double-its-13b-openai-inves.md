# Microsoft Has Recouped More Than Double Its $13B OpenAI Investment

data shows Microsoft has earned more than $30B in revenue from OpenAI-tied services, more than doubling its $13B investment in the startup. OpenAI's $23B in Azure server rentals materially powered the run-rate, even as direct OpenAI access has outpaced Azure resale for many enterprise buyers. Microsoft has since ended its exclusive cloud-reseller arrangement in exchange for other concessions, marking a structural reshaping of one of the defining partnerships of the AI era.
