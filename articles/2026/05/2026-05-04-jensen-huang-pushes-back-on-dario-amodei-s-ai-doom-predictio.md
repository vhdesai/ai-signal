# Jensen Huang pushes back on Dario Amodei's AI doom predictions

Nvidia CEO Jensen Huang publicly criticized industry leaders — singling out Anthropic's Dario Amodei and Elon Musk — for what he called insufficiently “mindful” rhetoric around AI's impact on jobs and humanity. Huang's comments mark one of the sharpest public splits to date among frontier AI CEOs over how to communicate risk. The remarks land as Nvidia continues its earnings-driven dominance of AI infrastructure.
