# General Compute raises $15M seed for inference-specialized neocloud

General Compute closed a $15M seed at a $60M post-money valuation led by FUSE VC to build an "inference neocloud" — renting compute optimized for serving (not training) AI models, with a chip strategy that explicitly leans away from H100/H200 GPUs. The pitch: inference is becoming the bigger long-run market and GPUs are the wrong primitive for it, echoing the original Cerebras and Groq theses but at the cloud layer.
