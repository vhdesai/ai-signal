# DeepSeek Closing $45–50B First External Funding Round

DeepSeek is closing in on its first-ever external funding round at a $45–50B valuation — more than double the $20B figure cited two weeks ago. China's IC Industry Investment Fund ("Big Fund III") is leading; Tencent is in late-stage talks. The round targets roughly $4B in primary capital and would place state capital, Tencent, and a sovereign AI lab running on Huawei Ascend silicon onto the same cap table for the first time. Note: Alibaba's involvement remains disputed (see below). ⚡
