# BreakingAnthropic ARR Confirmed Above $44 Billion After 80x Q1 2026 Growth

Anthropic disclosed on May 7 that its Q1 2026 revenue grew 80x year-over-year, pushing its annualized run rate above $44 billion. The revelation followed a sequence of major deals in early May: a $200 billion Google Cloud contract, a SpaceX Colossus 1 compute deal giving Claude access to 220,000+ Nvidia GPUs (300MW of capacity), ten financial-services agent deployments with JPMorgan, and the opening of the Claude Agent SDK to all external developers. Anthropic is simultaneously in the final stages of a $50 billion fundraise at a valuation approaching $900 billion. The Pentagon AI deal exclusion remains a headwind: the company refused to lift usage restrictions for autonomous weapons targeting, and litigation is ongoing.
