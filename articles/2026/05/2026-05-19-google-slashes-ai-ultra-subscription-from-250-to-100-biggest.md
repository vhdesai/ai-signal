# Google Slashes AI Ultra Subscription from $250 to $100 — Biggest AI Pricing Move of 2026

Google cut its top AI tier from $250 to $100/month, with the new plan bundling 5× higher Gemini usage limits, 20 TB of cloud storage, YouTube Premium, and beta access to Gemini Spark. Simultaneously, Google eliminated daily prompt caps across all tiers in favor of a compute-consumption model that refreshes every five hours. The move puts direct pricing pressure on OpenAI's $200/month ChatGPT Pro and Anthropic's Claude AI subscription — particularly given the addition of 24/7 Gemini Spark agent access and full Omni video generation at the new price point.
