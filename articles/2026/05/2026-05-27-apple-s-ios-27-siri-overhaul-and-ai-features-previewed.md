# Apple's iOS 27 Siri overhaul and AI features previewed

Bloomberg reported new internal images of Apple's redesigned iOS 27 Siri experience, with deeper on-device LLM grounding, an updated visual identity, and proactive task-completion behavior. The preview lands ahead of WWDC and is Apple's most aggressive consumer-AI signal since the Apple Intelligence relaunch. Senior execs are framing the rebuild as Apple's "second chance at the assistant category."
