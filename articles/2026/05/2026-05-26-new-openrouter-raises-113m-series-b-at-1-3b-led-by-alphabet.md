# New OpenRouter raises $113M Series B at $1.3B, led by Alphabet's CapitalG

AI gateway startup OpenRouter announced a $113M Series B led by CapitalG at a ~$1.3B post-money valuation — more than double its $547M valuation 11 months earlier. OpenRouter routes requests across 400+ models including Anthropic, Google, OpenAI, xAI and DeepSeek, and reports 5x usage growth over six months as the industry shifts toward agents and multi-model inference. The round signals strong investor conviction in multi-model orchestration as a durable AI infrastructure layer.
