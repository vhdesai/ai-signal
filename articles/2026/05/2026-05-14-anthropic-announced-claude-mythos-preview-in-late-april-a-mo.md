# Anthropic announced Claude Mythos Preview in late April — a model that excels at identifying software vulnerabilities…

Anthropic announced Claude Mythos Preview in late April — a model that excels at identifying software vulnerabilities and security flaws — with access intentionally restricted to selected companies, cleared organizations, and government agencies under a new cybersecurity initiative called Project Glasswing. The restricted rollout drew government scrutiny; Anthropic CEO Dario Amodei met with senior Trump administration officials at the White House, even as the Defense Department had declared Anthropic a "supply chain risk." UK's AISI subsequently reported that GPT-5.5 matches Mythos on cyber benchmarks — raising the stakes for the broader question of how cybersecurity-capable AI models should be governed. Anthropic is currently investigating unauthorized access to the Mythos preview (April 21).
