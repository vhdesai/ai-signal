# Microsoft rolled out its May 2026 Copilot update for Microsoft 365, introducing GPT-5.5 models across the productivity…

Microsoft rolled out its May 2026 Copilot update for Microsoft 365, introducing GPT-5.5 models across the productivity suite — improving reasoning quality, response speed, and context handling for tasks including email drafting, meeting summaries, and document creation. The update also upgrades the Researcher feature for deeper document analysis, adds new Copilot Notebooks capabilities for long-form knowledge management, and restores the app launcher "Waffle" for faster navigation across Microsoft 365 apps.
