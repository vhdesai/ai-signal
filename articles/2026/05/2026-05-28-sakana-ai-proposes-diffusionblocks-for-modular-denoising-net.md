# Sakana AI proposes DiffusionBlocks for modular denoising networks

Sakana AI proposed DiffusionBlocks, a block-wise training framework that converts residual networks into independently trainable denoising modules. The work points to more modular and potentially more efficient training patterns for diffusion-style architectures. If validated broadly, this kind of block-wise approach could make experimentation and scaling easier for image, video, and multimodal generation systems.
