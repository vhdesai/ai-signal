# The University of Washington's Math AI Laboratory announced five accepted papers at ICLR 2026 spanning topics in…

The University of Washington's Math AI Laboratory announced five accepted papers at ICLR 2026 spanning topics in theoretical machine learning and applied AI systems. Additionally, one of the lab's works earned a Spotlight designation at ICML 2026 — a recognition placing it in the top 2.2% of submissions. The papers reflect UW's growing strength at the intersection of mathematical theory and practical ML advances. Full paper titles and abstracts are available via the lab's announcement page.
