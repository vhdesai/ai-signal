# OpenAI Opens Singapore Applied AI Lab Alongside IMDA Agentic AI Framework Update

OpenAI launched its first Applied AI Lab in Singapore, co-timed with the Singapore IMDA's updated agentic AI governance framework. The lab will focus on enterprise AI deployment and localized AI safety research, and will collaborate with IMDA on governance standards for the Southeast Asian market. Singapore's IMDA framework update specifically addresses multi-agent system transparency, accountability chains for autonomous decisions, and incident reporting requirements — among the most detailed agentic AI governance documents published to date by any national authority.
