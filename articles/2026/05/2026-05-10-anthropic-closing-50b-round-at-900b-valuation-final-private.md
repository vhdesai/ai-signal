# Anthropic Closing ~$50B Round at $900B+ Valuation — Final Private Round Before IPO

Anthropic reportedly gave investors a 48-hour allocation window for a $50B raise at a valuation north of $900 billion — up from $380B just 11 weeks prior and 15x its $61.5B valuation in March 2025. The board is expected to decide in May, with this described as Anthropic's likely final private round before going public. If completed at these terms, Anthropic would surpass OpenAI's $852B March valuation as the most valuable private AI company in the world. (Sources: Forbes, TechCrunch)
