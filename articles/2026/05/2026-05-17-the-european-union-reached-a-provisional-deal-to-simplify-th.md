# The European Union reached a provisional deal to simplify the AI Act's compliance framework, delaying certain high-risk…

The European Union reached a provisional deal to simplify the AI Act's compliance framework, delaying certain high-risk AI obligations while explicitly banning non-consensual AI-generated explicit content (so-called "nudification" apps). The deal reflects industry pressure on Brussels to ease near-term compliance burdens. For enterprise teams, the most significant implication is a delayed enforcement calendar for high-risk AI applications — though the core legal framework remains unchanged.
