# OpenAI & Anthropic Launch Rival Enterprise Services Joint Ventures

Both AI giants announced separately backed enterprise deployment ventures within hours of each other. Anthropic's venture (unnamed, $1.5B) is backed by Goldman Sachs, Blackstone, Hellman & Friedman, Apollo, and General Atlantic — embedding engineers directly inside businesses to deploy Claude. OpenAI's "The Deployment Company" ($4B from 19 investors including TPG, Bain Capital, Brookfield) is in advanced stages on three acquisition targets for AI services firms. The Palantir-style "forward-deployed engineer" model is now standard across frontier AI labs. (Sources: TechCrunch, CNBC, Reuters)
