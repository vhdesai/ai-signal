# TRENDINGHangzhou court rules it illegal to fire a worker solely because AI can do the job

A Hangzhou court issued what is being described as the first major Chinese ruling holding that AI displacement alone is not lawful grounds for termination. The decision is likely to influence how Chinese employers structure AI-driven workforce transitions and will be closely read by HR and legal teams globally.
