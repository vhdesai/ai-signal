# TrendingMicrosoft 365 E7 "Frontier Suite" Goes Generally Available at $99/User/Month

Microsoft's new Microsoft 365 E7 tier — the company's first new enterprise license tier since E5 launched in 2015 — became generally available, bundling E5, Copilot, Microsoft Entra Suite, and the new Agent 365 control plane for governing AI agents across enterprise environments. Priced at $99/user/month, E7 introduces the concept of AI agents as licensable, governed identities alongside human users. The release reframes how enterprises will budget for AI: not just software seats but agent seats with identity, security, and audit trails.
