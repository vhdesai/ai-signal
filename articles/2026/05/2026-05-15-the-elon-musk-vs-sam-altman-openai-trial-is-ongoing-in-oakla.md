# The Elon Musk vs. Sam Altman / OpenAI trial is ongoing in Oakland, with two claims now remaining: breach of charitable…

The Elon Musk vs. Sam Altman / OpenAI trial is ongoing in Oakland, with two claims now remaining: breach of charitable trust and unjust enrichment. May 14 reporting details what the jury will actually decide — focusing narrowly on whether Musk's early contributions to OpenAI created legally enforceable charitable obligations. Testimony revealed Musk had at one point considered transferring OpenAI assets to his children. The case has broad implications for AI governance, nonprofit-to-for-profit conversions, and the enforceability of founding commitments in AI ventures.
