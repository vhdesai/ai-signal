# NVIDIA AI Releases Gated DeltaNet-2 for efficient long-context attention

Nvidia Research published Gated DeltaNet-2, a linear-attention layer that decouples the "erase" and "write" operations inside the delta rule. The design targets long-context throughput at sub-softmax cost — relevant for both training efficiency and serving long-context agents at scale. Research Breakthroughs HOT RESEARCH
