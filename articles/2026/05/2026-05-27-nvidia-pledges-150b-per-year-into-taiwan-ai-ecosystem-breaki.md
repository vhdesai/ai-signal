# Nvidia Pledges $150B per Year into Taiwan AI Ecosystem Breaking

Jensen Huang announced Nvidia will invest $150B annually to make Taiwan the "epicenter" of the AI revolution — a striking endorsement of Taiwan's irreplaceability in chip packaging, systems integration, and supply chain depth, and a counter-signal to the Trump administration's US-onshoring push. Nvidia separately disclosed its new "Vera" AI CPU represents a "brand new $200B market," with $20B in initial orders already booked.
