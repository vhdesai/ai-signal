# OpenAI strikes journalism partnership with Folha and UOL in Brazil

OpenAI's first media partnership in Brazil surfaces attributed Folha/UOL summaries inside ChatGPT and provides the two publishers with Codex, ChatGPT Enterprise, and API access. Brazil is one of ChatGPT's largest markets — 50M+ MAU and roughly 140M messages per day. The deal slots neatly into OpenAI's broader news-licensing pattern.
