# Anthropic overtakes OpenAI in U.S. business AI adoption — VentureBeat, May 13, 2026 The May 2026 Ramp AI Index reports…

Anthropic overtakes OpenAI in U.S. business AI adoption — VentureBeat, May 13, 2026 The May 2026 Ramp AI Index reports paid Anthropic adoption rose to 34.4% of U.S. businesses in April (up 3.8 pts), while OpenAI fell 2.9 pts to 32.3% — the first market-share crossover since the AI race began. Anthropic's share grew from under 1% in mid-2023 and now wins ~70% of head-to-head purchase decisions among first-time buyers. VentureBeat flags compute constraints and token-based pricing economics as structural risks.
