# Mistral AI Acquires Vienna's Emmi AI to Target Industrial Physics Simulations

Mistral AI has acquired Vienna-based startup Emmi AI for an undisclosed sum, marking the French lab's second acquisition of 2026 (following the purchase of cloud-infrastructure firm Koyeb in February). Emmi specializes in AI models that simulate physical phenomena — airflow, heat transfer, and material stress — with aerospace, automotive, and semiconductor manufacturers as the primary target customers. Emmi ran what local press described as Austria's largest 2025 funding round at €15M. The deal expands Mistral's industrial client thesis and adds hard-science simulation capabilities beyond its existing large language model and coding portfolio.
