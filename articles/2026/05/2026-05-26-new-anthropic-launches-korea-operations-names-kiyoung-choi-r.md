# New Anthropic launches Korea operations, names KiYoung Choi Representative Director

Anthropic formally launched its Korea operations and named KiYoung Choi — previously Korea GM at Snowflake — as Representative Director ahead of a Seoul office opening planned in the coming weeks. The company cited Claude usage in Korea running at roughly 3.5x the country's population-adjusted expectation, and named SK Telecom and Law&Company as flagship Korean Claude customers. The move pushes Anthropic into a market where OpenAI and Google are already actively localized.
