# Anthropic Hits First Profit: $559M on $10.9B Q2 Revenue

Anthropic projected its first-ever operating profit of $559M in Q2 2026 on $10.9B in revenue — a 130% jump from Q1. Claude Code enterprise deployments now generate $2.5B in annualized revenue, and the company is closing a $30B funding round at a ~$900B valuation, nearly tripling its February number. The milestone arrived two years ahead of Anthropic's own profitability target.
