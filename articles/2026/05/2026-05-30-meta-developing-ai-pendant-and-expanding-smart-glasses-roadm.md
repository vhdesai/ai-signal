# Meta Developing AI Pendant and Expanding Smart Glasses Roadmap

Leaked roadmap documents indicate Meta is developing an AI-powered pendant capable of transcribing and contextualizing conversations, alongside four new smart glasses models planned for 2026. The pendant would represent Meta's first standalone wearable AI device outside the glasses form factor, targeting ambient capture and recall—a direct response to Humane and emerging competition from Apple's on-device AI strategy.
