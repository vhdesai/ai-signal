# 🟢 NEW Google DeepMind Unveils Gemini-Powered AI Mouse Cursor

Google DeepMind demonstrated an experimental AI-powered mouse cursor that understands what users point at on screen — text, images, or video — responding to voice commands without requiring typed prompts. The project, framed as "Reimagining the mouse pointer for the AI era," represents a fundamental rethink of human-computer interaction by replacing long text prompts with natural gestures and speech. The system is experimental and live in demos, though early feedback has raised questions about privacy implications of always-on screen awareness. SECTION 03 🛠 Products & Tools
