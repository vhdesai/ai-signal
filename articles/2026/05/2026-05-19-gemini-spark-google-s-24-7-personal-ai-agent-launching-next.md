# Gemini Spark: Google's 24/7 personal AI agent, launching next week

Google's most ambitious I/O announcement is Gemini Spark — a persistent background agent that runs on Google Cloud VMs even when a user's device is off. Spark works autonomously across Gmail, Docs, Sheets, Slides, third-party apps, and the web, planning tasks (including events) while the user is offline. It rolls out next week for Ultra subscribers and is the most aggressive push yet from a hyperscaler into the "always-on agent" category.
