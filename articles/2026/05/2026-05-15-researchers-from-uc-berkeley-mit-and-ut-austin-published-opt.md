# Researchers from UC Berkeley, MIT, and UT Austin published "optimize_anything" (optany), a single LLM-based…

Researchers from UC Berkeley, MIT, and UT Austin published "optimize_anything" (optany), a single LLM-based optimization system that achieves state-of-the-art results across six diverse tasks simultaneously — nearly tripling Gemini Flash's ARC-AGI accuracy, cutting cloud scheduling costs 40%, and matching AlphaEvolve on mathematical packing problems. The results directly challenge the assumption that domain-specific optimization tools are necessary, with significant implications for the economics of enterprise AI customization and fine-tuning investments.
