# New arXiv May 2026: 1,200+ AI Papers — Agentic Reputation Systems, Jailbreak Causality & the Tool-Use Tax

The May 2026 AI arXiv archive has surpassed 1,200 submissions, with several papers generating immediate attention: Minimal, Local, Causal Explanations for Jailbreak Success in LLMs offers a structural causal framework for understanding why AI safety filters fail at the architectural level — directly relevant to enterprise risk management. Are Tools All We Need? quantifies the performance and token-overhead cost of enabling tool use in LLM agents (the "tool-use tax"), with implications for agentic AI cost modeling. AgentReputation, accepted at FSE 2026, proposes a decentralized trust framework for multi-agent AI systems. ICML 2026 accepted papers also began appearing, including advances in direct preference optimization.
