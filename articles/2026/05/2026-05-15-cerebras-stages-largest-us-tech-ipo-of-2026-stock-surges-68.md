# Cerebras Stages Largest US Tech IPO of 2026, Stock Surges 68% on Debut

AI inference chipmaker Cerebras Systems opened at $350 a share on Nasdaq Thursday and closed at $311.07 — a 68% pop — valuing the company at $56.4 billion. The offering was 20× oversubscribed and was upsized twice from an initial $115–$125 range to a final $185 IPO price, raising $5.5 billion. Investors framed Cerebras as the only US-headquartered, scaled independent alternative to Nvidia — signaling that purpose-built AI silicon is now a standalone investable category.
