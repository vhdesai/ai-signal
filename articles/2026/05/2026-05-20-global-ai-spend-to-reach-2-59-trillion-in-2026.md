# Global AI spend to reach $2.59 trillion in 2026

CIO Dive reports global AI spending is on track for $2.59 trillion in 2026, with infrastructure, agent platforms, and enterprise software absorbing the largest share. The data lands alongside CIO Dive's reporting on Liberty Mutual and Travelers IT overhauls — both insurers cite agentic AI as a primary driver of multi-year platform refreshes.
