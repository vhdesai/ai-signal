# Andrej Karpathy Joins Anthropic's Claude Pretraining Team

Andrej Karpathy — co-founder of OpenAI and former Tesla AI lead — joined Anthropic's pretraining team to work directly on Claude and stand up a new group focused on AI-assisted model research. The hire deepens Anthropic's bench just as the company prints its first operating profit and signals an intent to use its own models to design its next ones.
