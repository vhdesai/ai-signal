# Google Launches Gemini Spark — The Most Concrete 24/7 AI Agent Any Lab Has Shipped

Gemini Spark is Google's answer to OpenAI Operator: a personal AI agent running 24/7 on Google Cloud VMs, even when a user's device is offline. Powered by Gemini 3.5 Flash and the Antigravity 2.0 framework, it works autonomously across Google Workspace, third-party apps, and the web, surfacing progress updates via a new Android status-bar layer called Android Halo. A live I/O demo showed Spark planning a neighborhood event — pulling Gmail RSVPs, creating Google Sheets trackers, generating Slides decks, and drafting follow-up emails — each action queued for user approval. Spark launches next week for Google AI Ultra subscribers in the US ($100/month); MCP support for Canva, Instacart, and OpenTable arrives this summer.
