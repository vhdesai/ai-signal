# U.S. legal practitioners are now widely warning enterprise clients that conversations with AI chatbots — including…

U.S. legal practitioners are now widely warning enterprise clients that conversations with AI chatbots — including ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini — qualify as business records and are subject to legal discovery and subpoena. This development is particularly acute for Corp Dev teams using AI assistants for deal strategy, valuation work, or confidential target analysis. Firms are advised to review AI usage policies, implement session logging controls, and evaluate whether sensitive workflows should remain on enterprise-contracted (not consumer) AI platforms with appropriate data processing agreements.
