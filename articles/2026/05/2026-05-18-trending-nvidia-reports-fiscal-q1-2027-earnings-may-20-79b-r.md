# Trending Nvidia Reports Fiscal Q1 2027 Earnings May 20 — $79B Revenue Expected

Nvidia reports fiscal Q1 2027 earnings after market close on Wednesday May 20, with consensus expecting ~$79.17B in revenue and $1.78 EPS; data-center revenue is projected to contribute over 90% of the top line. The print is the largest near-term market catalyst in the AI semiconductor complex, including the recently IPO'd Cerebras. It is the most-watched financial event of the week given Nvidia's mega-cap weight in AI-infrastructure portfolios. 🎓 Academic Research
