# TrendingCerebras IPO on Track — Seeking $3.5B at $26.6B Valuation

Cerebras Systems disclosed IPO terms seeking to raise up to $3.5 billion by selling 28 million shares at $115–$125, implying a $26.6 billion market cap at the top of range. The AI chip specialist's revenue jumped 76% in 2025 to $290 million, and it turned an $87.9 million profit after a $485 million loss the prior year. The offering is one of the most watched AI IPOs of 2026, testing whether investor enthusiasm for pure-play AI infrastructure companies can sustain at scale following a broader AI software multiple compression earlier in the year.
