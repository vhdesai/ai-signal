# Subquadratic released the first commercially available non-transformer LLM, SubQ 1M-Preview, featuring subquadratic…

Subquadratic released the first commercially available non-transformer LLM, SubQ 1M-Preview, featuring subquadratic attention that enables a 1M+ effective working context and 12M-token archival context at a fraction of transformer inference costs at long sequence lengths. The B2B-only API preview targets document analysis and knowledge management workloads. While rated at roughly 20% of top frontier capability, the architecture represents a meaningful architectural diversification beyond the transformer paradigm that has dominated since 2017.
