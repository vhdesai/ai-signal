# AI Subscription Tiers Restructured Across OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, and xAI

A new pricing landscape emerged this week: Google cut AI Ultra from $250 to $200 and added a new $100 entry point; OpenAI introduced a $100 ChatGPT Pro tier for Codex-heavy users; Anthropic stabilized Claude Max at $100 and $200; and xAI bundled Grok Build into the $30/month SuperGrok tier. For the first time, every price point between $20 and $300 carries a meaningfully differentiated value proposition for enterprise AI buyers.
