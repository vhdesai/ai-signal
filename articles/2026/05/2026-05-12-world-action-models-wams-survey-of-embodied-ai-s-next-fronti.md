# World Action Models (WAMs): Survey of Embodied AI's Next Frontier

A landmark survey paper formalizes the World Action Models paradigm — embodied foundation models that unify predictive state modeling with action generation to anticipate physical environment changes under agent intervention, going beyond reactive VLA models. The paper provides the first structured taxonomy (Cascaded vs. Joint WAMs), analyzes data ecosystems from robot teleoperation to internet-scale video, and identifies critical open challenges. This work is the reference frame for understanding where physical AI is heading.
