# ServiceNow shifts toward hybrid seat-plus-consumption pricing as AI economics bite

ServiceNow CEO Bill McDermott told Business Insider the company has moved off pure seat-based pricing to a hybrid model blending licenses with consumption — a structural change driven by the variable cost of running AI inference. McDermott says ~50% of ServiceNow revenue is now non-seat-based, and frames the shift as a survival move for SaaS vendors whose AI features could otherwise destroy gross margins. ServiceNow also raised its long-range guidance to ~$32B by 2030.
