# Palantir Q1 2026: revenue +85% YoY, U.S. commercial +133%

Palantir reported Q1 2026 revenue of $1.63B (+85% YoY), U.S. revenue +104%, U.S. commercial +133% to $595M, with a Rule of 40 score of 145%. AIP-led growth prompted FY26 guidance to be raised to $7.65-$7.66B. Stock fell 6% intraday on profit-taking despite the beat.
