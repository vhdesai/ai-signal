# Breaking Cornell and Toyota Research Institute Launch 31-University AI & Robotics Partnership

Cornell joined Toyota Research Institute's University Research Program 3.0 alongside 30 other universities, with two Cornell-led projects newly funded. Hadas Kress-Gazit and Guy Hoffman will work on LBM-based human-robot collaboration failure detection; Angelina Wang (Cornell Bowers / Cornell Tech) will lead research on how AI personalization affects trust in conversational agents. The partnership is one of the most expansive multi-university AI research consortiums announced this year.
