# OpenAI briefs White House on GPT-Rosalind biodefense program

OpenAI told the White House it has launched a biodefense effort built around GPT-Rosalind, a specialized life-sciences model designed to support pandemic preparedness, vaccine discovery, and biothreat detection. The disclosure positions OpenAI's national-security work as a parallel to Anthropic's Glasswing program and signals deeper public-sector engagement ahead of the company's IPO.
