# Huawei Outlines Post-Moore "Tau Scaling Law" and 1.4nm-by-2031 Chip Roadmap

At ISCAS 2026 in Shanghai, Huawei researchers presented a "Tau Scaling Law" (also dubbed "Her's Law") and a LogicFolding 3D-stacking approach, laying out a path to 1.4nm-class chips by 2031 despite lithography constraints. The roadmap is being read as China's bid to sustain AI-hardware scaling under export controls by shifting from feature-size shrinks to architectural and packaging gains.
