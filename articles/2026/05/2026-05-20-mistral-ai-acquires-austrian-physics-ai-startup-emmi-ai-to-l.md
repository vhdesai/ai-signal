# Mistral AI acquires Austrian "Physics AI" startup Emmi AI to lead industrial simulation market

Mistral AI acquired Linz-based Emmi AI, a developer of Physics AI models for industrial simulation spanning aerospace, automotive, semiconductor, and energy sectors. Terms were not disclosed; Emmi's 30+ researchers join Mistral's Science and Applied AI divisions, and Linz becomes Mistral's eighth official office. CEO Arthur Mensch said the deal "cements Mistral AI's leadership in industrial AI" and positions it as "the partner of choice for manufacturers in high-stakes sectors."
