# NewMicrosoft Global AI Diffusion Report: 17.8% of Working-Age Population Now Uses AI

Microsoft's Global AI Diffusion Report published May 10 found that 17.8% of the world's working-age population actively uses AI tools — with the UAE leading at 70.1% adoption. Despite high-profile tech layoff headlines, U.S. software developer employment is at a record high, suggesting AI is augmenting rather than replacing developer roles at scale. The report arrives as Microsoft prepares to remove free Copilot Chat from Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and OneNote effective May 16, pushing non-licensed users toward paid plans. 5 · Academic Research
