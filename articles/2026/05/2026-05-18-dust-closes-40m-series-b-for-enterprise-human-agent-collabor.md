# Dust Closes $40M Series B for Enterprise Human-Agent Collaboration New

Dust, which builds specialized AI agents designed to operate alongside human employees rather than as autonomous replacements, raised a $40 million Series B led by Abstract and Sequoia. The platform enables enterprises to design, deploy, and audit domain-specific agents with clear human oversight mechanisms. The round reflects continued enterprise demand for "supervised agentic" architectures—where governance, explainability, and auditability are first-class requirements, particularly in financial services, healthcare, and legal sectors.
