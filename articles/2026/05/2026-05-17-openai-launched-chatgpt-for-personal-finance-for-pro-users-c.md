# OpenAI launched ChatGPT for personal finance for Pro users, connecting to financial accounts through Plaid with…

OpenAI launched ChatGPT for personal finance for Pro users, connecting to financial accounts through Plaid with read-only access to balances, transactions, investments, subscriptions, upcoming bills, and savings goals. The feature is a significant expansion of ChatGPT's footprint into fintech and consumer data, and signals OpenAI's push beyond pure productivity AI into personal financial intelligence.
