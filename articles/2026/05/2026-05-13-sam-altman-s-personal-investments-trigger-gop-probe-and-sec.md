# Sam Altman's Personal Investments Trigger GOP Probe and SEC Referral

The House Oversight Committee, chaired by James Comer, opened an investigation into OpenAI CEO Sam Altman's personal investments and their ties to OpenAI's commercial partnerships, including stakes in Helion and Stoke Space. Ten Republican attorneys general separately asked the SEC to review OpenAI's governance.
