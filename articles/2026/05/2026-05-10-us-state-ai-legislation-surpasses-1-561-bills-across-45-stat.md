# US State AI Legislation Surpasses 1,561 Bills Across 45 States

As of March 2026, lawmakers in 45 US states introduced 1,561 AI-related bills — surpassing all of 2024's total. Despite Trump's December EO directing the DOJ to challenge state AI laws that conflict with a "minimally burdensome" national framework, a 99-1 Senate vote stripped a 10-year moratorium on state AI laws from recent legislation. States with active regimes include California, Texas (TRAIGA), New York (RAISE Act, effective 2027), and Illinois. For multinational enterprises, the patchwork creates material compliance complexity.
