# At its Android Show event (May 12), Google announced Googlebook — a new premium laptop category running Android with…

At its Android Show event (May 12), Google announced Googlebook — a new premium laptop category running Android with Gemini AI embedded at the system level. Key features include Magic Pointer (select anything to invoke Gemini), Create My Widget (build widgets by asking), Cast My Apps (run phone apps on laptop wirelessly), and seamless phone file access. The announcement garnered 862 Hacker News points with 1,414 comments. Further details are expected at Google I/O 2026 (May 19). Note: a federal lawsuit alleging Google activated Gemini across Gmail, Chat, and Meet without consent was already pending at launch.
