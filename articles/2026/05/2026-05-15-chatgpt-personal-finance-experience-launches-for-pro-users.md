# ChatGPT Personal Finance Experience Launches for Pro Users

OpenAI launched a preview personal finance dashboard inside ChatGPT for Pro subscribers in the U.S., connecting to more than 12,000 financial institutions via Plaid. Users can view balances, track spending and subscriptions, surface upcoming bills, and ask GPT-5.5 questions grounded in their own financial context. The feature stores a Financial memories profile across sessions, with Intuit integration arriving shortly. A Plus-tier rollout is planned after the early preview period.
