# OpenAI preparing confidential S-1 targeting $1T IPO valuation

OpenAI is preparing a confidential S-1 with the SEC, advised by Goldman Sachs and Morgan Stanley, targeting a public listing as early as September 2026 at a valuation above $1 trillion. The company generates $25B in ARR and 900M weekly active users — but is operating at a loss while Anthropic hits profitability. The race to file first is a race to set the narrative.
