# Palantir Q1 2026: Revenue +85% YoY, FY Guidance Raised to $7.65B

Palantir reported Q1 2026 revenue of $1.63 billion, up 85% year-over-year, beating estimates of $1.54B. Adjusted EPS came in at $0.33 vs. $0.28 estimated. Full-year 2026 guidance was raised to $7.65–7.66B. The beat reinforces Palantir's role as a benchmark for the forward-deployed AI services model that both OpenAI and Anthropic are now racing to replicate via their enterprise JVs. (Source: Tech Market Briefs)
