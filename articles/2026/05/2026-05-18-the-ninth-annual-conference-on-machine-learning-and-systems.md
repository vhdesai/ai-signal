# The ninth annual Conference on Machine Learning and Systems opened today in Bellevue, WA, featuring keynotes from…

The ninth annual Conference on Machine Learning and Systems opened today in Bellevue, WA, featuring keynotes from researchers at NVIDIA, Microsoft Research Asia, Google (Amin Vahdat), University of Washington (Luke Zettlemoyer), and Stanford. This year's competition track includes an AWS Trainium2/3 MoE Kernel Challenge, a Google Graph Scheduling Competition, and an NVIDIA FlashInfer AI Kernel Generation Contest — signaling industry's push for more efficient AI inference and training infrastructure.
