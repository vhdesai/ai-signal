# BreakingTrump Administration Signs Pre-Deployment AI Testing Agreements with Google, Microsoft, xAI

In a significant policy reversal, the Trump administration's Center for AI Standards and Innovation (CAISI) announced voluntary pre-deployment evaluation agreements with Google DeepMind, Microsoft, and xAI on May 5 — joining OpenAI and Anthropic, which had already been cooperating since 2024. The catalyst was Anthropic's refusal to publicly release Claude Mythos, citing the model's advanced cybersecurity capabilities as too dangerous for open release. The White House is now reportedly drafting an executive order that would mandate government testing of frontier AI models prior to release. CAISI Director Chris Fall stated: "Independent, rigorous measurement science is essential to understanding frontier AI and its national security implications." The NSA has independently gained access to Mythos for evaluation.
