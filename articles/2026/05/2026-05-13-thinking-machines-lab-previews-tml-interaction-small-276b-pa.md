# Thinking Machines Lab Previews TML-Interaction-Small: 276B-Param Multimodal MoE With Real-Time Audio/Video

Mira Murati's Thinking Machines Lab previewed TML-Interaction-Small, a 276B-parameter Mixture-of-Experts model (12B active) featuring a multi-stream, time-aligned micro-turn architecture that processes 200ms audio, video, and text chunks simultaneously. A real-time interaction model runs in parallel with an asynchronous background reasoning model, eliminating external voice-activity-detection harnesses. The architecture targets the hardest unsolved problem in multimodal AI: natural real-time conversation across modalities.
