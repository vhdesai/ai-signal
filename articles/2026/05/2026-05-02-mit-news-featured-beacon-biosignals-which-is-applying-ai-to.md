# MIT News featured Beacon Biosignals, which is applying AI to neurological signal analysis for sleep medicine, enabling…

MIT News featured Beacon Biosignals, which is applying AI to neurological signal analysis for sleep medicine, enabling automated high-resolution brain activity mapping that previously required expert manual review. The technology can identify sleep disorder biomarkers with clinical-grade accuracy, potentially transforming diagnostic workflows in neurology and sleep clinics. The work exemplifies AI-for-science applications where machine learning operates on complex biological time-series data. Commercial deployments are underway at several academic medical centers.
