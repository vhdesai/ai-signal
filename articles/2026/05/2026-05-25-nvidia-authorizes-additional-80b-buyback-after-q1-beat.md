# Nvidia authorizes additional $80B buyback after Q1 beat

Following last week's Q1 print that beat both consensus and internal guidance, Nvidia authorized an additional $80B share repurchase. Analysts read the move as a management vote that AI-infrastructure demand has not peaked, with confirmed continued capex acceleration from hyperscaler customers including Meta and Amazon.
