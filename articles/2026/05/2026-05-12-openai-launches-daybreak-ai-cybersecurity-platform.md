# OpenAI Launches "Daybreak" AI Cybersecurity Platform

OpenAI announced Daybreak, an AI security system that detects software vulnerabilities, validates fixes, and accelerates the patching workflow end to end. The launch is widely read as a direct response to Anthropic's Claude Mythos and Project Glasswing, and signals that frontier labs now view continuous security operations as a defensible enterprise wedge. OpenAI is emphasizing defensive-only positioning, paired with rigorous verification and human oversight, to manage misuse risk.
