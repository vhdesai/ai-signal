# Google DeepMind Establishes Singapore National AI Partnership New

Google DeepMind announced a new national AI partnership with Singapore focused on research, talent development, and AI infrastructure — aligned with Singapore's Smart Nation 2.0 strategy. The deal follows similar partnerships with the Republic of Korea and the UAE. For Google, sovereign AI partnerships serve a dual purpose: securing regulatory goodwill in strategically critical markets and establishing Gemini as the preferred foundation model for government AI programs outside the U.S. and EU. Singapore's geographic position as a Southeast Asia technology hub makes the partnership particularly significant for regional enterprise AI expansion. 6 · AI Safety & Policy
