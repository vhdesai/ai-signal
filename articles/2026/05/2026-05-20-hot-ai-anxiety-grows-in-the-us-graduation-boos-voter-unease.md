# Hot AI Anxiety Grows in the US — Graduation Boos, Voter Unease, Data Center Bans

A meaningful cultural backlash against AI is crystallizing in the United States: speakers promoting AI are being booed at university commencement ceremonies, voters in multiple jurisdictions are organizing against new data center development, and even AI-friendly Trump administration officials are beginning to moderate their rhetoric. Analysts say the shift reflects a growing disconnect between elite-level AI enthusiasm and broader public anxiety about job displacement, community energy impact, and the pace of change. ArXiv simultaneously announced it will ban authors for one year if AI generates their entire paper.
