# Google I/O 2026 kicks off tomorrow (May 19–20) at the Shoreline Amphitheatre

Google I/O 2026 kicks off tomorrow (May 19–20) at the Shoreline Amphitheatre. Pre-announcements include "Gemini Intelligence," a deeply integrated agentic AI layer across Android; "Googlebooks," premium Android laptops replacing Chromebooks with full Gemini integration; Android XR smart glasses powered by Gemini 3.1 Pro in partnership with Samsung, Warby Parker, and Gentle Monster; and Android 17 with on-device AI features. This represents Google's most aggressive consumer AI integration push to date and is expected to feature further Gemini 3.1 model updates during the developer sessions.
