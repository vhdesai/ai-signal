# Google “Gemini Spark” Autonomous Agent Leaks Ahead of I/O 2026

A pre-launch leak reveals a new autonomous Google agent called Gemini Spark, expected to debut at I/O on May 19. Unlike standard Gemini features, Spark is designed to operate proactively without explicit prompts, accessing remote browser data and executing tasks on the user's behalf. The timing — four days before the developer conference — signals a significant agentic push positioned against OpenAI's Workspace Agents and Microsoft Copilot.
