# PC motherboard sales are forecast to decline more than 25% year-over-year in 2026 as consumers delay hardware upgrades…

PC motherboard sales are forecast to decline more than 25% year-over-year in 2026 as consumers delay hardware upgrades amid AI-driven price surges in memory, storage, and processors. The dynamic reflects AI's paradox in the semiconductor market: massive demand from cloud providers and AI labs is crowding out consumer-grade component supply while inflating prices across the board. Semiconductor sales overall surged 79% year-over-year in Q1 2026 — but virtually all of that growth is in data-center-grade accelerators, leaving the traditional consumer PC cycle in a prolonged slump.
