# Google DeepMind unveils Gemini Omni — a natively multimodal "any-to-any" model

DeepMind introduced Gemini Omni, a unified architecture that natively processes text, image, audio, and video — and outputs video grounded in world knowledge — rather than converting modalities to text tokens. Gemini Omni Flash ships immediately in the Gemini app, Google Flow, and YouTube Shorts and supports multi-turn conversational video editing with character continuity. It collapses Veo (video) and Nano Banana (image) into a single pipeline for paid Gemini AI Plus, Pro, and Ultra subscribers.
