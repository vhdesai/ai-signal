# Google DeepMind introduces an AI-enabled mouse pointer powered by Gemini — MarkTechPost / DeepMind Blog, May 13, 2026…

Google DeepMind introduces an AI-enabled mouse pointer powered by Gemini — MarkTechPost / DeepMind Blog, May 13, 2026 DeepMind published four interaction principles and live demos in Google AI Studio for a Gemini-powered cursor that captures real-time visual and semantic context around the pointer. A deeper integration called Magic Pointer is rolling out inside Chrome, with further integration planned for Google's new Googlebook laptops. The work targets a long-standing UX gap: text-in/text-out models with no awareness of screen state.
