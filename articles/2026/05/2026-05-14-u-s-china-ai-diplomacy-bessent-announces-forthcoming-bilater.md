# U.S.–China AI Diplomacy: Bessent Announces Forthcoming Bilateral AI Safety Protocol

At the Trump–Xi summit in Beijing, Treasury Secretary Scott Bessent announced a forthcoming bilateral U.S.–China AI safety protocol. The diplomatic move runs alongside the H200 sales clearance to roughly ten Chinese firms and Premier Li's remarks to U.S. CEOs that the two countries "should be friends and partners."
