# Google Launches Gemini 3.5 Flash & Gemini Omni at I/O 2026

Google kicked off its I/O 2026 keynote with two simultaneous model launches. Gemini 3.5 Flash is now live globally, powering Google Search AI Mode, AI Overviews, and Antigravity 2.0 with speeds described as "12x faster" in agentic workflows; Gemini 3.5 Pro will follow next month. Gemini Omni — available today for paid subscribers — is a unified multimodal model that accepts text, image, audio, and video inputs and outputs video grounded in world knowledge, combining the capabilities of Veo (video) and Nano Banana (image generation) into a single pipeline. Google DeepMind CEO Demis Hassabis told the I/O audience: "Artificial General Intelligence is just a few years away."
