# White House scraps planned AI safety executive order after CEO calls

Axios reports the Trump White House canceled the planned signing of an executive order that would have introduced pre-release model-vetting requirements, after direct calls from Mark Zuckerberg, Elon Musk, and David Sacks in the hours before signing. The cancellation removes the most concrete near-term U.S. federal AI safety mandate from the table. Applications & Adoption
