# Trump Delays AI Security Executive Order, Citing "Blocker" Language Concerns

President Trump delayed signing the long-anticipated AI security executive order, saying the proposed text contained language that "could have been a blocker" to AI development. The delay extends the regulatory ambiguity facing U.S. AI vendors and re-opens a debate that the December 2025 White House EO was meant to settle — particularly around pre-release model vetting and preemption of state AI laws.
