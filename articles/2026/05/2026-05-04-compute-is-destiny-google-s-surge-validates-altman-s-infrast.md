# “Compute is destiny”: Google's surge validates Altman's infrastructure thesis

A sharp Alphabet stock rally is being read by analysts as proof that compute capacity — not model quality alone — is the decisive lever in the AI race. The move vindicates Sam Altman's “compute is destiny” framing and intensifies pressure on rivals lacking comparable TPU/data-center leverage. Expect renewed scrutiny of capex disclosures across the hyperscalers.
