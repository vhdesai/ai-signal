# Stanford HAI 2026 AI Index: Capability Is Accelerating, Not Plateauing Trending

Stanford HAI's 2026 AI Index confirms that AI capability continues to accelerate rather than plateau, with industry producing over 90% of notable frontier models in 2025. Several top models now meet or exceed human baselines on PhD-level science questions, multimodal reasoning, and competition mathematics. On the SWE-bench Verified coding benchmark, performance jumped from 60% to near 100% in a single year. Enterprise adoption has reached 88%, and 4 in 5 university students now report using generative AI regularly — a figure that has significant implications for workforce development and educational policy.
