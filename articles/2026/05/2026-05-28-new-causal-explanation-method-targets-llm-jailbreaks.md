A new preprint, "Minimal, Local, Causal Explanations for Jailbreak Success in Large Language Models," proposes a framework for pinpointing the specific perturbations that cause frontier models to comply with disallowed prompts. The work is directly relevant for enterprise red-teaming pipelines and is one of several jailbreak-defense papers appearing as Anthropic and OpenAI publish updated frontier safety commitments. 3.
