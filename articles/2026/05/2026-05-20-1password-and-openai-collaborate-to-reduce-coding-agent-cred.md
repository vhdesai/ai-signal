# 1Password and OpenAI collaborate to reduce coding-agent credential leakage

SecurityWeek reported that 1Password and OpenAI are working together to prevent AI coding agents from leaking credentials. The collaboration addresses a practical enterprise risk as agents gain broader repository, log and environment access. Expect secrets management, identity scoping and permissioning to become standard parts of AI agent governance rather than adjacent security controls.
