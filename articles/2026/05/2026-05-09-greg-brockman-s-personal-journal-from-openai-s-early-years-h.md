# Greg Brockman's personal journal from OpenAI's early years has emerged as star evidence in the Musk v

Greg Brockman's personal journal from OpenAI's early years has emerged as star evidence in the Musk v. Altman trial, with Brockman confirming he stopped writing about OpenAI in it in 2023. His testimony included a vivid account of the 2017 confrontation where Musk demanded full company control, was refused, and "stormed around the table" before declaring "I decline" and exiting. The journal entries provide contemporaneous documentation of OpenAI's founding tensions and governance disputes, potentially strengthening Altman's legal position by grounding claims in real-time written records rather than memory alone.
