# U.S. Clears Nvidia H200 Chip Sales to 10 Chinese Firms; No Deliveries Yet

The U.S. Commerce Department has approved approximately ten Chinese firms — including Alibaba, Tencent, ByteDance, and JD.com — to purchase Nvidia's H200 AI chip. Three Reuters sources confirm that not a single delivery has yet been made, leaving the deals in limbo as CEO Jensen Huang travels with President Trump to seek a diplomatic breakthrough. The clearances mark a significant pivot in the U.S. AI export-controls posture, coinciding with Bessent's bilateral AI safety protocol announcement.
