# Google's SynthID AI Watermarking Adopted by OpenAI, Nvidia, and Major Partners

Google announced that its SynthID AI content watermarking technology — used to label over 100 billion images and videos and 60,000 years' worth of audio — is now being adopted beyond Google for the first time. OpenAI, Nvidia, and additional partners have joined the SynthID coalition, signaling an industry-wide push toward verifiable AI-generated content provenance. Google is also advancing C2PA (Content Credentials) metadata tagging in parallel. The move comes as hyperrealistic AI-generated media grows increasingly indistinguishable from authentic content, raising urgency for practical detection infrastructure at scale.
