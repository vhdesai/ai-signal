# Cisco Cuts 4,000 Jobs While Raising Revenue Forecast on $5.3B in AI Infrastructure Orders

Cisco announced ~4,000 layoffs while simultaneously raising its annual revenue forecast after reporting $5.3B in AI infrastructure orders this fiscal year, with $9B projected total. The pattern continues across the industry: Q1 2026 saw more than 80,000 tech layoffs, with nearly half attributed directly to AI-driven restructuring. Meta separately confirmed 8,000 layoffs beginning May 20.
