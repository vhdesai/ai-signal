# State AI Regulatory Roundup: Colorado, Connecticut & California Advancing Divergent AI Governance Frameworks Trending

Three U.S. states are advancing materially different AI governance frameworks in 2026: Colorado is focused on algorithmic accountability in high-stakes decision-making contexts; Connecticut is advancing a comprehensive, tiered AI governance structure with layered obligations; and California is deepening its earlier AI mandates with expanded enforcement mechanisms. With no federal preemption on the horizon, enterprise compliance teams face a complex patchwork of state requirements. Legal counsel are advising organizations to map AI deployments against state-specific frameworks now to avoid retroactive non-compliance exposure.
