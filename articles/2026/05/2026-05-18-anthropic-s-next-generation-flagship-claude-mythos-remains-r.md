# Anthropic's next-generation flagship, Claude Mythos, remains restricted to roughly 50 partner organizations — with…

Anthropic's next-generation flagship, Claude Mythos, remains restricted to roughly 50 partner organizations — with cybersecurity firms prioritized under "Project Glasswing." Leaked gated evaluations show 93.9% on SWE-bench Verified and 94.6% on GPQA Diamond, numbers that would reset performance expectations across the industry if confirmed publicly; for context, the current public leader (Claude Opus 4.7) scores 64.3% on SWE-Bench Pro. The Pentagon's assistant secretary for cyber policy has framed Mythos as an "opportunity" for U.S. cyber defense rather than purely a threat. No public release date has been announced; Anthropic cites the model's advanced cybersecurity exploitation capabilities as the reason for the controlled rollout.
