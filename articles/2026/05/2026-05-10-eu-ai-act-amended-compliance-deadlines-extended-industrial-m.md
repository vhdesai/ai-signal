# EU AI Act Amended — Compliance Deadlines Extended, Industrial Machinery Exempted

The European Parliament and Council reached a provisional agreement to amend the EU AI Act in an early-morning session on May 7, after marathon negotiations. High-risk AI systems (biometrics, critical infrastructure, education, employment, law enforcement) now face a December 2, 2027 compliance deadline; AI embedded in products faces August 2, 2028. Machinery is fully exempted from AI Act applicability where industrial regulation already applies — a concession Germany pushed hard for. The agreement is part of a broader Digital Omnibus AI simplification package. (Source: IAPP)
