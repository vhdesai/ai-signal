# Pentagon signs classified AI deals with seven companies — Anthropic excluded

The U.S. Department of Defense has signed deals with seven AI companies — xAI, OpenAI, Google, Nvidia, Microsoft, AWS, and Reflection — to use their technology on classified networks for any “lawful operational use.” Anthropic was excluded after asking for contractual carveouts that would prevent its AI from being used for domestic surveillance or to pilot autonomous drones; the Pentagon labeled Anthropic a supply-chain threat and banned its models from DoD work. The split is now the cleanest example yet of how safety posture is shaping federal AI procurement.
