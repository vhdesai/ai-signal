# The Elon Musk vs. Sam Altman civil trial in California has wrapped its evidentiary phase, with the jury now…

The Elon Musk vs. Sam Altman civil trial in California has wrapped its evidentiary phase, with the jury now deliberating on whether OpenAI's conversion from nonprofit to for-profit violated its founding mission. Trust and corporate governance are at the center of arguments. Separately, TechCrunch reports that Musk's xAI has been losing staff rapidly since its merger with X (formerly Twitter), and that xAI is operating roughly 50 gas turbines at its Memphis, Tennessee data center without environmental permits — drawing scrutiny from regulators and climate advocates.
