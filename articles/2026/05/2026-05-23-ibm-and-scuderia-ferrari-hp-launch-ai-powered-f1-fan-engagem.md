# IBM and Scuderia Ferrari HP Launch AI-Powered F1 Fan Engagement Partnership

IBM and Scuderia Ferrari HP announced an enterprise AI partnership to overhaul the Ferrari fan app, joining the broader F1 sponsor-tech landscape where AWS, Oracle, and Anthropic already hold relationships with other teams. Ferrari processes millions of telemetry data points per second during each race; IBM's role is converting that stream into personalized, easy-to-follow fan storytelling. The deal represents a continued trend of enterprise AI vendors embedding into elite-sport media infrastructure as a B2B distribution channel for AI-driven content personalization.
