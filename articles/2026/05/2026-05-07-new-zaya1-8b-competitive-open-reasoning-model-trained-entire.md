# New ZAYA1-8B: Competitive Open Reasoning Model Trained Entirely on AMD Instinct MI300 GPUs

Researchers released ZAYA1-8B, a strong open reasoning model whose defining characteristic is its training hardware: an exclusively AMD Instinct MI300 GPU stack — zero Nvidia silicon. The model performs competitively in its size class and arrives as independent validation that high-quality AI training is no longer exclusively Nvidia's domain. The release follows GLM-4.7 (Huawei Ascend silicon, $0.11/million tokens, 1.2% hallucination rate) and ZAYA1-8B together represent a quiet but significant shift in the AI hardware narrative. 3 · Products & Tools
