# NVIDIA Releases Nemotron 3 Nano Omni at GTC 2026

NVIDIA released Nemotron 3 Nano Omni, a unified multimodal reasoning model, alongside the Vera Rubin platform for autonomous workloads. GTC 2026 focused on agentic and physical AI, with NVIDIA positioning the new stack as a turnkey runtime for enterprise agent deployments. The announcements complement a co-developed agent runtime with SAP unveiled at SAP Sapphire.
