# Google's Genie World Model Can Now Simulate Real Streets Using Street View

Unveiled at Google I/O 2026, the Genie world-modeling system now incorporates Street View data to simulate photorealistic, interactive real-world environments — moving beyond synthetic game-world generation. The capability represents a step toward grounded world models that robots and agents can train in before real-world deployment. TechCrunch noted the demonstration generated navigable street-level simulations with physics-consistent behavior, a capability with significant implications for autonomous systems research.
