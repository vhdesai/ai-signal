# MIT Media Lab researchers used EEG to measure cognitive load during essay writing across three groups: LLM users,…

MIT Media Lab researchers used EEG to measure cognitive load during essay writing across three groups: LLM users, search engine users, and unassisted writers. Over four months, LLM users consistently showed the weakest brain connectivity — reduced alpha and beta neural networks — lower essay ownership, and difficulty quoting their own work. The paper raises concerns about the long-term educational and cognitive implications of heavy LLM reliance, especially as AI writing assistants become default tools in enterprise and academic settings. The study is circulating widely as AI adoption benchmarks reach all-time highs.
