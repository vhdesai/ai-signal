# Google's unreleased Gemini 3.2 Flash surfaces on Eleuther AI Arena

Google is externally testing Gemini 3.2 Flash on the Eleuther AI Arena, with early users reporting notable gains over the AI Studio production version of Gemini 3 Flash. Standout improvements include SVG generation, coding proficiency, 3D simulation, and richer animation processing. The model is widely expected to be unveiled at an upcoming Google developer conference and is positioned to compete directly with GPT-5.5.
