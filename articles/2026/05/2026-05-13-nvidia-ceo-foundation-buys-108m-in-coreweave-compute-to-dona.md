# Nvidia CEO Foundation Buys $108M in CoreWeave Compute to Donate to Academic Researchers

The Jensen and Lori Huang Foundation disclosed it is purchasing $108M of compute time from CoreWeave to donate to universities and nonprofit research institutes. The move positions an Nvidia-orbit philanthropic vehicle as a major new sponsor of academic compute access — directly addressing the GPU access gap that constrains academic AI research relative to well-resourced labs.
