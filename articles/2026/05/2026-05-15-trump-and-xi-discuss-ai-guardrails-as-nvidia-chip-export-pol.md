# Trump and Xi Discuss AI Guardrails as Nvidia Chip Export Policy Remains Unsettled

President Trump said he raised possible AI guardrails with President Xi as U.S. officials continue to weigh AI safety, China competition, and Nvidia chip-export rules. The exchange has reinforced uncertainty over the future of advanced-accelerator exports to China — a critical variable for Nvidia, hyperscalers, and Chinese frontier-lab roadmaps including DeepSeek and Alibaba.
