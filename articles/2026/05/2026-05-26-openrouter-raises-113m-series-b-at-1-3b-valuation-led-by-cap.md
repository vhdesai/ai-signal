# OpenRouter Raises $113M Series B at $1.3B Valuation Led by CapitalG

Model router OpenRouter raised a $113M Series B at a $1.3B valuation led by Alphabet's CapitalG, with participation from existing investors. The round validates the multi-model thesis: as no single model dominates across workloads, an abstraction layer that arbitrages cost and quality across Claude, GPT, Gemini, Qwen, Grok, and open-weights models has become structurally valuable to enterprise buyers.
