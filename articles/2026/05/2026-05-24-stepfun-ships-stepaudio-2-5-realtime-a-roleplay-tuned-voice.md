# StepFun ships StepAudio 2.5 Realtime, a roleplay-tuned voice rival to GPT-realtime

Chinese lab StepFun released an end-to-end realtime voice model that pairs paralinguistic comprehension (tone and prosody) with roleplay-specific RLHF. The model is positioned as a direct competitor to GPT-realtime, with low-latency speech-in/speech-out and explicit handling of vocal expression alongside semantic content. TRENDING RESEARCH
