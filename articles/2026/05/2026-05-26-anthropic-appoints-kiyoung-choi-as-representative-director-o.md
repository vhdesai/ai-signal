# Anthropic Appoints KiYoung Choi as Representative Director of Korea

Anthropic continues its APAC expansion with the appointment of KiYoung Choi to lead Korea operations, ahead of a Seoul office opening expected in Q3. The move follows the Tokyo opening earlier this year and signals an enterprise-led push into the Korean chaebol ecosystem.
