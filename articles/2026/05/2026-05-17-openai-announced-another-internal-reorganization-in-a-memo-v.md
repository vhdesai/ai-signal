# OpenAI announced another internal reorganization in a memo viewed by The Verge (~22 hours ago), consolidating product…

OpenAI announced another internal reorganization in a memo viewed by The Verge (~22 hours ago), consolidating product ownership under company president Greg Brockman. The company is also restructuring for the intensifying AI agent battle — a pattern that has produced multiple C-suite shifts at OpenAI this year. The move comes as OpenAI also faces a reported legal dispute with Apple over partnership terms and manages the ongoing Musk vs. Altman trial.
