# Replit Returns to iPhone App Store with Agent 4 After 4-Month Apple Dispute

Replit shipped its first iOS app update in four months following a protracted App Store review dispute with Apple, resolving a standoff that had blocked the company's AI coding agent from reaching iPhone users. The update brings Replit Agent 4 to mobile — capable of building and deploying full web apps from natural language prompts. The dispute reportedly centered on Apple's policies around in-app AI-generated code execution.
