# NVIDIA Research presents eight ICRA papers advancing sim-to-real robotics

At the International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA), NVIDIA Research presented eight papers spanning sim-to-real transfer, perception, dexterous manipulation, and multi-task policy learning. The work pushes embodied AI past scripted automation toward reliable, generalizable autonomy in production environments — building on the Vera Rubin agentic AI platform announced earlier in May.
