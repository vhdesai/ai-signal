# Andrej Karpathy, a founding member of OpenAI and former director of AI at Tesla, announced he is joining Anthropic.

Andrej Karpathy, a founding member of OpenAI and former director of AI at Tesla, announced he is joining Anthropic. "I think the next few years at the frontier of LLMs will be especially formative," he wrote on X. The hire is a significant talent coup for Anthropic, given Karpathy's legendary status in the AI community — he helped launch Stanford's first deep learning course and coined the term "vibe coding." The move counters the recent trend of researchers leaving major labs to start their own companies.
