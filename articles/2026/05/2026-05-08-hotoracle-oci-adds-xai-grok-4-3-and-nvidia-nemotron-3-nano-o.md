# HotOracle OCI Adds xAI Grok 4.3 and Nvidia Nemotron 3 Nano Omni

Oracle expanded its OCI AI model catalog on May 8 with xAI Grok 4.3 — reportedly scoring top-tier results on reasoning benchmarks — and Nvidia Nemotron 3 Nano Omni, an open-source multimodal model designed for efficient enterprise inference. The additions position Oracle's cloud as a multi-model enterprise hub at a moment when enterprises are demanding model choice and portability rather than lock-in with a single provider. 4 · Industry News
