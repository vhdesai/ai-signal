# Databricks AppKit + Replit: Native Connector for Enterprise Data Apps

Databricks announced AppKit, a new accelerator for enterprise apps, and a private-beta connector that lets Replit Agent build, query, and deploy directly against governed Databricks workspaces. Bain & Company is cited as an early reference customer that has displaced Tableau and Power BI internally. The deal is the most significant AI-IDE-meets-data-platform integration to date.
