# Microsoft Work Trend Index 2026: AI agents in M365 grew 15× year-over-year

Microsoft's 2026 Work Trend Index — drawn from trillions of M365 signals and a 20,000-worker survey across 10 countries — found active agents in M365 grew 15× year-over-year (18× in large enterprises), with 58% of AI users saying they produce work they couldn't have a year ago. Microsoft warns, however, that productivity gains are masking the harder, still-missing work of organizational redesign. The report's core argument: the next frontier is not better models but better org structures built around them.
