# Anthropic Potential $30B Funding Round Could Push Valuation to ~$900 Billion

Reports indicate Anthropic is in active discussions for a funding round of approximately $30 billion that would value the company at close to $900 billion — nearly tripling its February 2026 valuation. The potential raise comes after Anthropic disclosed Q1 2026 annualized revenue above $44 billion (80× year-over-year growth), a $200B Google Cloud compute contract, and the SpaceX Colossus 1 supercomputer deal (220,000+ Nvidia GPUs, 300MW). Both OpenAI and Anthropic are reportedly tracking toward IPOs later this year. 🛠️ Products & Developer Tools
