# Anthropic–SpaceX Colossus 1 Deal Doubles Claude Code Rate Limits

Anthropic signed a deal to utilize the full compute capacity of SpaceX's Colossus 1 supercomputer in Memphis — 220,000+ NVIDIA GPUs and 300 megawatts of capacity. The practical result: Claude Code's five-hour rate limits doubled for Pro and Max subscribers and peak-hour throttling was removed. Anthropic and SpaceX are also exploring "multiple gigawatts" of orbital compute as a long-term supply solution. The deal follows separate capacity agreements with Microsoft, Amazon, Google, and Nvidia.
