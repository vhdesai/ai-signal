# OpenAI established the OpenAI Deployment Company, a majority-controlled venture backed by more than $4 billion from 19…

OpenAI established the OpenAI Deployment Company, a majority-controlled venture backed by more than $4 billion from 19 investors, designed to help businesses integrate AI into real-world workflows. The initiative mirrors Anthropic's joint enterprise venture announced in early May (backed by Blackstone, Goldman Sachs, Hellman & Friedman at a $1.5B valuation). Both moves signal that the top AI labs are building enterprise deployment infrastructure as a distinct business — beyond API access — to capture value from the workflow-transformation layer.
