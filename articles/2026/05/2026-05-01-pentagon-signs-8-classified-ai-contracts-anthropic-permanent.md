# Pentagon Signs 8 Classified AI Contracts — Anthropic Permanently Excluded After Safety Dispute

The Department of Defense finalized AI agreements for Secret (IL6) and Top Secret (IL7) classified networks with OpenAI, Google, Microsoft, Amazon Web Services, Nvidia, SpaceX, Oracle, and startup Reflection AI — replacing Anthropic's prior $200M contract. Anthropic, which refused to remove safety restrictions around autonomous weapons and mass surveillance, was labeled a "supply chain risk" by Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth and is now challenging the designation in federal court. The contracts grant participating labs access to deploy AI across battlefield decision-making, logistics, and targeting analysis, with individual deal values estimated at $300–500M per firm. Trump also announced agreements allowing the Commerce Department to vet new AI models from Google DeepMind, Microsoft, and xAI before public release.
