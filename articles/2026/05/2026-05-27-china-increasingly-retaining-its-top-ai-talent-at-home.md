# China increasingly retaining its top AI talent at home

TechCrunch reports growing evidence that China's leading AI researchers — historically a major export to US labs — are increasingly staying in or returning to China. Factors include domestic compensation, restricted US visa pathways, and the maturity of China's own frontier-model ecosystem. Academic & Research Ecosystem
