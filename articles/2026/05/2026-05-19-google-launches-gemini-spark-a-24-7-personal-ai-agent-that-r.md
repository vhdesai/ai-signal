# Google launches Gemini Spark — a 24/7 personal AI agent that runs while you sleep

Gemini Spark is Google's first 24/7 personal AI agent, running on Google Cloud VMs even when the user's device is closed, with Android "Halo" surfacing progress notifications in the status bar. It launches next week for Google AI Ultra subscribers in the US — Ultra also dropped from $250 to $100/month — with Chrome integration this summer and MCP support for Canva, Instacart, and OpenTable within weeks. The launch puts Google directly against OpenAI's ChatGPT Tasks and Anthropic's Claude Managed Agents in the emerging 24/7 agent category.
