# Replit released its first iPhone app update in four months after CEO Amjad Masad announced they "worked things out with…

Replit released its first iPhone app update in four months after CEO Amjad Masad announced they "worked things out with Apple." The update brings Replit Agent 4 to mobile, including parallel agent execution, team collaboration via merge flows, and cross-workspace project viewing. The resolution may signal a broader shift in Apple's approach to vibe-coding tools following months of friction over dynamically executed code previews on iOS.
