# Mistral CEO says lab is exploring designing its own AI chips

Mistral CEO Arthur Mensch confirmed the French lab is exploring designing its own silicon as it ramps up its infrastructure footprint. The move would put Mistral alongside OpenAI, Google, Amazon, and Meta in the in-house-ASIC race — and is a strong signal that Europe's flagship AI lab intends to control more of its compute stack.
