# Mustafa Suleiman, Microsoft's AI Chief, stated today that most professional office roles are "very close" to…

Mustafa Suleiman, Microsoft's AI Chief, stated today that most professional office roles are "very close" to significant AI-driven disruption within an 18-month window, naming legal, marketing, and project management as the most vulnerable functions. The statement comes amid a broader context of real workforce reductions: Cloudflare has cut 1,100 jobs (20% of staff) and LinkedIn has reduced headcount by 5%, both citing the shift to AI-driven productivity. Microsoft's own Global AI Diffusion Report (May 10) found that 17.8% of the world's working-age population now uses AI tools regularly, with the UAE leading at 70.1%.
