# OpenAI Launches Personal Finance Experience Inside ChatGPT for Pro Users

OpenAI released a preview of a personal finance tool in ChatGPT for Pro subscribers in the US. Users can securely connect financial accounts, view a spending dashboard, and ask GPT-5.5-powered questions grounded in their real financial context. OpenAI notes 200M+ users already use ChatGPT monthly for budgeting and investment questions; the new feature adds structured financial data to sharpen those answers. The rollout is deliberately limited initially to gather real-world feedback before expanding.
