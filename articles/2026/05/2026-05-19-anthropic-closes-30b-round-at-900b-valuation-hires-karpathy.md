# Anthropic Closes $30B Round at $900B+ Valuation, Hires Karpathy for Pretraining

Anthropic closed its $30B funding round at a $900B+ valuation, co-led by Sequoia, Dragoneer, Greenoaks, and Altimeter — nearly tripling its February valuation. Earlier in the week, OpenAI co-founder and former Tesla AI director Andrej Karpathy announced he is joining Anthropic's pretraining team. Anthropic also acquired developer-tools startup Stainless for ~$300M, taking a key SDK and MCP-generation tool out of competitors' hands.
