# Pope Leo XIV released the Vatican's first-ever encyclical on artificial intelligence—co-presented alongside Anthropic…

Pope Leo XIV released the Vatican's first-ever encyclical on artificial intelligence—co-presented alongside Anthropic co-founder Christopher Olah—on May 25. Signed 135 years to the day after Leo XIII's Rerum Novarum, the document explicitly frames AI as the Industrial Revolution of our time, with detailed positions on human dignity, labour displacement, and autonomous weapons. Anthropic published Olah's accompanying remarks on May 25; full text is live at vatican.va.
