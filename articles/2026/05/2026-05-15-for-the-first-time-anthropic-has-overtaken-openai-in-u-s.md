# For the first time, Anthropic has overtaken OpenAI in U.S

For the first time, Anthropic has overtaken OpenAI in U.S. business AI adoption, with 34.4% of companies paying for Claude vs. 32.3% for ChatGPT, per the May 2026 Ramp AI Index tracking 50,000+ U.S. businesses. Anthropic quadrupled its business adoption over the past year while OpenAI grew only 0.3%. However, VentureBeat identifies three threats to Anthropic's lead: the Claude code quality regression, competition from Google's $40B Anthropic investment complicating independence, and enterprise concerns around the U.S. government's ban attempts. Google's planned $40B investment in Anthropic was confirmed this week.
