# SpaceX filed plans for a $55B chip fabrication facility in Texas ("Terafab"), positioning Elon Musk's aerospace company…

SpaceX filed plans for a $55B chip fabrication facility in Texas ("Terafab"), positioning Elon Musk's aerospace company as a vertically integrated AI infrastructure player spanning compute (Terafab), training (Colossus 1 leased to Anthropic), and developer tools (Cursor acquisition). Simultaneously, SpaceX is preparing for a June Nasdaq listing targeting $75B raised at a $1.75T valuation — which would make it the largest IPO in US history. The dual moves signal that SpaceX is positioning itself as foundational AI infrastructure rather than just a launch provider.
