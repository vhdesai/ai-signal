# NextEra to acquire Dominion Energy for ~$67B "to power the AI grid"

NextEra's planned ~$67B acquisition of Dominion Energy would be the largest U.S. utility merger in history and is being explicitly framed as an AI-data-center power play. The deal underscores how power generation and transmission are now strategic AI-infrastructure assets in their own right.
