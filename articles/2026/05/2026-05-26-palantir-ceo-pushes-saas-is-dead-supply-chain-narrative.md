# Palantir CEO pushes "SaaS is dead" supply-chain narrative

Palantir CEO Alex Karp publicly argued that "SaaS is dead" in the supply-chain context, positioning Palantir's ontology-based Foundry/AIP stack as the post-SaaS layer for enterprise AI decision-making. The framing is consistent with Palantir's recent commercial push and ongoing valuation debate. Critics note the rhetoric runs ahead of reported revenue mix. Source: TheStreet / Forbes (May 26, 2026)
