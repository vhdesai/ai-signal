# Andrej Karpathy leaves independent work to join Anthropic's pretraining team

OpenAI co-founder and former Tesla AI head Andrej Karpathy announced on X he is joining Anthropic to lead a new team applying Claude to accelerate pretraining research itself — an explicit "AI-builds-AI" bet — reporting to Pretraining lead Nicholas Joseph. The announcement landed on the same morning as Google I/O, making this one of the busiest 24-hour periods in AI talent history. The move is widely interpreted as Anthropic's thesis that AI-assisted research, rather than raw compute scaling, is the path to staying competitive with OpenAI and Google.
