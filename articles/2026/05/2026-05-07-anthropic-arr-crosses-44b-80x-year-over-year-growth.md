# Anthropic ARR Crosses $44B — 80x Year-over-Year Growth

Anthropic disclosed Q1 2026 revenue grew 80x year-over-year, with annualized recurring revenue now above $44B — a figure few analysts had modeled. This followed the company's $200B Google Cloud contract and SpaceX Colossus 1 compute deal (220,000+ NVIDIA GPUs, 300MW). Google's planned investment in Anthropic of up to $40B, disclosed April 25, would make it one of the largest capital commitments in AI history if fully deployed.
