# OmniVoice Studio debuts as an open-source ElevenLabs alternative

An independent research team released OmniVoice Studio, an open-source text-to-speech and voice cloning platform that pitches itself as a self-hostable alternative to ElevenLabs. The toolkit ships with a UI for cloning, multi-language synthesis, and emotion controls aimed at content creators and small studios. Research Breakthroughs
