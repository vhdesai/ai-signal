# Google released Gemma 4 today under the Apache 2.0 open license, achieving 89.2% on the AIME mathematics benchmark —…

Google released Gemma 4 today under the Apache 2.0 open license, achieving 89.2% on the AIME mathematics benchmark — placing it third among all open models worldwide. The model is designed to run on consumer-grade GPUs, dramatically lowering the barrier for enterprise deployment. This is Google's most competitive open-weight release to date and signals a direct challenge to Meta's Llama series. Availability is immediate via Hugging Face and Google AI Studio.
