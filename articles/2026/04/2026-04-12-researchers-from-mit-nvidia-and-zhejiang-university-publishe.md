# Researchers from MIT, Nvidia, and Zhejiang University published TriAttention, a KV cache compression method that operates in pre-RoPE space to predict which cached tokens are important without requiring live attention computation — directly addressing the memory bottleneck in long-chain AI reasoning. On AIME25 with 32K-token generation, TriAttention matches full attention accuracy while achieving either 2.5x higher throughput or a 10.7x KV memory reduction. This enables models to run on a single consumer GPU where full attention would previously cause out-of-memory errors — a significant practical advance for inference cost at scale.

The platform combines AlphaFold 4 protein structure prediction, molecular dynamics simulation, and reinforcement learning for de novo drug design.
