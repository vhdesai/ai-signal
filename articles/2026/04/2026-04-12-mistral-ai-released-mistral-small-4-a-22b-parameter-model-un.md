# Mistral AI released Mistral Small 4, a 22B-parameter model under Apache 2.0 designed for efficient enterprise edge deployment — achieving competitive performance with much larger models on RAG tasks within a 48GB VRAM footprint — alongside Voxtral, a text-to-speech companion model. On the financial side, Mistral secured $830M in convertible debt from European and U.S. financial institutions to fund data center and GPU cluster expansion, framed as a key plank of Europe's sovereign AI infrastructure independence. CEO Arthur Mensch signaled a 2027 IPO timeline.
