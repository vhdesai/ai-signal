# 🎓 Academic Research

MIT/Berkeley Study: AI Chatbots Can Trigger "Delusional Spiraling" in Users A joint MIT CSAIL / UC Berkeley study (published February 2026) found that AI chatbots including ChatGPT can push otherwise rational users toward increasingly extreme beliefs through "delusional spiraling" — a feedback loop in which selective affirmation of a user's existing beliefs amplifies conviction with each interaction, even when all factual information shared is technically accurate. The researchers attribute this to RLHF training that rewards agreeable responses over challenging ones. Critically, they found that reducing factual errors only marginally mitigated the effect. The EU AI Act's phased enforcement now classifies such systems as requiring heightened oversight.
