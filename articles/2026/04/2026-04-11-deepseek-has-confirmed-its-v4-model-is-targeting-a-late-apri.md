# DeepSeek has confirmed its V4 model is targeting a late-April 2026 release and is being trained entirely on Huawei Ascend chips — a significant milestone dem…

DeepSeek has confirmed its V4 model is targeting a late-April 2026 release and is being trained entirely on Huawei Ascend chips — a significant milestone demonstrating China's growing ability to develop frontier AI without Nvidia hardware. The announcement carries geopolitical weight given ongoing U.S. export controls, signaling that Chinese AI labs may be achieving hardware independence faster than anticipated. Notably, the model was trained and runs entirely without Nvidia GPUs, further evidence of China's determination to build sovereign AI infrastructure.
