# Tennessee Governor Bill Lee signed SB 1580 (passed both chambers unanimously), prohibiting AI systems from…

Tennessee Governor Bill Lee signed SB 1580 (passed both chambers unanimously), prohibiting AI systems from misrepresenting themselves as qualified mental health professionals. Nebraska, Georgia, Idaho, and South Carolina are advancing chatbot disclosure, health insurance AI restriction, and child safety bills. California Governor Newsom issued an executive order (March 30) requiring AI companies to meet privacy and safety standards for state contracts — a direct counter to the Trump administration's rollback of federal AI protections. Globally, 72 countries now have AI policies; the EU AI Act's enforcement phase for high-risk systems arrives in August 2026.
