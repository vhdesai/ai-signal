# A paper published in Nature Machine Intelligence demonstrated that LLMs can construct knowledge graphs from scientific…

A paper published in Nature Machine Intelligence demonstrated that LLMs can construct knowledge graphs from scientific abstracts to surface novel research directions not yet noticed by human scientists. The study showed that semantic concept graphs with ML prediction models outperform automated keyword methods in identifying innovative interdisciplinary combinations—and that domain experts found the model's suggestions genuinely inspiring in qualitative validation interviews. The work has broad implications for AI-accelerated discovery across fields from materials science to drug development.
