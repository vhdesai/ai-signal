# Grok 4.3 Beta Goes Live for SuperGrok Heavy

xAI quietly launched Grok 4.3 beta on grok.com, iOS, and Android, restricted to the $300/month SuperGrok Heavy tier. New native capabilities include PDF, PowerPoint, and spreadsheet generation, plus video input and sharper reasoning. Grok Computer, xAI's autonomous desktop agent, is rolling out in parallel.
