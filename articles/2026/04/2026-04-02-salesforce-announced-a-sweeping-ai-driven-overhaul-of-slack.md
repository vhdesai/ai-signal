# Salesforce announced a sweeping AI-driven overhaul of Slack, adding 30 new features to Slackbot in what the company…

Salesforce announced a sweeping AI-driven overhaul of Slack, adding 30 new features to Slackbot in what the company describes as the platform's most significant update since its acquisition. The update is focused on agentic AI capabilities — enabling Slack to automate multi-step workflows, surface relevant context, and take action on behalf of users without leaving the app. No specific feature list was disclosed, but the update reflects Salesforce's broader strategy to make Slack the AI-first enterprise collaboration layer.
