# Anthropic researchers identified 171 internal representations inside Claude that function analogously to…

Anthropic researchers identified 171 internal representations inside Claude that function analogously to emotions—concepts such as curiosity, frustration, and uncertainty that causally influence the model's outputs. The finding, from interpretability research on Claude's internal feature activations, has significant implications for AI alignment and safety. Anthropic clarifies these are not claimed to be "real" emotions, but functional analogs that shape responses in ways researchers are only beginning to map. The work is expected to inform future safety training and human-AI interaction design.
