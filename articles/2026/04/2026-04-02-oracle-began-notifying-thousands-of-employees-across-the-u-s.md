# Oracle began notifying thousands of employees across the U.S., India, Canada, Mexico, and Uruguay of layoffs via…

Oracle began notifying thousands of employees across the U.S., India, Canada, Mexico, and Uruguay of layoffs via pre-dawn emails — with TD Cowen estimating 20,000–30,000 roles eliminated, roughly 18% of its 162,000-person workforce. The cuts are designed to free $8–10 billion in annual cash flow to fund Oracle's $50 billion AI data center capex commitment, including its $300 billion partnership with OpenAI as part of the Stargate initiative. The move comes despite record Q3 FY2026 revenue of $17.2 billion (up 22% YoY) and $553 billion in remaining performance obligations — reflecting the intense financial pressure of competing in AI cloud infrastructure.
