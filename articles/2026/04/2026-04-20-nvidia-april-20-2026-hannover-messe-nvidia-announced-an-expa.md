# NVIDIA • April 20, 2026 (Hannover Messe) NVIDIA announced an expanded partnership with Adobe and WPP to deploy…

NVIDIA • April 20, 2026 (Hannover Messe) NVIDIA announced an expanded partnership with Adobe and WPP to deploy generative and agentic AI across global marketing production. The collaboration pairs NVIDIA inference infrastructure with Adobe Firefly/Experience Cloud and WPP's Open operating system. Several Fortune 500 brands are cited as early adopters.
