# Salesforce announced a major Slackbot upgrade, adding 30 new AI features and transforming it into an autonomous work…

Salesforce announced a major Slackbot upgrade, adding 30 new AI features and transforming it into an autonomous work assistant. New capabilities include reusable AI skills, Model Context Protocol integration with external tools, desktop-spanning operation, CRM data automation, and proactive action suggestions. Salesforce is positioning Slack as the primary interface for enterprise AI workflows, reducing direct interaction with underlying applications.
