# Oracle announced layoffs of approximately 30,000 employees globally as it redirects billions toward AI infrastructure…

Oracle announced layoffs of approximately 30,000 employees globally as it redirects billions toward AI infrastructure and data center expansion to compete with Amazon and Microsoft in the AI cloud market. The restructuring is framed as a strategic pivot from legacy database software to AI-native cloud services. This represents one of the largest single-company workforce reductions of the current AI transformation era—a template that analysts warn other enterprise software incumbents may be forced to follow.
