# The DOJ filed notice of appeal against U.S

The DOJ filed notice of appeal against U.S. District Judge Rita Lin's March 26 ruling, which blocked the Pentagon's "supply chain risk" designation of Anthropic and barred enforcement of Trump's directive prohibiting federal agencies from using Claude. Judge Lin found the real motivation was Anthropic's public opposition to the DoD's safety waivers—not any genuine security risk—calling the government's actions "Orwellian." The legal battle sets a precedent for AI safety guardrails versus government procurement authority, with the case proceeding to the appellate level.
