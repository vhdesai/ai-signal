# Anthropic has terminated the ability to use Claude subscriptions through OpenClaw and several other third-party client…

Anthropic has terminated the ability to use Claude subscriptions through OpenClaw and several other third-party client tools, redirecting users to Claude.ai or the official API. The move tightens control over how Claude is accessed outside official channels and may reflect both revenue protection and safety governance concerns. Affected users are reporting immediate disruption to established workflows. The policy aligns with a broader trend of AI labs consolidating their consumer distribution channels.
