# The Thunderbird team released Thunderbolt, an open-source AI framework centered on user choice of AI model, complete data ownership, and elimination of vendor lock-in. The project addresses growing enterprise and individual concerns about AI platform dependency, providing a framework for deploying AI capabilities without data leaving user-controlled infrastructure. It represents a meaningful open-source response to consolidation among major AI providers.
