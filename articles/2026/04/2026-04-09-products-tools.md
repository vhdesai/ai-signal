# 🛠 Products & Tools

Amazon CEO: $15B AI Revenue, $200B Capex Plan, $20B Custom Chip Business Amazon CEO Andy Jassy disclosed that the company's AI-related revenue has crossed $15 billion and unveiled a $200 billion capital expenditure plan heavily weighted toward AI infrastructure. Jassy also revealed that Amazon's custom silicon business (Trainium/Inferentia chips) has become a $20 billion business unit independently, highlighting the strategic importance of vertical integration in the AI arms race.
