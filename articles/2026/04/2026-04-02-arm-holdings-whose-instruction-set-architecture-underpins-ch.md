# Arm Holdings — whose instruction set architecture underpins chips from Apple, Amazon, Nvidia, and nearly every mobile…

Arm Holdings — whose instruction set architecture underpins chips from Apple, Amazon, Nvidia, and nearly every mobile device — unveiled its first-ever production chip: a CPU designed to manage agentic AI workloads in data centers. Arm's CEO noted that agentic AI has quadrupled CPU demand, and management guides for $1 billion in chip revenue by 2028 and $15 billion by 2031. The chip is positioned as additive to the market, not a direct competitor to its customers. Volume production is expected later this year.
