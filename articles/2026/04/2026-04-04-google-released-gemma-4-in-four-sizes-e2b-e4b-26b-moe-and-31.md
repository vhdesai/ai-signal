# Google released Gemma 4 in four sizes (E2B, E4B, 26B MoE, and 31B Dense) under an Apache 2.0 license—the most…

Google released Gemma 4 in four sizes (E2B, E4B, 26B MoE, and 31B Dense) under an Apache 2.0 license—the most permissive terms for any Gemma release. Built from the same research stack as Gemini 3, the 31B model ranks #3 globally on the Arena AI text leaderboard, outcompeting models 20x its size. The family supports 140+ languages, multimodal inputs (text, image, audio), and is optimized for agentic workflows. The 400M cumulative download milestone underscores the enormous developer ecosystem around Gemma. NVIDIA simultaneously announced Gemma 4 acceleration for local agentic AI via its RTX Spark platform.
