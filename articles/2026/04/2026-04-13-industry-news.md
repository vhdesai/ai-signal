# 🏢 Industry News

Elon Musk vs. OpenAI & Microsoft: $134B Fraud Trial Begins April 27 Jury selection for Musk v. OpenAI & Microsoft is set for April 27 in Oakland federal court. Musk is seeking up to $134 billion in "wrongful gains," arguing OpenAI defrauded him by converting from nonprofit to for-profit despite commitments at founding. He has also demanded the removal of CEO Sam Altman and President Greg Brockman. In parallel, Microsoft is threatening to sue OpenAI over a $50 billion AWS deal it views as a breach of their partnership agreement. A separate consumer antitrust class action targets the Microsoft-OpenAI partnership itself. OpenAI has called Musk's suit a "harassment campaign" and a "legal ambush."
