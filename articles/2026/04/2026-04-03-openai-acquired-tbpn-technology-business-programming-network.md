# OpenAI acquired TBPN (Technology Business Programming Network), a daily live tech talk show hosted by John Coogan and…

OpenAI acquired TBPN (Technology Business Programming Network), a daily live tech talk show hosted by John Coogan and Jordi Hays, on April 2 — the company's first media acquisition. TBPN averages ~70,000 viewers/episode and was on track for $30M+ in 2026 revenue. OpenAI will wind down the show's advertising model. TBPN will sit within OpenAI's Strategy org, reporting to Chief Global Affairs Officer Chris Lehane, with pledged editorial independence. OpenAI's Fidji Simo framed the deal as fulfilling OpenAI's responsibility to foster "constructive conversation around the changes AI creates." The acquisition raises media ethics questions given TBPN regularly covers OpenAI and competitors. Sam Altman on X: "TBPN is my favorite tech show. I don't expect them to go any easier on us."
