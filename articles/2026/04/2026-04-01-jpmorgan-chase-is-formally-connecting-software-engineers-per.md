# JPMorgan Chase is formally connecting software engineers' performance ratings to their usage of AI tools, signaling a…

JPMorgan Chase is formally connecting software engineers' performance ratings to their usage of AI tools, signaling a broader shift in how large enterprises are mandating AI adoption. Managers are now expected to evaluate developers not just on output quality but on demonstrated integration of AI-assisted workflows into daily work. The policy reflects growing confidence among corporate technology leaders that AI tooling delivers measurable productivity gains — and introduces new dynamics around workforce expectations, talent retention, and performance measurement in an AI-augmented environment.
