# Cerebras Systems is preparing for a Nasdaq listing (ticker: CBRS) targeting approximately $2 billion raise at a $22–25B…

Cerebras Systems is preparing for a Nasdaq listing (ticker: CBRS) targeting approximately $2 billion raise at a $22–25B valuation with Morgan Stanley as lead underwriter. Backed by a $10B compute deal with OpenAI, AWS partnership, and a $23B Series H round, Cerebras would be the first pure-play Nvidia alternative to go public during the AI infrastructure cycle.
