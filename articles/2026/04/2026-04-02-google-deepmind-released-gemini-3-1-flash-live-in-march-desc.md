# Google DeepMind released Gemini 3.1 Flash Live in March, described as making "audio AI more natural and reliable" with…

Google DeepMind released Gemini 3.1 Flash Live in March, described as making "audio AI more natural and reliable" with real-time multimodal voice capabilities. The model joins the Gemini 3.x family alongside Gemini 3.1 Pro (February, with a 2x reasoning improvement and 77.1% ARC-AGI-2 score) and Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite (March, for high-volume cost-efficient tasks). DeepMind also published a new research framework titled "Measuring Progress toward AGI: A Cognitive Framework," signaling continued investment in long-horizon capability evaluation.
