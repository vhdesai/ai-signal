# MIT researchers developed a new AI model that measures atomic-scale defects in materials — imperfections that can be…

MIT researchers developed a new AI model that measures atomic-scale defects in materials — imperfections that can be engineered to improve mechanical strength, heat transfer, and energy-conversion efficiency. Published March 30, the work has direct implications for semiconductor manufacturing, battery technology, and aerospace materials. By predicting how defects alter material behavior, engineers can design components with precisely tuned properties rather than relying on trial-and-error fabrication.
