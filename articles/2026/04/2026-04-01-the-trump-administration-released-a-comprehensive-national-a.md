# The Trump Administration released a comprehensive national AI policy framework in March 2026, outlining a roadmap for…

The Trump Administration released a comprehensive national AI policy framework in March 2026, outlining a roadmap for federal oversight while signaling intent to preempt a growing patchwork of state regulations. The framework includes the proposed "TRUMP AMERICA AI Act" and adopts a "hybrid model" allowing limited state flexibility on specific applications. Industry groups have raised concerns that compliance-focused disclosure requirements may fall short of addressing measurable real-world AI safety outcomes, particularly in high-stakes domains such as financial services and healthcare. Further executive action and agency-level guidance are expected in the coming months.
