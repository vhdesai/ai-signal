# Despite its record primary fundraise, OpenAI shares have effectively stalled on secondary markets, with roughly $600…

Despite its record primary fundraise, OpenAI shares have effectively stalled on secondary markets, with roughly $600 million in shares finding no buyers. Meanwhile, secondary marketplaces report record demand for Anthropic equity — valued at approximately $380 billion — with buyers signaling $2 billion in ready capital. Investors cite a more favorable risk-reward profile for Anthropic, whose valuation is seen as having more room to appreciate relative to OpenAI's lofty $852 billion mark. Goldman Sachs and Morgan Stanley have begun waiving carry fees on OpenAI shares for wealth clients, a notable concession.
