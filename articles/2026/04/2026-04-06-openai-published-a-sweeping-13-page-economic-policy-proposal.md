# OpenAI published a sweeping 13-page economic policy proposal advocating for robot and AI automation taxes on corporations, the creation of a publicly owned A…

OpenAI published a sweeping 13-page economic policy proposal advocating for robot and AI automation taxes on corporations, the creation of a publicly owned AI wealth fund to distribute AI productivity gains broadly, and encouragement for companies to pilot four-day workweeks as AI absorbs routine labor. The document represents OpenAI's most explicit foray into economic and labor policy, positioning the company as a proactive stakeholder in mitigating AI's societal disruptions rather than merely a technology provider.
