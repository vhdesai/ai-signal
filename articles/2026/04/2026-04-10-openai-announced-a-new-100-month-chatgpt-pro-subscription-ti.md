# OpenAI announced a new $100/month ChatGPT Pro subscription tier on April 9, bridging the previous gap between its…

OpenAI announced a new $100/month ChatGPT Pro subscription tier on April 9, bridging the previous gap between its $20/month Plus and $200/month Pro plans. The tier is anchored around Codex — OpenAI's AI coding agent — offering 5× more usage than Plus (with a promotional 10× through May 31). OpenAI says Codex now has over 3 million weekly active users, up 5× in three months with 70%+ month-over-month growth. The move is explicitly positioned against Anthropic's Claude Code, which has a comparable $100/month tier. OpenAI simultaneously rebalanced daily Codex caps for Plus subscribers to support more distributed usage.
