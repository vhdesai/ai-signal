# Microsoft introduced Copilot upgrades enabling multiple AI models — including OpenAI's GPT and Anthropic's Claude — to…

Microsoft introduced Copilot upgrades enabling multiple AI models — including OpenAI's GPT and Anthropic's Claude — to collaborate within a single workflow. The new Critique feature routes one model's output to a second model for accuracy review, while Model Council enables side-by-side comparisons. The company is also expanding access to Copilot Cowork, an agentic automation tool. The updates are designed to reduce hallucinations and improve output quality in competitive response to rival AI platforms.
