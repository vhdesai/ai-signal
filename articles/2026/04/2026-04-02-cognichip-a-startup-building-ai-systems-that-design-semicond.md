# Cognichip, a startup building AI systems that design semiconductor chips, raised $60 million in new funding

Cognichip, a startup building AI systems that design semiconductor chips, raised $60 million in new funding. The company's technology aims to compress the chip design cycle — typically a multi-year, highly manual process — using generative AI and reinforcement learning to automate layout, verification, and optimization. As the demand for custom AI silicon intensifies across hyperscalers, Cognichip is betting that AI-designed chips will be necessary to keep pace with compute requirements.
