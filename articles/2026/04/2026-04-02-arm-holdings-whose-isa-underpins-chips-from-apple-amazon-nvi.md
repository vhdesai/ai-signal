# Arm Holdings — whose ISA underpins chips from Apple, Amazon, Nvidia, and virtually every mobile device — unveiled its first-ever production chip: a CPU for agentic AI data center workloads. Arm's CEO notes agentic AI has quadrupled CPU demand. Guides $1B chip revenue by 2028, $15B by 2031. Volume production later this year. This marks a fundamental shift — Arm entering the market alongside its own customers for the first time.
