# Anthropic formally confirmed Claude Mythos Preview — first surfaced in a March data leak — as its most powerful model…

Anthropic formally confirmed Claude Mythos Preview — first surfaced in a March data leak — as its most powerful model to date, but has chosen not to release it publicly due to assessed cybersecurity risks. The model is being made available exclusively to Project Glasswing partners (see AI Safety section) for defensive security work. Anthropic says Mythos demonstrated the ability to identify "thousands of vulnerabilities" across every major operating system and web browser during internal testing. The company describes its capabilities as crossing "a threshold that fundamentally changes the urgency required to protect critical infrastructure."
