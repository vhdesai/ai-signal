# OpenAI GPT-5.5 ("Spud") rolling out to ChatGPT and Codex

GPT-5.5 (codename Spud) is now available to paid ChatGPT and Codex users, with reported improvements in context handling, coding, computer use, research workflows, cybersecurity, and token efficiency. The release is positioned as a step toward OpenAI's broader "AI super app" strategy and lands the same week as the company's new enterprise-focused fundraise.
