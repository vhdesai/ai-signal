# Microsoft launched its first-party MAI model suite — Transcribe-1 (speech-to-text rivaling Whisper Large v3), Voice-1 (conversational TTS), and Image-2 (image generation competitive with DALL-E 3) — all available via Azure AI Foundry and integrated into Copilot Studio. Microsoft described the MAI suite as reducing its dependency on OpenAI's API for consumer and enterprise features, while Microsoft Teams Copilot simultaneously received an update adding granular privacy controls for AI meeting recaps, multilingual transcription improvements, and real-time action-item extraction during live sessions.
