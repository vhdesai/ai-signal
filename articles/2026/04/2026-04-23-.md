# ✨

OpenAI shipped GPT-5.5 on April 23—six weeks after GPT-5.4—scoring 82.7% on Terminal-Bench 2.0 and 58.6% on SWE-Bench Pro, the strongest agentic coding results OpenAI has reported. The model advances context handling, computer use, and token efficiency and rolled out immediately to Plus, Pro, Business, and Enterprise tiers. UK's AI Safety Institute benchmarking noted GPT-5.5 matches Anthropic's restricted Mythos model on several cyber benchmarks—a comparison with national security implications.
