# Major AI labs are coordinating through the Frontier Model Forum to address the growing threat of unauthorized AI model…

Major AI labs are coordinating through the Frontier Model Forum to address the growing threat of unauthorized AI model distillation — where third parties reproduce proprietary model capabilities by training on their outputs. The coordinated response reflects shared concern that competitive advantages built on years of research investment can be rapidly eroded through distillation techniques. Policy and technical countermeasures are under active development across the consortium.
