# DeepSeek V4 Launches With 1M Context Window and Open Weights New

DeepSeek V4 entered preview via V4-Pro and V4-Flash variants, offering open weights, a 1-million-token context window, and claimed gains in coding and reasoning at significantly lower cost than U.S. frontier models. Early hands-on reports flagged concerns about real-world output quality relative to benchmark claims. The launch has accelerated orders for Huawei's Ascend 950PR processors, as the model was specifically optimized to run on domestic Chinese AI hardware, further eroding Nvidia's grip on China's AI infrastructure market. 🔬 2.
