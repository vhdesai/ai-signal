# Elon Musk and xAI held exploratory discussions with French AI startup Mistral and coding tool maker Cursor about a potential three-way collaboration, according to reporting sourced to insiders. The discussions reportedly centered on integrating Mistral's frontier model capabilities with Cursor's developer tooling and xAI/SpaceX infrastructure. A reported SpaceX option linked to a large acquisition figure adds strategic weight to the talks. The move signals a shift toward consolidation around model IP, compute, and developer tooling rather than purely organic model development.
