# The National Science Foundation has funded a new AI research institute at Carnegie Mellon University focused on…

The National Science Foundation has funded a new AI research institute at Carnegie Mellon University focused on harnessing AI for mathematical discovery. The institute aims to accelerate formal proof verification, conjecture generation, and symbolic reasoning — capabilities seen as foundational to next-generation AI reasoning systems. CMU's involvement spans the Computer Science and Mathematics departments, with collaborative ties to MIT and Princeton.
