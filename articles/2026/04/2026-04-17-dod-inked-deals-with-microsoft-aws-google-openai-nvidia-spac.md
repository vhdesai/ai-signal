# DOD inked deals with Microsoft, AWS, Google, OpenAI, NVIDIA, SpaceX, Oracle, and Reflection AI to deploy AI on the highest classification tiers, including support for targeting and combat decision-making. Anthropic was left out after a public dispute over Pentagon-requested removal of guardrails on autonomous weapons and mass surveillance — a federal judge blocked the administration's "supply-chain risk" designation in March, but Anthropic still got cut from this round. Over 1.3M DOD personnel already use the unclassified GenAI.mil platform.
