# Mistral AI secured $830M in its first-ever debt financing (BNP Paribas, HSBC, and five other banks — notably no U.S

Mistral AI secured $830M in its first-ever debt financing (BNP Paribas, HSBC, and five other banks — notably no U.S. banks) to purchase 13,800 Nvidia GB300 GPUs for a new data center in Bruyères-le-Châtel, south of Paris (44MW capacity, operational by Q2 2026). Mistral targets 200MW across Europe by end of 2027, with a second site in Sweden. CEO Arthur Mensch: "Europe needs an ambitious AI cloud infrastructure and an independent AI stack." The move positions Mistral as a sovereign AI infrastructure provider for European governments and enterprises seeking to avoid U.S. cloud dependency.
