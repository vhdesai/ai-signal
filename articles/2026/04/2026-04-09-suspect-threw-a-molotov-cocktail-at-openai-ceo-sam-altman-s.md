# suspect threw a Molotov cocktail at OpenAI CEO Sam Altman's San Francisco home at 3:45 AM on April 10; no one was injured and the suspect was arrested at OpenAI's headquarters shortly after. Altman responded with a personal blog post calling a recent Ronan Farrow / Andrew Marantz New Yorker investigative profile — headlined "Sam Altman May Control Our Future — Can He Be Trusted?" — "incendiary," and connecting rising anti-AI hostility to an increasingly charged media narrative. In the post, Altman acknowledged past mistakes but reaffirmed his mission around beneficial AI, while also referencing his upcoming trial with Elon Musk.
