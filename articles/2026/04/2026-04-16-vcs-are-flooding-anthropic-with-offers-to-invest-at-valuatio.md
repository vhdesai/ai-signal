# VCs are flooding Anthropic with offers to invest at valuations as high as $800 billion, per Bloomberg — more than…

VCs are flooding Anthropic with offers to invest at valuations as high as $800 billion, per Bloomberg — more than doubling its $380B mark from just two months ago. Anthropic's annualized revenue has surged from $1B at end of 2024 to $30B by early April 2026. The company has so far resisted the offers. Meanwhile, its Long-Term Benefit Trust took board majority via the appointment of former Novartis CEO Vas Narasimhan.
