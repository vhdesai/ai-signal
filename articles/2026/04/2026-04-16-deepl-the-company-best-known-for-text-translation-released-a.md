# DeepL, the company best known for text translation, released a real-time voice-to-voice translation suite covering…

DeepL, the company best known for text translation, released a real-time voice-to-voice translation suite covering meetings, mobile/web conversations, and frontline worker communication. An accompanying API lets developers build custom use cases such as call centers. CEO Jarek Kutylowski said the focus is on balancing latency reduction with translation quality.
