# Fox-owned Tubi shipped a @Tubi integration in the ChatGPT app store, letting users describe what they want to watch in…

Fox-owned Tubi shipped a @Tubi integration in the ChatGPT app store, letting users describe what they want to watch in natural language and get curated, watchable results from 300,000+ titles. Tubi's personalization system, trained on 1 billion+ monthly viewing hours from 100 million users, powers the recommendations.
