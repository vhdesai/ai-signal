# Florida Attorney General James Uthmeier announced a formal investigation into OpenAI following allegations that ChatGPT was used by the perpetrator of the Florida State University shooting. The probe marks the first U.S. state-level criminal referral involving a major AI company in connection with a mass shooting and sets a precedent for state-level AI accountability that other attorneys general are watching closely. OpenAI has stated it is cooperating with investigators.
