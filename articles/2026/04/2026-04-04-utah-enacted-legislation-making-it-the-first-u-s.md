# Utah enacted legislation making it the first U.S

Utah enacted legislation making it the first U.S. state to authorize AI systems to independently renew drug prescriptions, moving AI from diagnostic assistance to direct treatment decision-making. The initiative targets healthcare staffing shortages and rural access gaps. Supporters cite improved efficiency; critics raise concerns about AI reliability for complex medication regimens and the removal of human medical judgment from critical care decisions. The regulatory oversight framework for AI-driven prescription renewals is still being established.
