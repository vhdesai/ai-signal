# OpenAI GPT-5.5 ("Spud") — Strongest Agentic Coder to Date

OpenAI shipped GPT-5.5 six weeks after GPT-5.4, scoring 82.7% on Terminal-Bench 2.0 and 58.6% on SWE-Bench Pro — the strongest agentic coding performance the company has published to date. The model rolled out immediately to Plus, Pro, Business, and Enterprise users in ChatGPT and Codex. It advances OpenAI's broader "AI super app" strategy, integrating improved context handling, computer use, and cybersecurity capabilities.
