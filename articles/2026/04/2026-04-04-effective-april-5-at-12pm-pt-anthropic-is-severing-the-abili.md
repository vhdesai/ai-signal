# Effective April 5 at 12pm PT, Anthropic is severing the ability for Claude Pro and Max subscribers to use their plans…

Effective April 5 at 12pm PT, Anthropic is severing the ability for Claude Pro and Max subscribers to use their plans with third-party agentic tools such as OpenClaw. The company cited unsustainable compute strain from external tools that bypass Anthropic's prompt-cache optimizations. Users wanting to continue integrating Claude with third-party agents must shift to pay-as-you-go "extra usage" billing or the API. Anthropic's Head of Claude Code noted that first-party tools are built to maximize prompt cache efficiency—a capability most external harnesses do not replicate, making their usage economically untenable at scale. Claude Team and Enterprise plan holders have not been formally notified of similar restrictions.
