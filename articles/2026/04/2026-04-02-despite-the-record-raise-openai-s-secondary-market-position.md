# Despite the record raise, OpenAI's secondary market position is showing cracks

Despite the record raise, OpenAI's secondary market position is showing cracks. Bloomberg reports that institutional investors holding roughly $600 million of OpenAI shares cannot find buyers, while demand for Anthropic equity has surged to $2 billion ready to deploy. The valuation gap — OpenAI at $852B vs. Anthropic at $380B — is driving investors to Anthropic as the better risk-reward. Platforms like Augment and Hiive report record Anthropic demand. OpenAI warned separately that unauthorized SPV sales of its shares are void and may violate securities law.
