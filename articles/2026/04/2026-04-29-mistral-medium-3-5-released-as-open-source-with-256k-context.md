Mistral AI released Mistral Medium 3.5 on April 29 as an open-source model with a 256K-token context window, targeting the mid-tier enterprise segment that needs extended-context reasoning at lower cost than frontier closed-source alternatives. Mistral's continued open-source strategy — while Alibaba and other Chinese players close their weights — positions the French lab as the primary Western open-weight option for organizations requiring model transparency and self-hosting capability.
