# Stanford HAI 2026 AI Index continues to drive boardroom conversations

Stanford's 2026 AI Index — the year's most-cited independent measurement — remains a top reference this week as analysts use it to frame the Anthropic/OpenAI valuation race. Key data points: U.S.–China model-quality gap has compressed to 2.7%, SWE-bench Verified scores climbed from ~60% to nearly 100% in a year, global corporate AI investment hit $581.7B in 2025, and AI data-center capacity reached 29.6 GW — equivalent to powering New York State at peak demand.
