# Salesforce CEO Marc Benioff unveiled a comprehensive Slack overhaul featuring 30+ new AI capabilities, transforming…

Salesforce CEO Marc Benioff unveiled a comprehensive Slack overhaul featuring 30+ new AI capabilities, transforming Slackbot into an autonomous enterprise agent. Key features: reusable AI-skills (user-defined task templates triggered by natural language commands), MCP client integration connecting Slackbot to any external service or agent, meeting transcription and summarization, desktop activity monitoring, and deep Agentforce integration. The update directly competes with Microsoft Copilot and Teams AI for enterprise workflow automation mindshare.
