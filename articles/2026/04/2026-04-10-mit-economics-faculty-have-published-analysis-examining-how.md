# MIT Economics faculty have published analysis examining how AI tools are affecting knowledge worker productivity and…

MIT Economics faculty have published analysis examining how AI tools are affecting knowledge worker productivity and academic research workflows. The study finds measurable productivity gains in structured tasks (coding, writing, data analysis) but more nuanced effects in creative and exploratory research — where AI assistance can narrow the range of ideas explored. The findings contribute to ongoing academic debate about whether AI augments or constrains human intellectual range.
