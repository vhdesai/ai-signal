# Apple plans to send a significant chunk of its Siri team (fewer than 200 engineers) to a multi-week bootcamp to learn…

Apple plans to send a significant chunk of its Siri team (fewer than 200 engineers) to a multi-week bootcamp to learn AI-assisted coding, just two months before the company's expected major Siri revamp. The bootcamp news lands the same week Apple is rumored to be partnering with Google's Gemini to power the new Siri.
