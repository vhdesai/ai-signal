# Anthropic has begun requiring government ID verification (via Persona) before allowing subscriptions

Anthropic has begun requiring government ID verification (via Persona) before allowing subscriptions. The move went viral on X, with critics framing it as a competitive gift to ChatGPT and Gemini, neither of which requires identity verification. Combined with the Heppner ruling, the era of "AI as private confidant" faces new challenges.
