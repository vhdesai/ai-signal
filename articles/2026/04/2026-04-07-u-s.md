# U.S.

U.S. Treasury Secretary Scott Bessent and Federal Reserve Chair Jerome Powell convened an urgent closed-door meeting with major bank CEOs on April 10 to brief them on systemic cyber risks posed by Anthropic's Claude Mythos Preview model — which can autonomously discover and exploit zero-day vulnerabilities at scale. White House National Economic Council Director Kevin Hassett confirmed the briefing, and the IMF's Managing Director warned on CBS News that "the world currently lacks the capacity to protect the international monetary system against massive cyber risks." This is the first time a private AI model's capabilities have triggered a systemic risk summit at the highest levels of U.S. financial governance.
