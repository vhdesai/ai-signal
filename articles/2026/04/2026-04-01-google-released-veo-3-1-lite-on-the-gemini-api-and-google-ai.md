# Google released Veo 3.1 Lite on the Gemini API and Google AI Studio, pricing it at less than $0.08 per second for 1080p…

Google released Veo 3.1 Lite on the Gemini API and Google AI Studio, pricing it at less than $0.08 per second for 1080p video — under half the cost of its predecessor Veo 3.1 Fast, with equivalent generation speeds. The model supports both text-to-video and image-to-video in 720p/1080p at 16:9 and 9:16 ratios, with configurable 4-, 6-, and 8-second durations. The launch comes as OpenAI confirmed the Sora app will shut down April 26 after reportedly burning $15 million per day while generating only $2.1 million in lifetime revenue; Disney also terminated its planned $1 billion OpenAI partnership following the announcement. Google is further reducing Veo 3.1 Fast pricing on April 7, consolidating its position as the dominant AI video API provider.
