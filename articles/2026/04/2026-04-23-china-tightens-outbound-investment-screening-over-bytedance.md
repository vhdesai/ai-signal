# China tightens outbound-investment screening over ByteDance, Moonshot, StepFun stakes

Beijing is moving to restrict additional US investment into leading Chinese AI labs including ByteDance, Moonshot AI, and StepFun. The measures mirror US outbound-investment rules introduced last year. Expect follow-on implications for LP access, valuations, and secondary-market liquidity.
