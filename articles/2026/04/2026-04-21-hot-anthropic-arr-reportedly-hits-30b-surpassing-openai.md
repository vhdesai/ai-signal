# Hot Anthropic ARR Reportedly Hits $30B, Surpassing OpenAI

Anthropic has reportedly reached roughly $30B in annual recurring revenue versus OpenAI's $25B, capping 30x growth in 15 months. The surge is attributed to the April 16 release of Claude Opus 4.7, which tops most benchmarks despite a premium price point and is now live across Claude.ai, the API, Amazon Bedrock, Google Vertex AI, and Microsoft Foundry.
