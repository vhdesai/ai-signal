# Anthropic made its first major acquisition, purchasing stealth biotech AI startup Coefficient Bio in an all-stock deal…

Anthropic made its first major acquisition, purchasing stealth biotech AI startup Coefficient Bio in an all-stock deal valued at ~$400M—reportedly delivering a 38,513% IRR for early investor Dimension. Coefficient was founded eight months ago by former Genentech/Roche computational biologists Nathan Frey and Samuel Stanton, focused on AI for drug discovery, R&D planning, and regulatory strategy. The team joins Anthropic's healthcare division, which has been building integrations with PubMed, ClinicalTrials.gov, Medidata, and regulatory drafting platforms. The acquisition signals Anthropic's ambition to become a vertical AI platform in life sciences rather than just a general-purpose model provider.
