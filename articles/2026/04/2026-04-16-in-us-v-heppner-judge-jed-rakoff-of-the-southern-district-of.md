# In US v. Heppner, Judge Jed Rakoff of the Southern District of New York ruled that a defendant's conversations with…

In US v. Heppner, Judge Jed Rakoff of the Southern District of New York ruled that a defendant's conversations with Anthropic's Claude are not protected by attorney-client privilege or work-product doctrine, because an AI is not a lawyer and public AI platforms have no confidentiality obligation. More than a dozen major law firms have since issued client advisories warning against using AI chatbots for legal matters.
