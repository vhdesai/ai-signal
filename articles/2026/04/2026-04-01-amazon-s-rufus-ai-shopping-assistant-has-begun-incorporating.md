# Amazon's Rufus AI shopping assistant has begun incorporating "sponsored prompts" — an ad format embedded into AI-driven…

Amazon's Rufus AI shopping assistant has begun incorporating "sponsored prompts" — an ad format embedded into AI-driven product queries. Early data indicates sponsored prompts generate significantly lower traffic than traditional sponsored listings, but demonstrate stronger cost-efficiency metrics for advertisers. The format signals a broader industry shift toward monetizing AI assistants through native conversational advertising rather than traditional keyword-based models.
