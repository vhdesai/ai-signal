# DOD framing — "an architecture that prevents AI vendor lock-in and ensures long-term flexibility for the Joint Force" — formalizes multi-vendor sourcing as policy. Likely to be mirrored by allied procurement frameworks (UK, Australia, NATO) and accelerate sovereign-AI tendering globally.
