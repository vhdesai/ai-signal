# A Northern District of California ruling found that when a social media platform's AI exercises "ultimate authority"…

A Northern District of California ruling found that when a social media platform's AI exercises "ultimate authority" over assembled ad content, the platform may be considered a "maker" of fraudulent statements under Rule 10b-5. This creates new securities fraud exposure for Meta, Alphabet, Snap, TikTok, and X Corp.
