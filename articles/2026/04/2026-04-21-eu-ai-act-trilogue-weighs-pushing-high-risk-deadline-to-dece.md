# EU AI Act Trilogue Weighs Pushing High-Risk Deadline to December 2027

The European Parliament voted 569 in favor of a Digital Omnibus proposal to delay full enforcement of high-risk AI obligations from August 2, 2026 to December 2, 2027. The Council approved its negotiating mandate on March 13; trilogue negotiations are ongoing, but prohibited-practice and GPAI obligations remain enforceable today.
