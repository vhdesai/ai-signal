# Alibaba shipped four Qwen3.6 variants in two weeks, including the 27B open-weight reasoner (GPQA 87.8, SWE-bench 77.2) and Qwen3.6-Max-Preview. The cadence cements Alibaba as the most prolific open-weight frontier shipper of the quarter.

Open-weight competition intensified: GLM-5.1 (Z.ai) briefly held the #1 SWE-bench Pro spot — the first open model ever to do so. Meta Muse Spark debuted as Meta's first proprietary model. Tencent Hy3 Preview (295B/21B MoE) launched free. Mistral Medium 3.5 (128B, 256K context) shipped April 29 at $1.50/$7.50.
