# The most important AI developments across industry, research, and policy

Today's big picture: April 23, 2026 finds AI at a genuine inflection point — not just in capability, but in accountability. Google dominated headlines at Cloud Next with next-gen TPU chips and an ambitious enterprise agent ecosystem, while OpenAI quietly released its most capable image generation model and launched Workspace Agents. The day's defining tension, however, belongs to AI security: Anthropic's restricted Mythos model has leaked to unauthorized parties, OpenAI is briefing Five Eyes allies on a rival cyber model, and Mozilla confirmed Mythos found 271 zero-day vulnerabilities in Firefox. Meanwhile, Alibaba's Qwen3.6-27B is shaking up the open-weight landscape, and Jeff Bezos is raising $10B for a Physical AI venture. It is, by any measure, a consequential 24 hours.
