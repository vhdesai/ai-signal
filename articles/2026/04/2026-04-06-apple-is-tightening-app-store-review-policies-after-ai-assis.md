# Apple is tightening App Store review policies after AI-assisted "vibe coding" tools drove an 84% spike in new app…

Apple is tightening App Store review policies after AI-assisted "vibe coding" tools drove an 84% spike in new app submissions, many of low quality or with duplicated functionality. The company is applying stricter standards around originality and functionality thresholds. The surge reflects the downstream impact of tools like Cursor and Replit enabling rapid, low-code app generation. Apple's action raises questions about how app marketplaces will govern AI-generated software at scale.
