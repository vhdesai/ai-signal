# 🚀 Model Releases

OpenAI Launches GPT-5.4-Cyber — A Frontier Model Built for Defense OpenAI unveiled GPT-5.4-Cyber, a fine-tuned variant of GPT-5.4 specifically optimized for defensive cybersecurity work, with deliberately relaxed guardrails for security-relevant tasks. The model is being rolled out on a restricted basis to vetted vendors, researchers, and government teams through an expanded Trusted Access for Cyber (TAC) program. OpenAI's Codex Security agent, powered by the model, has already contributed to fixing over 3,000 critical and high-severity vulnerabilities. The release is widely read as a direct competitive response to Anthropic's Claude Mythos Preview, announced April 7.
