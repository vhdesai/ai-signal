# As of yesterday, Microsoft removed free Copilot Chat access from Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and OneNote for enterprise…

As of yesterday, Microsoft removed free Copilot Chat access from Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and OneNote for enterprise users (2,000+ seats) without a paid Copilot license ($30/user/month). Smaller organizations get throttled "standard access." Only about 3% of M365 customers pay for Copilot. New labels distinguish "Copilot Chat (Basic)" from "M365 Copilot (Premium)."
