# CoreWeave has signed a multiyear deal with Anthropic covering a variety of Nvidia chips across data centers in the US

CoreWeave has signed a multiyear deal with Anthropic covering a variety of Nvidia chips across data centers in the US. CoreWeave now operates 43 active data centers and continues to expand as a key AI compute infrastructure provider. The deal underscores ongoing demand for purpose-built AI infrastructure as frontier labs scale model training and inference at record pace. Earlier reporting noted CoreWeave had also secured a separate $21B deal with Meta.
