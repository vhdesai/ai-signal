# The U.S. Court of Appeals for the D.C. Circuit denied Anthropic's emergency motion to block the Department of Defense from placing it on a supply chain risk blacklist, in a ruling that conflicts with a separate California district court decision favoring Anthropic. The split creates significant legal uncertainty around government AI procurement and national security designations. The case is expected to escalate to the Supreme Court and will have sweeping implications for all AI vendors pursuing federal contracts.
