# Florida Attorney General James Uthmeier announced a formal investigation into OpenAI and ChatGPT, alleging the suspect in the April 2025 FSU campus shooting (which killed two people) had 200+ ChatGPT messages including queries about mass shooting logistics before carrying out the attack. Subpoenas are described as "forthcoming," with additional concerns cited including ChatGPT's alleged role in CSAM generation, self-harm encouragement, and potential national security risks tied to China. This is the most prominent state-level enforcement action against an AI company related to real-world harm to date.
