# Cornell and Purdue publish work on energy-efficient attention

A Cornell–Purdue team proposed a sparse attention variant that reduces inference energy by ~30% at comparable quality on long-context tasks. The approach targets data-center operators grappling with grid constraints. Implementations for open-weight models are promised within weeks.
