# The Financial Times reports that some of OpenAI's own investors are openly questioning whether the $852B post-money…

The Financial Times reports that some of OpenAI's own investors are openly questioning whether the $852B post-money valuation can hold as the company pivots to enterprise. One dual-backer called Anthropic's current mark "the relative bargain." SoftBank is inviting more banks to join its $40B loan facility backing the OpenAI investment. US late-stage venture funds have raised a record $23.6B this year, almost all earmarked for AI.
