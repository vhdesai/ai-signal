# Yupp AI, an Andreessen Horowitz-backed platform that aggregated responses from over 500 generative AI models —…

Yupp AI, an Andreessen Horowitz-backed platform that aggregated responses from over 500 generative AI models — including ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Mistral — has shut down operations. The platform had launched publicly in June 2025 and offered users a unique model-comparison interface where they could earn credits by rating AI responses. Its closure highlights the difficulty of sustaining multi-model aggregation businesses as frontier labs tighten API pricing, consolidate user engagement within their own platforms, and reduce the differentiation window for middleware players.
