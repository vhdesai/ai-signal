# Musk Explores Three-Way Alliance of xAI, Mistral & Cursor to Challenge Anthropic in AI Coding Race Trending

Elon Musk's xAI held discussions with both French AI startup Mistral and leading AI coding tool Cursor about a potential three-way partnership, with SpaceX (which owns xAI) announcing a deal giving it an option to acquire Cursor for $60 billion. xAI president Michael Nicolls stated publicly this month the company is "clearly behind" Anthropic and OpenAI in AI coding and agentic services. A Mistral co-founder, Devendra Chaplot, joined xAI in March to lead pretraining. If formalized, the alliance would combine a frontier model (xAI's Grok), an open-source European AI system (Mistral), and a dominant AI coding editor (Cursor) into a single competitive stack — directly challenging Anthropic's Claude-powered coding dominance.
