# Stanford HAI 2026 AI Index — US-China Gap Nearly Closed, AI Incidents Up 55% YoY, Adoption at 88%

Stanford's ninth annual AI Index Report delivers a sweeping portrait of accelerating capability and lagging governance. Key findings: SWE-bench coding scores jumped from 60% to nearly 100% in one year; organizational AI adoption reached 88%; generative AI hit 53% population penetration faster than the PC or internet. The US-China model gap has effectively closed — Anthropic's top model leads the best Chinese model by just 2.7% as of March 2026. Documented AI incidents rose from 233 to 362 year-over-year. Global corporate AI investment hit $581.7B in 2025, up 130%. AI data center power capacity reached 29.6 GW — equivalent to New York State at peak load.
