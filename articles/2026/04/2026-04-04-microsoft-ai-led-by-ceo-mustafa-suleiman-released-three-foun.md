# Microsoft AI, led by CEO Mustafa Suleiman, released three foundational models under its MAI brand—the first major…

Microsoft AI, led by CEO Mustafa Suleiman, released three foundational models under its MAI brand—the first major output from the MAI Superintelligence team formed in November 2025. MAI-Transcribe-1 claims the #1 global FLEURS Word Error Rate benchmark for speech-to-text, supporting 25 languages at 2.5x the speed of Azure Fast. MAI-Voice-1 generates 60 seconds of audio per second with custom voice cloning. MAI-Image-2 is an image/video generation model available on Microsoft Foundry. All three are priced below comparable Google and OpenAI offerings. This marks the clearest signal yet that Microsoft is building a parallel model stack independent of its $13B OpenAI partnership.
