# Google announced that AI Overviews — its AI-generated search summaries — are coming to Gmail for Google Workspace users, enabling AI-powered email intelligence and summarization directly in the inbox. Google also unveiled AI-enhanced Chrome for enterprise users, positioning Chrome as an "AI co-worker" that assists with web-based tasks. These moves extend Google's AI integration deep into the knowledge worker workflow beyond its core search and cloud products.
