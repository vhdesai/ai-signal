# The Verge reports that on April 7th — the same day Anthropic publicly announced its restricted Mythos model — unauthorized users gained access through a third-party contractor's environment, ultimately reaching a Discord group. Mythos is a frontier cybersecurity model capable of autonomously identifying and exploiting vulnerabilities across major operating systems and browsers, and was explicitly intended for access only by a short list of approved tech companies. Anthropic stated there is no evidence the breach extended beyond the vendor environment, but the incident raises serious questions about third-party access controls for restricted frontier AI systems.
