# Anthropic has launched an internal investigation after reports emerged that unauthorized users gained access to its unreleased Claude Mythos model through a third-party environment. Mythos is a cybersecurity-focused system designed to detect and analyze software vulnerabilities, and its release has been restricted due to potential misuse risks. The incident underscores the growing challenge of securing pre-release frontier AI systems — particularly those classified as high-risk applications.
