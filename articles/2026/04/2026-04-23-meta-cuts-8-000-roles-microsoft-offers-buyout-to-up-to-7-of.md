# Meta cuts ~8,000 roles; Microsoft offers buyout to up to 7% of US staff

Meta began notifying roughly 8,000 employees (~10% of workforce) of role eliminations effective May 20, citing a shift to AI-native org design. Microsoft separately opened a voluntary buyout window for up to 7% of US employees. Both moves are framed internally as productivity reallocation toward AI-priority workstreams.
