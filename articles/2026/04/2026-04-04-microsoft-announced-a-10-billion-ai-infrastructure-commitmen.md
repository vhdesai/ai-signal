# Microsoft announced a $10 billion AI infrastructure commitment to Japan in partnership with SoftBank and Sakura Internet

Microsoft announced a $10 billion AI infrastructure commitment to Japan in partnership with SoftBank and Sakura Internet. The investment is part of a global wave of AI sovereign infrastructure spending, as Microsoft's Azure AI revenue grows 39% year-over-year toward a $25B fiscal 2026 target. The company spent $37.5 billion on AI capital expenditures in a single quarter. Simultaneously, Microsoft's UK business software operations are facing a Competition and Markets Authority probe into whether it should receive "strategic market status" for special competition requirements.
