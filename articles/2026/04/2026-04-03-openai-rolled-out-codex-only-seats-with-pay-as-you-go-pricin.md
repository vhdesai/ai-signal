# OpenAI rolled out Codex-only seats with pay-as-you-go pricing in ChatGPT Business and Enterprise — no fixed seat fee,…

OpenAI rolled out Codex-only seats with pay-as-you-go pricing in ChatGPT Business and Enterprise — no fixed seat fee, billed on token consumption. Business seat pricing was cut from $25 to $20/seat/year. Codex usage within Business/Enterprise has grown 6x since January. Teams at Notion, Ramp, Braintrust, and Wasmer are active users. New Codex-only seats receive up to $500 in credits. The move is a direct competitive play against GitHub Copilot and Cursor.
