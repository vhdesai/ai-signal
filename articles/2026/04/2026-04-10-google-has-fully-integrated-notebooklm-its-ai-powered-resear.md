# Google has fully integrated NotebookLM, its AI-powered research assistant, into the Gemini chatbot interface — allowing…

Google has fully integrated NotebookLM, its AI-powered research assistant, into the Gemini chatbot interface — allowing users to build research notebooks without switching applications. The enhanced NotebookLM can ingest PDFs, documents, URLs, YouTube videos, and text directly through Gemini's side panel, generating study guides, infographics, and audio/video overviews. Rolling out to Google AI Ultra, Pro, and Plus subscribers on web, with mobile and free-tier access coming in subsequent weeks.
