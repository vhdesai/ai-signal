# DeepSeek previews V4 family: 1.6T-param Pro and 1M-token Flash

DeepSeek unveiled V4 Pro, a 1.6T-parameter mixture-of-experts model, and V4 Flash, a smaller model with a 1M-token context window targeting long-document enterprise workloads. The release continues the pattern of Chinese labs closing the frontier gap at dramatically lower training costs. Weights are expected to follow DeepSeek’s prior open-weight pattern later this quarter.
