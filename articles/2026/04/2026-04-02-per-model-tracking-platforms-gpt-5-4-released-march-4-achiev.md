# Per model tracking platforms, GPT-5.4 (released March 4) achieves 0.9 GPQA; Mistral Small 4 (March 15) is open source…

Per model tracking platforms, GPT-5.4 (released March 4) achieves 0.9 GPQA; Mistral Small 4 (March 15) is open source at 0.7 GPQA; Nvidia's Nemotron 3 Super 120B (March 10) hits 0.8 GPQA with open-source weights. Claude Sonnet 4.6 (February 17) offers near-Opus performance with Agent Teams support (orchestrating 2–16 instances) at 80.8% SWE-bench Verified. Zhipu AI's GLM-5 (February 11) — trained entirely on Huawei Ascend chips without Nvidia — achieved a #1 HLE score of 50.4% and a 1.2% hallucination rate, at 136x lower cost than Claude Opus 4.5.
