# More than 30 OpenAI and Google DeepMind employees — including DeepMind Chief Scientist Jeff Dean — filed an amicus…

More than 30 OpenAI and Google DeepMind employees — including DeepMind Chief Scientist Jeff Dean — filed an amicus brief supporting Anthropic's lawsuit against the U.S. Department of Defense. The Pentagon designated Anthropic a "supply chain risk to national security" after the lab refused to allow its models for domestic mass surveillance or autonomous lethal targeting. President Trump ordered all federal agencies to cease using Anthropic's technology. The DOD simultaneously signed a contract with OpenAI — drawing protests from its own employees. Nearly 900 combined Google/OpenAI staff signed a separate solidarity letter. Anthropic filed two lawsuits. The case is expected to set major precedent for how government engages with commercial AI labs on safety terms and deployment restrictions.
