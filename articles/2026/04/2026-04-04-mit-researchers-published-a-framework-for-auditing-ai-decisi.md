# MIT researchers published a framework for auditing AI decision-support systems for fairness and equity, identifying…

MIT researchers published a framework for auditing AI decision-support systems for fairness and equity, identifying situations where autonomous systems may treat communities differently based on demographic factors. The framework provides policymakers and developers with a structured methodology for pre-deployment evaluation in high-stakes contexts including criminal justice, lending, healthcare, and social services—areas where documented AI bias has generated significant regulatory and legal scrutiny globally.
