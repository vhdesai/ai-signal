# OpenAI completed a $122 billion fundraise, the largest single raise in corporate history, at a $852 billion post-money…

OpenAI completed a $122 billion fundraise, the largest single raise in corporate history, at a $852 billion post-money valuation. Ironically, secondary market demand for OpenAI shares has simultaneously collapsed—institutional investors cannot offload $600M in shares—as investors pivot to Anthropic (valued at $380B), betting on better risk-reward from the valuation gap. Secondary market buyers reportedly have $2 billion ready to deploy into Anthropic. The divergence between OpenAI's primary and secondary market dynamics reflects growing uncertainty about near-term returns at its current valuation.
