# Anthropic has introduced Claude Mythos 5, its most powerful model to date, featuring a reported 10 trillion parameters…

Anthropic has introduced Claude Mythos 5, its most powerful model to date, featuring a reported 10 trillion parameters with particular strengths in cybersecurity, complex coding, and academic reasoning. The model is designed for large enterprise deployments requiring proactive, sophisticated AI capabilities. Alongside it, Anthropic released Capabara, a mid-sized model aimed at smaller businesses and individual developers, signaling the company's strategy of serving both the high-end enterprise and the broader developer market simultaneously.
