# MIT's Computer Science and AI Laboratory published findings characterizing AI's workforce impact as a "rising tide"…

MIT's Computer Science and AI Laboratory published findings characterizing AI's workforce impact as a "rising tide" rather than a "crashing wave"—the technology reshapes task composition rather than eliminating jobs en masse. Researchers specifically caution that cutting entry-level hiring due to AI could be a long-term strategic mistake, as these roles develop organizational knowledge that makes senior workers effective. The study adds empirical rigor to policy debates over AI and labor markets, challenging both techno-optimist and techno-pessimist narratives.
