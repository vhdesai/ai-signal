# Georgia Tech and UT Austin release open benchmark for multi-agent coordination

Researchers at Georgia Tech and UT Austin published MA-Bench, an evaluation suite for multi-agent LLM coordination across logistics, negotiation, and code-review tasks. Early runs show frontier models plateau at about 55% on non-trivial coordination scenarios. The benchmark is meant to become a standard alongside SWE-bench and Terminal-Bench.
