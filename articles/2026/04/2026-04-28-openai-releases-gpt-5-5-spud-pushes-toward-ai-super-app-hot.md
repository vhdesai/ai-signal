# OpenAI Releases GPT-5.5 "Spud," Pushes Toward AI Super App Hot

OpenAI released GPT-5.5 (internally codenamed "Spud") to paid ChatGPT and Codex plan users, advancing context handling, coding ability, computer use, research workflows, and token efficiency. The release is part of OpenAI's broader strategy to evolve ChatGPT into a comprehensive AI "super app." The new model also improves cybersecurity analysis capabilities. GPT-5.5 is available on ChatGPT Plus, Pro, Business, and Enterprise plans.
