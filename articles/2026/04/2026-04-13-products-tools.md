# 🛠 Products & Tools

Palantir AIP Analyst Now Generally Available — Agentic Workflows for Enterprise Data Palantir's AIP Analyst, announced March 31, reached general availability the week of April 13. The interface allows users to query ontology-linked enterprise data in natural language, with the system autonomously searching, transforming, and summarizing information, then generating visualizations. Palantir, which reported Q4 2025 revenue of $1.406 billion (up 70% YoY) and U.S. commercial revenue of $507 million (up 137% YoY), is positioning AIP Analyst as its primary entry point for agentic enterprise AI workflows. Deployments are expanding well beyond pilot programs across defense, healthcare, and financial services.
