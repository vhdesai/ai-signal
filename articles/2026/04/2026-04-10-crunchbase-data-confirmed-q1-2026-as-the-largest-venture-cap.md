# Crunchbase data confirmed Q1 2026 as the largest venture capital quarter in history, with $242B flowing into AI…

Crunchbase data confirmed Q1 2026 as the largest venture capital quarter in history, with $242B flowing into AI companies — representing 80% of all global VC investment in the period. Just four companies — OpenAI, Anthropic, xAI, and Waymo — captured 65% of that total. The concentration signals that institutional capital is consolidating around a small number of frontier AI operators, raising barriers to entry for emerging challengers.
