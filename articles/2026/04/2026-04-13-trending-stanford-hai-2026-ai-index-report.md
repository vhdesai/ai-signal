# Trending Stanford HAI 2026 AI Index Report

Stanford's 2026 AI Index documents a "jagged frontier": top models now match PhD-level science and math and push SWE-bench Verified toward 100%, while still failing basics like reading an analog clock (50.1%). The U.S.–China performance gap has collapsed to ~2.7 points, and corporate AI investment hit $581.7B globally in 2025.
