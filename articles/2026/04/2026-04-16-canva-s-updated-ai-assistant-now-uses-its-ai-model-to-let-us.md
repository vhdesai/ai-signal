# Canva's updated AI assistant now uses its AI model to let users create editable, layered designs from text prompts

Canva's updated AI assistant now uses its AI model to let users create editable, layered designs from text prompts. The assistant autonomously calls the required design tools and generates multiple options. The update reflects Canva's strategy of making its AI assistant central to user workflows rather than a sidebar feature.
