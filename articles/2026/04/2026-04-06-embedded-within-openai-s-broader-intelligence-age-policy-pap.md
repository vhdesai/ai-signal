# Embedded within OpenAI's broader intelligence-age policy paper is a call for an international governance body —…

Embedded within OpenAI's broader intelligence-age policy paper is a call for an international governance body — analogous to the IAEA for nuclear technology — to oversee the development and deployment of AGI-class systems. The proposal includes mandatory capability reporting thresholds and cross-border compute monitoring. This is OpenAI's strongest public endorsement of multilateral AI governance and stands in notable contrast to its competitors' generally quieter posture on international frameworks.
