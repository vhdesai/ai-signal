# OpenAI released GPT-5.4 on April 3, described as shifting AI from "answering" to "operating" with enhanced agentic task…

OpenAI released GPT-5.4 on April 3, described as shifting AI from "answering" to "operating" with enhanced agentic task execution. ChatGPT Business launched simultaneously at $25/user/month for standard seats and usage-based Codex seats at no fixed cost. OpenAI is offering up to $500 in credits to new business and enterprise users as it accelerates enterprise lock-in ahead of its anticipated IPO. Codex is increasingly the foundation of OpenAI's product strategy, with the Codex App now its most-used surface globally.
