# Global venture investment reached $297 billion in Q1 2026, up 150% year-over-year and representing the highest…

Global venture investment reached $297 billion in Q1 2026, up 150% year-over-year and representing the highest quarterly total ever recorded. AI companies captured $239 billion — 81% of the total — driven by mega-rounds from OpenAI ($122B), Anthropic ($30B), xAI ($20B), and Waymo ($16B). Four of the five largest venture rounds in history were closed in this single quarter. The Crunchbase Unicorn Board added $900 billion in valuation during the period, also a quarterly record.
