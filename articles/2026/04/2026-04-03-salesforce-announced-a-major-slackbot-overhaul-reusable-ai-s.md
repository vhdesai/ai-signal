# Salesforce announced a major Slackbot overhaul: reusable AI skills, Model Context Protocol integration, desktop-wide…

Salesforce announced a major Slackbot overhaul: reusable AI skills, Model Context Protocol integration, desktop-wide operation, CRM data management, meeting summarization, and proactive action suggestions. The update positions Slack as a single autonomous interface for enterprise work, reducing reliance on underlying apps and challenging Microsoft Copilot as the primary AI productivity layer in the workplace.
