# Governor Gavin Newsom signed an executive order on March 30 requiring AI vendors to demonstrate responsible policies,…

Governor Gavin Newsom signed an executive order on March 30 requiring AI vendors to demonstrate responsible policies, robust privacy protections, and rigorous security standards before winning California state contracts. The order also expands state use of generative AI for citizen services, including a new AI-powered tool to help Californians navigate government programs by life event. The move explicitly positions California in contrast with the Trump administration's approach and sets a potential national benchmark for state-level AI procurement standards.
