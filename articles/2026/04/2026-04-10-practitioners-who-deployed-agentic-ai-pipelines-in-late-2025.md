# Practitioners who deployed agentic AI pipelines in late 2025 and Q1 2026 are now surfacing real-world failure patterns…

Practitioners who deployed agentic AI pipelines in late 2025 and Q1 2026 are now surfacing real-world failure patterns beyond controlled testing scenarios. Extended production runtime is revealing breakdowns related to tool-call errors, context drift, and coordination failures in multi-agent workflows — issues that were not apparent in benchmark evaluations. The findings are prompting enterprise teams to invest in observability and human-in-the-loop checkpoints for production AI agents.
