# Nothing ships AI-native dictation tool across its phones

Nothing launched an on-device dictation feature powered by a small speech-to-text model with live formatting and summarization. The rollout covers Nothing Phone 3 and 2a in select markets. The company positioned it as a privacy-forward alternative to cloud-based transcription.
