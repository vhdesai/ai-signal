# OpenAI's COO shifted into a new role, and Fidji Simo—CEO of OpenAI's AGI division—is taking medical leave, joined by…

OpenAI's COO shifted into a new role, and Fidji Simo—CEO of OpenAI's AGI division—is taking medical leave, joined by two other senior executives. The changes come weeks before what would be one of the most anticipated Wall Street debuts in history, with the company valued at $852 billion. OpenAI's Codex App has simultaneously surpassed its VS Code extension and CLI as the company's most-used product surface. Questions are mounting about organizational continuity during this critical pre-IPO window, with ChatGPT Business also launching at $25/user/month this week.
