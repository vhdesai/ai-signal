# OpenAI unveiled GPT-5.4-Cyber, a variant of its flagship model optimized for defensive cybersecurity

OpenAI unveiled GPT-5.4-Cyber, a variant of its flagship model optimized for defensive cybersecurity. The company is expanding its Trusted Access for Cyber (TAC) program to thousands of individual defenders and hundreds of security teams. Its Codex Security agent has now contributed to fixing over 3,000 critical and high-severity vulnerabilities. The release directly counters Anthropic's Claude Mythos, which recently uncovered thousands of zero-day vulnerabilities including a 27-year-old flaw in OpenBSD.
