# separate report from The Verge reveals that CISA — the U.S. agency primarily responsible for national cybersecurity coordination — does not have access to Claude Mythos Preview, even as the NSA and the Department of Commerce do. The gap is particularly striking given CISA's ongoing budget and workforce reductions under the current administration. Security policy experts describe this as a significant structural vulnerability: the one U.S. agency most responsible for defending critical infrastructure against the kind of attacks Mythos can generate is excluded from evaluating it defensively.
