# Carnegie Mellon University's Center for AI-Driven Biomedical Research (AI4BIO) selected its first four research…

Carnegie Mellon University's Center for AI-Driven Biomedical Research (AI4BIO) selected its first four research projects, which will use AI and machine learning to decode genomic and cellular data using automated laboratory platforms. One project will pair AI-led predictive modeling with automated experiments to understand how genes and environment shape microbial traits; another will use AI-guided experimentation to engineer biomolecular condensates for targeted therapeutic design. The center's long-term vision is to build virtual cell models that integrate with programmable cloud labs for autonomous biological discovery at scale.
