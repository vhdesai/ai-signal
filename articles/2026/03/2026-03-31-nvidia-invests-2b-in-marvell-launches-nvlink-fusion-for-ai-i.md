# Nvidia Invests $2B in Marvell, Launches NVLink Fusion for AI Infrastructure

Nvidia announced a $2B strategic investment in Marvell Technology with a NVLink Fusion partnership integrating Marvell's custom XPUs and silicon photonics into Nvidia's rack-scale AI infrastructure. The companies will also co-develop AI-RAN for 5G/6G telecom. Marvell shares surged 7-11%, and the deal directly extends the GTC 2026 ecosystem strategy — signaling Nvidia's ambition to be the connective tissue of heterogeneous AI data centers globally.
