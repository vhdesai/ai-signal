# Google rolled out Gemini 3.1 Flash Live to more than 200 countries, completing a major product push across its AI…

Google rolled out Gemini 3.1 Flash Live to more than 200 countries, completing a major product push across its AI portfolio. The multimodal model targets real-time conversational use cases and is positioned as a lower-latency companion to the flagship Gemini 3.1 line. Simultaneously, Google launched "switching tools" that allow users to import chat histories and personal data directly from ChatGPT and Claude into Gemini — a notable competitive maneuver targeting user lock-in.
