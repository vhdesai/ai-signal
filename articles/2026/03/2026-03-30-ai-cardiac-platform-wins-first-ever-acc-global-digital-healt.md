# AI Cardiac Platform Wins First-Ever ACC Global Digital Health Award

An AI clinical platform received the American College of Cardiology's inaugural Global Digital Health Award for real-world impact through 12-lead ECG analysis enabling earlier detection of multiple cardiac conditions with measurable accuracy improvements across diverse patient populations. The ACC institutional endorsement is expected to accelerate clinical adoption in hospital systems deferring to ACC guidance, as medical AI faces growing regulatory scrutiny for real-world efficacy data.
