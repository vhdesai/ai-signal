# OpenAI Turns ChatGPT into a Product Discovery Engine with Expanded Shopping

OpenAI is rolling out visual browsing, product comparisons, and price summaries across all ChatGPT tiers. The Agentic Commerce Protocol (ACP) enables merchants to feed product catalogs into ChatGPT while retaining checkout control — with Walmart as flagship partner. The move accelerates ChatGPT's transformation into an action-oriented commerce interface directly threatening Google Shopping and Amazon search.
