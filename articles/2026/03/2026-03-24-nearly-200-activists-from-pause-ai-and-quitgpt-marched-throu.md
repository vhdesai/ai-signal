# Nearly 200 activists from Pause AI and QuitGPT marched through San Francisco yesterday, stopping at the offices of…

Nearly 200 activists from Pause AI and QuitGPT marched through San Francisco yesterday, stopping at the offices of Anthropic, OpenAI, and xAI to demand that CEOs publicly commit to pausing development of frontier AI models. The protest marks an escalation in organized public pressure on leading AI labs at a moment when multiple companies are simultaneously announcing more powerful and autonomous AI capabilities.
