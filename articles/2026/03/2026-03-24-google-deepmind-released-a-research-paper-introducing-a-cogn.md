# Google DeepMind released a research paper introducing a cognitive framework for systematically measuring progress…

Google DeepMind released a research paper introducing a cognitive framework for systematically measuring progress toward Artificial General Intelligence. The paper defines capability milestones across reasoning, planning, memory, and generalization — offering a more rigorous vocabulary for a debate long hampered by definitional ambiguity. The release is timely given CEO Demis Hassabis's recent statement that "AGI is on the horizon" in 2026.
