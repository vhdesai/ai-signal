# OpenAI officially discontinued its Sora AI video generation platform, citing unsustainable compute costs estimated at…

OpenAI officially discontinued its Sora AI video generation platform, citing unsustainable compute costs estimated at $15 million per day. Simultaneously, Bloomberg reported that a landmark $1 billion partnership with Disney — which had been expected to make Sora the backbone of Disney's generative content pipeline — has been dissolved. The shutdown marks one of the most high-profile product retreats in OpenAI's history and cedes the enterprise video AI market to competitors.
