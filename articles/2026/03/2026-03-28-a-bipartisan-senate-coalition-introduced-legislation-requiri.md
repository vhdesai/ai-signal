# A bipartisan Senate coalition introduced legislation requiring mandatory energy consumption reporting for AI data…

A bipartisan Senate coalition introduced legislation requiring mandatory energy consumption reporting for AI data centers, framing it as an environmental accountability measure. Separately, Senators Bernie Sanders and Rep. Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez introduced the AI Data Center Moratorium Act, which would pause new large-scale data center construction pending environmental review. While the moratorium bill faces long odds, the bipartisan energy reporting push has broader support and could advance in committee.
