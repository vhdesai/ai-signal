# Mark Zuckerberg is personally testing a "CEO agent" that automates executive information retrieval and streamlines…

Mark Zuckerberg is personally testing a "CEO agent" that automates executive information retrieval and streamlines communications at Meta, according to The Wall Street Journal. Internally, Meta employees are also experimenting with "My Claw" (an agent for navigating internal chats) and "Second Brain" (a chief-of-staff agent for project documents), with AI adoption now factored into performance reviews.
