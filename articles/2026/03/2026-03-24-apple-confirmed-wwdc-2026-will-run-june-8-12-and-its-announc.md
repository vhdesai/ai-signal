# Apple confirmed WWDC 2026 will run June 8–12, and its announcement press release explicitly teased "AI advancements" —…

Apple confirmed WWDC 2026 will run June 8–12, and its announcement press release explicitly teased "AI advancements" — a departure from Apple's typically vague developer conference previews. Analysts interpret this as a commitment to finally deliver the long-delayed Siri overhaul, including deeper contextual awareness, on-screen intelligence, and multi-step task execution, potentially powered by Apple's Google partnership. iOS 27 is expected to be the primary vehicle for these updates.
