# AMD and Meta have officially expanded their multi-year AI infrastructure partnership around the deployment of up to 6…

AMD and Meta have officially expanded their multi-year AI infrastructure partnership around the deployment of up to 6 gigawatts of AMD Instinct GPUs based on a custom MI450 architecture. The deal, structured around AMD's Helios rack-scale design and 6th-Gen EPYC CPUs, marks one of the largest chip agreements in semiconductor history. Initial gigawatt shipments are expected in the second half of 2026.
