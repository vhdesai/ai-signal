# MIT researchers published findings on a new training approach they call "Humble AI" — a framework that teaches language…

MIT researchers published findings on a new training approach they call "Humble AI" — a framework that teaches language models to recognize the boundaries of their own knowledge and express calibrated uncertainty rather than generating confident but incorrect responses. In evaluations, models trained with the framework showed significantly lower rates of hallucination on factual queries while maintaining competitive performance on tasks where the model has reliable knowledge. The approach could have significant implications for deploying AI in high-stakes enterprise settings.
