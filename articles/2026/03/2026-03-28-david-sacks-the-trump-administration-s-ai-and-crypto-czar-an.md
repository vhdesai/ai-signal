# David Sacks, the Trump administration's AI and Crypto Czar, announced his resignation from the White House role

David Sacks, the Trump administration's AI and Crypto Czar, announced his resignation from the White House role. Sacks had been the administration's primary voice on AI policy since early 2025, championing a deregulatory posture toward AI development. His departure raises questions about continuity in US federal AI strategy at a pivotal moment for international AI governance negotiations.
