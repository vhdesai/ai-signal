# Defense AI startup Shield AI closed a $2 billion funding round at a $12.7 billion valuation, one of the largest raises…

Defense AI startup Shield AI closed a $2 billion funding round at a $12.7 billion valuation, one of the largest raises in the defense-tech AI sector to date. Shield AI develops autonomous pilot AI for military aircraft and drones. The raise reflects sustained investor appetite for AI-native defense companies amid growing government AI procurement budgets.
