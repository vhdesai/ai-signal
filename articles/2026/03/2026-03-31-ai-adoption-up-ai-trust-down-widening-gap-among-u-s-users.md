# AI Adoption Up, AI Trust Down — Widening Gap Among U.S. Users

A new TechCrunch survey reveals U.S. AI tool adoption is rising while confidence in AI accuracy is declining — diverging trends suggesting familiarity does not translate into trust. Greater experience with AI appears to expose more failure modes rather than build confidence, with significant implications for enterprise deployments where human oversight workflows remain unstandardized. The data reinforces growing evidence that AI adoption and AI confidence are moving in opposite directions.
