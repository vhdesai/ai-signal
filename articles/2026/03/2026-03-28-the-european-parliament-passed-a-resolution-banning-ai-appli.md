# The European Parliament passed a resolution banning AI applications designed to generate non-consensual intimate…

The European Parliament passed a resolution banning AI applications designed to generate non-consensual intimate imagery ("nudify" apps), the first such EU-wide restriction of its kind. Separately, a Dutch court issued an injunction ordering xAI to prevent Grok from generating non-consensual undressing images of real individuals. The dual actions signal accelerating European judicial and legislative pressure on generative AI companies over image-based harms.
