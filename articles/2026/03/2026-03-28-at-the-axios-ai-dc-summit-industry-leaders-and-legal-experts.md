# At the Axios AI+DC Summit, industry leaders and legal experts concluded that AI copyright protection frameworks will be…

At the Axios AI+DC Summit, industry leaders and legal experts concluded that AI copyright protection frameworks will be resolved by courts rather than Congress in the near term, as legislative momentum has stalled. The consensus view is that current copyright law is inadequate for AI-generated content, but there is no path to new legislation in the current Congressional session. Several pending federal cases are expected to set de facto industry standards before any statutory framework emerges.
