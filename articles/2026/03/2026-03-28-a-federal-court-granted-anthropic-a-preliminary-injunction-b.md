# A federal court granted Anthropic a preliminary injunction blocking the Trump administration's Department of Defense…

A federal court granted Anthropic a preliminary injunction blocking the Trump administration's Department of Defense from designating Anthropic as a "supply-chain risk" entity, which would have barred the company from government contracts. The ruling is a significant legal victory for Anthropic and signals judicial skepticism toward the administration's use of national security designations to restrict AI companies. The underlying case is expected to proceed to full trial.
