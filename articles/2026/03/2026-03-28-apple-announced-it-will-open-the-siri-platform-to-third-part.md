# Apple announced it will open the Siri platform to third-party AI models in iOS 27, allowing users to route requests to…

Apple announced it will open the Siri platform to third-party AI models in iOS 27, allowing users to route requests to models from OpenAI, Google, and others directly through Siri. The move represents a significant strategic pivot for Apple, which has historically kept its AI stack proprietary. Full details are expected at WWDC this year, where Apple has promised additional AI advancements across its hardware and software lineup.
