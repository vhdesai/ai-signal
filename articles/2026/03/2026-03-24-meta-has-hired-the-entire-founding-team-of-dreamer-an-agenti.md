# Meta has hired the entire founding team of Dreamer — an agentic AI startup valued at $500 million — to join its…

Meta has hired the entire founding team of Dreamer — an agentic AI startup valued at $500 million — to join its Superintelligence Labs division. The team includes David Singleton (ex-Stripe CTO), Hugo Barra (ex-Meta Oculus VP), and Nicholas Jitkoff (ex-Chrome OS design lead). Dreamer built technology for creating custom AI agents using natural language. The acqui-hire signals Meta's urgency to close the gap with OpenAI and Google on autonomous agent capabilities.
