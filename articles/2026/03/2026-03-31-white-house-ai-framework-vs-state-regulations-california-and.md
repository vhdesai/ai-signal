# White House AI Framework vs. State Regulations — California and Others Push Back

The Trump administration's national AI framework calling for federal preemption of state AI laws is facing its first concrete test. California announced its own AI regulations, Georgia is advancing AI bills, and Washington State signed four AI-related laws. The Transparency Coalition tracked active chatbot safety bills in five additional states. The dynamic sets up a constitutional conflict over AI governance authority with significant compliance implications for multi-jurisdiction deployments.
