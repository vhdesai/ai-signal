# Cerebras Eyes April IPO at $15-22B Valuation; AWS Partnership Strengthens Story

Cerebras re-filed confidentially for a U.S. IPO led by Morgan Stanley, targeting ~$2B raised as early as April 2026. The filing follows a $10B OpenAI commitment, Oracle as customer, and a new AWS collaboration deploying CS-3 Wafer Scale Engine chips via disaggregated inference — Trainium handles prompt prefill while Cerebras handles output decode. The diversified story substantially strengthens the IPO narrative after CFIUS concerns derailed the 2024 filing.
