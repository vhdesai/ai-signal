# ByteDance released Seedance 2.0, an upgraded video generation model with significantly improved temporal coherence and…

ByteDance released Seedance 2.0, an upgraded video generation model with significantly improved temporal coherence and prompt adherence. The release comes in the same week that OpenAI formally shut down its competing Sora platform, potentially positioning Seedance as the go-to open alternative for AI video generation. ByteDance has been aggressively investing in generative media capabilities as a strategic complement to its TikTok and CapCut platforms.
