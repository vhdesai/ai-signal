# Anthropic has agreed to terms on a $10 billion funding round co-led by Coatue and Singapore's sovereign wealth fund…

Anthropic has agreed to terms on a $10 billion funding round co-led by Coatue and Singapore's sovereign wealth fund GIC, pushing the company's valuation to roughly $350 billion — nearly double its $183 billion valuation just six months ago. The raise makes Anthropic one of the twenty most valuable private enterprises globally. The round follows explosive revenue growth: annual run-rate revenue surged from ~$1B to over $5B through 2025, with Claude Code alone exceeding $1B in annualized revenue within six months of launch.
