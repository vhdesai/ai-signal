# A federal hearing is underway today before Judge Rita Lin in California where Anthropic is contesting the Pentagon's…

A federal hearing is underway today before Judge Rita Lin in California where Anthropic is contesting the Pentagon's designation of the company as a "supply-chain national security risk" — the first time such a label has been applied to a US-based AI firm. Anthropic's head of policy and head of public sector filed sworn declarations denying that Anthropic sought veto authority over military operations. The dispute stems from Anthropic's refusal to permit unrestricted military deployment of Claude.
