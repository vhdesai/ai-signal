# As OpenAI — valued at $730 billion after its record $110B fundraise — eyes a public market debut, CEO Sam Altman has…

As OpenAI — valued at $730 billion after its record $110B fundraise — eyes a public market debut, CEO Sam Altman has pivoted toward more measured infrastructure spending. The company is scaling back plans to build its own massive data centers and instead leaning into purchased cloud capacity, including through its $500B Stargate partnership with Oracle and SoftBank. Analysts cite pressure from prospective public-market investors demanding fiscal discipline alongside sustained revenue growth.
