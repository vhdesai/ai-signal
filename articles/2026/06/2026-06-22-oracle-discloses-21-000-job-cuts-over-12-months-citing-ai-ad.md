# Oracle Discloses ~21,000 Job Cuts Over 12 Months, Citing AI Adoption

In an annual regulatory filing, Oracle disclosed it reduced headcount by ~21,000 (~13%) over the past year, stating AI deployment "has resulted, and may continue to result, in reductions to our workforce." The figure exceeds previously known cuts. Oracle booked $1.84B in severance costs, up from $374M the prior year. One of the most explicit admissions that AI is displacing workers while the company posts record revenue.
