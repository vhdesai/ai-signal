# Coinbase defaults to cheaper Chinese open-weight models to curb AI costs

Coinbase CEO Brian Armstrong outlined five tactics for containing AI spend without throttling usage — the first being a shift to cheaper Chinese open-weight models (e.g., GLM 5.2) as engineering defaults. The disclosure adds a prominent U.S. enterprise to a growing list routing routine workloads away from frontier American labs on cost grounds, and drew immediate questions about security and geopolitical exposure. It's a concrete data point in the industry's pivot from “tokenmaxxing” to efficiency.
