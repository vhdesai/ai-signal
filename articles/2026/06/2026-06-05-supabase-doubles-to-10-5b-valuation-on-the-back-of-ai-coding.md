# Supabase Doubles to ~$10.5B Valuation on the Back of AI Coding Tools

Open-source database company Supabase raised $500M at a ~$10.5B valuation, roughly doubling for the third time in two years. CEO Paul Copplestone credits AI coding tools — citing Claude Code and Codex — for driving database launches up over 600% YoY, with 60%+ now initiated by an AI tool. Supabase now serves nearly 10 million developers as the default backend for Bolt, Figma, Lovable, and Replit.
