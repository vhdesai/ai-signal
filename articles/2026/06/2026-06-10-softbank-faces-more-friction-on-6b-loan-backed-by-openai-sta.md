# SoftBank Faces More Friction on $6B Loan Backed by OpenAI Stake

SoftBank is reportedly running into additional problems borrowing $6 billion secured against its OpenAI stake. The financing difficulties show how even highly coveted AI equity can be hard to convert into debt capacity when the underlying company remains private, capital-intensive, and difficult for lenders to value. The issue is another reminder that the AI boom is increasingly tied to financial engineering as much as model performance: equity stakes, debt facilities, data center commitments, and secondary-market liquidity are all becoming strategic constraints.
