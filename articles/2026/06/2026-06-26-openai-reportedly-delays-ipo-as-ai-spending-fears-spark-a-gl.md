# OpenAI reportedly delays IPO as AI-spending fears spark a global tech sell-off

Global equities fell Friday amid a broad technology sell-off, as investors questioned stretched AI valuations and the mounting capital costs tied to data-center buildouts. The slide was compounded by a New York Times report that OpenAI is leaning toward holding off its IPO until next year. Asian and U.S. indices both came under pressure, with semiconductor and AI-infrastructure names leading declines.
