# ### Intel's Full-Stack AI Pivot Drives ~8.5% Rally

Intel shares climbed ~8.5% after three brokerages raised targets (Wells Fargo $110, Barclays $100, Mizuho $128), citing stronger AI data-center demand from agentic workloads. Intel repositioned from CPU vendor to full-stack AI infrastructure provider at Computex, pairing Xeon 6+ on the 18A node with SambaNova dataflow units. - [Tech Times](https://www.techtimes.com/articles/318008/20260608/intel-stock-surges-analyst-targets-climb-sambanova-foxconn-deals-anchor-ai-pivot.htm) --- **Tags:** `HOT`
