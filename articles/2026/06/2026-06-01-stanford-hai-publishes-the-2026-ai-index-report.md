# Stanford HAI publishes the 2026 AI Index Report

Stanford HAI's 2026 AI Index (page updated within the window) documents that the US–China frontier-model gap has effectively closed, with the leading US model ahead by only ~2.7% on key benchmarks as of early 2026. The report also notes the US hosts 5,427 data centers, that recorded AI incidents rose to 362, and that US private AI investment reached $285.9B in 2025. It remains the most authoritative single reference for executives tracking macro AI trends.
