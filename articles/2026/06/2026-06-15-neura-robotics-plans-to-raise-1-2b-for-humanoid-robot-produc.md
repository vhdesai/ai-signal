# Neura Robotics plans to raise ~$1.2B for humanoid-robot production

German startup Neura Robotics said it plans to raise up to €1B ($1.2B) in a new financing round to launch production of a humanoid robot. Neura positions itself against Tesla, OpenAI-backed 1X Technologies, and Figure AI in the AI-driven humanoid market. This is an intent-to-raise announcement, not a closed round; treat the figure as indicative pending a formal close.
