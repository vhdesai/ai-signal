# NYU's Damodaran Warns AI Correction Could Be More Painful Than Dot-Com Bust

The "Dean of Valuation" warned that the AI boom carries greater systemic risk than the dot-com crash because it is fueled by massive debt-funded infrastructure spending rather than light-asset models. When the correction comes, pain will extend beyond equity holders to lenders and the broader economy. He also cautioned that the rush to include SpaceX, OpenAI, and Anthropic in the S&P 500 could reshape passive investing.
