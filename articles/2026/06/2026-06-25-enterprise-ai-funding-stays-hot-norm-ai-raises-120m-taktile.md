# Enterprise-AI funding stays hot: Norm AI raises $120M; Taktile lands $110M Series C

Despite cooling public-market sentiment, private capital kept flowing into applied AI. Norm AI, which embeds legal and compliance rules into AI agents for regulated enterprises, disclosed a $120M raise across nineteen investors. Taktile, an AI decision platform for financial institutions, closed a $110M Series C led by Goldman Sachs Alternatives' growth-equity arm, with Balderton, Index, Tiger Global and Y Combinator participating — signaling continued appetite for vertical, workflow-embedded agents.
