# Global tech sell-off as investors reprice AI; Kospi triggers circuit breaker

Equities fell Friday in a global technology sell-off as investors questioned stretched AI valuations and the mounting cost of AI data centers, with the report of an OpenAI IPO delay adding to the unease. Asian markets dropped sharply — trading in South Korea was briefly halted after the Kospi fell 8% and triggered a circuit breaker, closing down 5.8%. For the week, the Nasdaq Composite lost 4.48% and the S&P 500 fell 1.96%, even as memory-maker Micron posted strong earnings.
