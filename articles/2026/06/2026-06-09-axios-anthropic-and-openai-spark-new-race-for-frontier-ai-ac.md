# Axios: Anthropic and OpenAI Spark New Race for Frontier AI Access

Axios published an analysis framing the simultaneous IPO filings and model releases from Anthropic and OpenAI as igniting a new race for frontier AI access. With both companies now publicly accountable, the competition shifts from private fundraising to public-market storytelling—where model capabilities, revenue growth, and safety credentials all become investable narratives. The piece notes FAANG has been replaced by "MANGOS" in market parlance.
