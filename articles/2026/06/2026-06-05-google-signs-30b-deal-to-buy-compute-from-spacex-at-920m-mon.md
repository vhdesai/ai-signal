# Google Signs $30B+ Deal to Buy Compute from SpaceX at $920M/Month

Google will pay SpaceX $920M/month for AI compute at xAI data centers, totaling over $30B. The deal validates xAI's buildout as commercially viable and entangles Google's compute with Musk's ecosystem ahead of SpaceX's record IPO.
