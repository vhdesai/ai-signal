# Nvidia’s Huang calls smuggled-chip data centers a “dead end,” says AI ROI is “answered”

At Nvidia’s annual stockholder meeting, Jensen Huang said national security takes priority over commercial opportunity and that data centers “cobbled together” from smuggled parts are unworkable without Nvidia’s support and repairs. He argued the AI return-on-investment question “has been answered,” citing GitHub pull requests nearly tripling on AI usage, and reiterated plans to return 50% of free cash flow to shareholders. About 9% of fiscal-2026 revenue came from China, a declining share amid export controls.
