# OpenAI's "Light" Balance Sheet Faces Scrutiny Ahead of IPO

The Information reviewed OpenAI's financial statements as of March 31, revealing an unusually lean appearance: zero debt, <$750M in lease liabilities, and only $46M in quarterly capex. But the company carries $665B in future chip and data center commitments—much of it flowing to investors who are also suppliers. Regulators may scrutinize these off-balance-sheet structures before any public listing.
