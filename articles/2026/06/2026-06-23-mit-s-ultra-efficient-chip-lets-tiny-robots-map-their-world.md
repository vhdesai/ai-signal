# MIT's ultra-efficient chip lets tiny robots map their world on an LED's worth of power

MIT researchers unveiled a custom chip that lets tiny, battery-limited robots build detailed 3D maps of their surroundings in real time using roughly the power of a single LED. By pairing an efficient algorithm with dedicated hardware, the system enables on-board obstacle avoidance and collision-free path planning for low-power drones operating in constrained spaces such as industrial HVAC ducts. The work targets autonomy in size- and energy-limited robotics, where conventional mapping pipelines are far too power-hungry. https://techxplore.com/news/2026-06-ultraefficient-chip-tiny-robots-traverse.html
