# The AP reports that Chinese chipmakers led by Huawei have overtaken Nvidia in China's domestic AI-accelerator market,…

The AP reports that Chinese chipmakers led by Huawei have overtaken Nvidia in China's domestic AI-accelerator market, as export controls and Beijing's "buy domestic" posture squeeze the US leader. Huawei's Ascend line has become the reference platform for Chinese frontier labs, with DeepSeek optimizing for Ascend 950 silicon. For global buyers, the bifurcation of the AI hardware stack along geopolitical lines is hardening into a durable feature of the market.
