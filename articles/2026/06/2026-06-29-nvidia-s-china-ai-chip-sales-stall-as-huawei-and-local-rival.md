# Nvidia's China AI-chip sales stall as Huawei and local rivals take the lead

Despite Jensen Huang's celebrity reception in Beijing, Nvidia's advanced-chip sales in China have stalled under U.S. export controls, and domestic chipmakers led by Huawei are now overtaking it in one of its largest markets. The shift signals an accelerating split of the global AI-hardware stack along geopolitical lines, with Chinese buyers increasingly designing around U.S. silicon. For suppliers and customers alike, China is hardening into a separate, locally-supplied AI-compute market.
