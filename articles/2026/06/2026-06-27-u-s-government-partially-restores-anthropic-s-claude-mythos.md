# U.S. government partially restores Anthropic's Claude Mythos 5 for critical infrastructure

Anthropic said the U.S. government has authorized it to redeploy Claude Mythos 5 — its strongest cybersecurity model — to a select set of organizations that operate and defend critical infrastructure, easing restrictions imposed earlier in June. The sibling Fable 5 model remains on hold pending further discussions. The partial reversal mirrors the gated access granted to OpenAI's GPT-5.6, reinforcing a pattern of Washington directly shaping who can use the most capable frontier models.
