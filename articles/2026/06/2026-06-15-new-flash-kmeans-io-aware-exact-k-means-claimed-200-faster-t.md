# New Flash-KMeans: IO-aware exact K-Means claimed >200× faster than FAISS on GPUs

MarkTechPost's lead June 15 research item describes Flash-KMeans, an IO-aware exact K-Means implementation claimed to run over 200× faster than FAISS on GPUs, targeting large-scale clustering and vector workloads. It is a systems/infrastructure advance rather than a new model, with direct relevance for vector-database and retrieval pipelines. Performance claims are single-sourced and not independently verified in-window; treat as a research signal pending corroboration.
