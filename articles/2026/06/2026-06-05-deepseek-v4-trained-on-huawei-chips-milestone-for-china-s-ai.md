# DeepSeek V4 Trained on Huawei Chips — Milestone for China's AI Self-Reliance

DeepSeek confirmed that its V4 model was trained on Huawei AI chips, after earlier success using the same chips for inference. The milestone weakens the assumption that U.S. export controls will durably constrain Chinese AI development, demonstrating domestic hardware can support frontier model training.
