# U.S. futures slip after AI-driven record highs

U.S. stock futures pointed lower Tuesday after major indexes hit all-time highs the prior session on AI enthusiasm, with the S&P 500 notching a ninth consecutive weekly gain led by Nvidia. Competing AI catalysts—Anthropic's IPO filing and Alphabet's $80 billion raise—are pulling investor attention in different directions. The pullback was modest, with Nasdaq 100 futures down about 0.1%.
