# Alibaba Releases Qwen3.7-Plus as a Multimodal Autonomous Agent

Alibaba released Qwen3.7-Plus, positioning it as a multimodal model designed to function as a "full-blown autonomous agent"—capable of vision, language, and tool use in integrated workflows. The release extends Alibaba's Qwen ecosystem play from platform to model, directly competing with OpenAI's Codex and Anthropic's Claude for agentic enterprise workloads. URL not verified.
