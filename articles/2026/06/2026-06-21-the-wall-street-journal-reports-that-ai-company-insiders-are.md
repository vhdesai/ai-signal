# The Wall Street Journal reports that AI company insiders are selling stock at an unprecedented rate, arguing this…

The Wall Street Journal reports that AI company insiders are selling stock at an unprecedented rate, arguing this pattern has historically preceded corrections. When corporate executives as a group become net sellers, it signals stocks are significantly overpriced. The piece follows NYU professor Damodaran's warning earlier this week that AI's debt-funded infrastructure boom could produce a correction worse than the dot-com bust.
