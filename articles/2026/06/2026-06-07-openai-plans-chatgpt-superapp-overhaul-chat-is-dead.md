# OpenAI Plans ChatGPT "Superapp" Overhaul — "Chat Is Dead"

OpenAI is folding Codex, agents, and partner apps into a single "superapp." Enterprise drives ~40% of revenue (targeting ~50%), and ChatGPT/Codex teams merged under unified leadership. CPO Sottiaux: a personal agent "across everything in your life." Clear repositioning toward task execution over conversation before IPO.
