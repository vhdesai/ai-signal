# Amazon commits an additional $13B to India AI and cloud

After CEO Andy Jassy met PM Narendra Modi in New Delhi, Amazon said it will invest an additional $13B through 2030 to expand AWS data-center capacity in Mumbai and Hyderabad, lifting its India cloud/AI commitment to ~$48B. The move follows Microsoft’s $17.5B and Google’s $15B India pledges, underscoring a hyperscaler race to lock in compute capacity in the fastest-growing data-center market.
