# OpenAI soft-launches GPT-5.6 (Sol, Terra, Luna) under restricted U.S.-government access

OpenAI began a limited preview of its GPT-5.6 series — flagship Sol, balanced Terra, and efficient Luna — initially restricting access to "trusted partners" at the U.S. government's request, with broader availability promised "in the coming weeks." Sol set a new state of the art on the Terminal-Bench 2.1 agentic coding benchmark (88.8 as a single model, 91.9 in subagent "ultra mode"). The gated rollout mirrors restrictions placed on Anthropic and reflects deepening government involvement in frontier-model deployment.
