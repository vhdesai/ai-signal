# ### Cornell researcher launches Health & AI Policy Index (HAPI)

A Cornell-affiliated researcher published the Health and AI Policy Index (HAPI), a public database tracking U.S. health-care AI legislation and governance across regulatory frameworks, in npj Digital Medicine. The work maps an increasingly fragmented policy patchwork as AI enters clinical settings, aiming to support patient safety, provider accountability, and equity. It offers a structured reference for organizations navigating health-AI compliance.
