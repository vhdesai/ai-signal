# OpenAI Launches GPT-5.5-Cyber and "Patch the Planet" Initiative

OpenAI shipped GPT-5.5-Cyber, a cybersecurity-specialized model scoring 85.6% on CyberGym (vs 81.8% for standard GPT-5.5), restricted to vetted defenders through a Trusted Access program. Alongside it, "Patch the Planet" — run with Trail of Bits and HackerOne — surfaced hundreds of issues across 30+ open-source projects including Linux, cURL, Go, and Python. A bundled Codex Security plugin embeds vulnerability scanning directly into the developer IDE. The move is a direct competitive and political answer to Anthropic's Project Glasswing.
