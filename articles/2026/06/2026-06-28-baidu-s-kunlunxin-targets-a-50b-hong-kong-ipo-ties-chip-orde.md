# Baidu's Kunlunxin targets a ~$50B Hong Kong IPO, ties chip orders to allocations

Baidu's AI-chip unit Kunlunxin is preparing a Hong Kong listing at a target valuation near $50B, per The Information — more than triple the ~$14.7B figure floated earlier this month. In an unusual structure, the company reportedly asked prospective IPO investors to also commit to buying its semiconductors, blurring the line between shareholder and customer. The move underscores how aggressively China is capitalizing domestic alternatives to Nvidia as export controls reshape its supply chain.
