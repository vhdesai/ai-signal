# Oracle adds four agentic supply-chain apps to Fusion Cloud SCM

Oracle launched four new agentic applications inside Oracle Fusion Cloud SCM — covering inventory planning, supplier qualification, production readiness, and Kanban replenishment — driven by coordinated teams of specialized AI agents operating within the existing Fusion security framework. The apps “move beyond assistance to execution,” autonomously progressing routine work and surfacing exceptions where human judgment can change the outcome. It continues Oracle's strategy of embedding outcome-driven agents directly into its application suite — a direct competitive response to SAP and Salesforce in agentic ERP/SCM.
