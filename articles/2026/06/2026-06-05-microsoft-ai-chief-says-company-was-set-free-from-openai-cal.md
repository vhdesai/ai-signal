# Microsoft AI Chief Says Company Was "Set Free" from OpenAI; Calls Anthropic "Too Expensive"

Microsoft's AI chief stated the company has been "set free" from OpenAI to pursue its own frontier AI ambitions, including building superintelligence independently. In separate remarks, he called Anthropic's AI services "too expensive," positioning Microsoft's in-house MAI models as a cost-competitive alternative. The rhetoric marks a notable escalation in the Microsoft–OpenAI relationship evolution, from exclusive partnership to declared independence with competitive positioning. URL not verified.
