# OpenAI Moves Toward Product-Specific Ads on ChatGPT

Advertisers can now create ads for specific products on ChatGPT by sharing feeds of product information with OpenAI, according to advertiser communications reviewed by The Information. The move points to a more commerce-oriented version of ChatGPT, where brand and product discovery could be embedded directly into conversational answers. It also raises familiar AI search questions: how clearly ads will be labeled, how product rankings will be determined, and whether paid feeds influence recommendation quality or user trust.
