# Over 100 Campaigners Back UK MP Suing xAI Over Grok Deepfake Images

More than 100 campaigners and organizations are now backing a UK MP's lawsuit against Elon Musk's xAI over deepfake images generated by Grok. The growing coalition follows xAI's controversial motion to strip victims of anonymity. The case is becoming a test for AI-generated image liability in UK courts, with potential precedent-setting implications for the industry. URL not verified.
