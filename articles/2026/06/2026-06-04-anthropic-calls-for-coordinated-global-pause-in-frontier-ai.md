# Anthropic Calls for Coordinated Global Pause in Frontier AI Development

Anthropic warned that frontier AI models are approaching recursive self-improvement capabilities faster than anticipated, calling for a coordinated "brake pedal" before control becomes difficult to maintain. Co-founder Jack Clark told the BBC the industry needs the option to halt frontier research if safety cannot be assured. Coming from a company that just filed for IPO at a near-trillion-dollar valuation, the warning carries unusual weight.
