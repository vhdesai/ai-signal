# ### Nvidia Authorizes Record $80B Buyback and Raises Dividend

Nvidia authorized an $80 billion share repurchase — its largest ever — and raised its dividend, finishing the week as the only "Magnificent 7" name to close higher. The move follows Q1 revenue of $81.6B (up 85% YoY), with data-center revenue alone at $75.2B. The program signals management views the stock as undervalued relative to its earnings trajectory. - [Money Morning](https://moneymorning.com/2026/06/05/nvidia-nvda-80-billion-buyback-dividend-june-2026) --- **Tags:** `HOT` `NEW`
