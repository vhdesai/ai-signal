# Alphabet Plans $80B Capital Raise for AI Infrastructure; Berkshire Commits $10B

Alphabet announced plans to raise $80 billion through stock sales for its AI buildout, anchored by a $10B private investment from Berkshire Hathaway — a notable first move into AI infrastructure from a historically tech-cautious investor. Shares slipped ~2.5% premarket as some analysts read the issuance as dilutive. The commitment signals that mainstream institutional capital now views frontier compute as core infrastructure.
