# New York Legislature Passes Landmark Bill Requiring Disclosure of AI-Generated News

York's legislature passed a bill requiring disclosure when news content is generated by AI—the first state law of its kind targeting AI transparency in media. The legislation adds to New York's growing AI regulatory footprint (following its data center moratorium) and could become a template for other states. URL not verified.
