# Embodied-AI firm X Square Robot tops a $2.8B valuation after four financing rounds

Shenzhen-based X Square Robot disclosed four consecutive financing rounds culminating in a Series C that lifts its valuation above $2.8B (RMB 20B), placing it among China's highest-valued embodied-AI startups. The company says it is the only embodied-AI firm backed by all four of China's major internet leaders, with proceeds going toward general-purpose robot foundation models, commercial deployments, and integrated robotics infrastructure. The raise signals continued aggressive Chinese capital formation around "physical AI." (Company press release.)
