# Leaked UI elements suggest OpenAI is developing a "ChatGPT for Science" subscription tier targeting universities and…

Leaked UI elements suggest OpenAI is developing a "ChatGPT for Science" subscription tier targeting universities and research institutions. The service appears to offer specialized capabilities for scientific research use cases, though it remains unclear whether access will be restricted to credentialed researchers. The move would expand OpenAI's vertical subscription strategy beyond its existing consumer and enterprise tiers.
