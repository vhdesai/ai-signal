# Digital Realty agreed to acquire a majority stake in three fully-leased Northern Virginia data centers from…

Digital Realty agreed to acquire a majority stake in three fully-leased Northern Virginia data centers from Blackstone-managed funds in a $3.5B cash-and-stock deal ($1.2B cash plus $2.3B in shares) that values the assets at roughly $7.8B. The transaction, expected to close within days, concentrates ownership of prime Ashburn-corridor capacity as AI workloads keep demand for leased data-center space elevated. It is a marker of how aggressively capital is consolidating around scarce, power-ready compute real estate.
