# ### Anthropic Faces Multi-Model Claude Outage; Notion Disables Anthropic Models

Anthropic experienced elevated error rates across multiple Claude models (Opus 4.6/4.7/4.8, Sonnet 4.6) for approximately 3 hours on June 5, attributed to infrastructure issues. In a cascading impact, Notion temporarily disabled Anthropic model access after performance disruptions. The incidents highlight that enterprise SaaS companies building on third-party AI models face availability risks traditional stacks don't — a single upstream model issue can cascade across an entire product's AI features. For enterprises deploying agents, single-vendor model dependencies are an operational risk warranting fallback routing and observability. - [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/06/07/notion-restores-access-to-anthropic-after-service-disruption/) --- **Tags:** `HOT`
