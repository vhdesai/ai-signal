# BIS warns the AI boom and record debt are raising global financial risks

The Bank for International Settlements' Annual Economic Report, published Sunday, named the sustainability of the AI investment boom — alongside record public debt, lingering supply shocks and the risk of renewed inflation — as a complex mix of vulnerabilities demanding disciplined policymaking. The central-bank umbrella group flagged a new "sovereign-financial stability nexus," adding institutional weight to mounting concern that AI capital spending has outrun returns. For executives, it is another signal that AI exposure is now being framed as a systemic, not just sectoral, risk.
