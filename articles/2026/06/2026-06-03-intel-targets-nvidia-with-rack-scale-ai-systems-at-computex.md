# Intel Targets Nvidia with Rack-Scale AI Systems at Computex

Intel introduced rack-scale AI infrastructure for agentic and inference workloads with a commercial timeline for Xeon 6+ on 18A. Partnerships with Foxconn, Siemens, and Hitachi push disaggregated full-system deployments—Intel’s clearest attempt to contest Nvidia at the data-center level.
