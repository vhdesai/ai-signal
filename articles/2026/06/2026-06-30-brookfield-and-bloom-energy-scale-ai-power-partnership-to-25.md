# Brookfield and Bloom Energy scale AI-power partnership to $25B

Brookfield and Bloom Energy announced a fivefold expansion of their partnership — to a $25 billion framework — to build and finance on-site "islanded" power for AI data centers using Bloom's fuel-cell platform. The deal positions Brookfield as an integrated "AI factory" infrastructure investor and responds to power availability emerging as the binding constraint on data-center growth. It reflects the same tokens-per-watt economics that chip and cloud vendors are now framing as the core metric for AI capacity. J INFRA POLICY
