# OpenAI launches GPT-5.6 (Sol, Terra, Luna) — gated to government-approved partners

OpenAI unveiled three new models — GPT-5.6 Sol, Terra, and Luna — and said it is complying with a U.S. government request to limit the initial rollout to a "small group of trusted partners," targeting general availability "in the coming weeks." Sol is OpenAI's most capable model to date, with gains in coding, biology, and cybersecurity, though the company says it stays below its "critical" cyber-risk threshold and is better at fixing vulnerabilities than executing end-to-end attacks. OpenAI publicly called the access-vetting process unsustainable as a long-term default. It is the first time Washington has shaped an OpenAI launch this directly.
