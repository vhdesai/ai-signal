# ### MiniMax releases M3, an open-weight model targeting frontier coding and 1M context

MiniMax launched M3, positioned as the first open-weight model to combine frontier-level coding (a reported 59.0% on SWE-Bench Pro), a 1M-token context window, and native multimodality. A new MiniMax Sparse Attention (MSA) mechanism is claimed to deliver up to 15.6× faster decoding at 1M-token context. Pricing is $0.60 / $2.40 per million input/output tokens, with a 50% launch promotion ($0.30 / $1.20). If the benchmarks hold up independently, this further compresses the price-performance gap between open and closed frontier models. --- ## Infrastructure **Tags:** `HOT` `CAPEX`
