# Breaking Anthropic to meet Trump officials over US government export-control order on Fable 5 and Mythos 5

Bloomberg reported Anthropic is set to meet Trump administration officials over AI security concerns stemming from a US government export-control directive — issued June 11 — that required Anthropic to disable Claude Fable 5 and Mythos 5 for all customers to ensure compliance. The order affected foreign nationals including Anthropic staff, and the abrupt suspension triggered wide developer backlash. The June 15 meeting with government officials is the first confirmed direct engagement between Anthropic and the Trump White House on the matter, signaling the dispute is moving from emergency compliance to active policy negotiation. (The underlying model suspension broke June 13 and is outside the strict 24-hour window; this item reflects the new in-window development only.)
