# Meituan discloses six ACL 2026 papers and General 365 reasoning benchmark

A cluster of Meituan disclosures was aggregated under June 15: acceptance of six papers at ACL 2026 spanning large-model evaluation, process reasoning, competition-math optimization, RL optimization, and generative recommendation; release of the General 365 reasoning benchmark, where top model Gemini 3 Pro reportedly scored only 62.8%, with most of 26 models tested failing the 60% threshold; and open-source releases LongCat-Next (native multimodal) and LongCat-Video-Avatar 1.5. The General 365 benchmark is the most research-relevant item. Treat as LOW confidence pending primary Meituan source confirmation.
