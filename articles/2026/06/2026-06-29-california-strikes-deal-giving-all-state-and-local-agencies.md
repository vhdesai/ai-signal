# California strikes deal giving all state and local agencies Claude at half price

Governor Gavin Newsom and Anthropic signed an agreement giving all California state agencies and local governments access to Claude at a 50% discount, bundled with Anthropic-provided training and support. The strategic signal is a widening state-vs-federal split in U.S. AI governance: California is deepening ties with Anthropic even as the Pentagon has labeled the company a “supply-chain risk” and barred it from working with other defense contractors. State CIO Chris Given said the federal designation “just didn't come up” in negotiations and indicated California will push departments onto the contract and seek similar discounts from other vendors.
