# OpenAI names ex-Uber India chief as its first India managing director

OpenAI appointed Prabhjeet Singh, outgoing president of Uber India and South Asia, as its first managing director for India — described by the company as its second-largest market, with roughly 100 million weekly ChatGPT users. Singh joins in September, reporting to APAC chief Kiran Mani, and will own consumer growth, enterprise adoption, partnerships and regulatory engagement. The hire signals a more deliberate go-to-market and policy posture in a market where the sovereign-AI debate is intensifying.
