# Meta and EssilorLuxottica launch $299 AI smart glasses

Meta and EssilorLuxottica unveiled a lower-cost line of AI smart glasses starting at US$299 — their first not tied to the Ray-Ban or Oakley brands — powered by Meta AI via "Muse Spark." The devices display text and enable AI interaction rather than full augmented-reality overlays, undercutting pricier competitors such as Snap's $2,195 AR glasses. Meta held roughly 76% of the 9.6 million smart glasses shipped in 2025, and the cheaper tier is aimed squarely at broadening mainstream adoption. https://www.straitstimes.com/world/meta-launches-cheaper-range-of-ai-smart-glasses-starting-at-390
