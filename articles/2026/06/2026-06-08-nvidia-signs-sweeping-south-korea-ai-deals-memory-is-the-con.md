# ### Nvidia Signs Sweeping South Korea AI Deals; Memory Is the Constraint

During Huang's Seoul visit, Nvidia announced a multi-year memory partnership with SK hynix, a gigawatt-scale AI cloud with SK Telecom (first factory 2027), and tie-ups with NAVER, Doosan, and LG spanning data centers, robotics, and physical AI. The agreements spotlight high-bandwidth memory as the binding constraint, with shortages forecast to persist toward 2030. - [Daily Sabah](https://www.dailysabah.com/business/tech/nvidia-strikes-deals-with-south-korean-giants-to-advance-ai-boom) --- **Tags:** `HOT`
