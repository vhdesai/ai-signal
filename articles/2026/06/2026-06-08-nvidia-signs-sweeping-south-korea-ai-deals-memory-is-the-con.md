# Nvidia Signs Sweeping South Korea AI Deals; Memory Is the Constraint

During Huang's Seoul visit, Nvidia announced a multi-year memory partnership with SK hynix, a gigawatt-scale AI cloud with SK Telecom (first factory 2027), and tie-ups with NAVER, Doosan, and LG spanning data centers, robotics, and physical AI. The agreements spotlight high-bandwidth memory as the binding constraint, with shortages forecast to persist toward 2030.
