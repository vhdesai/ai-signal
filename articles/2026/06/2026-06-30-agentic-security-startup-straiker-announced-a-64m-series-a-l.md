# Agentic-security startup Straiker announced a $64M Series A — led by Marathon Management Partners with Citi Ventures,…

Agentic-security startup Straiker announced a $64M Series A — led by Marathon Management Partners with Citi Ventures, Illuminate Financial, and Workday Ventures — bringing total funding to $85M. The company positions itself as defending AI agents, which it calls the enterprise's fastest-growing "workforce," and says it counts Fortune 500s and frontier AI labs as customers. The round is another data point in the rapid build-out of a security layer purpose-built for autonomous agents.
