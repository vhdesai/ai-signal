# Amazon is evaluating cheaper alternatives — including OpenAI — after a renegotiated contract will shift Anthropic's…

Amazon is evaluating cheaper alternatives — including OpenAI — after a renegotiated contract will shift Anthropic's Claude billing to token-based pricing next year, according to The Information. The change is consequential because Amazon's internal stack runs deep on Claude: its Kiro coding agent, the Quick workplace assistant, and Alexa for Shopping all depend on Anthropic models. The reporting points to a widening rift between two firms that were until recently inseparable AI partners, and underscores how model-cost inflation has become a board-level procurement question.
