# ByteDance Unveils Seedance 2.5 Video Generation Model

ByteDance introduced Seedance 2.5, an upgraded AI video generation model capable of producing clips up to 30 seconds long and accepting up to 50 reference inputs (up from 12). The release intensifies competition with Runway, Pika, and OpenAI's Sora in the rapidly maturing generative video space.
