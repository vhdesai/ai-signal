# Trump Tells Axios He No Longer Views Anthropic as National Security Threat

Trump said Anthropic has "behaved very responsibly" after meeting CEO Dario Amodei at a G7 lunch, signaling a potential path to restoring Fable 5 and Mythos 5 access. The shift comes after a week of intense Anthropic lobbying and bipartisan Congressional pressure on Commerce Secretary Lutnick.
