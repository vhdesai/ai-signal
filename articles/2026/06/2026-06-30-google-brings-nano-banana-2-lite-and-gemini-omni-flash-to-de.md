# Google brings Nano Banana 2 Lite and Gemini Omni Flash to developers

Google DeepMind moved Nano Banana 2 Lite (Gemini 3.1 Flash Lite Image) — its fastest, cheapest image model, at 4-second text-to-image and $0.034 per 1K images — into general availability across AI Studio, the Gemini API, and consumer surfaces including Search AI Mode and Google Photos. It also opened Gemini Omni Flash, a video-generation and conversational-editing model, to developers in public preview at $0.10 per second of output. Both carry SynthID watermarking. The pairing targets end-to-end image-to-video creative pipelines and pressures rival generative-media providers on cost and latency. Products & Tools C
