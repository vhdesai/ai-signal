# ### OpenAI turns ChatGPT into a career platform

OpenAI added a job-search feature to ChatGPT that surfaces personalized listings from Indeed, Upwork, and Appcast (initially U.S.-only), plus an in-product resume builder and CV editor. The move pushes ChatGPT further into vertical workflows and into competition with established career platforms.
