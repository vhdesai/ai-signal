# OpenAI turns ChatGPT into a career platform

OpenAI added a job-search feature to ChatGPT that surfaces personalized listings from Indeed, Upwork, and Appcast (initially U.S.-only), plus an in-product resume builder and CV editor. The move pushes ChatGPT further into vertical workflows and into competition with established career platforms. It extends OpenAI's pattern of converting general chat into task-specific destinations. https://the-decoder.com/openai-turns-chatgpt-into-a-career-platform-with-job-search-and-cv-editor/
