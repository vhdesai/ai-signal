# MIT Ethics of Computing Symposium Spotlights AI Alignment and Governance

MIT's third annual SERC symposium convened MIT, Google DeepMind, and OpenAI researchers on AI alignment, "moral meshing," and AI in education. Panelists argued the central alignment question is increasingly governance — who is entitled to set the values AI systems encode — rather than capability alone.
