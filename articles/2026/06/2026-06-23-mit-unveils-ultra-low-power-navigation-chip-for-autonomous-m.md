# MIT Unveils Ultra-Low-Power Navigation Chip for Autonomous Micro-Robots

MIT researchers published work on a new navigation chip designed for tiny autonomous robots, enabling spatial reasoning at extreme power constraints. The breakthrough addresses a key bottleneck in deploying AI at the physical edge—where energy budgets measured in microwatts preclude standard inference hardware.
