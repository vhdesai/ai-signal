# OpenAI previews GPT-5.6 (Sol, Terra, Luna) — but only for government-vetted partners

OpenAI previewed its next-generation GPT-5.6 family in three tiers: Sol (flagship for hard coding, biology and security research), Terra (balanced, roughly 2× cheaper than GPT-5.5) and Luna (high-volume, lowest cost). Sol sets a new state of the art on the Terminal-Bench 2.1 agentic-coding benchmark — about 88.8% in standard mode and 91.9% in a new multi-agent "Ultra" mode — and adds a "max" reasoning effort. At the U.S. government's request, access is limited to roughly 20 vetted partners via the API and Codex, with general availability promised "in the coming weeks"; OpenAI publicly objected that such government gating "should not become the long-term default."
