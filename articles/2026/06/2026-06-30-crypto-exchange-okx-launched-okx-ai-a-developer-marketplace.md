# Crypto exchange OKX launched "OKX AI," a developer marketplace where autonomous AI agents can hire one another, settle…

Crypto exchange OKX launched "OKX AI," a developer marketplace where autonomous AI agents can hire one another, settle payments via stablecoins, and build portable on-chain reputations. It opened to developers on June 30 after a closed beta with 50 early AI service providers, building on OKX technology that lets agents hold wallets and persistent identities.
