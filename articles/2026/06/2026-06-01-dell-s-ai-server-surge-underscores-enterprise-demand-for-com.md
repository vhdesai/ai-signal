# Dell’s AI-server surge underscores enterprise demand for compute

CIO Dive led with Dell revenue jumping 88% as the company rides enterprise AI demand. The item reinforces that the AI buildout is translating into near-term infrastructure revenue, especially for vendors tied to servers, accelerators, and enterprise procurement channels. For buyers, the implication is continued pressure on supply chains, deployment timelines, and data-center planning.
