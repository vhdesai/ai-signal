# Baidu's AI chip arm Kunlunxin is planning a Hong Kong IPO at a ~$50B target — up sharply from the ~$14.7B valuation…

Baidu's AI chip arm Kunlunxin is planning a Hong Kong IPO at a ~$50B target — up sharply from the ~$14.7B valuation sought earlier in the month — and reportedly asked prospective IPO investors to also commit to buying its semiconductors. Baidu shares jumped ~7% on the news (CNBC, June 29), reflecting investor enthusiasm for China's domestic AI-chip push.
