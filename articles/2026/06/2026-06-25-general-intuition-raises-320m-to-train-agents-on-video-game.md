# General Intuition raises $320M to train agents on video-game data

General Intuition closed a $320M round at a $2.3B valuation on the bet that millions of hours of gameplay can teach AI agents spatial reasoning and "intuition" transferable to the physical world. The New York startup demonstrated a single model simultaneously driving a game agent (running 100+ hours straight) and a quadrupedal robot. The thesis competes with synthetic-data and teleoperation approaches to the robotics data bottleneck.
