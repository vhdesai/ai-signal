# Per the FT, Google was forced to cap Meta's Gemini usage after Meta exceeded its allotted compute

Per the FT, Google was forced to cap Meta's Gemini usage after Meta exceeded its allotted compute. Meta uses Gemini — which outperformed its own Llama — for customer service, advertiser chatbots, coding, content takedowns and scam detection, and also leans on Anthropic's Claude. The episode underscores that even hyperscalers with their own LLMs are short on compute amid surging token prices.
