# OpenAI and Getty Images Strike Multi-Year Content Display Deal

Getty Images and OpenAI announced a multi-year agreement to surface Getty's licensed visual content within ChatGPT's search experiences. Getty says the deal covers display only, not model training. Shares spiked up to 200% premarket. A notable reversal for a company that sued Stability AI in 2023, signaling normalization of paid content-licensing deals.
