# ### Amazon Strikes Multibillion-Dollar Corning Fiber Deal for AI Data Centers

Amazon will pay Corning billions for optical fiber to connect its AI data centers, creating ~1,000 jobs in North Carolina. Follows Corning agreements with Meta and Nvidia.
