# Tencent Shares Surge 10% on AI Agent Plans for WeChat

Tencent shares rose over 10% after disclosing plans to test AI agent prototypes within WeChat, its messaging super-app with over a billion users. The move positions Tencent to embed agentic AI into one of the world's largest mobile ecosystems and could set the template for how Asian super-apps deploy autonomous AI at scale.
