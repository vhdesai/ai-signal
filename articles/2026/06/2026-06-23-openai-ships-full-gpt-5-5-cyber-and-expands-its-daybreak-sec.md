# OpenAI ships full GPT-5.5-Cyber and expands its Daybreak security program

OpenAI released the full version of GPT-5.5-Cyber to vetted "trusted defenders," claiming state-of-the-art performance of 85.6% on the CyberGym benchmark (versus 81.8% for standard GPT-5.5), alongside an updated Codex Security plugin and a new "Patch the Planet" open-source initiative with Trail of Bits. OpenAI argues the constraint has shifted from finding vulnerabilities to patching them, and is positioning the toolchain to automate the full discovery-to-fix loop. A commercial partner program extends the capability to Cisco, CrowdStrike, Palo Alto Networks, IBM and more than two dozen other vendors. The launch closely mirrors Anthropic's own defensive-security positioning. https://thehackernews.com/2026/06/openai-expands-daybreak-with-gpt-55.html
