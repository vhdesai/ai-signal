# Nvidia's China AI-chip sales stall as Huawei takes the lead

Chinese chipmakers led by Huawei are overtaking Nvidia in its home market, as Washington's export controls and Beijing's push for domestic alternatives erode Nvidia's once-dominant position in China. The shift marks a structural change in the world's second-largest AI market and a durable revenue headwind for Nvidia, even as global demand stays strong. For buyers, it signals a hardening, bifurcated supply chain split along U.S.–China lines.
