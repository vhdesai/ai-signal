# Coinbase cuts AI spend ~50% by defaulting engineers to Chinese open-weight models

Coinbase CEO Brian Armstrong said the exchange roughly halved its internal AI bill by routing engineers — through an internal LLM gateway — to two self-hosted Chinese open-weight models, Zhipu AI's GLM 5.2 and Moonshot's Kimi K2.7, rather than premium U.S. APIs. The move adds a high-profile name to a widening "efficiency over capability" shift as token costs climb, but drew scrutiny over data-governance and geopolitical risk. It lands amid mounting evidence that Chinese open models are gaining share on cost-sensitive enterprise workloads.
