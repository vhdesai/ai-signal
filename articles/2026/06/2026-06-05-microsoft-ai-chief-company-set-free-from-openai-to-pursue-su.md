# Microsoft AI Chief: Company "Set Free" from OpenAI to Pursue Superintelligence

Mustafa Suleyman told VentureBeat a contractual change ~6 months ago gave Microsoft formal authority to pursue superintelligence independently. Seven MAI models operate without OpenAI. He called Anthropic "too expensive," positioning Microsoft's models as cost-competitive. The timing — weeks ahead of OpenAI's expected IPO — is deliberate.
