# ### Nvidia Launches Cosmos 3 Open World Model for Physical AI

Nvidia released Cosmos 3, an open frontier foundation model designed for physical AI applications. The model integrates vision, audio understanding, and action planning—enabling robots and autonomous systems to perceive environments and plan multi-step actions. Released alongside a collection of open-source agent tools at GTC Taipei, Cosmos 3 positions Nvidia's software ecosystem as a counterpart to its hardware dominance in physical AI. --- **Tags:** `NEW`
