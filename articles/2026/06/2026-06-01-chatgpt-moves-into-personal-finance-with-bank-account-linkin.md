# ### ChatGPT moves into personal finance with bank-account linking

OpenAI is reported to be previewing a personal-finance capability in ChatGPT that lets users link bank accounts so the assistant can help track spending and manage money, initially limited to Pro users in the US. The feature would push ChatGPT deeper into regulated, high-sensitivity data territory and put it in closer competition with established fintech tools.
