# A24's $75M Google DeepMind "AI research partnership" sparks creative-industry backlash

Independent film studio A24's newly announced $75M AI research partnership with Google DeepMind drew swift criticism from its filmmaker base and audience within a day of disclosure. The episode highlights the widening tension between frontier-AI labs courting creative-industry deals and the creators wary of generative tooling — a reputational dynamic enterprises in media and brand-sensitive sectors will increasingly need to manage.
