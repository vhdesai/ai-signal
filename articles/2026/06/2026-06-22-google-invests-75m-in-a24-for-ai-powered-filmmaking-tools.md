# Google Invests $75M in A24 for AI-Powered Filmmaking Tools

Google is investing $75 million in A24 to develop AI-powered filmmaking tools, with Google DeepMind forming a parallel research partnership. The combined move adds a premium content partner to Google's creative-AI push while testing where studios and audiences draw production boundaries.
