# Nvidia Releases Alpamayo 2 Reasoning Model and Physical AI Toolkit at GTC Taipei

At GTC Taipei / COMPUTEX 2026, Nvidia also unveiled Alpamayo 2, an open reasoning model optimized for robotaxi decision-making, alongside DRIVE Hyperion as a global robotaxi platform, the Isaac GR00T reference humanoid robot for academic research, and a factory operations AI blueprint. The breadth of releases signals Nvidia is building a full-stack physical AI platform—from silicon through simulation to deployment.
