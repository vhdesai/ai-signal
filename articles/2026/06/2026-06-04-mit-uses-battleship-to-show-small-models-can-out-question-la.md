# MIT Uses “Battleship” to Show Small Models Can Out-Question Large Ones

MIT CSAIL and Harvard SEAS used Battleship as a testbed for agent inquiry under uncertainty, finding a small model lifted win rate from ~8% to 82% at ~1% cost. The work targets medical diagnosis and scientific discovery where strategic questioning outweighs raw scale.
