# DeepSeek Seals ~$7.4B Round Led by Tencent and CATL, with National AI Fund

DeepSeek is finalizing ~50 billion yuan (~$7.4B) in one of China’s largest-ever startup financings. Tencent and CATL are the two largest investors, with the state-backed National AI fund participating. CATL’s involvement signals Chinese industrial conglomerates view AI as strategically adjacent to their core businesses.
