# Trending Salesforce to acquire AI customer-service firm Fin for $3.6B

Bloomberg reported Salesforce has agreed to acquire Fin, an AI-native customer-service company, for $3.6B. The acquisition extends Salesforce's Agentforce agentic stack through an AI-native support vendor, accelerating its push beyond CRM into fully automated customer operations. Deal terms and formal Salesforce confirmation were not independently verified at publication time; treat specifics as Bloomberg-sourced pending official announcement.
