# Google Lays Off Cloud and Cybersecurity Staff While Doubling Down on AI

Google is quietly laying off Cloud division staff, including cybersecurity threat analysis team members, even as it pours billions into AI infrastructure. The cuts highlight resource reallocation at hyperscalers: headcount in traditional cloud and security is being compressed to fund AI compute.
