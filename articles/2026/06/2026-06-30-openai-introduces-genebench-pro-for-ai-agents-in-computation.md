# OpenAI introduces GeneBench-Pro for AI agents in computational biology

OpenAI released GeneBench-Pro, a research-level benchmark measuring how AI agents navigate ambiguity and make consequential judgments in computational biology. It expands GeneBench with 129 synthetically constructed, judgment-heavy tasks across genomics, quantitative biology, and translational medicine, and open-sources representative questions. OpenAI defines a "research taste" axis to test whether models choose the correct analytical path under uncertainty. https://openai.com/index/introducing-genebench-pro/
