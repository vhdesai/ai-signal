# WSJ: "The Artificial Intelligence Backlash"

The WSJ published a major feature on the emerging AI backlash, documenting how public sentiment, political opposition, and community resistance to data centers are converging into a broader anti-AI movement. The piece arrives as New York passes a data center moratorium, Politico warns that "guardrails may be too late," and anti-tech extremism rises. For executives, the analysis frames reputational and regulatory risk as an increasingly material concern. URL behind paywall.
