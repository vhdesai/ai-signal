# Washington gates OpenAI’s GPT-5.6 behind customer-by-customer government approval

Per a staff memo reported by The Information, OpenAI will initially release GPT-5.6 only to parties approved by the federal government, with leaders “approving access customer by customer during this preview period” before a wider release weeks later. Altman reportedly told employees this “is not our preferred long term model” and that OpenAI will push for a more sustainable approach — signaling an emerging de facto licensing posture for frontier releases.
