# Groq Confirms $650M Funding Round

Inference chip maker Groq confirmed a $650M raise, reinforcing investor appetite for custom silicon alternatives to NVIDIA's dominance. The round arrives as enterprises increasingly demand low-latency, cost-efficient inference at scale—a market segment growing faster than training compute.
