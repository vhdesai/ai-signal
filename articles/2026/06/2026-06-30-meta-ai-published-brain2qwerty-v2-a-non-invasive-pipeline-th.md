# Meta AI published Brain2Qwerty v2, a non-invasive pipeline that decodes typed sentences directly from…

Meta AI published Brain2Qwerty v2, a non-invasive pipeline that decodes typed sentences directly from magnetoencephalography (MEG) brain activity—no implanted electrodes required. The system reaches roughly 61% word-level accuracy, a meaningful advance for non-invasive neural decoding. It was the day's lead "new release" on MarkTechPost.
