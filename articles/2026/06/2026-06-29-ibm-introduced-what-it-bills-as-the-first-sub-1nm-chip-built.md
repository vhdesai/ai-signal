# IBM introduced what it bills as the first sub‑1nm chip, built on a new transistor architecture at the 0.7nm…

IBM introduced what it bills as the first sub‑1nm chip, built on a new transistor architecture at the 0.7nm (7‑angstrom) node. IBM says the chip packs nearly 100 billion transistors onto a fingernail-sized die — roughly twice the density of its 2021 2nm chip — a milestone as the industry nears the physical limits of traditional scaling.
