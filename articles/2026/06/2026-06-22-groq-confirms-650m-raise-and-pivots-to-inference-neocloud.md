# Groq Confirms $650M Raise and Pivots to Inference "Neocloud"

Groq closed a $650M round led by Disruptive and Infinitum, ~6 months after Nvidia licensed its core LPU technology and hired away founder Jonathan Ross (~$20B "not-acqui-hire"). Now leaning into a 13-data-center inference cloud with 5M+ developers. The episode highlights how incumbents absorb challenger IP through licensing-plus-talent deals.
