# Sina Weibo released VibeThinker‑3B, a 3-billion-parameter open model that matches systems up to ~333× larger (DeepSeek…

The team credits multi-stage post-training rather than scale, arguing that logical reasoning compresses well into small models while broad world knowledge does not. It was the only confirmed net-new frontier model inside the 24-hour window.
