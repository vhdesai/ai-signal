# OpenAI's GPT-5.6 family (Sol, Terra, Luna) stays limited to government-approved partners

OpenAI's new three-tier GPT-5.6 lineup — flagship Sol, balanced Terra, and low-cost Luna — remained restricted to a "small group of trusted partners" through the weekend after the Trump administration asked OpenAI to hold back a broad release. Sol is positioned as OpenAI's strongest model yet for coding, biology and cybersecurity, with a new "max" reasoning effort and an "ultra" multi-agent mode; OpenAI publicly pushed back on the access process even as it complied. The company expects wider availability "in the coming weeks" while it negotiates a repeatable federal review framework.
