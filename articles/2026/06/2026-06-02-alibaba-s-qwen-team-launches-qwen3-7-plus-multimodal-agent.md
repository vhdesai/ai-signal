# Alibaba's Qwen team launches Qwen3.7-Plus multimodal agent

Alibaba released Qwen3.7-Plus on its Bailian platform, a multimodal agent model that understands images and video and adds self-programming, deep reasoning, tool invocation, and autonomous iteration. It is positioned for agentic enterprise workflows rather than single-turn tasks. The release is distinct from the earlier Qwen3.7-Max (May 21). https://www.marktechpost.com/category/editors-pick/new-releases/
