# MIT CSAIL researchers introduced "Masked IRL," a method that helps robots learn tasks from human "show and tell"…

MIT CSAIL researchers introduced "Masked IRL," a method that helps robots learn tasks from human "show and tell" demonstrations. One LLM first elaborates on a user's ambiguous spoken instructions using demonstration data; a second model then narrows down which details a motion‑planning algorithm should actually use, letting the robot ignore irrelevant information and act safely. The approach targets real‑world chores in homes, warehouses, and factories where instructions are naturally imprecise.
