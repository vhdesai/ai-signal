# Samsung Deploys ChatGPT Enterprise and Codex — One of OpenAI's Largest Rollouts

Samsung Electronics will make ChatGPT Enterprise and Codex available to all employees in South Korea and its entire global Device eXperience division. OpenAI described it as one of its largest enterprise deployments. Samsung plans use across software development, marketing, manufacturing, and corporate support.
