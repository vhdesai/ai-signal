# Anthropic, Previously Blacklisted, Eases White House Tensions Ahead of IPO

Reuters reported that Anthropic—which had been blacklisted by the White House—has quietly eased tensions with the administration ahead of its IPO. The rapprochement is significant: Anthropic needs regulatory goodwill for a smooth public listing, while the White House needs cooperation from frontier labs on its voluntary safety framework. The backstory of a near-trillion-dollar company resolving a government blacklisting weeks before going public underscores how entangled AI business and policy have become. URL not verified.
