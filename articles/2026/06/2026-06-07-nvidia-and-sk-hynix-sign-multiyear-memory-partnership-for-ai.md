# ### Nvidia and SK Hynix Sign Multiyear Memory Partnership for AI Factories

Nvidia and SK hynix announced a multiyear agreement to co-develop next-generation memory aligned to Nvidia's roadmap — Vera Rubin supercomputers, Vera CPUs, RTX Spark PCs, Jetson Thor robotics. The deal secures advanced-memory supply at a moment when memory, not logic, is the binding constraint on AI-factory scale-out. Extends to AI-accelerated chip design (CUDA-X, PhysicsNeMo) and Omniverse-based fab digital twins. - [NVIDIA Newsroom](https://nvidianews.nvidia.com/news/sk-hynix-ai-factory) - [Business Insider](https://markets.businessinsider.com/news/stocks/nvidia-and-sk-hynix-announce-multiyear-technology-partnership-to-advance-memory-for-ai-factories-1036230519) --- **Tags:** `HOT` `NEW`
