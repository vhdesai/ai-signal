# Nvidia and SK Hynix Sign Multiyear Memory Partnership for AI Factories

Nvidia and SK hynix announced a multiyear agreement to co-develop next-generation memory aligned to Nvidia's roadmap — Vera Rubin supercomputers, Vera CPUs, RTX Spark PCs, Jetson Thor robotics. The deal secures advanced-memory supply at a moment when memory, not logic, is the binding constraint on AI-factory scale-out. Extends to AI-accelerated chip design (CUDA-X, PhysicsNeMo) and Omniverse-based fab digital twins.
