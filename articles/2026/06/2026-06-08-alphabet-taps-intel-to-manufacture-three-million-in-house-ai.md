# Alphabet Taps Intel to Manufacture Three Million In-House AI Chips

Alphabet tapped Intel to manufacture three million in-house AI chips — a significant win for Intel's foundry ambitions and Google's effort to diversify its chip supply chain beyond TSMC. Validates Intel's 18A process as production-ready for a major hyperscaler customer.
