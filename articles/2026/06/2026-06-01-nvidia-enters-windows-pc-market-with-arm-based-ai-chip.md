# ### Nvidia Enters Windows PC Market with Arm-Based AI Chip

Nvidia announced its first processor for Windows personal computers—an Arm-based chip designed around on-device AI workloads—debuting in laptops from Microsoft, Dell, and HP. The move positions Nvidia as a direct competitor to Intel and AMD in the PC silicon market and reflects a strategic bet that personal AI computing will require GPU-class inference on the edge, not just in the cloud. --- **Tags:** `HOT` `NEW`
