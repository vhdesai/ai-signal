# Sam Altman Admits Token Costs Are “a Huge Issue”; Rules Out 2026 Election Spending

Sam Altman publicly acknowledged AI token costs are becoming “a huge issue,” with OpenAI’s top customer consuming 100 billion tokens monthly. On a June 3 Capitol Hill visit—including a meeting with Senator Sanders—he ruled out 2026 midterm spending, positioning OpenAI as a policy interlocutor rather than partisan funder ahead of its IPO filing.
