# SpaceX Prices Record IPO at $135/Share, Targeting $1.77T Valuation with xAI at the Center

SpaceX set its IPO price at a fixed $135 per share—bypassing the traditional pricing range—targeting at least $74.4 billion in net proceeds (rising to $85.7B with the greenshoe) and a $1.77 trillion market cap. The prospectus frames a $28.5 trillion addressable market, the vast majority in AI through xAI. However, Morningstar recently valued SpaceX significantly lower, citing AI business costs and risks, and xAI is considered behind Anthropic and OpenAI. The listing will absorb enormous investor capital, raising the stakes for upcoming AI IPOs.
