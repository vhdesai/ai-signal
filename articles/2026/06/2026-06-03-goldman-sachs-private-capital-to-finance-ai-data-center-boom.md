# Goldman Sachs: Private Capital to Finance AI Data Center Boom

Goldman Sachs projected that private infrastructure and real estate capital will play an increasingly central role in financing the AI data center buildout, as traditional tech balance sheets prove insufficient. The report aligns with Alphabet’s $80B capital raise and the broader trend of sovereign wealth funds and private equity entering the AI infrastructure stack. URL not verified.
