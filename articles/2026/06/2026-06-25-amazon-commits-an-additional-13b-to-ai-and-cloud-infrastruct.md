# Amazon commits an additional $13B to AI and cloud infrastructure in India

Amazon said it will invest a further $13 billion through 2030 to expand AWS data-center capacity in Mumbai and Hyderabad, announced after CEO Andy Jassy met India’s Prime Minister Modi. The commitment brings Amazon’s cumulative India pledges to roughly $48 billion, tracking a broader race among hyperscalers to secure AI compute footprint in the country.
