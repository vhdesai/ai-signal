# Anthropic Says Claude May Ask Users to Verify Their Government ID

A revised privacy policy (effective July 8) states Anthropic may request government-issued ID in "certain circumstances." Framed as an appeal path for flagged accounts rather than an outright ban. Lands amid the Trump administration standoff over who may access Anthropic's tools. Material implications for enterprise data-governance and regional access.
