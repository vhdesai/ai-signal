# Trump Signs Sweeping Quantum Computing Executive Orders

President Trump signed two executive orders Monday: one directing federal agencies to develop a government-hosted quantum computer by 2028 via public-private partnerships, and another mandating cryptographic safeguards against quantum attacks on critical infrastructure. The Commerce Department announced $2B in grants including equity stakes in quantum companies. The orders explicitly link quantum supremacy to national security alongside AI leadership.
