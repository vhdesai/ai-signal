# Anthropic Expands Glasswing to ~200 Partners; Maps Year of AI Cyberattacks onto MITRE ATT&CK

Project Glasswing expanded to ~200 organizations (including Okta, Samsung, SK Hynix, NATO, ENISA) across 15+ countries, surfacing 10,000+ critical flaws. Separately, Anthropic’s Red Team mapped 832 banned accounts onto MITRE ATT&CK, finding AI-enabled attacks growing more autonomous. Portions appeared in Verizon’s 2026 DBIR.
