# ### Ardian and Verne target a €5B, 500MW AI gigafactory near Paris

Ardian and Verne unveiled plans for a 500MW data-center campus near Paris, a roughly €5B build with a first 200MW phase targeted by 2030. The project is tied to the AION consortium's bid for an EU "AI gigafactory," part of Europe's push to localize frontier-scale compute. The scale and timeline signal that European sovereign-compute ambitions are moving from policy rhetoric to financed construction. **Tags:** `FUNDING` `NETWORKING`
