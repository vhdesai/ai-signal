# OpenAI Publishes Governance Blueprint; Altman Urges Against Mandatory Model Approvals

OpenAI published “A Blueprint for Democratic Governance of Frontier AI,” advocating a single federal regime over state-level patchwork while opposing mandatory pre-release approvals—diverging from the Trump EO’s voluntary review framework.
