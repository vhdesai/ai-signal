# Politico: "It's a Hurricane Warning"—AI Guardrails May Be Too Late

Politico published a long-form analysis arguing that the window for establishing effective guardrails around powerful AI models may have already closed. The piece draws on Anthropic's pause call, the recursive self-improvement warning, and the pace of model releases to build the case that regulatory frameworks are structurally lagging capability development. For policy-engaged executives, the piece captures the growing disconnect between safety rhetoric and deployment reality. URL not verified.
