# Anthropic launches Claude Tag, a persistent Claude teammate inside Slack

Anthropic launched Claude Tag, replacing its existing Claude-in-Slack app with a persistent, shared AI teammate that anyone on a team can delegate work to by typing @Claude. Available in beta for Claude Enterprise and Team customers, it reads threads, breaks down tasks, retains context across conversations, and surfaces relevant updates from across an organization under its own identity. It is Anthropic's most aggressive move yet into the enterprise collaboration layer — where work is assigned and institutional knowledge accumulates in real time. https://venturebeat.com/technology/anthropic-launches-claude-tag-replacing-its-slack-app-with-a-persistent-ai-teammate-that-learns-monitors-and-works-autonomously
