# Z.ai launches GLM-5.2 with a usable 1M-token context and two reasoning-effort levels

Zhipu AI's Z.ai released GLM-5.2, notable for a genuinely usable 1M-token context window and two selectable thinking-effort levels, shipped without benchmark numbers at launch. No monitored frontier lab (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Meta, Mistral, xAI, DeepSeek) released a new frontier model inside the window — a relatively quiet period for top-tier model launches following the June 8–9 wave (Apple AFM 3, Claude Fable 5).
