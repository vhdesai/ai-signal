# Unitree’s H2 Plus gives academic robotics a NVIDIA Isaac GR00T reference platform

Unitree announced H2 Plus, a humanoid robot positioned as an NVIDIA Isaac GR00T reference platform for academic research. The significance is standardization: embodied-AI progress depends on comparable hardware and software stacks for evaluating policies, simulation-to-real transfer, and robot learning. A tighter NVIDIA robotics stack could accelerate university and lab experimentation in physical AI.
