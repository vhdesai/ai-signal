# ### Apollo and Blackstone Finalize $35B Debt Deal to Supercharge Anthropic's AI Infrastructure

Apollo and Blackstone finalized a $35 billion debt facility for Anthropic — the largest AI-specific debt deal to date — to fund data center buildout ahead of IPO. Private credit is stepping in as a major capital source, complementing equity raises from Alphabet ($85B), Meta (planned), and DeepSeek ($7.4B).
