# ### OpenAI Confidentially Files for IPO, Following Anthropic and SpaceX

OpenAI confirmed it has submitted a confidential draft S-1 to the SEC, joining Anthropic and SpaceX in the largest wave of tech IPOs in history. Fortune quoted the company: "We expect it to leak so we're just announcing it." Perplexity's CEO cheered the filings and announced 2028 IPO plans. All three major frontier labs will soon face public-market disclosure and quarterly earnings scrutiny. - [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/06/08/following-anthropic-openai-files-confidentially-for-ipo/) - [Yahoo Finance](https://finance.yahoo.com/markets/stocks/articles/openai-submits-confidential-ipo-filing-084707842.html) --- **Tags:** `HOT`
