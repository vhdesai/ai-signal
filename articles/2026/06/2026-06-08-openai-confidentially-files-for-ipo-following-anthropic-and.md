# OpenAI Confidentially Files for IPO, Following Anthropic and SpaceX

OpenAI confirmed it has submitted a confidential draft S-1 to the SEC, joining Anthropic and SpaceX in the largest wave of tech IPOs in history. Fortune quoted the company: "We expect it to leak so we're just announcing it." Perplexity's CEO cheered the filings and announced 2028 IPO plans. All three major frontier labs will soon face public-market disclosure and quarterly earnings scrutiny.
