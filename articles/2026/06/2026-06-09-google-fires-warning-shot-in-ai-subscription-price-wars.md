# Google Fires Warning Shot in AI Subscription Price Wars

Google cut pricing on AI subscriptions, in what TechCrunch called "a warning shot." The move pressures OpenAI, Anthropic, and Microsoft at a moment when enterprise buyers are rebelling against token costs. Combined with DeepSeek's low-end traction, the pricing squeeze is tightening from both directions.
