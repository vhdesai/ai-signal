# ### Google Fires Warning Shot in AI Subscription Price Wars

Google cut pricing on AI subscriptions, in what TechCrunch called "a warning shot." The move pressures OpenAI, Anthropic, and Microsoft at a moment when enterprise buyers are rebelling against token costs. Combined with DeepSeek's low-end traction, the pricing squeeze is tightening from both directions. - [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/06/09/google-just-fired-a-warning-shot-in-the-ai-subscription-price-wars/) --- **Tags:** `NEW`
