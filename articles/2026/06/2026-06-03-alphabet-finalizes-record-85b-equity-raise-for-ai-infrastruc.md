# Alphabet Finalizes Record $85B Equity Raise for AI Infrastructure

Alphabet finalized an $85 billion equity raise—the largest in technology history. Barron’s noted Meta and other tech giants could follow. The raise funds data center expansion, custom silicon, and AI research, validating that even the largest hyperscalers cannot self-fund the AI buildout from operating cash flow alone.
