# Per a Financial Times report, Google told Meta around March that it could not supply the full Gemini capacity Meta…

Per a Financial Times report, Google told Meta around March that it could not supply the full Gemini capacity Meta sought to purchase, disrupting and delaying some of Meta's internal AI projects and prompting Meta to tell staff to conserve "AI tokens." Meta had relied on Gemini — which outperformed its own Llama models — for content-moderation and safety workloads, and is now shifting them to its internal Muse Spark model. The episode underscores that even hyperscalers spending $180B+ in capex cannot fully meet AI demand; Google is separately renting roughly 110,000 GPUs from SpaceX as bridge capacity.
