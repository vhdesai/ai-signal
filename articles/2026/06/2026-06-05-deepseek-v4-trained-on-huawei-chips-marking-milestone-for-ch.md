# DeepSeek V4 Trained on Huawei Chips, Marking Milestone for China's AI Self-Reliance

DeepSeek confirmed that its V4 model was trained on Huawei AI chips, after earlier success using the same chips for inference. The South China Morning Post framed this as a leap for China's AI self-reliance, demonstrating that domestic hardware can support frontier model training—not just inference. For the geopolitical AI landscape, the milestone weakens the assumption that U.S. export controls on advanced chips will durably constrain Chinese AI development. URL not verified.
