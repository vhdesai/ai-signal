# Tencent Poaches Former OpenAI Researcher as New AI Chief, Targets AGI

Tencent appointed Yao Shunyu — a former OpenAI researcher — as its new AI chief, with a mandate to build AGI using "OpenAI's playbook." The hire signals Tencent is moving from cautious AI integration to an aggressive frontier research posture, part of a broader pattern of China poaching U.S. AI talent.
