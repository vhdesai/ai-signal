# Trump Calls for Military to Accelerate Use of AI

President Trump called for the military to accelerate its adoption of AI technology, with the Washington Post framing it as a directive to fast-track AI integration across defense operations. The announcement follows the Pentagon's ongoing effort to rewrite its AI procurement processes and aligns with the administration's broader push to position the U.S. as the global leader in AI-driven defense capabilities.
