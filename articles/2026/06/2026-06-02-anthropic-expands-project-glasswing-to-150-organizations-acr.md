# Anthropic Expands Project Glasswing to 150 Organizations Across 15+ Countries

Anthropic scaled its restricted defensive-cybersecurity program, Project Glasswing, from roughly 50 to 150 partner organizations in more than 15 countries. An earlier first-month report cited 23,019 vulnerabilities found across 1,000+ open-source projects, with ~90.6% confirmed real. The expansion underscores the dual-use tension: the same capability that hardens critical infrastructure is being deliberately kept out of general release.
