# AI Music Startup Suno Raises Capital at $5.4B Valuation

Suno closed roughly $400M at a $5.4B valuation, a sharp markup that reflects continued investor appetite for generative-media platforms despite unresolved music-licensing litigation. The raise positions Suno to scale compute and pursue rights deals with labels. The valuation is a useful datapoint on how the market is pricing consumer generative-media risk relative to ongoing copyright exposure.
