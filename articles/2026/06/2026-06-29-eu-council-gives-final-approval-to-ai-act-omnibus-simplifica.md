# EU Council gives final approval to AI Act “Omnibus” simplification

The Council of the EU gave final adoption to the “Omnibus VII” simplification package, formally delaying high-risk AI obligations to December 2, 2027 (stand-alone systems) and August 2, 2028 (embedded in products), and pushing national regulatory-sandbox deadlines to August 2027. The same law adds a new prohibition on AI-generated non-consensual intimate imagery and CSAM (effective December 2026) and shortens the synthetic-content labeling grace period to three months. It is the first substantive amendment to the AI Act since 2024 — and note that August 2, 2026 still activates GPAI penalty powers and Article 50 transparency duties, a live compliance trigger for anyone in the EU market.
