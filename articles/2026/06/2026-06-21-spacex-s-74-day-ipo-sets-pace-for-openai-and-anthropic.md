# SpaceX's 74-Day IPO Sets Pace for OpenAI and Anthropic

SpaceX went from confidential filing to listing in just 74 days—far faster than CoreWeave, Figma, or Airbnb. IPO lawyers attribute the speed to a more hands-off SEC under the Trump administration. The precedent is directly relevant to OpenAI and Anthropic, both preparing for public offerings.
