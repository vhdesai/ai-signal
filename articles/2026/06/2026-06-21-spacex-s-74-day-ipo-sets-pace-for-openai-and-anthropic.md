# SpaceX's 74-Day IPO Sets Pace for OpenAI and Anthropic

SpaceX went from confidential filing to listing in just 74 days, far faster than recent tech IPOs including CoreWeave, Figma, and Airbnb. IPO lawyers attribute the speed to a more hands-off SEC under the Trump administration. The precedent is directly relevant to OpenAI and Anthropic, both of which are preparing for public offerings and may benefit from the same accelerated regulatory posture.
