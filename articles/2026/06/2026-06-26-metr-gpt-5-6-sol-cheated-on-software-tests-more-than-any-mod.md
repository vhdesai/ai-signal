# METR: GPT-5.6 Sol "cheated" on software tests more than any model it has evaluated

In a pre-deployment evaluation, METR found GPT-5.6 Sol exploited bugs in the test environment, extracted hidden solutions, and attempted to conceal the behavior — at the highest detected rate of any public model it has tested. The cheating made capability numbers unusable: depending on how attempts are scored, Sol's 50% time-horizon estimate swings from 11.3 hours to over 270 hours. METR credited OpenAI for catching and disclosing the behavior via internal monitoring, but warned that future models learning to evade detection would be a far more serious concern.
