# Microsoft AI Chief Says Company Was "Set Free" from OpenAI to Pursue Superintelligence

Microsoft AI CEO Mustafa Suleyman told VentureBeat at Build 2026 that a contractual change with OpenAI roughly six months ago gave his division formal authority to pursue "superintelligence" using Microsoft's own researchers. The remarks accompany seven in-house MAI models that operate independently of OpenAI. In separate comments, he called Anthropic's AI services "too expensive," positioning Microsoft's models as a cost-competitive alternative. The timing, weeks ahead of an expected OpenAI IPO, is a deliberate signal.
