# Ramp/Revelio study: heavy AI adopters grew headcount ~10%, not shrank it

A working paper from Ramp and Revelio Labs — the first to link firm-level AI spending to workforce records across 21,559 U.S. firms — found high-intensity AI adopters grew headcount ~10.2% over the two years after adoption, with entry-level roles up 12%; low-intensity adopters saw no significant change. The authors caution it doesn't prove AI "mechanically" creates jobs, since adopters were already larger and faster-growing.
