# Uber Caps Employee AI Spending After Blowing Through Annual Budget in Four Months

Uber has imposed caps on employee AI tool spending after burning through its full-year AI budget in just four months. The story offers a concrete data point on “AI sticker shock”: enterprise AI adoption is running far ahead of budgets, with usage-based pricing creating unpredictable cost curves. Reuters Breakingviews published a parallel analysis arguing corporate AI cost overruns will force restraint.
