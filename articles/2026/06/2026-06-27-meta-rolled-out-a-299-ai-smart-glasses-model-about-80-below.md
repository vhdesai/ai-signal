# Meta rolled out a $299 AI smart‑glasses model — about $80 below its prior entry tier — positioning the device as its…

Meta rolled out a $299 AI smart‑glasses model — about $80 below its prior entry tier — positioning the device as its strongest consumer on‑ramp for AI even as it absorbs roughly $20B/year in AI losses. With at least 7M glasses sold last year, the company is betting the form factor becomes a catalyst for a stock down about 17% year‑to‑date.
