# NYC funding day: Norm AI raises $120M, Taktile raises $110M

Two enterprise-AI rounds led the day’s New York deal flow: Norm AI ($120M, per an SEC filing) embeds regulatory rules into AI agents to automate legal and compliance workflows, and Taktile ($110M Series C, led by Goldman Sachs Alternatives’ Growth Equity) automates high-stakes financial decisions with agents. Both target regulated-industry automation — a segment drawing outsized capital as buyers prioritize auditable, controllable agent deployments.
