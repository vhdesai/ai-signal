# Wimbledon rolls out new IBM AI features for live match coverage

As first-round play began Monday, the All England Club switched on new IBM-built AI features across the Wimbledon app and website — an upgraded “Match Chat” natural-language assistant and a “Key Moments” feature, both surfaced through IBM Slamtracker. The work is part of a five-year digital-transformation engagement focused on consolidating services in-house and reducing technical debt. With ~730M engagements and 18B impressions last year, it is a high-visibility reference deployment for IBM's enterprise AI.
