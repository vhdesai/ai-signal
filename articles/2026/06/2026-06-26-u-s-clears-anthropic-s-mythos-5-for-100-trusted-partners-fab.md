# U.S. clears Anthropic's Mythos 5 for ~100 trusted partners; Fable 5 stays dark

The Commerce Department granted Anthropic permission to release its Mythos 5 model to roughly 100 vetted companies and federal agencies that "operate and defend critical infrastructure," easing a two-week standoff that began when an export-control directive forced Anthropic to pull Mythos 5 and the public Fable 5 offline worldwide. Commerce Secretary Howard Lutnick's letter — addressed to co-founder Tom Brown, who is now leading the company's Washington negotiations — did not restore Fable 5. The clearance landed the same day OpenAI accepted a parallel government-gated rollout for GPT-5.6, underscoring a new federal posture toward frontier-model releases.
