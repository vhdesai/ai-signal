# ### Reuters: Will AI Become a Public Utility?

Reuters published an analysis exploring whether AI should be regulated as a public utility, given its growing role in critical infrastructure, healthcare, education, and government services. The piece draws on the government equity-stake debate, Anthropic's pause call, and the "Great American AI Act" to argue that the utility model may be the logical endpoint of current policy trajectories.
