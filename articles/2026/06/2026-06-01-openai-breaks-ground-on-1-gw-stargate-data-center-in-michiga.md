# OpenAI Breaks Ground on 1 GW Stargate Data Center in Michigan

OpenAI announced a 1-gigawatt data-center project in Michigan as part of Stargate, framing it as infrastructure for the "Intelligence Age." The project exemplifies frontier labs moving directly into physical infrastructure, power procurement, and regional economic development — while making the AI buildout politically legible through jobs and community investment.
