# Apple and Microsoft raise hardware prices as AI demand drives up chip costs

Apple said it will raise prices on certain MacBooks and iPads by up to $300, and Microsoft announced Xbox console price increases effective August 1, with both citing surging memory and storage chip costs. “We have never seen a component price increase this much, this quickly,” Apple said. The increases trace directly to AI data-center expansion competing for memory and storage capacity — a sign that AI infrastructure costs are now reaching consumers.
