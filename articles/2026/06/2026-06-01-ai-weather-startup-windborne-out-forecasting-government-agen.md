# AI Weather Startup WindBorne Out-Forecasting Government Agencies

WindBorne Systems is outperforming government forecasting agencies by combining model-building with proprietary atmospheric data from hundreds of sensor-equipped balloons deployed globally. The case demonstrates that differentiated data pipelines can matter as much as model architecture in scientific and operational forecasting — one of the strongest recent applied-AI signals outside enterprise software.
