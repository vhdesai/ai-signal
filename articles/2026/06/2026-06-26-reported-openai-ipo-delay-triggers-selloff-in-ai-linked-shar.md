# Reported OpenAI IPO delay triggers selloff in AI-linked shares

Shares of Japanese NAND-flash maker Kioxia fell about 12% on Friday after reports that OpenAI is considering delaying its IPO, sparking a broader selloff in AI-exposed equities. Separate reporting indicated OpenAI’s advisers are urging CEO Sam Altman to move cautiously following volatility in related stocks — underscoring how tightly public-market sentiment is now coupled to OpenAI’s financing timeline.
