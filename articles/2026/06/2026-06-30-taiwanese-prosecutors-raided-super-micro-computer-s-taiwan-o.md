# Taiwanese prosecutors raided Super Micro Computer's Taiwan offices and two other firms as part of an expanding…

Taiwanese prosecutors raided Super Micro Computer's Taiwan offices and two other firms as part of an expanding investigation into alleged smuggling of high-end AI servers containing advanced Nvidia chips to China, Macau, and Hong Kong in violation of US export controls. Nine people are now under investigation. The case underscores intensifying enforcement around the global AI-hardware supply chain.
