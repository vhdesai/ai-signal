# AI fuels a record $200bn M&A boom in the US power sector

The Financial Times reports a record ~$200B in M&A across the U.S. power and utilities sector, as companies race to secure the generation and grid capacity needed to feed AI data centers. The surge marks a structural repricing of a historically slow-moving industry and tightens the link between AI strategy and energy strategy. Power availability — not chips alone — is emerging as the gating factor for data-center expansion. URL not verified.
