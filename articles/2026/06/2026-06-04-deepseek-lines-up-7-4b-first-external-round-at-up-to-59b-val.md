# DeepSeek Lines Up ~$7.4B First External Round at Up to $59B Valuation

DeepSeek is raising ~50B yuan (~$7.4B) in its first-ever outside funding at $49–59B valuation. Founder Liang Wenfeng is anchoring with a large personal commitment alongside fewer than 10 investors, with Tencent and CATL among backers. The structure — concentrated founder control, minimal dilution — contrasts with the IPO path Anthropic and OpenAI are pursuing.
