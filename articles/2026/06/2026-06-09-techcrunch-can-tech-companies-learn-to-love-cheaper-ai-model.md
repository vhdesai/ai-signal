# ### TechCrunch: Can Tech Companies Learn to Love Cheaper AI Models?

TechCrunch published an analysis examining whether the AI industry can adapt to a future where cheaper models—rather than the most capable ones—capture the majority of enterprise spend. The piece argues that the current pricing crisis will force a stratification of the market: frontier models for high-stakes applications, cheaper models for everything else. For enterprise strategists, the implication is that multi-model procurement will become the norm rather than single-vendor standardization. --- **Tags:** `TRENDING`
