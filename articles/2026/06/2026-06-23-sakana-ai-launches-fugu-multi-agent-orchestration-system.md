# Sakana AI Launches "Fugu" Multi-Agent Orchestration System

Tokyo-based Sakana AI (valued at $2.6B) launched Fugu, a system that coordinates multiple AI models through a single interface. Benchmarks show performance comparable to Anthropic's models on coding and reasoning tasks. The release marks a credible multi-agent challenger from outside the US-China duopoly, reinforcing the trend of "agents that prompt agents" becoming the dominant orchestration paradigm.
