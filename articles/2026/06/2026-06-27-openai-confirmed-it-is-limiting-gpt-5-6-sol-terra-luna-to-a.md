# OpenAI confirmed it is limiting GPT‑5.6 (Sol, Terra, Luna) to a small group of trusted partners at the U.S

OpenAI confirmed it is limiting GPT‑5.6 (Sol, Terra, Luna) to a small group of trusted partners at the U.S. government's behest, sharing participation with officials before any broader release. The company pushed back publicly, warning that such a government‑access process should not become the long‑term default because it "keeps the best tools from users, developers, enterprises, cyber defenders, and global partners." OpenAI also published a GPT‑5.6 Preview System Card the same day.
