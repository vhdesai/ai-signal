# Google was forced to limit Meta's consumption of Gemini after Meta blew through its allotted compute capacity, sources…

Google was forced to limit Meta's consumption of Gemini after Meta blew through its allotted compute capacity, sources told the Financial Times. Google reportedly warned Meta about the ceiling back in March, prompting Meta to ask staff to use tokens more efficiently. The episode is a striking signal that even hyperscalers with their own frontier models are compute-constrained — Meta, which lacks a cloud business, has pledged roughly $600B in cloud and data-center buildout over two years to close the gap.
