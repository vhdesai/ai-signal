# Anthropic confidentially files for U.S. IPO, edging ahead of OpenAI

Anthropic said it has confidentially submitted a draft S-1 to the SEC, setting up one of the most consequential AI listings to date and getting ahead of rival OpenAI, which is preparing its own filing. The move follows a $65 billion raise that lifted its valuation to roughly $965 billion, with an annualized revenue run rate reported at $47 billion. A confidential submission lets the company advance preparations while shielding financials, with no fixed timeline to trade.
