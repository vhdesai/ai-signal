# SpaceX Signs $6.3B Compute Deal with Reflection AI; Shares Fall 10%

Reflection AI agreed to pay SpaceX $150M/month from July 2026 through 2029 for Nvidia GB300 access at Colossus 2 near Memphis, with a 90-day exit clause. SpaceX's third major compute tenant after Anthropic ($1.25B/month) and Google ($920M/month). Despite the deal, SPCX fell ~10% on margin concerns — cementing that investors are scrutinizing AI capex intensity.
