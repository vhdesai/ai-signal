# ### Global Financial Watchdog Calls for Tighter Controls on Agentic AI in Finance

Global financial regulators are calling for tighter controls on agentic AI in financial services, citing systemic risks from autonomous agents making trading, lending, and compliance decisions. Adds to sector-specific regulation alongside the EU AI Act, the Great American AI Act, and New York's disclosure requirements. --- **Tags:** `NEW`
