# Anthropic asks Congress to act, alleging a large-scale Alibaba "distillation attack"

In a June 10 letter to the Senate Banking Committee (seen by CNBC), Anthropic accused Alibaba’s AI lab and affiliates of using ~25,000 fake accounts to run nearly 29 million interactions over six weeks to "distill" Claude’s capabilities — the largest such attack it has recorded. Anthropic framed illicit distillation as turning U.S. R&D into "a massive subsidy for our geopolitical competitors" and urged coordinated government-industry action. It escalates the U.S.–China model-IP fight that began with similar claims against DeepSeek, Moonshot and MiniMax.
