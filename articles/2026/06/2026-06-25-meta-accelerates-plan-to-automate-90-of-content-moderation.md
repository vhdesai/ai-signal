# Meta accelerates plan to automate 90%+ of content moderation

Per Financial Times reporting summarized this Thursday, Meta has shifted roughly half of content-review tasks to large language models and aims to exceed 90% AI-driven moderation for certain categories before year-end — an acceleration of a previously multi-year, human-in-the-loop plan. The shift accompanies broader cost cuts (≈8,000 roles eliminated) and Zuckerberg’s "personal superintelligence" push. A recent AI chatbot security incident has raised questions about whether the transition is moving too fast for the nuance moderation requires.
