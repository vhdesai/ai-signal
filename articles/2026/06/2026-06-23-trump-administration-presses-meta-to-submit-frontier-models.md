# Trump administration presses Meta to submit frontier models for federal testing

Administration officials are urging Meta to join a voluntary program giving federal agencies pre-release access to advanced models for national-security evaluation, the NYT reported. Meta — whose Muse Spark model launched in April — is reportedly the last major U.S. developer not yet participating; OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Microsoft, and xAI have already agreed. Meta says it supports the effort and expects to finalize an agreement soon, signaling that pre-deployment government review is becoming an industry norm. URL behind paywall.
