# OpenAI previews GPT‑5.6 family — Sol, Terra, and Luna — in a limited release

OpenAI launched a limited preview of GPT‑5.6 across three tiers: Sol, its strongest model to date with agentic gains in coding, biology, and cybersecurity; Terra, a balanced everyday model with GPT‑5.5-class performance at roughly half the cost; and Luna, its lowest-cost option. The release introduces a new “max” reasoning effort and an “ultra” mode that orchestrates sub-agents for complex work. OpenAI says GPT‑5.6 Sol ships with its most robust safety stack yet, positioned to aid defensive security while limiting offensive misuse.
