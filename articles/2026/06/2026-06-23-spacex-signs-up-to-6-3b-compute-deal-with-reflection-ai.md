# SpaceX Signs Up to $6.3B Compute Deal with Reflection AI

Open-source AI startup Reflection will pay SpaceX $150M/month for compute access from its Colossus 2 data center, with the contract running July 2029 (cancelable with 90 days' notice). For context, Anthropic reportedly pays $1.25B/month and Google $920M/month to SpaceX. The deal cements SpaceX as a hyperscale compute landlord and underscores that infrastructure access—not model architecture—is the binding constraint for frontier labs.
