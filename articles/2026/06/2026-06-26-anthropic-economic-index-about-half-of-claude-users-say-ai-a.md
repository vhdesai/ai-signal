# Anthropic Economic Index: about half of Claude users say AI already handles half their work

Anthropic's sixth Economic Index ("Cadences") pairs hourly usage telemetry and a new artifact classifier with a linked survey of roughly 9,700 users. About half of respondents say AI can already handle 50% or more of their work tasks, and roughly a third expect it to cover most of their tasks within 12 months. Notably, the heaviest "delegators" are the most optimistic about pay and job security, while 33% believe junior colleagues face a high probability of displacement.
