# Anthropic accuses Alibaba's Qwen lab of largest-ever Claude "distillation" campaign

In a letter to the Senate Banking Committee — dated June 10 and made public this week — Anthropic alleged that operators tied to Alibaba's Qwen lab opened roughly 25,000 fraudulent accounts and ran more than 28.8 million Claude conversations between April 22 and June 5 to "distill" its frontier coding and agentic-reasoning capabilities. Anthropic framed the effort as an attempt to convert hundreds of billions of dollars of U.S. AI investment into a subsidy for a strategic competitor. The claims are unverified by outside parties, and Alibaba has not addressed the specifics; the dispute sharpens the IP-and-national-security backdrop to Anthropic's IPO plans.
