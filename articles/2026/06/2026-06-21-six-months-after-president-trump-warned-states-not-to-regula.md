# Six months after President Trump warned states not to regulate AI, they are increasingly doing so

Six months after President Trump warned states not to regulate AI, they are increasingly doing so. With federal legislation stalled, states including Connecticut, Rhode Island, and others have enacted or proposed laws covering AI in employment decisions, legal practice, and consumer protection. The piece notes a growing regulatory patchwork that could create compliance complexity for AI companies operating nationally.
