# Paul Meade, Apple VP of hardware engineering for the Vision Products Group and lead on Apple's forthcoming smart…

Paul Meade, Apple VP of hardware engineering for the Vision Products Group and lead on Apple's forthcoming smart glasses, is reportedly leaving next week to join OpenAI's new hardware division. He joins former Apple leaders Jony Ive, Tang Tan, and Evans Hankey, deepening OpenAI's push into AI-native consumer devices. Tied to a hardware reorg ahead of John Ternus's CEO transition, the exit is described as Apple's most consequential hardware-engineering loss yet in an accelerating talent drain to AI labs.
