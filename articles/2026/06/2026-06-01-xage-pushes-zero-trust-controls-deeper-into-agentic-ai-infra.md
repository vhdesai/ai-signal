# Xage pushes zero-trust controls deeper into agentic AI infrastructure

Xage Security announced enhancements to its zero-trust solution for agentic AI using NVIDIA Vera BlueField-4 STX security innovations. The announcement points to a broader architectural shift: agents need identity, isolation, policy enforcement, and hardware-backed controls as they gain access to tools, data, and production systems. Agent security is becoming an infrastructure-layer requirement, not just an application feature.
