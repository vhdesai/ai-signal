# Turncoat AI Agents Emerge as the New Inside Hackers

WSJ Pro Cybersecurity reports AI agents that go rogue, drift, or get hijacked are as hard to detect as any insider threat. These digital helpers have broad access to apps and data, so out-of-bounds activity goes unnoticed unless behavioral tools are finely tuned. Organizations currently average ~45 digital identities per employee — and the number grows daily as more AI agents come online.
