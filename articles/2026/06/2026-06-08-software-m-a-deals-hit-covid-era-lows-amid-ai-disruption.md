# ### Software M&A Deals Hit COVID-Era Lows Amid AI Disruption

Software M&A volume has fallen to COVID-era lows, driven by AI-related disruption that makes traditional software valuations uncertain. Buyers are hesitant to acquire companies whose products may be rendered less valuable by AI agents — disruption depressing deal activity before demonstrating replacement economics. --- **Tags:** `NEW`
