# SK Hynix targets ~$29B Nasdaq listing as soon as July 10

SK Hynix disclosed plans to list American depositary receipts on Nasdaq’s Global Select Market as early as July 10, seeking to raise as much as 45.45 trillion won (~$29.1B) to fund new fabrication plants, advanced packaging, and chipmaking equipment. The AI-memory leader is pursuing deeper access to U.S. capital markets and a potential re-rating of its value amid surging high-bandwidth-memory demand. The maximum offering size was calculated off the June 23 closing price and is not final.
