# Stock-Music Watermarks Surface in Gemini-Generated Songs

Songs generated by Google's Gemini app (via DeepMind's Lyria model) included spoken watermarks from stock-music preview tracks — suggesting unlicensed audio may have entered training data. The finding intensifies IP and provenance scrutiny of generative-audio systems.
