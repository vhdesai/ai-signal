# Altman downplays OpenAI IPO timing after Anthropic's filing

OpenAI CEO Sam Altman said he is not focused on the timing of a potential IPO, adding the company will go public "when it makes sense." Analysts framed Anthropic's first-mover filing two ways: it seizes the narrative, but also lets OpenAI watch how institutional investors react to audited frontier-AI financials before committing to its own price. Prediction markets had largely expected OpenAI to file first.
