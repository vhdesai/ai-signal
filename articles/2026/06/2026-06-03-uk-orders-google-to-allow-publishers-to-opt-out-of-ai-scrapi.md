# UK Orders Google to Allow Publishers to Opt Out of AI Scraping for Search Summaries

The UK’s Competition and Markets Authority ordered Google to provide publishers with a mechanism to opt out of having their content used for AI-generated search summaries (AI Overviews). The ruling is the first major regulatory action directly addressing the tension between AI-powered search and publisher content rights, and could set a precedent for other jurisdictions.
