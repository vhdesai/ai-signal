# Can Tech Companies Learn to Love Cheaper AI Models?

TechCrunch examined whether the industry can adapt to a future where cheaper models capture the majority of enterprise spend. The pricing crisis will force market stratification: frontier models for high-stakes applications, cheaper models for everything else. Multi-model procurement will become the norm.
