# OpenAI and Broadcom unveil "Jalapeño," their first custom AI inference chip

OpenAI and Broadcom revealed Jalapeño, an ASIC "Intelligence Processor" built for LLM inference and designed end-to-end in nine months — with OpenAI’s own models accelerating the work. Made by Broadcom with Celestica, initial deployment is targeted for late 2026, ramping in 2027–28, as OpenAI moves to "build the full stack" and reduce reliance on Nvidia GPUs. President Greg Brockman said OpenAI "cannot get compute fast enough," and Broadcom’s Hock Tan called customer demand "insatiable."
