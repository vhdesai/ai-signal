# Anthropic's Mythos 5 cleared by the U.S. for wider (still-limited) use; Fable 5 stays restricted

Anthropic won U.S. approval to redeploy its most powerful cybersecurity model, Mythos 5, to a vetted set of critical-infrastructure operators and cyber defenders — reported as more than 100 trusted U.S. organizations — after Commerce Secretary Howard Lutnick said the company's risk-mitigation work had "yielded significant progress." The companion Fable 5 model remains restricted, making this a selective reprieve rather than a general release, two weeks after a June 12 export-control order forced both offline. For enterprises, access to top-tier models increasingly hinges on fitting inside a discretionary "trusted partner" framework.
