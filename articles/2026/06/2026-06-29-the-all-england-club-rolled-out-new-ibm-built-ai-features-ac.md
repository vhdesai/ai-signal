# The All England Club rolled out new IBM-built AI features across the Wimbledon app and website as first-round matches…

The All England Club rolled out new IBM-built AI features across the Wimbledon app and website as first-round matches began, including an upgraded natural-language "Match Chat" assistant and a new "Key Moments" feature surfaced via IBM Slamtracker. IBM frames it as part of a five-year digital transformation to bring services in-house and operate on an AI-first model.
