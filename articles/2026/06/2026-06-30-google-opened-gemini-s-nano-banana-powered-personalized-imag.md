# Google opened Gemini's Nano Banana–powered personalized image generation to all eligible US users for free, a feature…

Google opened Gemini's Nano Banana–powered personalized image generation to all eligible US users for free, a feature previously limited to Plus, Pro, and Ultra subscribers. The "Personal Intelligence" capability draws on a user's connected Google data — Gmail, Photos, YouTube, and Search — to generate images that reflect their interests without explicit prompting. The expansion deepens Google's consumer-AI distribution but will renew scrutiny over how personal data feeds generative features.
