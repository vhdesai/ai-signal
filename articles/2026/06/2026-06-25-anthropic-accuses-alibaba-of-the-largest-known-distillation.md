# Anthropic accuses Alibaba of the largest known “distillation attack” on Claude

In a June 10 letter to Senators Tim Scott and Elizabeth Warren, Anthropic alleged that operators affiliated with Alibaba’s Qwen lab used roughly 25,000 fraudulent accounts to run nearly 28.8 million exchanges against Claude between late April and June 5 — the largest distillation attempt it has documented. The campaign reportedly targeted Claude’s software-engineering and agentic-reasoning capabilities, the skills most material to frontier-model valuations. It lands amid broader US–China friction, days after Alibaba separately sued the Pentagon over a blacklist designation.
