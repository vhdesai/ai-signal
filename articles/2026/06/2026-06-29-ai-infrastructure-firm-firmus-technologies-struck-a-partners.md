# AI-infrastructure firm Firmus Technologies struck a partnership to buy Nvidia infrastructure and resell Nvidia-powered…

AI-infrastructure firm Firmus Technologies struck a partnership to buy Nvidia infrastructure and resell Nvidia-powered cloud to "AI-native" customers, delivering 170,000 GPUs from Q1 2027 to early 2028 in Batam, Indonesia. Firmus expects up to $30B in revenue over six years; Nvidia, already an investor, earns product revenue plus a share of cloud revenue.
