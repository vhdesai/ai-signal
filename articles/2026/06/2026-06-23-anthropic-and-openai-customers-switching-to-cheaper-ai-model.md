# Anthropic and OpenAI Customers Switching to Cheaper AI Models

As flagship model prices soar, enterprise customers are aggressively optimizing costs. Ensemble Health Partners (planning $100M in AI spend this year) reported success switching to an OpenAI model 23x cheaper than advanced tiers. Open-source model usage surged to 65% of OpenRouter tokens processed in June—a structural shift that could pressure premium pricing power.
