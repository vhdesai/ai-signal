# Forbes: Energy Sovereignty Will Decide the AI Race — Europe Must Compete or Become a "Colony"

Cites Macron's VivaTech positioning of France leveraging cheap nuclear power, and warns that U.S. weaponization of AI access (via Anthropic export controls) has accelerated Europe's push for sovereignty.
