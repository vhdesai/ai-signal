# Pentagon Designates Alibaba, Baidu, and Other Chinese Tech Firms as Aiding China's Military

The Pentagon added Alibaba, Baidu, and other Chinese tech companies to its CMC List. The move has immediate implications for U.S. investors and could trigger institutional divestment, intensifying U.S.–China AI decoupling at a moment when DeepSeek is gaining traction with U.S. enterprise customers.
