# Five Eyes Intelligence Alliance Issues Rare Joint Warning on AI Cyber Threats

US officials and allied intelligence partners issued a joint statement urging leaders to "act now," warning that some AI models may be months from being able to launch cyberattacks powerful enough to overwhelm governments and major companies. Elevates frontier-AI cyber capability from a research concern to a national-security priority.
