# IBM Study Finds a Widening Enterprise "AI Control Gap"

A survey of 2,000 C-level tech executives found two-thirds are held accountable for AI systems they don't fully control, 70% say teams deploy technology faster than IT can track, and only 11% feel prepared for the scale of AI-agent deployment. Organizations designing control into AI systems achieve materially stronger outcomes — a governance warning as agentic deployment accelerates.
