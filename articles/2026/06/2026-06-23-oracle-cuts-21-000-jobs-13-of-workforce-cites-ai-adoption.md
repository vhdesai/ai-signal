# Oracle Cuts ~21,000 Jobs (~13% of Workforce), Cites AI Adoption

Oracle disclosed approximately 21,000 layoffs—roughly 13% of its workforce—explicitly tied to AI-driven automation. This represents one of the largest single-company workforce reductions directly attributed to AI adoption rather than macroeconomic pressures. The move signals that AI-driven headcount reduction is shifting from theoretical to operational at enterprise scale.
