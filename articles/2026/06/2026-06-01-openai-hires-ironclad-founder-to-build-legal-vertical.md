# OpenAI Hires Ironclad Founder to Build Legal Vertical

Jason Boehmig, founder of contract-management pioneer Ironclad, has joined OpenAI to lead product for a new legal vertical, with a stated focus on "building AGI for law." The hire signals intensifying competition for high-value professional-services workflows where AI can directly displace billable hours.
