# ### Senate Republicans Break with Trump on AI Equity-Stake Idea

Senate Republicans publicly broke with Trump on government equity stakes in AI companies. The pushback suggests the concept — despite bipartisan interest from Trump and Sanders — faces a difficult legislative path. For AI companies preparing IPOs, the reduced likelihood of mandatory government ownership removes a near-term structural risk to deal terms. --- **Tags:** `HOT`
