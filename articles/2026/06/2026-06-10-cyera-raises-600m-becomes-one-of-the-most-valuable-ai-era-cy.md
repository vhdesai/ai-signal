# ### Cyera Raises $600M, Becomes One of the Most Valuable AI-Era Cybersecurity Startups

The NYT reported that Cyera raised $600 million, positioning it as one of the most valuable cybersecurity startups of the AI era. The company focuses on data security posture management—a category made more urgent by the proliferation of AI agents that access, process, and generate sensitive enterprise data.
