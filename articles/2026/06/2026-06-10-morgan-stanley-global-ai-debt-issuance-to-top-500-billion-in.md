# Morgan Stanley: Global AI Debt Issuance to Top $500 Billion in 2026

Morgan Stanley projects global AI-related debt issuance will exceed $500 billion in 2026, including hyperscaler bonds, startup debt facilities (like Anthropic's $35B Apollo/Blackstone deal), and infrastructure financing. AI debt is becoming a distinct asset class with its own risk profile.
