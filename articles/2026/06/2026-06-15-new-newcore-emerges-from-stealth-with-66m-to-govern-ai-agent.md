# New NewCore emerges from stealth with $66M to govern AI-agent identities at enterprise scale

Cybersecurity startup NewCore emerged from stealth with $66M to authenticate, govern, and control AI agents at scale — the seed round was led by Cyberstarts with Index Ventures and Evolution Equity Partners, valuing NewCore at $300M post-investment. The thesis is that AI agents need the same identity and permission rigor as human employees: a named owner, scoped credentials, and auditable action trails. It is the lone confirmed in-window venture round; no research-lab or large-cap AI funding round closed on June 14–15.
