# Google caps Meta's Gemini usage amid a compute crunch

Google was forced to cap Meta's use of Gemini after Meta exceeded allotted capacity, sources told the Financial Times — a striking sign that even trillion-dollar firms with their own models are short on compute. Google reportedly warned Meta about limits in March, prompting Meta to tell staff to use tokens more efficiently while it races to expand its own data centers (a pledged ~$600B over two years). The episode reframes capacity, not capability, as the binding constraint of the current cycle.
