# Broadcom Slides ~12% as AI Guidance Disappoints Lofty Expectations

Broadcom fell ~12–13% after fiscal Q2 results paired strong AI revenue growth with guidance short of elevated expectations, erasing a meaningful slice of its ~$2T market cap. The reaction was a "sell the news" event — not a demand warning — but it signals investors are beginning to price AI infrastructure on delivery, not narrative.
