# OpenAI Extends Codex to Business with Plugins, Sites, and Annotations

OpenAI announced six new Codex agent plugins, an annotations feature, and a “Sites” feature, and said it will bring Codex functionality into the ChatGPT app broadly “in the next few weeks.” The intent is to convert Codex from a developer-only agentic coding tool into a general enterprise productivity surface riding ChatGPT’s existing footprint. For enterprises already standardized on ChatGPT, this lowers the adoption barrier to agentic workflows.
