# OpenAI and Anthropic Urge Congress to Regulate Synthetic DNA to Prevent AI Bioweapons

OpenAI and Anthropic CEOs co-signed a letter urging Congress to regulate synthetic DNA purchases, warning AI is lowering barriers to biological weapons. The WSJ reported it as an exclusive—the first joint legislative ask by the two leading frontier labs.
