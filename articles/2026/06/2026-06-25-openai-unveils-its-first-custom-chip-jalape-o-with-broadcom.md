# OpenAI unveils its first custom chip, "Jalapeño," with Broadcom

OpenAI revealed its first custom accelerator, "Jalapeño," co-designed with Broadcom (engaged last October) and architected to reduce data movement while balancing compute, memory and networking for inference. Broadcom’s Hock Tan and Charlie Kawwas presented the first unit to Sam Altman and Greg Brockman this week. Billed as a "blank-slate" inference design, it marks OpenAI’s first concrete step toward reducing dependence on third-party GPUs.
