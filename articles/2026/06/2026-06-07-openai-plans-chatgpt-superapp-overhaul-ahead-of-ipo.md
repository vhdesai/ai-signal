# OpenAI Plans ChatGPT "Superapp" Overhaul Ahead of IPO

Reuters reported that OpenAI is planning a major overhaul of ChatGPT into a consolidated "superapp" ahead of its public listing. The FT report indicates the restructuring will integrate Codex, DALL-E, voice, and agent capabilities into a single unified interface—mirroring the WeChat super-app model. The move aims to increase user engagement and simplify the product surface before IPO investors evaluate the company's consumer metrics. URL not verified.
