# A structural look at DeepMind's sharpest talent losses in years: Denny Zhou and Dawn Song to Meta's Superintelligence…

A structural look at DeepMind's sharpest talent losses in years: Denny Zhou and Dawn Song to Meta's Superintelligence Lab; Nobel laureate John Jumper, plus Jonas Adler and Alexander Pritzel, to Anthropic; and Noam Shazeer to OpenAI. The piece ties the departures to DeepMind's coding/product pivot, noting prominent exits earlier contributed to a ~5% Alphabet share drop (~$225B in market cap).
