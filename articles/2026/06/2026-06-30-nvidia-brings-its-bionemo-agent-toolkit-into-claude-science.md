# NVIDIA brings its BioNeMo Agent Toolkit into Claude Science

NVIDIA published a June 30 post extending its BioNeMo agent tools (Nemotron, NemoClaw, OpenShell, BioNeMo) to life-sciences researchers inside Anthropic's newly launched Claude Science — a same-day cross-confirmation of the Claude Science debut. The underlying BioNeMo Agent Toolkit was first announced June 23; the June 30 news is the Claude Science integration. NVIDIA also posted a companion piece on how its inference software stack drives the lowest token cost. https://nvidianews.nvidia.com/
