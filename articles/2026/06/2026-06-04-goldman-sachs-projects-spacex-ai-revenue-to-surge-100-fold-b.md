# Goldman Sachs Projects SpaceX AI Revenue to Surge 100-Fold by 2030

Goldman Sachs projects SpaceX's AI division (xAI) could see revenue surge 100-fold by 2030, reaching $322 billion. The forecast underpins the $1.77T IPO valuation and suggests the bank sees xAI — not rockets or Starlink — as the primary long-term value driver.
