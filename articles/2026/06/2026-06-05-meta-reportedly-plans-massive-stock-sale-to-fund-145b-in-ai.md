# Meta Reportedly Plans Massive Stock Sale to Fund $145B in AI Investments; Stock Drops

Meta is considering raising tens of billions through equity sales to fund ~$145B in AI investments. Stock dropped 5.5%. The move follows Alphabet's $85B raise and signals the AI infrastructure arms race is forcing public equity dilution to finance compute buildouts.
