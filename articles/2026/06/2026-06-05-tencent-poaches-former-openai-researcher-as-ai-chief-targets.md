# Tencent Poaches Former OpenAI Researcher as AI Chief, Targets AGI

Tencent appointed Yao Shunyu — a former OpenAI researcher — as AI chief with a mandate to build AGI using "OpenAI's playbook." The hire signals Tencent's shift from cautious integration to aggressive frontier research, part of China's broader talent acquisition pattern. ________________________________
