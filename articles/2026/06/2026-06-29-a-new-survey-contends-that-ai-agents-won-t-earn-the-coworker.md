# A new survey contends that AI agents won't earn the "coworker" label until they move from answering questions to…

A new survey contends that AI agents won't earn the "coworker" label until they move from answering questions to delivering finished work end-to-end, reviewing agentic frameworks such as OpenHands and SWE-agent. It frames task-completion — not response quality — as the next benchmark frontier for agentic systems.
