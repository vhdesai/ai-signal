# The pro-AI movement splinters over security controls vs. staying ahead of China

Axios reports an open, public rift among AI's boosters: David Sacks warned that restricting access to America's best models betrays the administration's "pro-innovation" AI-race strategy, while investors called staged, government-gated rollouts "hugely bearish" and warned of "re-rating pressure" on lab valuations. The flashpoint is the staggered GPT-5.6 release and the Anthropic restrictions, set against two security evaluations suggesting Chinese models have caught up to top U.S. systems on cybersecurity. Across the divide, executives converged on one ask: clear, benchmarked rules rather than ad hoc access decisions. URL not verified.
