# Nvidia Introduces Halos for Robotics — Full-Stack Safety System for Physical AI

At the Automate show, Nvidia announced Halos for Robotics spanning IGX Thor compute, a Halos OS software layer, and an ANAB-accredited inspection lab. Humanoid maker Agility is the first adopter, integrating Halos into robots deployed for Amazon, GXO, and Toyota.
