# OpenAI unveiled the GPT‑5.6 family — Sol (flagship), Terra (balanced, roughly 2x cheaper than GPT‑5.5), and Luna…

OpenAI unveiled the GPT‑5.6 family — Sol (flagship), Terra (balanced, roughly 2x cheaper than GPT‑5.5), and Luna (fastest, cheapest). Sol adds a new "max" reasoning effort and an "ultra" mode that orchestrates subagents, setting new highs on Terminal‑Bench 2.1 (coding), ExploitBench (cybersecurity), and SecureBio. Pricing runs $5/$30 (Sol), $2.50/$15 (Terra), and $1/$6 (Luna) per 1M input/output tokens. The launch began as a limited preview to trusted partners at the U.S. government's request.
