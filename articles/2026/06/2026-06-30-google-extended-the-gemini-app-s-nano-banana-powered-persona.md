# Google extended the Gemini app's Nano Banana–powered "Personal Intelligence" image generation to all eligible free US…

Google extended the Gemini app's Nano Banana–powered "Personal Intelligence" image generation to all eligible free US users, a capability previously reserved for Plus, Pro, and Ultra subscribers. It can generate images informed by a user's connected Google data—Gmail, Photos, YouTube, and Search—without explicit prompt details. Coverage flagged privacy trade-offs, since that connected data can also train Google's services.
