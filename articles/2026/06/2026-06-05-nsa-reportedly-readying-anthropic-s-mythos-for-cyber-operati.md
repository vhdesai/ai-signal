# NSA Reportedly Readying Anthropic's Mythos for Cyber Operations

Anthropic has deployed ~6 engineers to the NSA to help the agency use Mythos, its frontier cybersecurity model. Whether Mythos is in active offensive operations remains unclear. The reporting is notable given both a DoD designation and a prior federal ban on the agency's use of Anthropic technology.
