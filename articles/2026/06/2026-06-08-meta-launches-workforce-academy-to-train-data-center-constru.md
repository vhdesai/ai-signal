# ### Meta Launches "Workforce Academy" to Train Data Center Construction Workers

Meta launched a "Workforce Academy" to train skilled trades workers for AI data center construction, funding the program as part of its buildout strategy. Reuters noted Meta is also funding separate skilled trades jobs programs. The initiative addresses the labor bottleneck that is increasingly constraining data center expansion timelines—a practical response to the infrastructure race that goes beyond capital investment to workforce capacity.
