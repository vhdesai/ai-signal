# AMD Commits £2B to Accelerate UK AI at London Tech Week

AMD committed up to £2B for five-year UK AI investment — collaborations with Imperial College London, ARIA's "Scaling Inference Lab" on photonic networks, and AMD-Dell systems at Cambridge (Zenith AI supercomputer, Sunrise fusion-AI platform). Positions AMD as a serious Nvidia alternative in European AI compute.
