# The U.S. Commerce Department granted Anthropic permission to release Claude Mythos 5 to roughly 100 trusted companies…

The U.S. Commerce Department granted Anthropic permission to release Claude Mythos 5 to roughly 100 trusted companies and federal agencies, easing a two‑week standoff over an export‑control directive citing national‑security authorities. Commerce Secretary Howard Lutnick's letter did not restore access to the broader Fable 5 model. The decision landed the same day OpenAI limited its own GPT‑5.6 rollout.
