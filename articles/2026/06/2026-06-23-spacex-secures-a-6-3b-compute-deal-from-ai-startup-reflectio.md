# SpaceX secures a $6.3B compute deal from AI startup Reflection

SpaceX signed a compute-capacity agreement with open-source AI startup Reflection AI worth up to $6.3B, leasing Nvidia GB300 access at the xAI-linked Colossus 2 data center near Memphis for $150M per month from July 2026 through 2029 (per CNBC). The arrangement deepens the entanglement between Musk's compute infrastructure and the wider model ecosystem. It is another sign that committed, multi-year compute contracts are becoming a core financing mechanism — and a key revenue line — for frontier-scale AI. https://www.datacenterdynamics.com/en/news/spacex-secures-63bn-compute-capacity-deal-from-ai-startup-reflection/
