# ### Meta Signs First AI Data Center Deal in India with Reliance

Meta signed its first AI data center deal in India through Reliance Industries. The deal positions Meta to serve India's 500M+ WhatsApp users with locally hosted AI — critical for latency-sensitive Business Agent deployments. India is rapidly becoming a contested AI infrastructure market. - [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/06/10/meta-signs-first-ai-data-center-deal-in-india-with-reliance/) --- ## Industry News **Tags:** `HOT`
