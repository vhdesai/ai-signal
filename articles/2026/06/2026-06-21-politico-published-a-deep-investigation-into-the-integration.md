# Politico published a deep investigation into the integration of AI into nuclear command-and-control systems, calling it…

Politico published a deep investigation into the integration of AI into nuclear command-and-control systems, calling it "a reality we're hurtling toward, ready or not." The piece follows disclosures earlier this week that the Pentagon used xAI's Grok in targeting during Iran military operations, and comes as the Bulletin of the Atomic Scientists warned that AI is changing both biological and nuclear risks faster than governance can adapt.
