# Apple Approves First AI Agent on Messages for Business

Apple approved Poke as the first AI agent on Messages for Business, enabling conversational AI within iMessage. The move signals Apple is opening its walled garden to third-party AI agents, directly competing with Meta's WhatsApp Business Agent.
