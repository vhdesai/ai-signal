# Microsoft Debuts In-House MAI Models to Cut OpenAI Dependence

Microsoft unveiled new first-party models—MAI-Code-1-Flash and MAI-Thinking-1—positioned to lower inference costs and reduce reliance on OpenAI for core Copilot workloads. MAI-Thinking-1 is Microsoft’s first in-house reasoning model, explicitly trained without OpenAI data. The move continues Microsoft’s vertical-integration push as its commercial and capacity arrangements with OpenAI evolve. For enterprise buyers, it signals more model optionality inside the Microsoft stack and potential pricing pressure on premium reasoning tiers.
