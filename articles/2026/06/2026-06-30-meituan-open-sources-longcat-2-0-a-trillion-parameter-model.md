# Meituan open-sources LongCat-2.0, a trillion-parameter model trained on domestic GPUs

Meituan open-sourced LongCat-2.0, described as an industry-first trillion-parameter model trained entirely on a domestic cluster of roughly 50,000 GPUs. The release stood out among China's June 30 AI developments as a signal of domestic-compute training capability amid tightening export controls.
