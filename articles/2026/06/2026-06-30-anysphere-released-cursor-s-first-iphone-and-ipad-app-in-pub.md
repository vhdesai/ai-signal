# Anysphere released Cursor's first iPhone and iPad app in public beta across all paid plans, letting developers launch…

Anysphere released Cursor's first iPhone and iPad app in public beta across all paid plans, letting developers launch and steer cloud or desktop coding agents from a phone with voice input, slash commands, Remote Control, Live Activities, and PR merging. The launch follows SpaceX's reported $60B acquisition of Anysphere. Cursor is offering 75% off Composer 2.5 runs in the app through July 5, 2026.
