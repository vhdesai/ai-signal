# ### Microsoft's Xbox Unit Plans Staff Cuts as Margins Deteriorate

Microsoft's Xbox gaming unit plans to cut staff in the coming months as its financial picture worsens, according to a person familiar with the plans. In a note to staff, CEO Asha Sharma said Xbox's "accountability margins" have fallen to 3% in the fiscal year ending this month, a trend she said "cannot continue." The reported cuts show continued pressure inside gaming despite the broader AI-driven strength in Microsoft's cloud and platform businesses.
