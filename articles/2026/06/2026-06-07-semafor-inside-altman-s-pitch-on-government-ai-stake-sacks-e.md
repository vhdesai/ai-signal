# Semafor: Inside Altman's Pitch on Government AI Stake; Sacks Expresses Concerns

Semafor published details of Sam Altman's pitch to the government on an AI equity-stake arrangement, while Fortune reported that David Sacks—Trump's AI czar—has concerns about the proposal. The NYT covered Trump's continued interest in "a piece of A.I. giants," and Axios analyzed what's driving the administration's pursuit of an AI windfall. The divergence between Altman's openness and Sacks' skepticism suggests the deal structure is far from settled, with implications for OpenAI's IPO terms. URL not verified.
