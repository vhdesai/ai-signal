# Sakana AI Launches Fugu — Orchestration Model That Routes Across Frontier LLMs

Japan's Sakana AI released Fugu and Fugu Ultra, a multi-agent orchestration system that delivers frontier-level performance through a single OpenAI-compatible API by dynamically routing to a swappable pool of specialized models. CEO David Ha positioned it explicitly as a hedge against vendor lock-in and export controls: "access to top models can disappear overnight." Claims Fugu Ultra edges Claude Fable 5 on LiveCodeBench (93.2 vs 89.8). Reframes orchestration, not scale, as the path to resilience.
