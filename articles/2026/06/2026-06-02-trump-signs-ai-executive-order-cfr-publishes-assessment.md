# Trump Signs AI Executive Order; CFR Publishes Assessment

President Trump signed an executive order (“Promoting Advanced Artificial Intelligence Innovation and Security”), and the Council on Foreign Relations published a same-day assessment. The final version was significantly narrower than earlier drafts, calling for voluntary pre-release review of frontier models. Politico characterized it as “tiptoeing toward real AI oversight.” Executives with government-facing or compliance-sensitive AI deployments should track the implementing details.
