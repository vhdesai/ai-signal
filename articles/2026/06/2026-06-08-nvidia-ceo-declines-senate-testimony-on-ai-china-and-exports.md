# Nvidia CEO Declines Senate Testimony on AI, China, and Exports

Jensen Huang declined an invitation to testify before the Senate on AI, China, and export controls. The refusal comes as Nvidia faces increasing scrutiny over its role in U.S.–China chip competition and may invite subpoena discussions.
