# U.S. Commerce clears Anthropic's Mythos 5 for ~100 vetted orgs; Fable 5 stays dark

The U.S. Commerce Department, in a letter from Secretary Howard Lutnick, cleared Anthropic to restore its strongest cybersecurity model, Mythos 5, to roughly 100 vetted U.S. organizations defending critical infrastructure — two weeks after a June 12 export-control directive forced both Mythos 5 and Fable 5 offline for all users, including foreign nationals. Fable 5, the public-facing variant that had reached hundreds of millions, remains suspended with no timeline. Anthropic confirmed the partial restoration publicly on June 27. The asymmetry is telling: a model fenced to ~100 named organizations is far easier to police than a consumer release.
