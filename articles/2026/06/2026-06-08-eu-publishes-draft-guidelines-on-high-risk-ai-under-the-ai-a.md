# EU Publishes Draft Guidelines on High-Risk AI Under the AI Act

The European Commission published draft guidelines defining high-risk AI systems under the EU AI Act — the most detailed regulatory guidance to date. Clarifies obligations around transparency, human oversight, and risk assessment for healthcare, hiring, law enforcement, and critical infrastructure AI. The first concrete compliance blueprint for European enterprises.
