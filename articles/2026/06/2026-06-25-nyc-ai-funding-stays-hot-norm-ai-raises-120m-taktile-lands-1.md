# NYC AI funding stays hot: Norm AI raises $120M, Taktile lands $110M Series C

Norm AI, which embeds legal and compliance rules into AI agents for regulated enterprises, disclosed a $120M raise via SEC filing with nineteen investors. The same day, AI decision platform Taktile closed a $110M Series C led by Growth Equity at Goldman Sachs Alternatives, with Balderton, Index Ventures, Tiger Global, and Y Combinator participating — concentrated signals that enterprise-grade, governance-oriented AI remains the venture market’s preferred bet.
