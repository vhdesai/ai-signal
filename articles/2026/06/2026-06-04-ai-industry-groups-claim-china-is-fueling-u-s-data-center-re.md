# AI Industry Groups Claim China Is Fueling U.S. Data Center Resistance

AI industry groups are alleging Chinese influence behind grassroots resistance to U.S. data center construction, with Republicans launching a congressional probe. The narrative reframes data center opposition from a local environmental issue into a national security concern, potentially accelerating permitting.
