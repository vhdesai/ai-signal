# DysLexLens is a low-resource LLM approach that converts online forum posts by and about dyslexic learners into…

DysLexLens is a low-resource LLM approach that converts online forum posts by and about dyslexic learners into traceable knowledge-graph insights, aiming to map how dyslexic users actually rely on AI for everyday academic tasks. The work targets accessibility and human-AI interaction for an under-studied user population.
