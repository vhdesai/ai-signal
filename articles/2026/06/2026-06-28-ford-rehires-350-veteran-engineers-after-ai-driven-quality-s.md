# Ford rehires ~350 veteran engineers after AI-driven quality systems fell short

Ford executives told Bloomberg the automaker brought back about 350 experienced engineers — many former employees or supplier staff — after leaning heavily on automated, AI-based quality systems produced disappointing results. COO Kumar Galhotra said the specialists "hunt for failure points before a part ever reaches the plant floor," while VP Charles Poon conceded the company wrongly assumed that AI plus its design requirements would deliver quality on its own. It's a pointed real-world caution on the limits of automation for hard engineering judgment.
