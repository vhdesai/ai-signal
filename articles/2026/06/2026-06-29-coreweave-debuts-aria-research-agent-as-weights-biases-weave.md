# CoreWeave debuts ARIA research agent as Weights & Biases Weave hits GA

AI-cloud operator CoreWeave debuted ARIA (AI Research and Iteration Agent), embedded in its Weights & Biases platform, to autonomously analyze thousands of experiment runs, build live dashboards, and recommend model and agent improvements; the W&B Weave agent-building platform reached general availability the same day. The move pushes CoreWeave up the stack from raw GPU rental into the MLOps and agent-tooling layer — a margin and stickiness play as bare compute commoditizes. ARIA is available in public preview.
