# As enterprises curb "tokenmaxxing," OpenAI and Anthropic face a new spending reality

Enterprises are beginning to throttle once-unconstrained AI spend, with companies such as Uber imposing per-seat tool budgets and startups like Lindy shifting traffic to cheaper open-weight models such as DeepSeek. Analysts warn the model leaders' growth rates — Anthropic at a reported $47B annualized run rate, OpenAI nearer $25B — may be peaking as customers demand clearer ROI. The shift adds urgency to both labs' confidential IPO filings while the headline numbers still impress.
