# Both OpenAI and Anthropic are approaching public markets at valuations near $1 trillion while spending billions…

Both OpenAI and Anthropic are approaching public markets at valuations near $1 trillion while spending billions annually on compute and remaining deeply unprofitable. The analysis raises questions about passive investors--including retirement portfolios--being exposed to these companies once they enter major indices. The piece follows OpenAI's disclosed $3.7B cash burn in Q1 2026 on $5.7B in revenue.
