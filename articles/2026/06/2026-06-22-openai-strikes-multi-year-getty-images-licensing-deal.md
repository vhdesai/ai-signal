# OpenAI Strikes Multi-Year Getty Images Licensing Deal

OpenAI signed a multi-year agreement to integrate Getty Images' licensed visual library into ChatGPT. Getty shares surged approximately 200% on the news. The deal continues OpenAI's strategy of securing legitimate content partnerships ahead of its IPO, reducing copyright litigation risk while expanding ChatGPT's multimodal capabilities.
