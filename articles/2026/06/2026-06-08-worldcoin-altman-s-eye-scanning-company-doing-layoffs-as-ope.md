# Worldcoin (Altman's Eye-Scanning Company) Doing Layoffs as OpenAI Files for IPO

As OpenAI filed its IPO paperwork, reports emerged that Worldcoin—Sam Altman's separate eye-scanning identity company—is conducting layoffs. The juxtaposition is notable: Altman's primary venture is approaching a historic public listing while his adjacent venture is contracting, raising questions about the sustainability of the broader Altman portfolio.
