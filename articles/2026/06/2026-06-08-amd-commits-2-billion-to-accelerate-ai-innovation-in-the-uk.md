# AMD Commits £2 Billion to Accelerate AI Innovation in the UK

AMD committed up to £2B for five-year AI investment in the UK — collaborations with Imperial College London, ARIA's "Scaling Inference Lab" on photonic networks, and AMD-Dell systems at Cambridge (Zenith AI supercomputer, Sunrise fusion-AI platform). Sharpens the AMD-vs-Nvidia contest for sovereign-AI mindshare at London Tech Week.
