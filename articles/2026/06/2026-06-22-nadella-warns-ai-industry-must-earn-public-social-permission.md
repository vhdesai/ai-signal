# Nadella Warns AI Industry Must Earn Public "Social Permission"

In a WSJ interview, Nadella cautioned that AI leaders cannot simultaneously claim white-collar jobs will disappear and demand vast resources for data-center buildouts without losing public trust. Urged executives to focus on "reorganizing the job" rather than replacement. Lands amid record AI-attributed layoffs.
