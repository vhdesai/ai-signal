# EU Orders Meta to Reopen WhatsApp to Rival AI Chatbots for Free

The EU ordered Meta to allow rival AI assistants back onto WhatsApp under Digital Markets Act interoperability requirements. The ruling prevents Meta from locking businesses into its own Business Agent by blocking competitors — a significant precedent that platforms designated as gatekeepers cannot use AI agent distribution as a competitive moat.
