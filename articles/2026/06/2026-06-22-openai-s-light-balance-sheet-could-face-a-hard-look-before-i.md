# OpenAI's Light Balance Sheet Could Face a Hard Look Before IPO

OpenAI's financials resemble a lean software business—zero debt, under $750M in lease liabilities, just $46M quarterly capex. But this "light" balance sheet obscures massive compute costs borne by Microsoft under their partnership, raising questions about how investors will evaluate the company's true economic structure.
