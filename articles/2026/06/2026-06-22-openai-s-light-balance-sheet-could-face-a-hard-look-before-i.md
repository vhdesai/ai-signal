# OpenAI's Light Balance Sheet Could Face a Hard Look Before IPO

The Information reviewed OpenAI's financial statements and found a company that at first glance resembles a lean, low-debt software business—zero debt, under $750 million in lease liabilities, and just $46 million in quarterly capex. However, this "light" balance sheet obscures the massive compute costs borne by Microsoft under their partnership, raising questions about how investors will evaluate the company's true economic structure ahead of its public offering.
