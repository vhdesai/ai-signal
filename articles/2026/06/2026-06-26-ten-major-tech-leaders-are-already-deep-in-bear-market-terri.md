# Ten major tech leaders are already deep in bear-market territory

A widening AI sell-off has dragged several marquee technology names well below their recent highs, with declines ranging from roughly 50% to nearly 70% for the worst-hit. Coinbase, Oracle, ServiceNow and Palantir are among those flagged as having slipped into bear-market territory. The piece attributes the pressure to demands for stronger earnings, more disciplined AI spending, and proof that large capital outlays will generate real returns.
