# Venture Capital Concentrates on AI "Bottlenecks" — $3.37B in 10 Rounds

Nearly $3B of the day's ~$3.4B went to four infrastructure-layer deals: Baseten ($1.5B, inference), Upscale AI ($190M, networking), Nearfield Instruments ($380M, semiconductor metrology), and CRED. Strategic and sovereign investors (Nvidia, Meta, Temasek, QIA) featured prominently. Capital is rewarding the layers that determine latency, utilization, and yield.
