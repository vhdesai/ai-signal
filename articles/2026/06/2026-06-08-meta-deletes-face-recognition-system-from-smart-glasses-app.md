# ### Meta Deletes Face-Recognition System from Smart Glasses App After Wired Report

Meta removed a face-recognition system from its Meta AI smart glasses companion app after Wired reported the app contained three undisclosed AI models capable of detecting faces, generating biometric fingerprints, and firing person-recognition notifications. Underscores privacy risks in AI-enabled wearables. - [Wired](https://www.wired.com/story/meta-removes-face-recognition-code-meta-ai-app-smart-glasses/) --- ## Industry News **Tags:** `BREAKING` `HOT`
