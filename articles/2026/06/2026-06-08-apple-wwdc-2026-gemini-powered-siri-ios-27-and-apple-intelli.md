# ### Apple WWDC 2026: Gemini-Powered Siri, iOS 27, and Apple Intelligence Overhaul Begin Today

Apple's WWDC keynote begins at 10 AM PT today — Tim Cook's final as CEO before handing the role to hardware chief John Ternus on September 1. Reporting points to a rebuilt Siri running on a custom Google Gemini model, an Extensions system letting users route Apple Intelligence to ChatGPT, Gemini, or Claude (ending OpenAI's de facto exclusivity on the iPhone), and a redesigned Apple Intelligence stack.
