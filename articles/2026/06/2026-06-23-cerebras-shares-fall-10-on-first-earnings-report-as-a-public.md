# Cerebras shares fall ~10% on first earnings report as a public company

In its debut report since last month's $5.55B IPO, Cerebras Systems posted nearly doubled quarterly revenue but guided full-year profit margins below its first-quarter level and below Nvidia, sending shares down about 10% in extended trading. The inference-focused chipmaker has tied much of its growth to OpenAI, including a reported $20B-scale relationship.
