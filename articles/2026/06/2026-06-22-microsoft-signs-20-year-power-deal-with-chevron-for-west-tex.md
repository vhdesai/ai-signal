# Microsoft Signs 20-Year Power Deal with Chevron for West Texas AI Data Center

Chevron will supply dedicated natural-gas power to Microsoft's "Project Kilby" in West Texas — roughly 2.67 GW off-grid via GE Vernova and Caterpillar turbines under a 20-year agreement. First power targeted for 2028. AI compute demand is pushing hyperscalers toward fossil generation, complicating Microsoft's 2030 carbon-negative pledge.
