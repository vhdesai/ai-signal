# Google DeepMind Invests ~$75M in Film Studio A24

Google DeepMind made a "first-of-its-kind" ~$75M investment in indie film studio A24 for a filmmaking research partnership. Critically, this is not an IP or data-training deal—DeepMind will not influence creative decisions. The structure signals a new licensing-as-trust model: AI labs investing in content companies without acquiring training rights, building relationships for future collaboration.
