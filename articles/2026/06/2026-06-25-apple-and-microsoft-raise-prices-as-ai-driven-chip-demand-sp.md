# Apple and Microsoft raise prices as AI-driven chip demand spikes component costs

Apple said it will raise prices on certain MacBooks and iPads by up to $300, and hours later Microsoft announced Xbox price increases effective August 1, with both citing surging memory and storage chip costs. Apple’s statement — "We have never seen a component price increase this much, this quickly" — ties the hikes directly to AI data-center demand straining the chip supply. This is the first time the AI build-out’s cost pressure has visibly reached consumer hardware pricing, a margin signal worth watching across the device ecosystem. https://www.cbsnews.com/news/apple-price-hikes-macbook-ipad-2026/
