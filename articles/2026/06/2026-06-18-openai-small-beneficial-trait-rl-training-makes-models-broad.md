# OpenAI: Small "Beneficial-Trait" RL Training Makes Models Broadly Safer

~5% of RL training allocated to truthfulness/corrigibility/transparency produced broad gains — beating baselines on 44/53 benchmarks (+9.1 pts avg). Gains generalized out of domain and persisted under adversarial prompting. Suggests RL is a tool for durable safety, not just an alignment risk.
