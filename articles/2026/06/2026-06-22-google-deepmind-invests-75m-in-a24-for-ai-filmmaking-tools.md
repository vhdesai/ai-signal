# Google DeepMind Invests ~$75M in A24 for AI Filmmaking Tools

Google DeepMind's first-ever stake in a film studio — a "first-of-its-kind" research partnership to co-develop AI production tools with direct filmmaker input. Both sides stressed it is "not a production, IP, or data-training deal." Hands DeepMind a creative showcase at a moment it is shedding scientific talent.
