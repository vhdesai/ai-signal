# Anthropic says Claude now writes ~65% of its product team's code, via new "Claude Tag" Slack agent

Anthropic reports that about 65% of its product team's code is now AI-generated by Claude, driven by Claude Tag — an AI teammate embedded in Slack that retains context and works asynchronously. The analysis cautions that the figure reflects a model builder's uniquely favorable position and won't generalize cleanly to enterprises still wrestling with data integration. The strategic read: value accrues to organizations that bring agents to their existing systems of record rather than replacing them.
