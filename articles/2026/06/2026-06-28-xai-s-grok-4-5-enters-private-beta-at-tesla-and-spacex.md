# xAI's Grok 4.5 enters private beta at Tesla and SpaceX

Elon Musk said xAI's Grok 4.5 has entered private beta inside Tesla and SpaceX — the first confirmed internal deployment ahead of a wider release. Per Musk's post, the model is built on xAI's 1.5-trillion-parameter “V9” foundation with supplemental training on Cursor coding data, and early internal evaluations put its performance close to, or above, Anthropic's Claude Opus. Access is currently limited to internal teams, so independent benchmarks are not yet available.
