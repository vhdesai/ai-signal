# Trump Endorses U.S. Equity Stake in AI Giants: "Could Be a Beautiful Thing"

Trump publicly endorsed government equity stakes in major AI companies. CNBC reported active discussions with OpenAI. Fortune noted bipartisan convergence: both Trump and Senator Sanders support public ownership. This shifts from debate to near-term deal structure — potentially affecting IPO terms for OpenAI, Anthropic, and others.
