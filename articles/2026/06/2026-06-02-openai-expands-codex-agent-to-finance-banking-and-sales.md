# OpenAI Expands Codex Agent to Finance, Banking, and Sales

OpenAI unveiled plugins extending Codex to non-engineering roles—public-equity investment, banking, sales—with legal and corporate-finance planned. Codex will integrate into ChatGPT broadly. The expansion directly targets Anthropic for enterprise customers as both firms approach IPOs.
