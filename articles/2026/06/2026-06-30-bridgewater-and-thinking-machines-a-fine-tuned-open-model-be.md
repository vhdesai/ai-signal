# Bridgewater and Thinking Machines: a fine-tuned open model beats frontier LLMs on finance tasks

Bridgewater's AIA Labs and Mira Murati's Thinking Machines Lab showed a fine-tuned Qwen3-235B model — trained on proprietary, expert-corrected labels via TML's Tinker platform — reached 84.7% accuracy on six financial document-triage tasks (vs. 78.2% for the best expert-prompted frontier model) at ~13.8x lower inference cost. The authors call it "differentiated intelligence": proprietary judgment data, not raw scale, is the moat — a concrete argument that domain fine-tuning on private data can beat renting the largest general model on both accuracy and unit economics.
