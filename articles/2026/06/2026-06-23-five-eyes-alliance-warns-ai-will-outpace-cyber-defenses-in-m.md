# Five Eyes alliance warns AI will outpace cyber defenses "in months, not years"

The intelligence agencies of the United States, United Kingdom, Canada, Australia and New Zealand issued a joint advisory warning that frontier AI models are improving fast enough to outsmart prevailing cybersecurity defenses within months. The statement urges governments and businesses to act now — accelerating patching, addressing legacy systems, and deliberately integrating AI into defensive operations — noting that AI "lowers barriers for malicious actors and increases the speed and complexity of attacks." Landing the same week as OpenAI's Daybreak expansion, the warning reframes AI cyber-risk from a medium-term concern into an immediate board-level priority. https://www.cbsnews.com/news/ai-bypass-cybersecurity-systems-months-not-years-five-eyes/
