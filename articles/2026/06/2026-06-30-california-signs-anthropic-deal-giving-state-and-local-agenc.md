# California signs Anthropic deal giving state and local agencies Claude at a 50% discount

California signed a first-of-its-kind agreement with Anthropic giving state agencies, cities, and counties access to Claude at a 50% discount, paired with free workforce training and developer support; Governor Newsom announced it June 29. Access runs through the state's new Statewide Information Technology Shared Services portal and is framed around responsible adoption and cybersecurity. It is a notable template for public-sector AI procurement — and lands as Washington tightens federal review of frontier models.
