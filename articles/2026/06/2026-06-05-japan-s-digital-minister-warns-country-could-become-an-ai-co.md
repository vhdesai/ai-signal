# Japan's Digital Minister Warns Country Could Become an "AI Colony"

Japan's digital minister warned the country risks becoming an "AI colony" if it falls behind in domestic AI. The statement signals Japan may accelerate public investment in AI infrastructure — echoing EU concerns and the global trend toward AI sovereignty.
