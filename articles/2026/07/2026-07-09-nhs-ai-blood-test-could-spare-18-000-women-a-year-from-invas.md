# NHS AI Blood Test Could Spare 18,000 Women a Year From Invasive Checks

NHS trusts in Leeds and Mid Yorkshire are deploying PinPoint, a machine-learning-enabled blood test that achieved 99% accuracy in identifying and ruling out gynaecological cancers across a Yorkshire evaluation of 16,481 GP-referred patients. Officials estimate it could spare roughly 18,000 women a year from unnecessary invasive investigations for suspected womb cancer. Developed with the University of Leeds, it is a concrete example of AI moving from the lab into frontline clinical triage.
