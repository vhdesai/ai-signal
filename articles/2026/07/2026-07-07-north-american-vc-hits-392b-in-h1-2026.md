# North American VC Hits $392B in H1 2026

North American venture capital investment reached $392 billion in the first half of 2026, driven overwhelmingly by AI infrastructure, foundation model companies, and AI-native applications. The pace is on track to shatter the full-year record set during the 2021 boom.
