# Hon Hai Precision (Foxconn), Nvidia's key server-assembly partner, said June revenue grew 52%, implying June-quarter…

Hon Hai Precision (Foxconn), Nvidia's key server-assembly partner, said June revenue grew 52%, implying June-quarter sales rose ~40% to roughly NT$2.51 trillion (~$79 billion) — well ahead of the ~NT$2.37 trillion analyst consensus. The beat is a fresh data point that AI hardware and server demand held strong through Q2. It lands as Alphabet, Amazon, Meta, and Microsoft collectively earmark roughly $725 billion for AI spending this year.
