# Samsung pulls first Yongin fab forward to 2029 for AI memory

Samsung will start operations 1–2 years ahead of schedule at the first of six Yongin fabs. Part of Korea's ~$880B national chip commitment. Land, power, and water — not tooling — are the pacing constraints.
