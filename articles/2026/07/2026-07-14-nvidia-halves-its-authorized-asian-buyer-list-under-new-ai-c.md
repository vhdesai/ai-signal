# Nvidia halves its authorized Asian buyer list under new AI-chip compliance whitelist

Nvidia reportedly cut its approved AI-chip customers in Asia by more than half and introduced a vetted white list across Singapore, Malaysia, and Japan. Enforcement is shifting from policing shipments to policing customers, with on-site data-center inspections becoming part of export compliance.
