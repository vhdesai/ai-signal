# Apple announces $30B+ custom-silicon deal with Broadcom (15B+ chips through 2031)

Apple CEO Tim Cook announced a multiyear partnership with Broadcom worth more than $30 billion to design and produce custom silicon and advanced wireless-connectivity components under Apple's American Manufacturing Program. Apple committed to buying more than 15 billion U.S.-made chips through 2031. The deal underscores the intensifying race to secure domestic advanced-chip supply for AI-era hardware.
