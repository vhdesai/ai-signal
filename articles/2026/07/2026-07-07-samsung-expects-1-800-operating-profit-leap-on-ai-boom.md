# Samsung expects 1,800% operating profit leap on AI boom

Samsung forecast an approximately 1,800% operating-profit increase, driven by AI-related demand for advanced memory used in data centers. The result underscores that the AI infrastructure cycle is not only a GPU story; HBM and memory capacity are becoming strategic constraints and major profit pools.
