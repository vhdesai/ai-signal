# OpenAI rolls out AI-generated ad creation inside its ChatGPT Ads platform

OpenAI has begun letting advertisers auto-generate ads within its ChatGPT Ads platform: a new "generate ads for you" option produces an ad variation from the advertiser's website and campaign settings for review and activation. The feature deepens OpenAI's build-out of an advertising business around ChatGPT and further blurs the line between AI assistant and ad network. For enterprises, it raises fresh questions about brand safety, disclosure, and where AI-generated commercial content belongs in the funnel.
