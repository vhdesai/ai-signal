# OpenAI Confirms GPT-5.6 (Sol, Terra, Luna) Public Launch for Thursday, July 9

OpenAI announced on July 8 that all three GPT-5.6 variants — Sol (flagship), Terra (balanced), and Luna (volume tier) — will launch publicly on Thursday, July 9, with global preview access expanding immediately. The models were previously limited in late June to roughly 20 government-approved partners under a federal cybersecurity order requiring a 30-day pre-release review of frontier models. This is the first firm public-launch date and follows reporting that OpenAI secured a regulatory green light. Sol leads on agentic and cyber benchmarks, with Sol Ultra reportedly hitting 91.9% on Terminal-Bench 2.1.
