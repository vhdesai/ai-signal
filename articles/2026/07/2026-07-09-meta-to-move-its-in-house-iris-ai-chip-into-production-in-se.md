# Meta to move its in-house "Iris" AI chip into production in September

An internal memo reported by Reuters shows Meta plans to put its next data-center accelerator, code-named "Iris," into production in September as it aims to roughly double computing capacity. Iris is part of Meta's four-generation MTIA (Meta Training and Inference Accelerator) program to design silicon in-house and reduce dependence on Nvidia GPUs. Continued hyperscaler vertical integration in AI silicon is a structural cost and supply-chain signal for the broader compute market.
