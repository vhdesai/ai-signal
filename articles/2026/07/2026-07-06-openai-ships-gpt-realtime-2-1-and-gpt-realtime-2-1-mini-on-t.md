# OpenAI Ships GPT-Realtime-2.1 and GPT-Realtime-2.1-mini on the API

OpenAI shipped two new Realtime API models for low-latency voice and multimodal agents, cutting p95 latency by at least 25% through improved caching. The full model improves alphanumeric recognition, noise and silence handling, tool use, and instruction following; the mini variant adds reasoning and tool use at the prior mini-tier price.
