# Mistral introduces Robostral Navigate for single-camera robot navigation

Mistral released Robostral Navigate, an 8B embodied-navigation model that uses a single RGB camera and natural-language instructions to move robots through real environments. It reaches 76.6% success on R2R-CE validation unseen, beating single-camera and depth/multi-camera baselines, trained entirely in simulation across ~400,000 trajectories and 6,000 scenes.
