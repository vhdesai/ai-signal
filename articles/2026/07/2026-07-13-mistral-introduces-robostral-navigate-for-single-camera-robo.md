# Mistral introduces Robostral Navigate for single-camera robot navigation

Mistral released an 8B embodied-navigation model that uses a single RGB camera and natural-language instructions to move robots through real environments. The company says it reaches 76.6% success on R2R-CE validation unseen and was trained entirely in simulation across roughly 400,000 trajectories.
