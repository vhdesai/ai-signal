# Microsoft quietly replaces partner models with MAI models in Excel and Outlook

Microsoft is reportedly routing tens of thousands of prompts per week in Excel and Outlook to in-house MAI models instead of OpenAI or Anthropic systems. The shift signals widening strategic distance between Microsoft and its AI partners as model routing becomes a cost, sovereignty, and product-control lever.
