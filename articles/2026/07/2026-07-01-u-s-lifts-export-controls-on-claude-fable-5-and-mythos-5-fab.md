# U.S. lifts export controls on Claude Fable 5 and Mythos 5; Fable 5 restored globally

Anthropic said the U.S. Department of Commerce lifted the export controls it had imposed on Claude Fable 5 and Mythos 5, ending a 20-day standoff triggered by an Amazon-reported jailbreak that produced exploit code. Controls were lifted after testing showed the same exploit reproduced on Opus 4.8, GPT-5.5, and Kimi K2.7 — i.e., no capability unique to Fable 5 needed containment. Fable 5 restored globally on July 1; Mythos 5 remains limited to ~100 vetted U.S. organizations. The episode establishes that a frontier model can now be switched off — or turned back on — by government directive, making regulatory exposure a first-class procurement variable.
