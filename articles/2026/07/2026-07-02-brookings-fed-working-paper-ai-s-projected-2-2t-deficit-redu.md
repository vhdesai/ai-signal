# Brookings/Fed working paper: AI's projected $2.2T deficit reduction may be "half fake"

A new working paper from economists at Brookings and the Federal Reserve finds AI productivity gains could cut the U.S. deficit by roughly $2.2 trillion through 2036 — but more than half of those savings could be erased by AI‑driven labor disruption and second‑order effects. The paper injects needed rigor into the "AI as fiscal fix" narrative that has circulated in Washington.
