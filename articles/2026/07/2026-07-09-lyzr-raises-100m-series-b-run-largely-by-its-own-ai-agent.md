# Lyzr raises $100M Series B — run largely by its own AI agent

Enterprise-agent startup Lyzr closed a $100M Series B at roughly a $500M valuation, with its own agent — “SivaClaw” — fielding questions from 130+ investors, drafting investment memos, and tracking which slides backers lingered on (via Bloomberg). The company says it drew $400M in interest without founders doing the traditional Sand Hill Road circuit. Beyond the novelty, it is a concrete data point on agentic automation reaching into high-trust, relationship-driven functions like fundraising.
