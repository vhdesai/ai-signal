# Infrastructure Nvidia's flagship Kyber NVL144 rack slips ~12 months to 2028 July 6, 2026 · The Next Web Nvidia has…

Infrastructure Nvidia's flagship Kyber NVL144 rack slips ~12 months to 2028 July 6, 2026 · The Next Web Nvidia has delayed its next-generation Kyber NVL144 rack — the cabinet designed to house its 2027 Rubin Ultra GPUs — by more than a year to 2028, and cancelled the NVL72x2 architecture, per research firm SemiAnalysis (first reported by CNBC). The holdup is a hard-to-manufacture multi-layer PCB "midplane" that packs 144 GPUs into a single system. The slip leaves Nvidia without a proven path to scale its most powerful training clusters and hands AMD and Google a rare opening at the rack level.
