# Samsung posts record Q2 profit but shares fall as AI-capex concerns spook investors

Samsung guided to a record ~89.4 trillion won ($58.4B) Q2 operating profit — up roughly 19x year over year on AI-fueled demand and sharply higher DRAM/NAND prices — yet shares fell ~7% as the rally had already priced the beat in. Analysts flagged concern that AI-infrastructure capex may not sustain the memory-price surge, a "sell-the-news" reaction echoing broader questions about the buildout's durability. Full divisional results are due July 30; for anyone tracking AI cost curves, memory pricing is now a leading indicator of infrastructure economics. Academic Research a RESEARCH
