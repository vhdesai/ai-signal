# Illinois Enacts First-in-Nation Law Requiring Independent Safety Audits of Frontier AI Developers

Governor JB Pritzker signed SB 315, the AI Safety Measures Act — the first U.S. law to require large AI developers (>$500M revenue) to retain a third party for annual independent safety audits. Fines up to $1M first violation, $3M thereafter; core requirements take effect January 1, 2028. Both Anthropic and OpenAI supported the bill.
