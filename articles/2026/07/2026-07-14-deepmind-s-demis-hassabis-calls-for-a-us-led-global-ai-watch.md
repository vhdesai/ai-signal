# DeepMind’s Demis Hassabis calls for a US-led global AI watchdog

Hassabis proposed a US-based self-regulator for “frontier-class” AI modeled on FINRA, asking labs to voluntarily share models 30 days before release for safety assessment. The framework would apply regardless of a model’s country of origin or open/closed status, and would give government authority to mandate development slowdowns if risks grow severe — one of the most concrete industry-led governance proposals to date.
