# Google's Waze rolls out Gemini‑powered features: Motorcycle mode and "Less Chatty" navigation

Waze introduced an AI‑built Motorcycle mode that accounts for rider‑specific shortcuts and hazards (potholes, speed bumps, narrow bridges), a "Less Chatty" voice option, and route suggestions based on prior trips — part of a broader push to embed Gemini across Google's navigation apps. The features still lean on Waze's real‑time traffic map and human, motorcycle‑focused editors for accuracy. It's a modest but concrete example of Gemini moving into Google's consumer surfaces.
