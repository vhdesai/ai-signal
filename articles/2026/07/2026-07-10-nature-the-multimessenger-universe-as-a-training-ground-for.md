# Nature: the “multimessenger Universe” as a training ground for frontier AI

A Nature Astronomy Perspective argues that multimessenger astronomy’s coming data deluge — combining electromagnetic, gravitational-wave and neutrino signals — offers an ideal proving ground for physics-informed frontier AI. The authors frame the domain as one where AI can deliver “transformative assistance and insight” while being disciplined by hard physical constraints. For technologists, it is a marker of where scientific foundation-model research is heading beyond language and code.
