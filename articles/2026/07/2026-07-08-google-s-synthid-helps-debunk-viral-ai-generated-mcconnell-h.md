# Google's SynthID helps debunk viral AI-generated McConnell hoax image

Google's SynthID watermarking system was used by Snopes to debunk a viral AI-generated image purporting to show Senator Mitch McConnell in medical distress. The case validates invisible AI watermarking while showing that provenance only works at scale when major model providers participate and downstream platforms preserve detection signals.
