# OpenAI’s ad business on pace to miss its own 2026 forecast by ~90%

eMarketer projects standalone chatbots (including ChatGPT and Google AI Mode) will generate under $1B in US ad revenue in 2026 — roughly 90% below OpenAI’s own $2.5B forecast. With OpenAI’s projection scaling to $100B by 2030 versus eMarketer’s ~$5.4B call, the gap spotlights growing skepticism about AI ad monetization.
