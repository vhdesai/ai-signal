# U.S. officials urge AI memory suppliers to build domestic fabs

In coverage of SK Hynix’s record listing, U.S. officials pressed SK Hynix and Samsung to expand AI memory manufacturing in the United States. The policy signal is that HBM supply is now treated as a strategic national asset, with industrial policy increasingly tied to AI infrastructure resilience.
