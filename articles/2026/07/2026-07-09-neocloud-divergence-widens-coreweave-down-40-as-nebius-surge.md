# Neocloud divergence widens: CoreWeave down ~40% as Nebius surges and overtakes it on market cap

CoreWeave grew Q1 2026 revenue 111% to $2.08B but burned roughly $4.7B in free cash flow against about $50.8B in liabilities, and its stock is down ~40% over the past year. Rival Nebius, up ~360%, has flipped EBITDA-positive and now carries a larger market cap despite far smaller revenue. The split shows capital intensity and debt loads — not AI demand — increasingly separating GPU “neocloud” winners from losers; some analysts still cite a $142 target on CoreWeave's ~$99B backlog.
