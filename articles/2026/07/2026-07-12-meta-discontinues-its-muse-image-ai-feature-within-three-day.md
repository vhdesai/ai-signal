# Meta discontinues its Muse Image AI feature within three days of launch after backlash

Meta pulled a feature — launched July 7 alongside Muse Image, its first in-house generator from Meta Superintelligence Labs — that let users generate AI images from photos on public adult Instagram accounts by entering a username, without notifying the account holder. Public accounts for users 18+ were opted in by default. Meta said the feature "missed the mark" and is "no longer available" following public backlash.
