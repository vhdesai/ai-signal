# New Orleans pauses data-center approvals amid AI backlash

Orleans has paused data-center activity amid rising local concern over AI-driven infrastructure demand. Local zoning, water and power availability, and ratepayer exposure are becoming practical choke points for national AI buildout. For enterprises depending on cloud AI capacity, permitting friction is now part of infrastructure risk.
