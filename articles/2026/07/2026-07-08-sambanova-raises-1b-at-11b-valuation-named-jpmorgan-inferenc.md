# SambaNova Raises $1B at $11B Valuation; Named JPMorgan Inference Partner

AI chip maker SambaNova raised $1 billion at an $11 billion valuation in its Series F led by General Atlantic. JPMorgan Chase selected SambaNova as an "inference-infrastructure partner" for secure on-premises inference — signaling that large banks want heterogeneous infrastructure rather than full cloud dependence.
