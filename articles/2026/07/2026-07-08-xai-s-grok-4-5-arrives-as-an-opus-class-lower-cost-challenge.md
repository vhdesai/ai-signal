# xAI's Grok 4.5 arrives as an "Opus-class," lower-cost challenger — independent benchmarks temper the claims

SpaceXAI (the merged xAI/SpaceX) released Grok 4.5 at $2/$6 per million input/output tokens, with Musk calling it "Opus-class — faster, more token-efficient and lower cost," later clarifying the comparison is to Opus 4.7. First-day independent benchmarking (July 10) placed it around #4 on Artificial Analysis's Intelligence Index with the board's best agentic tool-use score, but also a hallucination rate that climbed to roughly 54%; it is not yet available in the EU. For enterprises, Grok 4.5 is a strong intelligence-per-dollar option for tool-calling workflows where outputs are validated before action — less so where factual accuracy of the output itself is the primary metric.
