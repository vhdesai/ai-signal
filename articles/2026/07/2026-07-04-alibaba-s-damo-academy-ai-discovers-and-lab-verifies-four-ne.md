# Alibaba’s DAMO Academy AI discovers — and lab-verifies — four new superconductors

Alibaba’s DAMO Academy, with Renmin University of China and the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, unveiled ElementsClaw, an AI agent purpose-built for superconductor discovery. Powered by a 1B-parameter atomic model trained on 125M molecular and crystal structures, it screened 2.4M crystals in ~28 GPU-hours, flagged ~68,000 candidates, and yielded four entirely new superconductors that were synthesized and confirmed in the lab (critical temperatures up to 6.5 K). DAMO open-sourced the full 2.4M-crystal database and reported lifting the discovery “hit rate” from ~3% to ~40% — a credible Chinese entry alongside DeepMind and Microsoft in AI-for-science.
