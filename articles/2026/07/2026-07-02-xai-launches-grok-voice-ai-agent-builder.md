# xAI launches Grok Voice AI Agent Builder

xAI introduced a no-code Grok Voice Agent Builder (in beta) that lets users and businesses stand up human-like phone agents in under two minutes, with browser-based testing, enterprise integrations, and custom knowledge bases. It targets customer support, sales, and personal-assistant workflows, with reported pricing around $0.05 per minute. The release pushes xAI directly into the voice-agent tooling market alongside incumbents.
