# Beijing Weighs Export Controls on Its Own Best AI Models

Beijing is considering export controls on China's most capable AI models, mirroring U.S. chip export restrictions. The move would restrict foreign access to models like DeepSeek and Qwen, marking a shift from China's previous open-model strategy and potentially fragmenting the global AI ecosystem further.
