# Nadella warns enterprises they may be paying for AI twice

Satya Nadella warned that companies using proprietary AI models may pay once in token fees and again by exposing proprietary knowledge through prompts, corrections, tool use, and workflow data. Microsoft is framing enterprise AI advantage around control of data, routing, and proprietary learning loops.
