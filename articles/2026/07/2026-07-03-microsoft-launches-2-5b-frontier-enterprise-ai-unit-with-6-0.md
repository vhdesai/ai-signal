# Microsoft launches $2.5B "Frontier" enterprise‑AI unit with 6,000 forward‑deployed engineers

Microsoft unveiled Microsoft Frontier, a $2.5B commercial unit staffed by 6,000 "forward‑deployed engineers" tasked with turning stalled enterprise AI pilots into measurable ROI. The move mirrors Amazon's recent $1B FDE push and similar OpenAI and Anthropic programs, and leans on a deliberately model‑agnostic pitch (customers pick OpenAI, Anthropic, or open models). Nadella and commercial chief Judson Althoff framed it as the platform play as Microsoft shares sit down roughly 20% over the past year.
