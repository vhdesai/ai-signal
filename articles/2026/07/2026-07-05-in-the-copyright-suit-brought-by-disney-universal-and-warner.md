# In the copyright suit brought by Disney, Universal, and Warner Bros., Midjourney filed to compel the studios to reveal…

In the copyright suit brought by Disney, Universal, and Warner Bros., Midjourney filed to compel the studios to reveal how they use generative AI internally — for storyboarding and ideation, for example — arguing it would establish an “industry custom” of training on copyrighted content and bolster its fair-use defense. The company is seeking all studio prompts and outputs, not only allegedly infringing ones. It is a notable escalation in the generative-AI copyright fight.
