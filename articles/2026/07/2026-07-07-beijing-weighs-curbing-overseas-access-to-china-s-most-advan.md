# Beijing Weighs Curbing Overseas Access to China's Most Advanced AI Models

Reuters reported exclusively that China's Ministry of Commerce held talks with Alibaba, ByteDance, and Zhipu AI about restricting foreign access to top domestic models — including unreleased and open-weight systems — plus new limits on foreign funding of Chinese AI startups. The move mirrors U.S. treatment of frontier models as national-security assets and signals Beijing increasingly viewing its leading models as strategic, export-controlled assets.
