# OpenAI opens GPT-5.6 (Sol, Terra, Luna) to the public

OpenAI made its GPT-5.6 family broadly available after initially limiting the June debut to government-approved or trusted partners. Sol is positioned as OpenAI's new flagship for coding, biology, and cybersecurity; Terra is the mid-range tier; Luna is the low-cost option. The launch intensifies the frontier release cycle as Anthropic restores access to Fable 5 and Mythos 5.
