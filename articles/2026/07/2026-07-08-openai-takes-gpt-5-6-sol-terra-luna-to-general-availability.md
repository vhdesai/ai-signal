# OpenAI takes GPT-5.6 (Sol, Terra, Luna) to general availability

After a government-gated preview, OpenAI began the public rollout of its GPT-5.6 family, completing a global rollout across ChatGPT, the API, Codex and GitHub Copilot on July 9. Sol is the flagship ($5/$30 per million tokens); Terra targets GPT-5.5-level quality at roughly half the cost ($2.50/$15); Luna is the low-cost, latency-optimized tier ($1/$6). First-day independent benchmarks on July 10 showed Sol setting a Terminal-Bench 2.1 record (88.8%), but OpenAI declined to publish a SWE-bench Pro figure — leaving Anthropic's Claude Fable 5 with the published agentic-coding lead. For most teams, Terra's cost curve is the more consequential story than Sol's headline scores. x
