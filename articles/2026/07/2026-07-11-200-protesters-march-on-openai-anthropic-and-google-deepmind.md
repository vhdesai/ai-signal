# ~200 Protesters March on OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google DeepMind: "Stop the AI Race"

About 200 people marched through SF from OpenAI to Anthropic and DeepMind, demanding the labs pause all new model training. Protesters linked AI to rising rents, job losses, and environmental impact. The action reflects maturing, organized public opposition to the pace of AI development.
