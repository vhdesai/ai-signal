# Satya Nadella takes a veiled swipe at model-only AI companies

Microsoft CEO Satya Nadella indirectly criticized AI companies whose value is concentrated in foundation models rather than products, distribution, and workflow integration. The comments position Microsoft's enterprise AI advantage around application surfaces and customer workflows, reflecting intensifying partner tension as labs converge on enterprise agents.
