# 200+ economists and 16 Nobel laureates warn of “large-scale job displacement,” urge action now

A joint statement titled “We Must Act Now” — signed by more than 200 economists and researchers, including 16 Nobel laureates and staff from OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google DeepMind — warns AI could drive an economic transformation larger than the Industrial Revolution but “vastly shorter” in timeframe, risking large-scale job displacement. Organizers (Brynjolfsson, Korinek, Agrawal, and Cunningham) framed the signing of past skeptics such as Nobel laureates Daron Acemoglu and Simon Johnson as a notable shift in the profession. Released Monday, it remained a leading story through July 14 and stops short of specific policy prescriptions.
