# Amazon to raise at least $25B in bond sale to fund AI buildout

Amazon is raising at least $25 billion through an eight-part bond sale to support its AI infrastructure investment, and has told underwriters it won't issue additional debt this year. The raise, disclosed in an SEC filing, underscores how hyperscalers are turning to debt markets to finance data-center and chip capex. It is a clear signal that AI capacity spending is now large enough to reshape the majors' balance sheets.
