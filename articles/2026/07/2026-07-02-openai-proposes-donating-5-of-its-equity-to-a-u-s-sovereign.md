# OpenAI proposes donating 5% of its equity to a U.S. sovereign wealth fund

Sam Altman has proposed giving 5% of OpenAI's equity to a U.S. sovereign wealth fund, per the Financial Times, with the concept that other AI companies would contribute similar stakes. The stated rationale is to "secure good relations with the administration" and blunt political blowback as scrutiny of AI's economic gains intensifies. Talks remain preliminary, but they echo earlier discussions — since confirmed by President Trump — about letting the American public "become a partner" in AI's upside.
