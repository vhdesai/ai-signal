# Google Photos adds AI "Video Remix" powered by Gemini Omni

Google rolled out a "Video Remix" feature in Google Photos that edits and restyles clips in seconds — cinematic relighting, background swaps, and artistic styles such as watercolor and oil painting — using its recently released Gemini Omni model. The tool sits in the app's Create tab and is part of Google's push to embed generative AI across consumer products and keep users inside its ecosystem, competing with Apple, OpenAI, and Adobe. Incremental in capability, but high-distribution given Google Photos' very large user base.
