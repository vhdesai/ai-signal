# Microsoft reportedly plans a unified Copilot app and an always-on "Autopilot" agent

Microsoft is reportedly preparing a Copilot overhaul that would consolidate its consumer and workplace Copilot apps into a single experience, add an always-on "Autopilot" agent, and cut lower-usage features such as Copilot Podcasts and Labs — per WinBuzzer's account of an internal memo attributed to EVP Jacob Andreou. The memo reportedly framed the reset as a way for the assistant to "earn the right to exist" with users, an unusually candid admission that paid Copilot adoption trails ChatGPT despite Microsoft 365's installed base. Details are unconfirmed and Microsoft has not publicly announced the changes; treat as a well-sourced report, not a formal roadmap.
