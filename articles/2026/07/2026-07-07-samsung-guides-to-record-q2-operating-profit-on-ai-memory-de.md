# Samsung guides to record Q2 operating profit on AI-memory demand — but shares fall

Samsung's preliminary second-quarter guidance showed a record 89.4 trillion won (about $58.4 billion) in operating profit — roughly 19x higher than a year earlier — driven by surging demand for the memory chips that power AI servers. Yet the stock fell nearly 7% on July 7 as large investors trimmed positions, a rare divergence between blockbuster fundamentals and cautious money flows. The signal for executives: AI-memory demand remains white-hot, but the market is beginning to price in cyclicality at the top.
