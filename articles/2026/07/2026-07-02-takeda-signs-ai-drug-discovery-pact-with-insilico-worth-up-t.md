# Takeda signs AI drug-discovery pact with Insilico worth up to ~$600M

Insilico Medicine and Takeda unveiled a collaboration applying Insilico’s Pharma.AI platform to discover drug candidates across Takeda’s core therapeutic areas. The deal carries about $60M in initial and near-term payments and up to ~$600M in total value plus tiered royalties, with Takeda taking global rights to advance any selected AI-designed molecules through development and commercialization. It is another marker of AI moving into high-value pharma R&D rather than peripheral workflows.
