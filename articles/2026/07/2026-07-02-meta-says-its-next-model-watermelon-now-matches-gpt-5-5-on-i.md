# Meta says its next model, "Watermelon," now matches GPT‑5.5 on internal benchmarks

At an internal town hall, Meta Superintelligence Labs chief Alexandr Wang told employees that Watermelon — the successor to "Avocado"/Muse Spark, still in training and using ~10x more compute — has caught up to OpenAI's flagship GPT‑5.5 on closely watched benchmarks. The claim is unverified externally and Meta named no specific benchmarks. Notably, Zuckerberg simultaneously acknowledged the company's AI restructuring is running behind expectations.
