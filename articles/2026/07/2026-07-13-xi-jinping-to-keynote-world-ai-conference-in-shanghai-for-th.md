# Xi Jinping to keynote World AI Conference in Shanghai for the first time

China's foreign ministry said President Xi Jinping will keynote the 2026 World AI Conference (Jul 17–20). Analysts expect him to advance a Chinese-led World AI Cooperation Organization, positioning open weights and multilateral membership against Washington's export-control regime.
