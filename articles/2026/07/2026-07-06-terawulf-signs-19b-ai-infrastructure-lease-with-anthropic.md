# TeraWulf Signs ~$19B AI-Infrastructure Lease With Anthropic

TeraWulf (Nasdaq: WULF) announced a long-term lease with Anthropic at its "Justified" data campus expected to generate approximately $19 billion of contracted revenue over the initial term. In a paired transaction, it sold a majority interest in its Abernathy joint venture to Fluidstack, monetizing roughly a $450 million investment at a premium to fund expansion of its wholly owned AI portfolio. The deal deepens the trend of frontier labs underwriting purpose-built campuses years in advance to lock in dedicated compute capacity.
