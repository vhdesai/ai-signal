# Reuters: DeepSeek developing its own inference chip to cut Nvidia and Huawei reliance

Reuters reported exclusively (three sources) that DeepSeek is designing its own chip focused on inference rather than training — an effort begun about a year ago that could reduce its dependence on both Nvidia and Huawei. The Hangzhou company is in talks with chip-design, foundry, and memory partners and has quietly expanded chip-engineering hiring, though manufacturing remains a major hurdle given U.S. curbs on advanced foundry access and high-bandwidth memory. Nvidia shares slipped ~1.6% pre-market on the news. The move mirrors OpenAI (its “Jalapeno” inference chip) and Anthropic, and coincides with DeepSeek’s first outside fundraise (~$7B at a $52–59B valuation).
