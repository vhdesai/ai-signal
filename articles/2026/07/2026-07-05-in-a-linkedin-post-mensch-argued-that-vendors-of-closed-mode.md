# In a LinkedIn post, Mensch argued that vendors of closed models accumulate customer data that gives them “a front-row…

In a LinkedIn post, Mensch argued that vendors of closed models accumulate customer data that gives them “a front-row seat” to clients' business processes, alleging some labs have “a track record of going after their most successful customers.” He urged firms to keep data in open systems and control their own weights — echoing recent remarks from Palantir's Alex Karp. The Decoder notes the self-interest at play: Mistral leans on EU-sovereignty positioning and trails frontier models on raw performance.
