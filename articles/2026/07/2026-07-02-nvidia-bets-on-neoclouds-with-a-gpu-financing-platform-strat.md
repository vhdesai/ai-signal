# NVIDIA bets on "neoclouds" with a GPU-financing platform strategy

NVIDIA's AI Compute Partnership lets neocloud providers access GPU infrastructure without large upfront costs, earning NVIDIA both hardware revenue and ongoing usage-based income. Early partners SharonAI and Firmus Technologies plan to deploy up to 210,000 GPUs targeting AI-native inference workloads. Bulls see a platform expansion beyond hyperscaler GPU sales; skeptics see echoes of vendor financing — though NVIDIA is targeting existing inference demand rather than speculative capacity.
