# SambaNova raises $1B at an $11B valuation, lands JPMorgan as an inference partner

AI chip startup SambaNova said it raised $1 billion in a Series F led by General Atlantic at an $11 billion post-money valuation, with participation from T. Rowe Price, Capital Group, BlackRock-managed funds, Intel Capital and the Qatar Investment Authority. The company — which builds custom silicon, systems and cloud services tuned for inference — said JPMorgan Chase has selected it as an inference-infrastructure partner and will deploy its SN40 and SN50 systems. Coming after a $350M raise in February, the round reflects investor conviction that inference, not training, is where near-term AI economics are hardening.
