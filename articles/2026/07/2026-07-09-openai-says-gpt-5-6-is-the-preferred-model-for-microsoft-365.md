# OpenAI says GPT-5.6 is the preferred model for Microsoft 365 Copilot amid breakup chatter

OpenAI said GPT-5.6 will be the preferred model across Microsoft 365 Copilot in Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Chat, and Cowork. The disclosure follows reporting that Microsoft was substituting in-house MAI models to reduce costs, leaving competing narratives: strategic diversification versus deeper model integration. Microsoft surfaces the model as “Quick Response” and “Think Deeper.”
