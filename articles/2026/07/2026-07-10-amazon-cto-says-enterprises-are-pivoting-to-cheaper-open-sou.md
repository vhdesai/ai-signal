# Amazon CTO says enterprises are pivoting to cheaper open-source models

At the UN AI for Good summit, Amazon CTO Werner Vogels said enterprises are shifting workloads from expensive frontier APIs to cheaper open-weight models to control runaway token bills. He described companies burning through AI budgets in months and framed model choice as an architecture decision, reinforcing cost governance as a board-level AI issue.
