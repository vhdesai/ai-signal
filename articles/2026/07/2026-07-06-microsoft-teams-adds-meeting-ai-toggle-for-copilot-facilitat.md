# Microsoft Teams Adds "Meeting AI" Toggle for Copilot, Facilitator, and Recap

Microsoft is rolling out a "Meeting AI" control that lets licensed organizers and presenters turn Copilot, Facilitator, and Recap on or off during live Teams meetings. Launching in early July across Windows, macOS, mobile, and web, the toggle gives hosts granular per-meeting control for privacy, sensitivity, and compliance scenarios.
