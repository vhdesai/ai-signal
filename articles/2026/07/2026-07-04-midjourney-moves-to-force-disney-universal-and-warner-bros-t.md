# Midjourney moves to force Disney, Universal and Warner Bros. to disclose their own AI use

In the studios' 2025 copyright suit, Midjourney is asking the court to compel Disney, Universal and Warner Bros. Discovery to hand over their AI business plans, research reports, training datasets, model weights and even board-meeting presentations — arguing the studios train on copyrighted material under the same fair-use logic Midjourney invokes. A magistrate judge had, in mid-June, allowed the studios to withhold most such material. The discovery fight could shape how "fair use" and disclosure obligations apply symmetrically to AI plaintiffs and defendants.
