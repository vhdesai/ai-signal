# Nous Research in talks for new funding at $1.5 billion valuation

Nous Research, maker of the open-source Hermes agent, is reportedly finalizing at least $75 million in new funding led by Robot Ventures with significant participation from USV, at a $1.5 billion valuation. Hermes has gained traction as a locally run or hosted AI agent with built-in skills such as search, coding, and image understanding. The deal points to continued investor conviction in open-source agent ecosystems despite competition from major model labs.
