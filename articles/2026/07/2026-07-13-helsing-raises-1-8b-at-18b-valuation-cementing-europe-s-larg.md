# Helsing raises $1.8B at $18B valuation, cementing Europe’s largest defense-AI startup

German AI-defense startup Helsing raised $1.8 billion at an $18 billion valuation, with backers including JPMorgan Chase, Lightspeed, and Iconiq. The company said investor demand significantly exceeded the available allocation, cementing its position as a direct rival to Anduril and underscoring how AI-defense is becoming a standalone investment category.
