# AI Safety & Policy POLICY ANTHROPIC FREE SPEECH

The New York Times reports on what the government's fight with Anthropic reveals about free speech in America. The article points to a growing policy question: how far governments can go in pressuring, regulating, or conditioning AI model behavior without crossing speech and viewpoint boundaries. For AI companies and enterprise buyers, this signals that legal risk around model outputs is expanding beyond copyright and safety into constitutional and governance debates. URL not verified.
