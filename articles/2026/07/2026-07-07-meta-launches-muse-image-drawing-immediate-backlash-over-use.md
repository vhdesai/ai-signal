# Meta launches Muse Image, drawing immediate backlash over use of users’ photos

Meta unveiled Muse Image (code-named “Mango”), a free AI image generator from its Meta Superintelligence Labs unit, available via the Meta AI app, Instagram Stories, and WhatsApp. The launch drew immediate criticism over an opt-out feature that lets users generate AI images from any public Instagram user’s photos simply by tagging them — which The Verge and privacy critics flagged as a consent “landmine.” Meta says users can disable the behavior in settings and teased a Muse Video generator “in development.” The rollout is Meta’s latest attempt to show consumer output from its heavily reorganized AI unit while reviving old privacy concerns.
