# U.S. nears voluntary framework with AI companies for releasing powerful models

Reuters, relaying an FT report, says Washington is in advanced talks with AI companies on voluntary standards for releasing new frontier models, with an announcement possible within a week. The effort is driven by concerns that advanced models could be misused by foreign military intelligence; it is a voluntary framework, not a licensing mandate. The White House, Anthropic, and OpenAI declined to comment.
