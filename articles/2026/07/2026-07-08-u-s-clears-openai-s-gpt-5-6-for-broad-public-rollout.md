# U.S. Clears OpenAI's GPT-5.6 for Broad Public Rollout

The Trump administration cleared a broad rollout of GPT-5.6 (the Sol, Terra, and Luna tiers), lifting restrictions that had limited the model to roughly 20 vetted government partners since its June 26 debut. OpenAI reportedly floated a 5% government equity stake, with wider public and API access expanding this week. The move is notable against the backdrop of Anthropic's Fable 5, which was released and then recalled at the administration's direction — evidence that model access is increasingly a policy lever, not just a product decision.
