# Microsoft lays off nearly 5,000 employees across Xbox, commercial sales

Microsoft cut roughly 4,800 roles, with Xbox and commercial sales among the affected areas. The company framed the action as organizational restructuring while acknowledging AI is changing how work gets done; the executive signal is that AI productivity programs are now being paired with material workforce redesign.
