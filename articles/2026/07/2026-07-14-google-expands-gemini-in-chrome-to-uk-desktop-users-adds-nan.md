# Google expands Gemini in Chrome to UK desktop users, adds Nano Banana 2 image editing

Google began rolling out Gemini in Chrome to desktop users in the U.K., bringing tab-aware summarization and comparison, cross-app actions across Calendar, Gmail, Maps, and YouTube, persistent conversation memory, and Nano Banana 2 in-browser image editing; iOS follows next month. Google says the assistant is trained to recognize known prompt-injection threats and asks for confirmation before sensitive actions. The security framing is a tacit acknowledgment of the attack surface that agentic, in-browser AI creates.
