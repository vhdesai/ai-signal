# Meta plans a cloud business to sell excess AI compute

Bloomberg, via TechCrunch, reports Meta is developing a cloud infrastructure business to sell access to both raw AI compute and its models, putting it in competition with AWS, Google Cloud and Azure. The effort — reportedly dubbed Meta Compute — follows Meta's roughly $183B AI-infrastructure commitment and mirrors moves by SpaceX/xAI to monetize spare capacity. Meta shares rose on the report. It signals that infrastructure ownership is becoming a primary competitive moat in AI.
