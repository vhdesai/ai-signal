# Video-generation startup PixVerse raises $439M, valuation tops $2B

Singapore-based PixVerse closed a Series C extension totaling $439 million, pushing its valuation past $2 billion on the strength of 15 million monthly active users. Investors in the extension include Alibaba, Mirae Asset, and Eastern Bell Capital, alongside returning backers. The company plans to expand its “world model” offering and grow across new geographies — a signal that capital for generative video remains abundant.
