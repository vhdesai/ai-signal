# AI chip maker SambaNova raises $1B at $11B valuation

SambaNova raised a $1 billion first close for its Series F at an $11 billion valuation, with JPMorganChase selecting the company as an inference infrastructure partner for secure on-premises workloads. The financing highlights continued demand for AI chip alternatives and for deployment models that keep sensitive inference outside public cloud environments.
