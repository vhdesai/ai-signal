# SK Hynix rides AI-memory demand to a blockbuster U.S. listing

Memory-chip maker SK Hynix made its U.S. market debut on July 10, reportedly raising about $26.5B as demand for AI memory (HBM) soars. The listing is widely read as a barometer of public-market appetite ahead of anticipated Anthropic and OpenAI IPOs. While outside the core company watchlist, it is a meaningful signal on the health of the AI-infrastructure supply chain.
