# Anthropic launches “Reflect,” a usage-analytics recap for Claude

Anthropic launched Reflect on July 9, giving Claude users monthly recaps of recurring topics, conversation activity, peak usage times and tasks they hand off to the assistant, built on Claude’s persistent memory. It is limited to Free, Pro and Max accounts on web and desktop — Team and Enterprise are excluded — leaving IT teams to weigh how employees might build persistent summaries of company work through personal accounts outside managed controls. A modest feature with real governance implications for enterprise data.
