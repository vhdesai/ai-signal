# Intelligence is Free, Now What? Data Systems for, of, and by Agents

UC Berkeley researchers argue that rapidly falling inference costs are shifting the bottleneck from intelligence access to data-system design for agentic workloads. The piece lays out an agenda for data systems built for agents, composed of agents, and eventually built by agents, making it relevant to enterprise architecture teams planning for persistent, stateful, multi-agent systems.
