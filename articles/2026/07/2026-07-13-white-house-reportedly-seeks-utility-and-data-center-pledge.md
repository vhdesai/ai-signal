# White House reportedly seeks utility and data-center pledge on AI power costs

Reuters reports the White House is preparing to rally utilities and data-center companies around commitments tied to AI power costs. The policy focus reflects growing federal concern that AI load growth could feed through to consumer electricity prices. Energy procurement, grid interconnection, and public cost allocation are becoming strategic constraints.
