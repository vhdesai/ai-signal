# OpenAI opens GPT-5.6 to the public after U.S. national-security review

OpenAI is making its GPT-5.6 family — the flagship Sol plus the lower-cost Terra and Luna — publicly available on July 9, ending a roughly two-week restricted preview that had been limited to about 20 vetted partners at the U.S. government's request. The company says the models bring stronger agentic capabilities and notably improved performance in coding, biology, and cybersecurity. The rollout followed additional safety testing and government scrutiny over potential misuse. For enterprises, the biology and cybersecurity gains — and the security review that gated release — are the signals worth tracking.
