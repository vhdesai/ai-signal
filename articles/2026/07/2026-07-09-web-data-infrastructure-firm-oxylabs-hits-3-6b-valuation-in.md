# Web-Data Infrastructure Firm Oxylabs Hits $3.6B Valuation in First Funding Round

Lithuanian web-data and scraping company Oxylabs raised $130M from Warburg Pincus — its first outside capital after bootstrapping since 2015 — at a $3.6B valuation. Oxylabs supplies AI developers the large-scale public-web data used to train frontier models. The deal underscores how the data-supply layer beneath model training now commands unicorn-scale valuations.
