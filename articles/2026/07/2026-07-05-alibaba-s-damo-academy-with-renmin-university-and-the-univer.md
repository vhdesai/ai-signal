# Alibaba's DAMO Academy, with Renmin University and the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, introduced…

Alibaba's DAMO Academy, with Renmin University and the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, introduced ElementsClaw — described as the first end-to-end AI agent purpose-built to discover superconducting materials. The roughly 1B-parameter model, trained on ~125M molecular and crystal structures, screened 2.4 million crystals in 28 GPU-hours, surfaced 68,000 candidates, and lab-verified four previously unknown superconductors (top critical temperature ~6.5 K). The full 2.4M-crystal dataset was released publicly, though independent replication is still pending.
