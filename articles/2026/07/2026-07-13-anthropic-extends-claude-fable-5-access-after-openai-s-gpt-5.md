# Anthropic extends Claude Fable 5 access after OpenAI’s GPT-5.6 “Sol” launch

Anthropic extended free subscriber access to Claude Fable 5 for the second time in a week — an apparent response to OpenAI’s public release of its GPT-5.6 “Sol” flagship, which OpenAI claims sets a “new standard.” Fable’s debut had been overshadowed by a clash with the U.S. government, which briefly blocked Anthropic’s restricted “Mythos” models. The tit-for-tat underscores how quickly the two leaders are now trading blows for developer mindshare.
