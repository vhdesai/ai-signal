# Microsoft launches “Frontier Company,” an outcome-driven AI-deployment engineering org

Microsoft introduced Microsoft Frontier Company, a new operating business focused on “Frontier Transformation” — combining deep industry knowledge, change management and enterprise-grade AI engineering to deliver measurable customer outcomes and protect customer IP. Announced by Commercial Business CEO Judson Althoff, it is positioned as Microsoft’s largest, most capable outcome-driven engineering organization, going beyond “forward-deployed engineering” (press reports put initial scale at roughly 6,000 people). The move signals a shift from selling AI tools to owning enterprise AI results.
