# Nvidia unveils revenue-sharing "capital partners" model to finance AI-factory buildout

Nvidia introduced a model in which it partners with "AI clouds" to deploy large multi-tenant AI factories, earning both hardware revenue and a share of the resulting cloud revenue through credit-support and revenue-sharing arrangements. First partners include Australia's Sharon AI (up to 40,000 GB300 GPUs) and Indonesia's Firmus. The structure lowers upfront capital barriers for GPU-hungry startups while giving Nvidia a recurring, usage-linked revenue stream — and a hedge against custom-silicon rivals. It further entrenches Nvidia across the AI value chain.
