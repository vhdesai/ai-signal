# Reflection AI signs a $1B-plus compute deal with Nebius for Nvidia chips

Open-model startup Reflection — founded by two former Google DeepMind researchers — said it signed a more-than-$1 billion agreement to secure computing capacity from Nebius, including access to Nvidia's latest GPUs through 2029. It follows Reflection's June compute pact with SpaceX (reported at ~$150M/month). The deal underscores how open-weight labs are racing to lock in scarce capacity, and how last month's U.S. curbs on Anthropic's models have made open, harder-to-cut-off alternatives more attractive.
