# Reuters: DeepSeek Is Developing Its Own AI Inference Chip

Reuters reported, citing three sources, that DeepSeek is developing an in-house chip optimized for inference to reduce its reliance on Nvidia and Huawei silicon. The effort is early-stage — the company is in talks with chip-design, foundry, and memory partners and has quietly hired chip engineers — and coincides with DeepSeek's first outside funding round of roughly $7 billion.
