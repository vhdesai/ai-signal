# Traditional SaaS vendors lose budget share as AI spending rises

The Information reports that as businesses spend more on AI from Anthropic and other new providers, traditional enterprise apps and IT services providers are sometimes fighting for a smaller budget pool. AI is becoming a reallocating force inside enterprise software budgets, not just a new spend category.
