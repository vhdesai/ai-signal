# OpenAI and Google supply frontier AI to blacklisted Chinese firms via Singapore entities

A Financial Times investigation found that OpenAI and Google supply advanced AI services to Singapore-registered subsidiaries of Alibaba, Baidu, and Tencent, whose parent companies are on the Pentagon's military-ties blacklist. The sales are legal, exposing a gap between the intent of U.S. export controls and their enforcement.
