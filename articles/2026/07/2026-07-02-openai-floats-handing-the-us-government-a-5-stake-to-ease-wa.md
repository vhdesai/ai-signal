# OpenAI floats handing the US government a ~5% stake to ease Washington pressure

OpenAI has proposed giving the US government a roughly 5% equity stake — worth about $42.6B against its $852B March valuation — as it seeks to defuse mounting political pressure, per an FT report relayed by CNBC. CEO Sam Altman reportedly framed public ownership as the best way to share AI’s upside with taxpayers. The talks are early-stage and could require Congress; Reuters separately reported the Trump administration and Anthropic had not discussed a comparable arrangement, so this remains OpenAI-specific for now.
