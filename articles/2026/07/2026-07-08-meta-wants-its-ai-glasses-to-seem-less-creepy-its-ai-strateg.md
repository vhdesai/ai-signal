# Meta wants its AI glasses to seem less creepy. Its AI strategy says otherwise.

Meta announced that Ray-Ban AI glasses will disable camera capture if the recording LED is physically tampered with, acknowledging misuse risk around covert recording. TechCrunch contrasted the privacy control with Meta's broader AI data strategy, including public Instagram photo use in AI image generation, making this a governance case study in consumer AI hardware trust.
