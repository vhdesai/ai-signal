# Meta Launches Muse Image Generator, Drawing Immediate Consent Backlash

Meta unveiled Muse Image, a free AI image generator from its Meta Superintelligence Labs unit, available via Instagram Stories and WhatsApp. The launch drew immediate criticism over an opt-out feature letting users generate AI images from any public Instagram user's photos simply by tagging them — flagged by The Verge and privacy critics as a consent "landmine."
