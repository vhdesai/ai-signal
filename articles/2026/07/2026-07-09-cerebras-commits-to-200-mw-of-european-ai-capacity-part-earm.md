# Cerebras commits to 200 MW of European AI capacity, part earmarked for OpenAI

Announced at the RAISE Summit in Paris, Cerebras will bring its first European data-center capacity online by the end of 2026, with build-out across France and the Nordics and total capacity reaching 200 MW by the end of 2027. A portion is expected to support OpenAI workloads under the companies’ existing multi-year, $20B+ inference partnership. The move pushes fast-inference capacity closer to European users and signals continued sovereign-compute demand in the EU.
