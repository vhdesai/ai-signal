# Inexpensive Chinese model GLM-5.2 gains ground with Western developers

Reuters reports that Z.ai's open-weight GLM-5.2, released in June, is drawing notable Western developer interest at roughly a quarter of the per-token cost of leading U.S. models — a dynamic some analysts liken to a second "DeepSeek moment." On cost-adjusted usage it is competing directly with comparable Anthropic and OpenAI offerings. Observers tie the momentum partly to the recent Anthropic export-control disruption and delays in OpenAI's newest release. It sharpens pricing and market-share pressure on U.S. frontier labs ahead of anticipated public listings. URL not verified — reported by Reuters; no confirmed article-level link.
