# MIT Names AI-for-Science Leader Jesse Thaler Director of the Laboratory for Nuclear Science

MIT physics professor Jesse Thaler — inaugural director of the NSF AI Institute for Artificial Intelligence and Fundamental Interactions (IAIFI) — was named director of the Laboratory for Nuclear Science. The appointment matters to the AI-for-science community because Thaler bridges machine learning and fundamental physics, and he will continue leading work at the AI/particle-physics frontier.
