# Anthropic Brings Claude Cowork to Mobile and Web

Anthropic extended Claude Cowork — its agentic assistant that handles long-running tasks and local files — to mobile and web, letting users start a task at their desk and monitor or continue it from a phone while Claude keeps working in the background. The beta rolls out over the coming weeks, starting with Max subscribers before expanding to other paid plans. On mobile and web it acts primarily as a remote control for desktop and cloud tasks rather than accessing local files directly.
