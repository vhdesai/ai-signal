# DeepSeek Is Developing Its Own AI Inference Chip

Reuters reports, citing three sources, that China's DeepSeek is designing an in-house chip optimized for inference — not training — to reduce its reliance on Nvidia and Huawei silicon. The effort is early-stage, with the company in talks with chip-design, foundry, and memory partners and quietly hiring chip engineers, coinciding with DeepSeek's first outside funding round of roughly $7 billion.
