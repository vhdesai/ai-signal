# DeepSeek is developing its own AI inference chip

Chinese AI startup DeepSeek is developing its own inference chip to reduce reliance on Nvidia and Huawei silicon, according to three people familiar with the matter. The chip targets inference — generating responses from trained models — rather than training. If successful, it would mark a significant strategic shift toward vertical integration and add to the evidence of China's drive for semiconductor self-sufficiency.
