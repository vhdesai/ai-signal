China's foreign ministry said President Xi Jinping will attend the opening ceremony and deliver a keynote at the 2026 World AI Conference and High-Level Meeting on Global AI Governance. Analysts expect Xi to advance a Chinese-led World AI Cooperation Organization, positioning open weights and multilateral membership against Washington's export-control regime.
