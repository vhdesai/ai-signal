# Carnegie Mellon Launches FLARE-AI, an Open AI-Flaw Reporting Platform

Carnegie Mellon's Software Engineering Institute released FLARE-AI (Flaw Reporting for AI), an open-source platform for filing standardized, machine-readable reports of AI flaws, vulnerabilities, and incidents. It connects into the SEI CERT/CC's VINCE system, enabling CVE IDs and coordinated disclosure — addressing a structural gap in cross-vendor AI vulnerability coordination.
