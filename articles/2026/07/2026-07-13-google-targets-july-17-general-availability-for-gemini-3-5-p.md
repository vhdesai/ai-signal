# Google targets July 17 general availability for Gemini 3.5 Pro (2M-token context, “Deep Think”)

Google is reportedly targeting Thursday, July 17 for Gemini 3.5 Pro’s general availability — with what would be the largest production context window (2M tokens, roughly 2× rivals) and a “Deep Think” extended-reasoning mode gated to its $250/mo Ultra tier. Note this is reporting on a planned launch, not a shipped release; the model is not yet available.
