# Majority of US workers back an AI 'sovereign wealth fund'

A Verasight survey of 1,690 US adults found that 69% support requiring AI companies to transfer 50% of their equity into a public sovereign wealth fund — a response to rising tech-sector layoffs even amid strong corporate profits. The finding points to widening public unease over how AI's economic gains are distributed. For enterprises, it foreshadows growing political pressure on AI-driven workforce decisions.
