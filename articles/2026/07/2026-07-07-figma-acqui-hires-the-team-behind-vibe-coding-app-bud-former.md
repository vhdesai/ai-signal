# Figma acqui-hires the team behind vibe-coding app Bud (formerly Orchids)

Figma acqui-hired the team behind Bud (formerly Orchids), a Y Combinator-backed vibe-coding and AI-agent platform, pulling a coding-and-prototyping layer closer to its design canvas; both products will shut down by July 18, forcing user migration. Terms were undisclosed. The deal extends Figma’s AI push — following Figma Make and integrations with Codex and Claude Code — and signals continued consolidation among vibe-coding startups as design and software creation converge.
