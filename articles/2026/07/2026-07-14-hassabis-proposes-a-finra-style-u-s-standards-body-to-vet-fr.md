# Hassabis proposes a FINRA-style U.S. standards body to vet frontier models

Google DeepMind CEO Demis Hassabis proposed governing frontier AI through a self-regulatory standards body modeled on FINRA, with labs voluntarily sharing models roughly 30 days before release for evaluation. Lab leaders are increasingly drafting the regulatory template themselves rather than waiting for government-led rules.
