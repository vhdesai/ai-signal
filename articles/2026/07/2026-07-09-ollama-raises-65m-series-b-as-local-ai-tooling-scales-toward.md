# Ollama raises $65M Series B as local-AI tooling scales toward 9M users

Ollama, the open-source tool for running open-weight models locally, raised a $65 million Series B led by Theory Ventures, bringing total funding to $88 million. The company reports nearly 9 million users and monetizes through a hosted neocloud billed on GPU time rather than tokens. The round signals investor conviction that developer-owned local and edge inference will persist alongside frontier APIs.
