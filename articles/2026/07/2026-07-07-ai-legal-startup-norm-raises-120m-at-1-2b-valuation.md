# AI legal startup Norm raises $120M at ~$1.2B valuation

Legal-AI company Norm raised $120 million at a roughly $1.2 billion post-money valuation in a round led by Khosla Ventures, with Blackstone, Bain Capital Ventures and Coatue participating. Rather than selling software to law firms, Norm operates its own firm and bills on outcomes. URL behind paywall.
