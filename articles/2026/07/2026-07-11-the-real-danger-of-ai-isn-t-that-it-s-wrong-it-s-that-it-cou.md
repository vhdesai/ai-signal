# The real danger of AI isn't that it's wrong — it's that it could make us stop thinking for ourselves

Business Insider summarized research on “cognitive surrender” and “epistemic atrophy,” where users accept confident AI outputs even when they are wrong. The executive implication is that AI adoption programs should measure more than productivity; product design, training, and review workflows must preserve verification habits and human judgment.
