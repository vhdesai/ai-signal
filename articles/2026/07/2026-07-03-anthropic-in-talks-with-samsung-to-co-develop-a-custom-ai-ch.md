# Anthropic in talks with Samsung to co‑develop a custom AI chip

Per The Information, Anthropic is exploring its own custom silicon and has held discussions with Samsung on a potential collaboration — part of a broader push by frontier labs to reduce dependence on Nvidia. It follows earlier Reuters reporting on Anthropic's chip ambitions and lands the same day as its China access‑control moves, underscoring how supply chain and geopolitics now shape lab strategy.
