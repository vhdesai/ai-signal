# White House reportedly asked OpenAI and Anthropic to delay their newest models

The Hill reports the administration's request that OpenAI and Anthropic delay or suspend their newest models — weeks after signaling it would not let regulation impede innovation — is drawing bipartisan criticism as inconsistent. Analysts warn the intervention into private release decisions could undercut the administration's stated light-touch AI agenda. The episode is directly linked to the temporary restrictions on Anthropic's Fable 5/Mythos 5 and OpenAI's latest release. It raises fresh questions about the predictability of U.S. frontier-AI policy.
