# Cornell introduces Co-LMLM for externalized, inspectable factual memory

Cornell researchers introduced Co-LMLM, a limited-memory language model that externalizes factual knowledge into a continuous-query key-value store rather than encoding all facts in model weights. The work points toward smaller, more attributable models whose factual knowledge can be inspected, updated, and governed.
