# Apple sues OpenAI, alleging theft of trade secrets on unreleased products

Apple filed suit accusing OpenAI of misappropriating trade secrets and confidential information about unreleased products, centered on specific employees who allegedly accessed Apple’s proprietary materials during job interviews. The case escalates the talent-and-IP war between the two as they compete over next-generation personal devices.
