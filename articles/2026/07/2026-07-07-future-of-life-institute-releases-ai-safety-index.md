# Future of Life Institute Releases AI Safety Index

The Future of Life Institute published its inaugural AI Safety Index, scoring major AI labs across categories including transparency, safety testing, deployment practices, and governance. The index is designed to provide a standardized benchmark for comparing lab safety practices and informing enterprise procurement decisions.
