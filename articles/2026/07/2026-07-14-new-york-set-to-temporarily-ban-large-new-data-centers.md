# New York set to temporarily ban large new data centers

WSJ reports that New York is set to temporarily ban large new data centers, reflecting growing state-level concern over energy demand, environmental impact, and local infrastructure pressure. The move follows broader pushback in multiple jurisdictions as AI workloads drive new capacity requirements. For AI-dependent enterprises, permitting and power policy are now material operational risks, not background real-estate issues. URL not verified.
