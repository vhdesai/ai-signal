# Musk's xAI is renamed SpaceXAI following SpaceX's acquisition

xAI, the maker of the Grok chatbot, has been rebranded as SpaceXAI, consolidating its identity under Elon Musk's SpaceX, which acquired the company earlier in 2026 and owns X. The move is largely cosmetic in the near term but underscores the tightening integration of Musk's AI, social-media, and space assets under a single corporate umbrella following SpaceX's recent public listing.
