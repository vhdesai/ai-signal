# Remote Labor Index update: Fable 5 hits a record 16.1% automation rate on real freelance work

The Center for AI Safety and Scale AI published updated Remote Labor Index results, which score how often an agent can complete real, paid freelance projects at a quality a client would accept. Claude Fable 5 reached 16.1% — roughly double Opus 4.8 (8.3%) and well ahead of GPT-5.5 (6.3%) — versus a 2.5% field ceiling when the benchmark launched eight months ago. The signal for executives: economically capable agents are improving fast, but 84% of real projects still require humans.
