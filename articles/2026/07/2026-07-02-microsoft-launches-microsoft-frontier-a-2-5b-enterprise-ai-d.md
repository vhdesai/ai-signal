# Microsoft launches "Microsoft Frontier," a $2.5B enterprise AI-deployment business

Microsoft unveiled Microsoft Frontier, a new operating unit backed by a $2.5 billion investment and roughly 6,000 industry and engineering experts to drive enterprise AI deployments on Microsoft's existing tools. Commercial Business CEO Judson Althoff framed it as bigger than the "forward-deployed engineer" model, calling it the industry's largest outcome-driven engineering organization, with early work cited at the London Stock Exchange Group, Unilever, Land O'Lakes, and Accenture. It follows AWS's $1B FDE commitment two days earlier and comparable ventures from OpenAI and Anthropic — a clear signal that advantage is shifting from model quality to deployment muscle.
