# OpenAI Clears Government Review; GPT-5.6 (Sol, Terra, Luna) Goes Public July 9

OpenAI will make all three GPT-5.6 variants publicly available Thursday, July 9, ending a rollout restricted to government-approved partners since late June under the Trump administration’s AI executive order. Commerce cleared the wider launch after review. OpenAI called Sol its “strongest model yet” across coding, biology, and cybersecurity, and said it does not want government pre-release review to “become the long-term default.”
