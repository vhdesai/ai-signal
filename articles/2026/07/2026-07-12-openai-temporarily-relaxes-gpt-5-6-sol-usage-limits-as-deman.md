# OpenAI temporarily relaxes GPT-5.6 Sol usage limits as demand doubles traffic

OpenAI removed the rolling five-hour usage cap for Plus, Pro, and Business plans after Codex and ChatGPT Work demand “doubled traffic in 48 hours.” Efficiency improvements cut Sol quota per task ~10%, and the platform has crossed 6 million active users.
