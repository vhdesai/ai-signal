# Amazon CTO says enterprises are moving workloads to cheaper open-source models

At the UN AI for Good summit, Amazon CTO Werner Vogels said enterprises are shifting workloads from expensive frontier APIs toward cheaper open-weight models to control runaway token bills. He framed model choice as an architecture decision — not every workflow needs the highest-end model — and flagged training-data transparency as an increasingly important procurement requirement.
