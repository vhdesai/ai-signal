# Ant Group open-sources SingGuard-NSFA — a 133-language guardrail model for autonomous AI agents

Ant Group’s AI Safety Lab released SingGuard-NSFA, a guardrail that intercepts agent actions before execution to catch prompt injection, data theft, malicious code execution, and permission misuse. It spans 7 risk categories and 133 languages, ships in 0.8B/2B/4B/9B sizes, and renders a risk judgment in roughly 50ms — one of the more comprehensive Chinese open-weight agent-security releases to date.
