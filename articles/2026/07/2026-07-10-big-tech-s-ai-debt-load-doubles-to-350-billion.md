# Big Tech’s AI debt load doubles to $350 billion

Alphabet, Amazon, Meta, Microsoft and Oracle have collectively added about $350B in debt over five years to fund data-center buildouts, according to Bloomberg data. Investors gave Amazon’s $25B issuance this week an unusually cool reception, and S&P cut Oracle to its lowest investment-grade rating over AI spending.
