# OpenAI introduces ChatGPT Work as a GPT-5.6-powered professional super app

OpenAI launched ChatGPT Work, a workspace that fuses ChatGPT with Codex to generate documents, presentations, websites, and other work products from natural-language prompts. Powered by GPT-5.6 and available on desktop and web, it is OpenAI's clearest move toward an all-in-one professional agent platform spanning writing, coding, research, and automation.
