# Microsoft Cuts ~4,800 Roles, Citing AI-Driven Changes to Work

Microsoft said Monday it eliminated roughly 4,800 roles — about 2.1% of its global workforce — concentrated in Xbox and commercial sales. The company said the roles are "not being replaced by AI" but acknowledged that "AI is changing how work gets done," adding to a 2026 tally of more than 120,000 tech cuts where employers have cited AI.
