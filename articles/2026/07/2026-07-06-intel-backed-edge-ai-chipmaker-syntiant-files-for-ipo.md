# Intel-backed edge-AI chipmaker Syntiant files for IPO

Syntiant, an Intel-backed maker of ultra-low-power edge-AI processors, has filed for an IPO — roughly two months after Cerebras Systems' public debut. The company reported a Q1 net loss of $20.9 million on $64.5 million in revenue. The filing extends a widening wave of AI-silicon public offerings even as chip-stock performance diverges, a reminder that investor appetite is spreading beyond training GPUs into inference and edge specialists.
