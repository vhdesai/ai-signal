# Future of Life Institute: Top AI Labs Retreat From Safety Pledges

A new FLI AI Safety Index found leading labs have weakened or eliminated commitments to pause development if systems approach danger thresholds. Anthropic ranked first but earned only a C+; OpenAI and Google DeepMind at C; xAI, DeepSeek, and Mistral received failing grades. The voluntary safety regime is eroding before governments have a durable alternative.
