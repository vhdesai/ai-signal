# Meta Tests Always-On “Super Sensing” AI Glasses That Record Throughout the Day

Meta is testing a prototype AI headset with “Super Sensing” that uses a camera and microphone to continuously record the wearer’s surroundings. The concept raises fresh privacy and bystander-consent questions and lands alongside Meta’s broader push into wearables and agentic AI. It remains an internal prototype, not a shipping product.
