# Senator Markey unveils an “AI accountability” package of bills

Senator Ed Markey (D-MA) rolled out a roughly dozen-bill “AI accountability agenda” targeting data-center certification, automated hiring, workplace surveillance, healthcare AI and child safety. A forthcoming bill would require AI data-center owners to obtain FCC certification — weighing air and water quality, energy costs and grid reliability — before construction begins. The package signals intensifying federal appetite to regulate both AI infrastructure and deployment, with direct cost and compliance implications for operators.
