# Illinois AI Safety Law Toughens Reporting Requirements

Illinois enacted the Artificial Intelligence Safety Measures Act, requiring tech companies to disclose AI incidents and potential model issues, including possible misuse in cyberattacks or weapons-building. Governor JB Pritzker signed the law, positioning Illinois alongside California and New York in creating a de facto national standard.
