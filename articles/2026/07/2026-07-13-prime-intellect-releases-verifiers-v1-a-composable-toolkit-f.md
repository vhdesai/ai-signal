# Prime Intellect releases Verifiers v1 — a composable toolkit for agentic RL training and evaluation

Verifiers v1 provides composable tasksets, harnesses, and runtimes for agentic reinforcement-learning training and evaluation, including task management and training-trace recording. It aims to standardize how developers build and benchmark interactive agent environments.
