# Microsoft Teams adds a "Meeting AI" toggle for Copilot, Facilitator, and Recap

Microsoft is rolling out a new in-meeting "Meeting AI" control in Teams that lets licensed organizers and presenters turn Copilot, Facilitator, and Recap on or off during a live call. Launching in early July across Windows, macOS, mobile, and web, the toggle gives hosts granular control for privacy-, sensitivity-, or compliance-driven scenarios. It reflects a broader enterprise shift toward per-meeting governance of AI assistance rather than static admin settings.
