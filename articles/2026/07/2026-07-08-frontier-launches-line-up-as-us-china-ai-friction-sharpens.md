# Frontier Launches Line Up as US–China AI Friction Sharpens

________________________________ The past 24 hours set up a blockbuster launch week. OpenAI and xAI both locked in Thursday, July 9 public debuts — GPT-5.6 (Sol/Terra/Luna) and an “Opus-class” Grok 4.5 — while Meta shipped Muse Image, its first model from Superintelligence Labs. Capital kept concentrating, with SambaNova drawing $1B at an $11B valuation and JPMorganChase as an inference partner, even as US–China friction sharpened around China’s security warning over Anthropic’s Claude Code. On the research front, UC Berkeley, MIT, NVIDIA, and Liquid AI published notable work on agent economics, verification-ready multimodal models, and reasoning reliability.
