# SpaceX reportedly showed investors a "handset-like" AI device prototype

Per a Wall Street Journal report relayed by TechCrunch, SpaceX showed investors a prototype of a slim, phone-like AI device ahead of a recent share sale, signaling potential expansion into consumer hardware alongside Starlink connectivity. Elon Musk publicly denied the reporting, calling it "utterly false." If real, it would position SpaceX — with its manufacturing scale and chip access — as a new entrant in AI-native hardware. Treat as unconfirmed given the denial.
