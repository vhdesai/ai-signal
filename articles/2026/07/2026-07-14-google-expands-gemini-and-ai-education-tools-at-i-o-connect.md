# Google expands Gemini and AI education tools at I/O Connect India

At Google I/O Connect India 2026 in Bengaluru, Google unveiled a slate of AI initiatives — expanded Gemini Live, AI-powered education tools including ATL Saathi (confirmed on the Google DeepMind blog), new agentic-AI safety partnerships, and data-localization support for enterprises. The announcements reflect an aggressive push to entrench Gemini across developers, startups, and enterprises in one of the world’s largest markets. PRODUCT POLICY
