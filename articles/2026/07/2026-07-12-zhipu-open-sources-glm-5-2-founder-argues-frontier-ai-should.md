# Zhipu Open-Sources GLM-5.2; Founder Argues Frontier AI Should Stay Open

Zhipu founder Tang Jie wrote that frontier AI should remain openly accessible, releasing GLM-5.2 open-source and pledging to prioritize long-horizon reasoning, agents, and self-training over near-term monetization for two years. The stance contrasts sharply with Anthropic's and the U.S. government's moves to restrict frontier access.
