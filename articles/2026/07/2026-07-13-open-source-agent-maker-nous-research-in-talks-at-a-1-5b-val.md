# Open-source agent maker Nous Research in talks at a $1.5B valuation

Nous Research, the startup behind the open-source Hermes agent, is finalizing a round of at least $75 million led by Robot Ventures, with participation from USV, at a $1.5 billion valuation, according to three sources cited by TechCrunch. The company had previously raised about $70 million from investors including Paradigm.
