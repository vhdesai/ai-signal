# SK hynix surges ~13% in Nasdaq debut

SK hynix's US depositary receipts jumped roughly 13% in their Nasdaq debut, opening at $170 against a $149 offer price and closing near $168 — implying a market value around $1.27 trillion. Billed as the largest foreign IPO in US history, the listing reflects investor conviction in sustained high-bandwidth-memory (HBM) demand for AI accelerators. Its reception is being read as a barometer for the AI-infrastructure IPO pipeline, including expected Anthropic and OpenAI offerings.
