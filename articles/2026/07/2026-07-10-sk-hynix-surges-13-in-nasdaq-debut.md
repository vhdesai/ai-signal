# SK hynix Surges ~13% in Nasdaq Debut

SK hynix's ADRs jumped ~13% on Nasdaq debut, opening at $170 vs $149 offer — implying ~$1.27T market value. The largest foreign IPO in U.S. history, it reflects conviction in sustained HBM demand and is a barometer for AI-infra IPOs ahead (Anthropic, OpenAI).
