# OpenAI bets on families as ChatGPT goes deeper into households

OpenAI is hiring a product manager focused on ChatGPT experiences for families, caregivers, and older adults, signaling a move from individual productivity toward household-level AI adoption. The strategy has clear growth logic as ChatGPT usage rises among older adults and parents, but it also increases safety, parental-control, and child-protection scrutiny at a time when OpenAI already faces litigation related to minors.
