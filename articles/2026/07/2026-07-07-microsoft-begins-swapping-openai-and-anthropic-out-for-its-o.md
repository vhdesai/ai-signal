# Microsoft begins swapping OpenAI and Anthropic out for its own MAI models in some app features

Per Bloomberg, Microsoft has begun routing selected Copilot and app features to its own in-house MAI models where cost or data residency favor them — testing MAI-Transcribe-1 across Teams and Copilot and rolling MAI-Image-2 into Bing and PowerPoint. The shift is incremental (OpenAI and Anthropic still handle most production traffic) but strategically significant, enabled by the 2025 contract renegotiation that ended Microsoft’s exclusivity while preserving an OpenAI license through 2032. Microsoft claims one McKinsey-tuned MAI model beat GPT-5.5 on cost efficiency by ~10x. The rebalancing reflects Satya Nadella’s reported concern about over-relying on a single partner — owning the model, not just renting it.
