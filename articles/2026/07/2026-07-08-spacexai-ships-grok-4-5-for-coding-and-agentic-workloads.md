# SpaceXAI ships Grok 4.5 for coding and agentic workloads

SpaceXAI released Grok 4.5, calling it its most capable model to date and positioning it for coding and long-running agentic tasks. It was trained across tens of thousands of Nvidia GB300 GPUs with Cursor as a training partner. The launch reinforces the consolidation of model, tooling, and compute under the SpaceX/xAI/Cursor orbit.
