# Anthropic said it will use its new Claude Science "workbench" — which integrates more than 60 preconfigured research…

Anthropic said it will use its new Claude Science "workbench" — which integrates more than 60 preconfigured research tools and connectors — not only as a product but to run its own drug-development programs aimed at diseases the pharmaceutical industry considers unprofitable. It is the lab's most significant move into life sciences to date. The effort positions Claude Science as both a platform for outside researchers and an internal R&D engine.
