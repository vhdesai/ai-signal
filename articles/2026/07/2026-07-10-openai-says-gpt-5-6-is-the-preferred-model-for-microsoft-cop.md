# OpenAI says GPT-5.6 is the preferred model for Microsoft Copilot 365 amid breakup chatter

OpenAI said GPT-5.6 is now the preferred model for Microsoft 365 Copilot across Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Chat, and Cowork. The announcement is strategically timed: it counters recent reporting that Microsoft is routing more prompts to its own MAI models, but it also confirms enterprise customers should expect dynamic model routing and provenance to become part of Copilot governance and procurement discussions.
