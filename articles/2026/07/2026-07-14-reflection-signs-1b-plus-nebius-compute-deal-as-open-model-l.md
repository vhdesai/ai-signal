# Reflection signs $1B-plus Nebius compute deal as open-model labs lock up GPUs

Open-model startup Reflection signed a more-than-$1 billion agreement to secure computing capacity from Nebius, including Nvidia's latest GPUs through 2029. The deal follows Reflection's June compute pact with SpaceX, reportedly worth roughly $150M per month.
