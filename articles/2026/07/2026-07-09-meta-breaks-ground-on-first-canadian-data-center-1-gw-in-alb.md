# Meta Breaks Ground on First Canadian Data Center — 1 GW in Alberta

Meta broke ground on a 1-gigawatt AI data center in Sturgeon County, Alberta, an investment of roughly $9 billion (C$13 billion) — its 33rd facility and its largest outside the United States. The site is designed for AI workloads across Facebook and Instagram, with about 3,000 construction workers at peak and 300+ permanent roles. Its natural-gas-adjacent siting reignited debate over AI's escalating power demand and climate footprint.
