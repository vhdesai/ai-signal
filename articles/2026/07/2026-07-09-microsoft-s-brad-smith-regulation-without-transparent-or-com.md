# Microsoft's Brad Smith: “Regulation Without Transparent or Complete Rules”

Speaking to Fortune on the sidelines of the AI for Good Global Summit, Microsoft President Brad Smith criticized Washington's AI posture as “regulation without transparent or complete rules,” warning that “without rules, businesses can't plan.” His remarks follow Commerce Department decisions to restrict access to two of the industry's most advanced models, which have left even the labs building them uncertain about federal policy. The comments capture growing industry frustration that ad hoc access controls are outpacing any coherent framework.
