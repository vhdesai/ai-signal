# Neocloud Together AI raises $800M, leaps to an $8.3B valuation

AI "neocloud" Together AI raised an $800 million Series C at an $8.3 billion valuation — more than double its $3.3 billion mark from early 2025 — led by Aramco Ventures with Nvidia, Vista Equity Partners, and General Catalyst participating. The company rents Nvidia GPU clusters and specializes in hosting open-source models, placing it squarely in the fast-growing market for cheaper, open-weight AI infrastructure. The raise underscores continued investor appetite for the "picks and shovels" layer beneath the frontier labs.
