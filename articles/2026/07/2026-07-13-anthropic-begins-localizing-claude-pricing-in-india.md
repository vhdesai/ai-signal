# Anthropic begins localizing Claude pricing in India

Anthropic has started localizing Claude’s pricing in India — its largest market after the U.S. — with rupee-denominated plans appearing on the web and mobile apps, though it has not yet enabled UPI payments (unlike OpenAI, which added rupee pricing with UPI support last year). The move reflects the intensifying race to localize pricing and payments for India’s fast-growing user base. Research Breakthroughs RESEARCH
