# Anthropic appoints former Fed Chair Ben Bernanke to its Long-Term Benefit Trust

Anthropic named Nobel laureate and former Federal Reserve Chair Ben Bernanke to its Long-Term Benefit Trust, the independent body that helps hold the company to its public-benefit mission. Bernanke, who led the Fed from 2006 to 2014 through the 2008 financial crisis, brings prominent economic-governance credibility as Anthropic scales and reportedly weighs an IPO.
