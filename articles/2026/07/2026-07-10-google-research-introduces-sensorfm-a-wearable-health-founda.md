# Google Research introduces SensorFM, a wearable-health foundation model

Google Research unveiled SensorFM, a foundation model for wearable health pretrained on roughly one trillion minutes of sensor data. It is designed to generalize across the health and activity signals collected from wearable devices, a step toward general-purpose models for continuous physiological data.
