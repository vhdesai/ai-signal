# Meta moves to sell excess AI compute through a new cloud business

Meta shares closed up nearly 9% after reports (first from Bloomberg, confirmed by CNBC) that the company is building a cloud business to sell excess computing capacity to outside customers. The move is a potential path to recoup the billions Meta has poured into AI infrastructure, and a notable strategic pivot for a company that has historically consumed rather than resold compute. It is still deciding whether to offer hosted models or raw compute.
