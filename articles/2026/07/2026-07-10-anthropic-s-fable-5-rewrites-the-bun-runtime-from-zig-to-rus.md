# Anthropic's Fable 5 rewrites the Bun runtime from Zig to Rust in 11 days

Bun creator Jarred Sumner said a pre-release Claude Fable 5 did most of the work rewriting the JavaScript runtime's engine from Zig to Rust — about 64 Claude instances running in parallel for 11 days, producing over one million lines of code at roughly $165,000 in API cost. Sumner estimated a human team would have needed about a year; the resulting Bun v1.4.0 canary fixes 128 bugs and runs 2–5% faster. It is one of the most concrete demonstrations yet of parallelized, long-horizon agentic engineering on a production codebase. (Anthropic acquired Bun's team in December 2025.)
