# Hot French startup ZML releases free product to speed inference across lots of AI chips

ZML released a free LLM inference server designed to run across Nvidia, AMD, Google TPU, Apple Metal, and Intel Arc hardware. The product targets a core infrastructure concern for CTOs: avoiding lock-in at the inference layer while optimizing cost, energy use, and chip availability across heterogeneous fleets.
