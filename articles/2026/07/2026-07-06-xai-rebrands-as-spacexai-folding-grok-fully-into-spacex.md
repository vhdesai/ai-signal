# xAI rebrands as SpaceXAI, folding Grok fully into SpaceX

Elon Musk's xAI has formally rebranded as SpaceXAI, consolidating the Grok developer under the SpaceX corporate umbrella following SpaceX's acquisition of xAI earlier this year and its recent public listing. The change is cosmetic on the surface but signals tighter integration of Musk's AI, launch, and social-media (X) assets inside one publicly traded entity.
