# Xi Jinping to deliver his first-ever keynote at China's World AI Conference (Shanghai, July 17)

China's foreign ministry announced Monday that President Xi Jinping will attend the opening ceremony of the 2026 World AI Conference in Shanghai on July 17 and deliver a keynote "systematically elaborating" China's AI policies — his first appearance at the flagship summit. The four-day event will host 140+ forums and 1,100+ exhibitors, underscoring Beijing's escalating AI-governance push amid deepening US rivalry.
