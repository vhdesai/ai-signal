# OpenAI introduces “ChatGPT Work,” a GPT-5.6-powered super app

OpenAI launched ChatGPT Work, a workspace that fuses ChatGPT with its Codex coding agent to generate documents, presentations and websites from natural-language prompts, bringing coding-grade automation to non-programmers. Powered by the new GPT-5.6 model and live on desktop and web, it is OpenAI’s clearest move yet toward an all-in-one professional “super app” spanning writing, coding, research and automation. It positions OpenAI directly against enterprise-assistant offerings from Anthropic and Microsoft.
