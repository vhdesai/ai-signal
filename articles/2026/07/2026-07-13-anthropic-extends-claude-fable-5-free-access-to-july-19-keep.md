# Anthropic extends Claude Fable 5 free access to July 19, keeps Claude Code limits 50% higher

Anthropic gave Pro, Max, and Team subscribers a second one-week extension of free access to its flagship "Mythos-class" Claude Fable 5 model, moving the cutoff from July 12 to July 19, alongside a 50% boost to Claude Code weekly rate limits. It is the second extension in a week, widely read as a competitive response to OpenAI's GPT‑5.6 launch. After July 19, Fable 5 transitions to a credit-based usage model.
