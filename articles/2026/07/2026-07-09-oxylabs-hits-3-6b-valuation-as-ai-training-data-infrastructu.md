# Oxylabs hits $3.6B valuation as AI-training data infrastructure draws capital

Lithuanian web-data and scraping-infrastructure company Oxylabs raised $130 million from Warburg Pincus at a $3.6 billion valuation. Oxylabs supplies AI developers with large-scale public-web data collection used to train frontier models. The deal shows the data-supply layer beneath model training now commands unicorn-scale valuations.
