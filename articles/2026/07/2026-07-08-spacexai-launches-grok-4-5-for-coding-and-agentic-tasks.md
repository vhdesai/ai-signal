# SpaceXAI launches Grok 4.5 for coding and agentic tasks

SpaceXAI (Elon Musk's xAI) released Grok 4.5 on July 8, calling it its most intelligent model to date, purpose-built for coding and agentic tasks and trained across tens of thousands of Nvidia GB300 GPUs. AI coding agent Cursor confirmed it partnered with SpaceXAI to train the model; SpaceX said last month it would acquire Cursor-maker Anysphere in an all-stock deal worth roughly $60 billion. The move deepens vertical integration across frontier compute, model, and developer tooling under a single owner.
