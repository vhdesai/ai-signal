# Goldman Sachs Names Its Favorite Chinese AI Models

Goldman published research naming Zhipu as its top pick alongside DeepSeek and ByteDance, citing GLM-5.2 reaching "near-frontier" performance. The note underscores how quickly Chinese open-weight models are being treated as an investable, cost-competitive alternative to U.S. labs.
