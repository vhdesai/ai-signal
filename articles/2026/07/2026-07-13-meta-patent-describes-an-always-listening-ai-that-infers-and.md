# Meta patent describes an always‑listening AI that infers and logs your emotions

A Meta patent application (US 2026/0182881, published July 2) describes an AI that reads emotional state from a user's voice — each read timestamped and tagged with location, activity and phone use — with some variants listening throughout the day. Meta has not announced a product, and a filing only stakes a claim on an idea, but the disclosure sharpens privacy and consent questions around ambient AI and wearables. For enterprises weighing always‑on assistants, it previews the regulatory scrutiny such features will attract.
