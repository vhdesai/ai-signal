# OpenAI launches ChatGPT Work (agentic assistant) and GPT-Live voice

Alongside GPT-5.6, OpenAI released ChatGPT Work — an agent with built-in Codex that reads data from connected apps, runs multi-step workflows in the background across web, mobile, and desktop, supports plugins, and includes a “Sites” beta for building live dashboards and web apps. OpenAI also introduced GPT-Live (voice generation with simultaneous listen-and-speak), merged the Codex desktop app into ChatGPT (renaming the old app “ChatGPT Classic”), and set its standalone Atlas browser to shut down on Aug 9, 2026. ChatGPT Work is available immediately for Pro, Enterprise, and Edu, with Plus and Business following within days.
