# Nvidia and Valar Atomics demo a nuclear-powered, "waterless" data center in Utah

At a demonstration in Orangeville, Utah, Nvidia and nuclear startup Valar Atomics ran an AI chip powered directly by a small modular reactor and cooled with helium rather than water — a "waterless" data-center concept aimed at AI's mounting power and cooling constraints. The demo, which served a live website off the reactor, is early-stage but lands amid intensifying scrutiny of AI data centers' water and energy footprints. For infrastructure planners, it signals growing interest in co-locating compute with dedicated, high-temperature nuclear power.
