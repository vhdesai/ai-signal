# White House Disputes "Clearing" GPT-5.6 as US Frontier Review Regime Takes Shape

Axios reported Commerce cleared OpenAI's GPT-5.6, but the White House told CNBC it gave "no green light, approval or clearance," insisting decisions "rest entirely with the companies." The episode reflects a maturing — and contested — voluntary pre-deployment review. It arrives as OpenAI CEO Altman floats giving the US government a 5% equity stake.
