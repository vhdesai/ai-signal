# The power of collaboration: How we can reduce traffic congestion

Google Research published results from a large-scale routing intervention study across 10 U.S. cities, showing that small changes in route recommendations can improve network-wide congestion and emissions. The broader AI lesson is that optimization systems can move from individually optimal recommendations toward cooperative, system-level outcomes when platforms control enough decision surface area.
