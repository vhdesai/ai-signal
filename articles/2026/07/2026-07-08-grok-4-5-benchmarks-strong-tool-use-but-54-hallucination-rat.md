# Grok 4.5 Benchmarks: Strong Tool-Use, but 54% Hallucination Rate and No EU Availability

SpaceXAI's Grok 4.5 ($2/$6 per M tokens) ranked #4 on AI Intelligence Index with the board's best agentic tool-use score. But hallucination rates hit ~54% and context window was cut to 500K. Best suited for tool-calling workflows where outputs are verified before action — not where factual accuracy of the output itself is the primary metric.
