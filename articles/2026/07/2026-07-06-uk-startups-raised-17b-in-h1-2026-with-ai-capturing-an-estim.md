# UK startups raised ~$17B in H1 2026, with AI capturing an estimated 74% of venture capital

UK startups raised about $17 billion in the first half of 2026 — roughly double the prior year — with AI ventures capturing an estimated 74% of venture capital, per Tech Funding News (citing figures also reported by The Times). The UK's share of European deep-tech funding nearly doubled to about 41%. The data underscores how sharply capital is concentrating into AI, prompting concern that non-AI sectors are being crowded out of funding. URL behind paywall.
