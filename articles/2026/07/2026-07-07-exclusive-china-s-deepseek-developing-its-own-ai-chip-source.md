# Exclusive: China's DeepSeek developing its own AI chip, sources say

Reuters reported that DeepSeek has been designing an inference-focused AI chip for roughly a year, including hiring chip engineers and holding discussions across foundry, design, and memory supply chains. If validated, this would mark another step toward a bifurcated AI stack in which leading Chinese model developers reduce reliance on Nvidia and Huawei for inference economics.
