# Economists and AI researchers warn that labor disruption may outpace institutions

More than 200 economists and AI researchers, including 16 Nobel laureates and leaders from OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google DeepMind, signed a statement urging faster preparation for AI's economic impact. The breadth of signatories reframes AI labor disruption from a research topic into an active governance demand.
