# SambaNova Raises $1B for Inference Silicon

SambaNova Systems raised $1 billion in new funding focused on inference-optimized AI chips. The round values the company at approximately $12 billion and reflects growing investor conviction that inference workloads — not training — will drive the next wave of AI compute demand.
