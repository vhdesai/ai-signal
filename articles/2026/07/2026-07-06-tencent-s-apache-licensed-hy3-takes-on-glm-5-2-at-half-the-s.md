# Tencent's Apache-licensed Hy3 takes on GLM-5.2 at half the size

Tencent released Hy3 as a 295B-parameter mixture-of-experts model with 21B active parameters under an Apache 2.0 license. The licensing shift is strategically important for enterprise buyers because it removes geographic restrictions that often block commercial evaluation of Chinese open-weight models, though coding performance remains a comparative gap versus GLM-5.2.
