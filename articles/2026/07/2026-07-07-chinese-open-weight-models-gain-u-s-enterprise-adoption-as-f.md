# Chinese Open-Weight Models Gain U.S. Enterprise Adoption as Frontier Costs Rise

U.S. companies are increasingly routing production workloads to Chinese-built models such as DeepSeek and Z.ai, which now rival frontier U.S. systems on capability while costing materially less. Chinese models regularly surpass 30% of usage on the OpenRouter routing platform. The shift is driven by rising token prices at U.S. labs — with real implications for pricing power and data-governance posture.
