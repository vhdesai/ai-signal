# Micron expands U.S. investment to about $250B through 2035 on AI-memory demand

Micron unveiled an expanded U.S. manufacturing plan worth roughly $250 billion through 2035, tied to surging demand for high-bandwidth memory used in AI accelerators. Shares rose about 7% on the announcement and are up more than 250% year to date. The commitment reinforces that the AI supply-chain bottleneck is shifting toward advanced memory, not just GPUs.
