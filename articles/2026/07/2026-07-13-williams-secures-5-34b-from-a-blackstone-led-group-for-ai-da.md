# Williams secures $5.34B from a Blackstone-led group for AI data-center power plants

Williams (NYSE: WMB) announced that a Blackstone Credit & Insurance-led group (with Apollo and KKR) will invest $5.34B for a 49% stake in five behind-the-meter gas “Power Innovation” plants being built to feed AI data centers. It is another marker of the energy-financing scramble behind AI compute.
