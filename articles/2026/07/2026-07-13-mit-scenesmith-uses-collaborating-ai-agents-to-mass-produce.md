# MIT SceneSmith uses collaborating AI agents to mass-produce robot-training worlds

MIT CSAIL and Toyota Research Institute introduced SceneSmith, which orchestrates designer, critic, and orchestrator agents to auto-generate realistic 3D indoor scenes for robot simulation. The team produced more than 1,300 scenes with up to six times more objects than prior methods.
