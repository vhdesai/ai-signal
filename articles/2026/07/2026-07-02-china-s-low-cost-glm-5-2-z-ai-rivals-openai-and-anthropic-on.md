# China's low‑cost GLM‑5.2 (Z.ai) rivals OpenAI and Anthropic on coding — a "mini‑DeepSeek moment"

Reuters reports that GLM‑5.2, an open‑weight model from Beijing startup Z.ai, is drawing serious Western interest for coding and agentic performance approaching top U.S. models at a fraction of the cost. Analysts are calling it a "mini‑DeepSeek moment," reinforcing the Stanford AI Index finding that the U.S.–China capability gap has narrowed to low single digits. The signal for buyers: credible, cheaper alternatives are reaching the evaluation shortlist.
