# Satya Nadella warns enterprises about dependence on AI model labs

In a Monday blog post, Microsoft CEO Satya Nadella joined a growing chorus — including VC Jason Calacanis and Palantir’s Alex Karp — cautioning that companies relying on proprietary models from labs like OpenAI and Anthropic risk handing those labs access to their most sensitive business data. The concern is that model providers could then use that knowledge to compete with their own customers. Coming from OpenAI’s largest backer, the warning carries unusual weight for enterprise buyers.
