# Reuters: Beijing weighs curbing overseas access to China’s most advanced AI models

Reuters reported exclusively that China’s Ministry of Commerce held talks over the past month with Alibaba, ByteDance, and startup Z.ai (Zhipu) about restricting foreign access to top domestic models — including unreleased and open-weight systems — plus new limits on foreign funding of Chinese AI startups. The move would mirror U.S. treatment of frontier models as national-security assets, such as June controls on Anthropic’s Fable 5. Sources describe discussions rather than a signed decree, and scope remains unsettled. The deliberations signal Beijing increasingly viewing its leading models as strategic, export-controlled assets.
