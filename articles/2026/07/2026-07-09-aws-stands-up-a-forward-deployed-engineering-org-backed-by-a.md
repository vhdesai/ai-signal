# AWS stands up a “Forward Deployed Engineering” org backed by a $1B Amazon investment

AWS launched a Forward Deployed Engineering (FDE) organization, backed by a $1 billion Amazon commitment, to embed engineers directly with customers and co-develop agentic AI systems in days rather than months. AWS frames FDE as a response to enterprise AI outgrowing the traditional advisory/roadmap model, mirroring the forward-deployed approach pioneered by Palantir and adopted by OpenAI. Channel partners characterized it as a “force multiplier” rather than direct competition for systems-integrator work.
