# Amazon CTO Werner Vogels: enterprises pivoting to cheaper open-source models

Speaking on the sidelines of the UN's AI for Good summit, Amazon CTO Werner Vogels said enterprises alarmed by runaway token bills are increasingly shifting from the largest frontier models — OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google DeepMind — toward cheaper open-source alternatives. He cited companies burning through AI budgets in months, pointing to a cost-driven rebalancing of the enterprise AI stack. The comments dovetail with Meta's same-day price-war push.
