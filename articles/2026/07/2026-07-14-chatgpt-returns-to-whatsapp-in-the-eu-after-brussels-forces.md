# ChatGPT returns to WhatsApp in the EU after Brussels forces Meta to reopen

OpenAI re-enabled ChatGPT inside WhatsApp across the European Economic Area — live since July 13 via the verified 1-800-CHATGPT contact, no account required — after the European Commission’s June interim order compelled Meta to reopen its WhatsApp Business API to rival assistants amid an antitrust probe. Meta had removed general-purpose bots (including Microsoft Copilot and Perplexity) in January 2026, leaving only its own assistant. The episode is a concrete case of EU competition enforcement reshaping AI distribution — and further evidence the fight is moving from model quality to platform access. DEV TOOLS
