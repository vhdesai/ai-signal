# OpenAI temporarily removes GPT‑5.6 Sol's 5-hour usage limit after demand surge

OpenAI said it is temporarily removing the rolling five-hour usage cap for Plus, Pro, and Business plans and resetting all users' usage, after Codex and ChatGPT Work demand "doubled traffic in 48 hours." The company is also rolling out efficiency improvements so GPT‑5.6 Sol consumes less quota per task (roughly 10% more effective usage), and disclosed the platform has crossed 6 million active users. Corroborated by Android Authority and Dataconomy.
