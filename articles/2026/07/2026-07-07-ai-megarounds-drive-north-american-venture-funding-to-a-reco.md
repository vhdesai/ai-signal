# AI megarounds drive North American venture funding to a record $392B in H1 2026

North American startup investment hit an all-time high of $392 billion in the first half of 2026, powered by late-stage AI megadeals rather than rising deal counts, per Crunchbase data. Q2 alone totaled $137.2 billion, with a single Anthropic financing — $65 billion at a $965 billion post-money valuation — accounting for roughly half the quarter. The period also produced record exits, including SpaceX’s largest-ever IPO and its record-setting acquisition of Cursor. The concentration underscores how capital is consolidating around a handful of frontier players. 🔗 https://news.crunchbase.com/venture/na-startup-funding-ma-shattered-records-ai-q2-2026/
