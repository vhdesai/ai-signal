# Samsung Guides to Record Q2 Profit on AI-Memory Demand — But Shares Fall

Samsung guided to a record ~89.4 trillion won ($58.4B) Q2 operating profit — up roughly 19× year over year on AI-fueled demand — yet shares fell ~7% as the rally had already priced the beat in. Analysts flagged concern that AI-infrastructure capex may not sustain the memory-price surge, echoing broader questions about the buildout's durability.
