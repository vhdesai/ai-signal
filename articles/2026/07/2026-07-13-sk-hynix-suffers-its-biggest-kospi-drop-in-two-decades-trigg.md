# SK Hynix suffers its biggest Kospi drop in ~two decades, triggering a circuit breaker

SK Hynix shares fell more than 12–15% in Seoul, dragging the Kospi into a steep selloff and triggering a circuit breaker, as investors "sold the news" after the AI-memory maker's record ~$26B Nasdaq debut and amid concerns that its HBM4 shipment ramp is lagging Q2 expectations. The move signals rising volatility across the AI memory complex.
