# Cerebras accelerates European expansion with 200MW of AI compute

Cerebras Systems said it will bring its first European data-center capacity online by the end of 2026, with build-out across France and the Nordics, scaling to 200 MW by the end of 2027. A portion of that capacity is earmarked for OpenAI workloads under the companies' existing partnership. The expansion moves Cerebras' high-speed inference infrastructure closer to European users and reflects rising demand for sovereign, low-latency compute in the region.
