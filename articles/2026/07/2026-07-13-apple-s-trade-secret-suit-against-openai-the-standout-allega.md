# Apple’s trade-secret suit against OpenAI: the standout allegations

TechCrunch broke down the most striking claims in Apple’s 41-page trade-secret complaint against OpenAI, filed Friday, which alleges a coordinated effort to extract confidential information from current and former Apple employees. One cited internal message reads, “LOL, I found out I can access the [network storage], so funny.” Apple characterizes the alleged conduct as “normalized and exemplified by leadership,” escalating a high-profile talent-and-IP fight between the two companies.
