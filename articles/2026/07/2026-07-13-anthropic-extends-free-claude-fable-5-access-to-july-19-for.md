# Anthropic extends free Claude Fable 5 access to July 19 for Pro/Max/Team subscribers

Anthropic pushed the free-access deadline for its flagship “Mythos-class” Fable 5 model from July 12 to July 19 and raised Claude Code usage limits. The repeated extensions are widely read as competitive positioning against OpenAI’s newly available GPT-5.6 family.
