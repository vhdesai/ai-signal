# OpenAI launches GPT-5.6 family — Sol, Terra, and Luna — to general availability

OpenAI moved its GPT-5.6 series to general availability after a restricted late-June preview held pending a U.S. cybersecurity review. The lineup spans three tiers — flagship Sol, mid-range Terra, and low-cost Luna — priced at $5/$30, $2.50/$15, and $1/$6 per million input/output tokens, with the pitch emphasizing token efficiency and cost-per-result over peak capability. OpenAI claims Sol edges Anthropic's Fable 5 on the Artificial Analysis Coding Agent Index (80 vs 77.2) at roughly a third less cost, adds an “ultra” mode that coordinates parallel agents, and calls it its strongest cybersecurity model to date.
