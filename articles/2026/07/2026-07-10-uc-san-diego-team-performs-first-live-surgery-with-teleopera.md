# UC San Diego team performs first live surgery with teleoperated humanoid robots

A UC San Diego team reportedly used two off-the-shelf Unitree G1 humanoid robots, teleoperated by surgeons, to remove gallbladders from live pigs. The procedure required constant human oversight and remains far from clinical deployment, but it is an early marker of physical AI moving into high-precision medical tasks.
