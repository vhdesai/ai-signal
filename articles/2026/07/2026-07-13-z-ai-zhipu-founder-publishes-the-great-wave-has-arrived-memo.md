# Z.ai (Zhipu) founder publishes "The Great Wave Has Arrived" memo, reaffirms open frontier AI and GLM-5.2

Zhipu (Z.ai) founder and Tsinghua professor Tang Jie published an internal memo arguing frontier AI must stay "as open and widely accessible as possible" — "real safety comes from broad participation, sharing, and oversight, not from technological barriers" — and reaffirming GLM-5.2 under an MIT open-source license, committing Zhipu to two years without short-term app monetization.
