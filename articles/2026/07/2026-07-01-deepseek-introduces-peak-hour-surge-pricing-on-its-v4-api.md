# DeepSeek introduces peak-hour surge pricing on its V4 API

DeepSeek told API customers it will double V4 model prices during two Beijing peak windows (9am–noon and 2–6pm) when the full V4 launches in mid-July — its first use of time-based pricing; off-peak rates are unchanged. For deepseek-v4-pro, peak output roughly doubles to about $1.70 per million tokens, still far below U.S. frontier APIs. The move, framed as "better distribution of resources," signals that even the price-war leader is hitting GPU-capacity limits. It marks a subtle inflection in the era of ever-falling token prices.
