# MiniMax Plans a 2.7-Trillion-Parameter Open-Weight Model

MiniMax is developing a 2.7-trillion-parameter model — roughly six times its current M3 flagship and potentially the largest open-weight model in the world — which it plans to open-source as early as Q3. The move intensifies pricing pressure from Chinese open-weight labs on U.S. frontier margins. MiniMax is also pursuing a second listing on Shanghai's STAR Market.
