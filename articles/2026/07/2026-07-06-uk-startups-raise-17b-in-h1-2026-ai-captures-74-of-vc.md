# UK Startups Raise ~$17B in H1 2026, AI Captures 74% of VC

UK startups raised roughly $17 billion in the first half of 2026 — about double the prior year — with approximately 74% of venture capital flowing to AI-focused companies. The UK's share of European deep-tech funding nearly doubled to about 41%. The data underscores how sharply capital is concentrating into AI, raising concern that non-AI sectors are being crowded out.
