# Europe's Anduril rival Helsing raises $1.8 billion at an $18 billion valuation

German AI-defense startup Helsing said it raised $1.8 billion at an $18 billion valuation, with new and existing backers including JPMorgan Chase, Lightspeed Venture Partners, and Iconiq. The company said "investor demand significantly exceeded the available allocation, reflecting strong and growing confidence in AI-driven and software-defined defence technology," cementing its position as Europe's largest defense startup and a direct rival to Anduril.
