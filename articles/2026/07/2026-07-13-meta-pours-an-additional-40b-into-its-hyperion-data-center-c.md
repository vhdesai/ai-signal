# Meta pours an additional $40B into its Hyperion data-center campus in Louisiana

Meta is adding roughly $40 billion to its Hyperion campus in Richland Parish, taking total site investment from about $10B toward as much as $50B and lifting aggregate regional commitments past $250B. The 3,200-acre site is more than four times the size of Central Park — a vivid marker of the hyperscaler compute arms race.
