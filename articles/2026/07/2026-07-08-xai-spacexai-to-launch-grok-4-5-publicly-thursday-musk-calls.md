# xAI (SpaceXAI) to Launch Grok 4.5 Publicly Thursday; Musk Calls It “Opus-Class”

Elon Musk said Grok 4.5 will be made publicly available Thursday, July 9, positioning it as an “Opus-class” model rivaling Anthropic’s Claude while claiming faster performance, greater token efficiency, and lower operating cost. Grok 4.5 is built on xAI’s new “V9” foundation model and is the flagship from xAI, which was folded into SpaceX earlier this year and rebranded “SpaceXAI.” It follows Grok 4.3 in April and a recent private beta across SpaceX and Tesla.
