# Visual Studio adds Copilot usage tracking to curb “bill shock”

Microsoft’s July Visual Studio update introduced real-time GitHub Copilot usage tracking and configurable quota alerts, responding to developer complaints after Copilot’s June 1 shift to token-based, usage-based billing; it also added MCP-server trust validation to protect against unexpected tool changes. The update is relevant to enterprises trying to forecast and govern AI developer-tool spend as consumption-based pricing spreads. ADOPTION
