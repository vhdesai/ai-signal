# Meta signals AI compute may be overbuilt — even as it commits $50B+ to a 5GW data center

Forbes examined an apparent contradiction in Meta’s recent moves: the company committed more than $50 billion to expand a Louisiana data center to 5 gigawatts while simultaneously revealing plans to sell “excess” AI compute. The analysis frames this as a bellwether for a broader question — how much acquired AI compute is actually being used — with the absence of clear utilization metrics making it hard to separate strategic foresight from overbuilding. Wall Street cheered the announcements regardless.
