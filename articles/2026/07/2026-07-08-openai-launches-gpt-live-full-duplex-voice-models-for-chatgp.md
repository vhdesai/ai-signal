# OpenAI launches GPT-Live full-duplex voice models for ChatGPT

OpenAI released GPT-Live-1 and GPT-Live-1 mini, voice models that listen and speak simultaneously, enabling natural interruptions and live translation. The mini model replaces Advanced Voice Mode by default, while paid tiers get the larger model. For harder prompts, GPT-Live can route to GPT-5.5-class reasoning and search mid-conversation.
