# OpenAI doubles its biosecurity jailbreak bounty to $50,000 for GPT-5.6

OpenAI converted its GPT-5.5 Bio Bug Bounty into an ongoing private program and raised the top reward for a universal jailbreak of its biosafety controls from $25,000 to $50,000, starting with GPT-5.6. GPT-5.5 remains in scope through July 27, 2026; participation is invitation-only and NDA-bound. The framing matters for CISOs: OpenAI is treating frontier-model safety like recurring vulnerability management rather than a one-time system-card claim — a self-regulatory posture that also pre-empts heavier government intervention.
