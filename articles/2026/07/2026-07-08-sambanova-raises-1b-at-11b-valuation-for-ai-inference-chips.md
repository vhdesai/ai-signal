# SambaNova Raises $1B at $11B Valuation for AI Inference Chips

SambaNova raised $1 billion in a Series F led by General Atlantic at an $11 billion post-money valuation, funding its custom inference chips, systems, and cloud services. The raise lands as the broader AI trade cools — Nvidia has lagged its semiconductor peers in 2026 — and signals continued investor conviction specifically in inference-optimized silicon as the workload mix shifts from training to serving. It adds another well-capitalized challenger to Nvidia’s data-center dominance. https://money.usnews.com/investing/news/articles/2026-07-08/ai-chip-startup-sambanova-valued-at-11-billion-in-1-billion-funding-round
