# Synthetic Sciences open-sources "OpenScience," a model-agnostic research workbench

Synthetic Sciences released OpenScience, an open-source, model-agnostic AI workbench spanning machine learning, biology, physics, and chemistry research. It positions as a vendor-neutral alternative to closed research environments — a category several labs have moved into in recent days. For research teams, the model-agnostic design is the notable hook, letting groups plug in their preferred models rather than committing to a single provider.
