# OpenAI leans into families as ChatGPT pushes deeper into households

OpenAI is hiring a dedicated product manager to build ChatGPT experiences for families, caregivers, and older adults — a signal it is moving beyond individual power users toward trust-sensitive household use. TechCrunch cites Sensor Tower estimates that the share of ChatGPT users aged 35 and older rose to 31% in Q2 (from 26% a year earlier), while the 18–24 share fell to 29%. In the U.S., nearly one in four parents who own a smartphone used ChatGPT during the quarter, up from 16% a year ago.
