# Micron expands U.S. manufacturing plan to about $250B on AI-memory demand

Micron unveiled an expanded U.S. manufacturing plan worth roughly $250 billion through 2035, tied to surging demand for high-bandwidth memory used in AI accelerators. The commitment reinforces that the AI supply-chain bottleneck is shifting toward advanced memory, not just GPUs.
