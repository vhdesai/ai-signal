# Cursor builds AI agent “Sand” to rival Claude Cowork and ChatGPT Work

Cursor’s internally code-named “Sand” is a general-purpose assistant for emails, texts, spreadsheets, and documents aimed at non-developers — its first product beyond coding. Internal rollout began in late June; a public launch remains uncertain given the pending ~$60B acquisition of Cursor's parent, Anysphere.
