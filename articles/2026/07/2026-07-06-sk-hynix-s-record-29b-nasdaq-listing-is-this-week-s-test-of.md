# SK Hynix's record ~$29B Nasdaq listing is this week's test of AI investor appetite

SK Hynix's roughly $29 billion Nasdaq listing is set to begin trading around July 10 and is being cast as the week's key gauge of appetite for AI-exposed stocks. The offering — American depositary receipts representing about 2.5% of the company — would rank among the largest ever, with proceeds earmarked for new fabs and high-bandwidth-memory (HBM) packaging that feed AI accelerators. SK Hynix, a dominant HBM supplier to Nvidia and Google, has more than tripled in 2026 and recently overtook Samsung as South Korea's most valuable company. Analysts caution that the memory cycle remains notoriously volatile. URL behind paywall.
