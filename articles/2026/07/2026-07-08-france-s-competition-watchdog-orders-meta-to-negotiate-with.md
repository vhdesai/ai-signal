# France’s Competition Watchdog Orders Meta to Negotiate with News Publishers

France’s antitrust authority ordered Meta to negotiate “in good faith” with news organizations over payment for their content, following complaints filed by two publisher groups in 2025. The order adds to mounting international pressure on large platforms over compensation for journalistic and media content used in AI and advertising products.
