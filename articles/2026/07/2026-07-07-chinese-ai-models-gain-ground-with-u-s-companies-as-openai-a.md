# Chinese AI models gain ground with U.S. companies as OpenAI and Anthropic costs surge

CNBC reports that U.S. companies are increasingly routing production workloads to Chinese-built models such as DeepSeek and Z.ai, which now rival frontier U.S. systems on capability while costing materially less. The shift is being driven by rising token prices at U.S. labs as Anthropic and OpenAI push advanced-model costs higher. For enterprise buyers, model sourcing is becoming a cost-optimization decision — with real implications for U.S. lab pricing power and data-governance posture.
