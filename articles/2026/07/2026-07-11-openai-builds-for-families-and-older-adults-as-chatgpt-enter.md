# OpenAI Builds for Families and Older Adults as ChatGPT Enters the Household

OpenAI is hiring a PM for families, caregivers, and older adults. ChatGPT's 35+ cohort rose to 31% of global audience in Q2 (from 26% YoY), while 18–24 fell to 29%. The shift toward household-scale, trust-sensitive experiences responds to mounting scrutiny over how AI handles minors.
