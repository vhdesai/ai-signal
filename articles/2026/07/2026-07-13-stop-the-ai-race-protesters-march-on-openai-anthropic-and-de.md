# “Stop the AI Race” protesters march on OpenAI, Anthropic, and DeepMind

Around 200 demonstrators marched between the San Francisco offices of OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google DeepMind, calling for a pause on training new frontier models while keeping existing systems available. Organized by the group “Stop the AI Race,” the protest broadened beyond safety to encompass job losses, energy and environmental impact, and rising housing costs. It reflects mounting public friction as AI-linked layoffs accumulate.
