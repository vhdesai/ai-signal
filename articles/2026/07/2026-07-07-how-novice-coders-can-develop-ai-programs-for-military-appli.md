# How novice coders can develop AI programs for military applications

MIT Lincoln Laboratory and the U.S. Air Force-MIT AI Accelerator described a project in which novice coders used LLM chatbots to build a military AI application prototype. The important signal is less the specific tool and more the democratization of specialized software development: domain experts can now prototype AI-enabled workflows directly, shifting bottlenecks toward validation, governance, and operational safety.
