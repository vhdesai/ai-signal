# Meta launches Muse Image generator, drawing immediate backlash over user photos

Meta unveiled Muse Image, a new AI image generator built by its Meta Superintelligence Labs unit (internally code-named "Mango"), available free through the Meta AI app, Instagram Stories, and WhatsApp. The launch drew immediate criticism over how the model may use people's photos. The rollout extends Meta's consumer-AI push but reprises the data-consent tensions that have shadowed its generative products.
