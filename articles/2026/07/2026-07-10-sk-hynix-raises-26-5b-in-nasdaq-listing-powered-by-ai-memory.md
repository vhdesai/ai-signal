# SK Hynix raises $26.5B in Nasdaq listing powered by AI-memory demand

SK Hynix priced one of the largest equity deals on record, raising $26.5 billion in a Nasdaq listing driven by demand for its AI memory chips. The sale was reportedly more than seven times oversubscribed, reflecting investor appetite for high-bandwidth-memory exposure.
