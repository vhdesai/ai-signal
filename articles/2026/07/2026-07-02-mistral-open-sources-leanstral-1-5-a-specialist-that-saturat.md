# Mistral open-sources Leanstral 1.5, a specialist that "saturates" formal-math benchmarks

Mistral released Leanstral 1.5, an Apache-2.0-licensed Lean 4 proof-engineering model (119B total / ~6B active mixture-of-experts) that hits 100% on the miniF2F benchmark and solves 587 of 672 PutnamBench problems at roughly $4 per problem — versus an estimated $300+ for rival provers. Beyond mathematics it flagged five previously unknown bugs in open-source repositories, including an overflow in the Rust varinteger library. The executive signal: formal verification is becoming cheap and permissively licensed — a tailwind for high-assurance software in regulated sectors and another marker in Europe's push for sovereign, open alternatives. Widely re-covered July 3–4 by MarkTechPost and The Decoder.
