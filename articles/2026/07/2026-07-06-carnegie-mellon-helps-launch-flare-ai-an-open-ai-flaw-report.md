# Carnegie Mellon Helps Launch FLARE-AI, an Open AI-Flaw Reporting Platform

Researchers at Carnegie Mellon's Software Engineering Institute, with academic, industry, and non-profit collaborators, released FLARE-AI (Flaw Reporting for AI), an open-source platform for filing standardized, machine-readable reports of AI flaws, vulnerabilities, and incidents and routing them to developers, vendors, agencies, and incident databases. It addresses a structural gap: the AI community has lacked a formal pathway to report and coordinate cross-vendor responses to shared model weaknesses. FLARE-AI connects into the SEI CERT/CC's VINCE system, enabling CVE IDs and coordinated disclosure.
