# Apple's trade-secret suit against OpenAI becomes a test of AI talent migration

Apple's complaint alleges a former employee exploited a rare bug to download confidential files after leaving for OpenAI, tied to Apple's unreleased AI-hardware program. The case could set a template for how courts view senior technical hiring, device ambitions, and trade-secret controls in the AI talent wars.
