# MIT Cybersecurity Clinic highlights AI's changing role in public-sector cyber defense

MIT News profiled the MIT Cybersecurity Clinic, which provides free vulnerability assessments to municipalities, hospitals, and other resource-constrained organizations. The article notes that AI is changing both cyber offense and defense, with AI increasingly able to identify vulnerabilities and assist attacks while organizations still struggle with human-centered security practices. The operational lesson is that AI security readiness remains as much an organizational-capacity problem as a tooling problem.
