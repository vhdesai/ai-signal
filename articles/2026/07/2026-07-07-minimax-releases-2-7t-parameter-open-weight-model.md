# MiniMax Releases 2.7T-Parameter Open-Weight Model

MiniMax released its 2.7-trillion parameter open-weight model, the largest open model to date. The release challenges the assumption that only closed-source labs can operate at extreme scale. The model reportedly matches or exceeds GPT-5.5 on several benchmarks while being available for download and fine-tuning.
