# Grok 4.5 benchmarks show strong tool use, but high hallucination risk

SpaceXAI's Grok 4.5 entered public benchmarking as a lower-cost “Opus-class” model for coding and agentic workflows. Independent results placed it high on agentic tool use, but also reported a hallucination rate around 54% and no EU availability. It looks strongest for tool-calling workflows with verification in the loop.
