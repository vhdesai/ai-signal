# Meta launches Muse Image generator, immediately drawing consent backlash

Meta unveiled Muse Image, a new AI image generator built by its Meta Superintelligence Labs unit, available free in the Meta AI app and across Instagram Stories and WhatsApp. Alongside standard prompt-based generation and preset templates, a feature that lets users manipulate other Instagram users’ images with AI drew immediate criticism over consent and likeness rights. The launch extends Meta’s push to embed generative tools directly into its highest-traffic consumer surfaces. 🔗 https://techcrunch.com/2026/07/07/meta-rolls-out-muse-a-new-ai-image-generator/
