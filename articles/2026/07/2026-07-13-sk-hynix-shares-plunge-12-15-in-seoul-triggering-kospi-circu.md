# SK Hynix shares plunge 12–15% in Seoul, triggering Kospi circuit breaker

SK Hynix shares fell 12–15% in Seoul, dragging the Kospi into a steep selloff and triggering a circuit breaker, as investors “sold the news” after the AI-memory maker's record ~$26B Nasdaq debut and amid concerns that its HBM4 shipment ramp is lagging Q2 expectations. Rising volatility across the AI memory complex.
