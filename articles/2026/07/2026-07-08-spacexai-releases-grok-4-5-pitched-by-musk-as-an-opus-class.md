# SpaceXAI releases Grok 4.5, pitched by Musk as an “Opus-class” workhorse

Grok 4.5 is the first model from SpaceXAI since xAI’s merger into SpaceX and the company’s public listing. SpaceXAI positions it as a general-purpose workhorse for coding, clerical work, research and writing, and claims roughly twice the token efficiency of leading rivals — a direct play on rising inference costs. Published benchmarks showed competitiveness with top models but stopped just short of best-in-class, per TechCrunch.
