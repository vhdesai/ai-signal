# SambaNova raises $1B at an $11B valuation

AI chip maker SambaNova Systems raised $1 billion at an $11 billion valuation, led by General Atlantic, in a first close of its Series F, with additional investors expected in a second close. The round lands roughly five months after its SN50 chip debut and a $350M Series E, and follows reported Intel acquisition interest that valued it near $1.6B. The step-up valuation signals continued investor appetite for credible Nvidia alternatives in AI silicon.
