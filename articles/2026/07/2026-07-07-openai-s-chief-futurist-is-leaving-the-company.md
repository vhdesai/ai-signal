# OpenAI’s Chief Futurist is leaving the company

WIRED reported that Joshua Achiam, OpenAI’s Chief Futurist and a long-tenured safety-adjacent leader, is leaving the company. The departure matters because it comes during a period of heightened scrutiny around OpenAI’s model release governance, government review, and the broader tension between deployment speed and frontier safety processes.
