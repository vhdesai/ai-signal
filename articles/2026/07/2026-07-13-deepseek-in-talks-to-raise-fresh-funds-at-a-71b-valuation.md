# DeepSeek in talks to raise fresh funds at a ~$71B valuation

DeepSeek is reportedly in preliminary talks to raise at roughly a $71 billion valuation — about a $19B markup from the ~$52B post-money set in late May, when it closed its first external round (~$7B, led by Tencent and CATL). Separately, Bloomberg reported July 14 that founder Liang Wenfeng has overtaken Dario Amodei and Greg Brockman as the richest AI founder.
