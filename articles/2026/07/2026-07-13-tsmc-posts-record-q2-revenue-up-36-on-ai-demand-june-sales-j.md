# TSMC posts record Q2 revenue, up 36% on AI demand; June sales jump ~68%

TSMC reported second‑quarter revenue of NT$1.27 trillion (~$39.6B), up 36% year‑over‑year and above its own guidance, with June sales alone up 67.9%. Analysts at SemiAnalysis note the foundry is "sold out" on its N3 node — targeted by essentially every leading AI GPU and CPU this year — and estimate TSMC is tracking to over $40B in AI‑chip revenue in 2026. Full Q2 earnings land Thursday, July 16. It is the cleanest read yet that AI demand is still converting into hard revenue across the supply chain.
