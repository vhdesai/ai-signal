# Hassabis proposes a FINRA-style U.S.-led AI watchdog for frontier models

Google DeepMind CEO Demis Hassabis proposed an industry-funded, expert-staffed standards body modeled loosely on FINRA to screen frontier models before release and coordinate an industry-wide slowdown if risks escalate. One of the leading frontier labs is explicitly inviting external constraints on release processes.
