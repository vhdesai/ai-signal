# Anthropic Extends Claude Fable 5 Access to All Paid Plans Through July 12

Anthropic gave paid users five extra days of Claude Fable 5 access — now through July 12 at 11:59pm PT — after the promotional window was originally set to close July 7. Eligible subscribers can use up to 50% of their weekly limits on Fable 5 at no extra cost. After the deadline, continued use requires purchasing added Fable 5 usage limits.
