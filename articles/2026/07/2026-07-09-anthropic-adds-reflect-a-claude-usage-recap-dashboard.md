# Anthropic adds Reflect, a Claude usage-recap dashboard

Anthropic launched Reflect, a dashboard that uses Claude memory to surface a user's recurring subjects, conversation activity, peak usage times, and delegated tasks across 1-, 3-, 6-, and 12-month windows. It is limited to Free, Pro, and Max accounts on web and desktop, with Team and Enterprise excluded — a gap that raises governance questions about employees building persistent summaries of company work through personal accounts.
