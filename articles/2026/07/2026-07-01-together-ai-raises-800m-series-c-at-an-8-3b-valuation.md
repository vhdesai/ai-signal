# Together AI raises $800M Series C at an $8.3B valuation

Together AI announced an $800 million Series C at an $8.3 billion post-money valuation, more than doubling its prior mark. The round was led by Aramco Ventures, with participation from Vista Equity Partners, General Catalyst, Emergence Capital, and — notably — NVIDIA. The company runs an open-model inference and training platform (serving DeepSeek, MiniMax, and Kimi) that undercuts closed providers on cost, positioning the raise as a bet on the "neocloud" layer.
