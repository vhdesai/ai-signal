# OpenAI creates a dedicated product role for families as ChatGPT's audience ages up

OpenAI is hiring a San Francisco product manager to build ChatGPT experiences for families, caregivers, and older adults, signaling a shift from an individual-productivity tool toward household technology. TechCrunch cites Sensor Tower data showing users aged 35+ rose to 31% of ChatGPT's global base in Q2 (from 26% a year earlier) while the 18–24 share fell to 29%. The move follows multiple parental-harm lawsuits and newly added safety features (parental controls, a "Trusted Contact" alert), making it as much a trust-and-safety signal as a demographics one.
