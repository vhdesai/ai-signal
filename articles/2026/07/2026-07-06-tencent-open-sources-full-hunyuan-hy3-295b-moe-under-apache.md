# Tencent Open-Sources Full Hunyuan Hy3 (295B MoE) Under Apache-2.0

Tencent open-sourced the full Hy3 — a 295-billion-parameter Mixture-of-Experts model with 21B active parameters and a 256K context window — under a permissive Apache-2.0 license that removes regional restrictions which had blocked EU, UK, and South Korea deployments. Coverage reports it roughly halves hallucination rates versus its predecessor and runs on export-compliant Nvidia GPUs.
