# NHS to embed an AI triage tool in the NHS App for 200,000+ patients

NHS England said it will roll out an AI triage tool inside the NHS App that asks patients a series of questions and directs them to a GP appointment, pharmacy, A&E, community service or self-care advice. It will reach more than 200,000 patients over the next year and all app users by April 2028, part of a “major overhaul” of NHS technology; an initial Sussex trial cut phone waits by ~29%. Health bodies broadly welcomed it but urged priority on patient safety, confidentiality and inclusion as the service leans harder on AI.
