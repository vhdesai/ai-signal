# Meta readies its custom “Iris” AI chip for September production

Internal documents show Meta plans to begin manufacturing its custom data-center accelerator, codenamed Iris, in September as part of a four-generation MTIA roadmap scaling toward 14 GW of compute by 2027. Built with Broadcom and TSMC, it reportedly passed testing in six weeks — Meta’s most aggressive push yet to reduce reliance on Nvidia and AMD GPUs.
