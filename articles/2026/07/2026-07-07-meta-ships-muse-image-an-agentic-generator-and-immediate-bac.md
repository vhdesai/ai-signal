# Meta Ships Muse Image, an Agentic Generator — and Immediate Backlash

Meta’s Superintelligence Labs released Muse Image, a free image generator available through the Meta AI app, Instagram Stories, and WhatsApp. Like OpenAI’s GPT Image 2, it operates agentically — using tools such as code execution and web search to refine its own output. The launch immediately drew criticism over a feature that lets users manipulate other Instagram users’ public photos, reviving consent and training-data-provenance questions. https://techcrunch.com/2026/07/07/meta-rolls-out-muse-a-new-ai-image-generator/
