# SoftBank's Son says AI will need $5 trillion a year by 2040, calls bubble talk “absurd”

At SoftBank World 2026 in Tokyo, Masayoshi Son projected that building out AI will require roughly $5 trillion of investment annually by 2040 and dismissed bubble concerns as “absurd.” He forecast AI data centers consuming 3 terawatts — about 1.8x today's total global power draw — initially gas-fired before fusion, and a 2040 world of “100 trillion AI agents.” The remarks land as SoftBank's cumulative OpenAI commitment nears $60 billion, making the group a live test of whether AI returns can justify the capital expenditure.
