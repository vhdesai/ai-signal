# Majority of U.S. Workers Back an AI 'Sovereign Wealth Fund'

A Verasight survey found 69% of U.S. adults support requiring AI companies to transfer 50% of equity into a public sovereign wealth fund — a response to tech layoffs amid strong profits. Widening public unease over AI's economic distribution foreshadows growing political pressure.
