# Daily AI News Digest Monday, July 13, 2026 · 12 items · Source window: July 12–13, 2026

Meta will scale its Hyperion data-center project in Louisiana to 5 gigawatts at a cost above $50 billion, up from the earlier roughly $27 billion plan. Partner Entergy is expected to build roughly 7 GW of new generation, and aggregate Louisiana AI infrastructure commitments now exceed $250 billion. The announcement reinforces that power, siting, and grid access remain binding constraints in the AI buildout.
