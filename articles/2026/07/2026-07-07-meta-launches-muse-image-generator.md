# Meta Launches Muse Image Generator

Meta launched Muse, its next-generation image generation model, integrated directly into Instagram, WhatsApp, and the Meta AI assistant. The model features real-time generation capabilities and style-consistent multi-image output, positioning Meta to compete with Midjourney and OpenAI's image generation tools at platform scale.
