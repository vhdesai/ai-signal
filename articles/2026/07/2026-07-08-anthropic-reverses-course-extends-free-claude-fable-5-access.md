# Anthropic reverses course, extends free Claude Fable 5 access to July 12 after backlash

Anthropic had planned to move its flagship Claude Fable 5 model off standard subscriptions and onto a credit-based payment system starting July 8, but reversed the change following user backlash, extending included access for existing subscribers to July 12. Fable 5 — re-released worldwide only last week after earlier US export curbs tied to its cyber-offensive capabilities — returned to Amazon Bedrock in parallel.
