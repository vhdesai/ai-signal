# xAI Officially Rebrands as SpaceXAI, Completing Merger into SpaceX

Elon Musk's xAI has officially rebranded as SpaceXAI, completing its absorption into SpaceX following the February all-stock acquisition and June Nasdaq IPO, which left the combined company valued near $2.1 trillion. Grok and X now operate under the SpaceXAI identity. The move consolidates Musk's AI, social, and launch assets under one public ticker — a structure without precedent among frontier-AI players.
