# Meta plans a cloud business ("Meta Compute") to sell excess AI capacity

Meta is drawing up plans for a cloud venture that would sell outside customers access to its AI models and raw compute, putting it in direct competition with AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud. An internal group called Meta Compute — led by infrastructure chief Santosh Janardhan, Superintelligence Labs' Daniel Gross, and president Dina Powell McCormick — would let developers run queries against models including Meta's Muse Spark. Investors pushed Meta shares up roughly 9% on the prospect of monetizing its enormous infrastructure buildout. URL behind paywall.
