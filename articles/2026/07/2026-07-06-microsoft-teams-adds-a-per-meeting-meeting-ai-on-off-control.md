# Microsoft Teams adds a per-meeting "Meeting AI" on/off control

Microsoft is adding a per-meeting "Meeting AI" control that lets organizers and presenters switch Copilot, Facilitator, and Recap on or off during a live call, with rollout beginning in early July across Windows, macOS, mobile, and web. The feature moves AI governance from tenant-level configuration into the meeting itself, addressing privacy, sensitivity, and compliance objections.
