# OpenAI launches GPT-Live, a full-duplex voice upgrade for ChatGPT

GPT-Live-1 and GPT-Live-1 mini replace Advanced Voice Mode with an architecture that can listen and speak simultaneously, targeting more natural conversation. Rollout began globally across iOS, Android and the web, with GPT-Live-1 as the default for paid Go/Plus/Pro tiers and the mini serving free users; API access is planned. It is the third generation of ChatGPT voice in about two years and OpenAI’s clearest push to make the assistant an always-on interface rather than a text box.
