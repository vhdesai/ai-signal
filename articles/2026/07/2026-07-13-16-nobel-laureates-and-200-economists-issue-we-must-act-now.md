# 16 Nobel laureates and 200+ economists issue “We Must Act Now” statement on AI’s economic transformation

Organized by Erik Brynjolfsson, Ajay Agrawal, Anton Korinek, and Tom Cunningham, the statement warns AI could reshape the economy faster than the Industrial Revolution and urges economists, policymakers, and tech leaders to prepare now. Signatories include Nobel laureates Michael Spence and Daron Acemoglu; it was echoed July 14 in Fortune under the headline “Almost 200 economists warn of AI-driven job displacement.”
