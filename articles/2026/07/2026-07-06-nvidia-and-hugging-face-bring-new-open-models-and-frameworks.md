# NVIDIA and Hugging Face Bring New Open Models and Frameworks to LeRobot

NVIDIA and Hugging Face announced new open models and frameworks for LeRobot, aimed at lowering the cost and fragmentation barriers that slow physical-AI and robotics development for the open-source community. The release extends NVIDIA's broader open-robotics push and its bet that shared model infrastructure will accelerate the field. It was one of several NVIDIA newsroom posts on open models this week.
