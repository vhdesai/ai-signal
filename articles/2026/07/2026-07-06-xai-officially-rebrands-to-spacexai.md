# xAI Officially Rebrands to "SpaceXAI"

Elon Musk's xAI officially rebranded to "SpaceXAI" on Monday, debuting a new logo and switching its handle to @SpaceXAI. The move completes the folding of xAI — including Grok and X — into SpaceX, following SpaceX's acquisition of xAI in February and its June Nasdaq IPO.
