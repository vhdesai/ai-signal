# Mistral releases Robostral Navigate for single-camera embodied navigation

Mistral introduced Robostral Navigate, an 8B model that takes RGB images and natural-language instructions to navigate robots through physical spaces. The company says the model reaches 76.6% success on R2R-CE validation unseen while using one ordinary RGB camera and no LiDAR or depth sensor. The release is strategically relevant because it suggests robotics navigation may become cheaper and more deployable through compact, task-specific embodied models.
