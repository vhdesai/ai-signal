# Meta enters the crowded AI coding battle with Muse Spark 1.1

Meta launched Muse Spark 1.1, a multimodal reasoning and coding model aimed at agentic software engineering and enterprise workflow automation. The launch, paired with the Meta Model API, marks Meta's clearest move yet into direct developer and enterprise model distribution, competing on both capability and low per-token pricing.
