# "LLM-as-a-Verifier": Verification Proposed as a New Scaling Axis

Researchers affiliated with UC Berkeley, Stanford, and NVIDIA propose verification — judging whether a solution is correct — as a new scaling axis for LLMs. The training-free method reports state-of-the-art results on Terminal-Bench V2 (86.5%), SWE-Bench Verified (78.2%), and RoboRewardBench (87.4%), aligning with rising enterprise demand for auditable AI outputs.
