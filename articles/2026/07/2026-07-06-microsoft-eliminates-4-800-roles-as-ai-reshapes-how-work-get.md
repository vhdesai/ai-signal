# Microsoft Eliminates ~4,800 Roles as AI Reshapes How Work Gets Done

Microsoft cut about 4,800 roles — 2.1% of its global workforce — concentrated in Xbox and commercial sales. The company framed the roles as "not being replaced by AI" while acknowledging that AI is automating many everyday tasks. TechCrunch's running tally puts total 2026 tech-sector cuts at roughly 120,000, with AI the most-cited rationale.
