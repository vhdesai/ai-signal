# Anthropic ships Claude Sonnet 5 and restores Fable and Mythos after export-control review

Anthropic launched Claude Sonnet 5 and restored its Fable and Mythos frontier models, ending an 18-day suspension imposed after a June 12 U.S. export-control directive; the pause followed Amazon researchers documenting a jailbreak that made Fable 5 surface software vulnerabilities and exploitation code. Sonnet 5 scores 63.2% on SWE-bench Pro and 80.4% on Terminal-Bench 2.1 (up from 58.1% / 67.0% for Sonnet 4.6) at the same $3 / $15 per-million-token price, with introductory rates through August 31. Anthropic patched the bypass with a new safety classifier that reroutes flagged prompts to the older Opus 4.8. Alongside the release, Anthropic, Amazon, Microsoft, and Google proposed a shared four-criteria framework for scoring the severity of model security breaches. G MODEL
