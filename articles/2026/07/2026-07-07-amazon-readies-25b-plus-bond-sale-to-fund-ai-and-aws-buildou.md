# Amazon Readies $25B-Plus Bond Sale to Fund AI and AWS Buildout

Amazon is preparing to raise at least $25 billion through an eight-part bond offering to fund AI compute capacity across AWS. It follows a roughly $42 billion multi-part offering in March. Amazon, Alphabet, Microsoft and Meta are together expected to invest more than $700 billion in AI infrastructure this year.
