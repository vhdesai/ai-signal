# Hassabis calls for a U.S.-led global AI watchdog able to pause dangerous frontier models

In a manifesto (“A Framework for Frontier AI and the Dawning of a New Age”) and an exclusive Axios interview, Google DeepMind CEO Demis Hassabis proposed an industry-funded, expert-staffed standards body — modeled loosely on FINRA — empowered to screen frontier models before release and coordinate an industry-wide slowdown if risks escalate. He argued the U.S. should lead “given its economic and technical standing,” and warned of a “precious window” before AGI, which he believes is only a few years away. Notably, the proposal invites external constraints on his own company.
