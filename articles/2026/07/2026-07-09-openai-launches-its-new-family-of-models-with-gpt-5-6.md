# OpenAI launches its new family of models with GPT-5.6

OpenAI launched the GPT-5.6 family in three tiers: Sol, Terra, and Luna, with Sol positioned as the flagship workhorse for coding, cybersecurity, and agentic knowledge work. The release is directly framed against Anthropic and other frontier providers on token efficiency and cost, while ChatGPT Work extends the model family into enterprise document, spreadsheet, presentation, and workflow execution.
