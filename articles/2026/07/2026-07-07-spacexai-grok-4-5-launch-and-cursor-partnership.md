# SpaceXAI Grok 4.5 Launch and Cursor Partnership

SpaceXAI plans to release Grok 4.5 on the same day as OpenAI's GPT-5.6 launch. The company also announced a partnership with Cursor to integrate Grok models directly into the AI coding assistant. The simultaneous launches signal intensifying competition in the frontier model space.
