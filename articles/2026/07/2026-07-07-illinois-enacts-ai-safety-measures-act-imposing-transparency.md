# Illinois enacts AI Safety Measures Act, imposing transparency duties on frontier developers

Illinois Governor JB Pritzker signed Senate Bill 315, the AI Safety Measures Act, into law, positioning the state as a leader in frontier-model regulation. The act requires developers of the most advanced, costly models — the tier occupied by OpenAI and Anthropic — to publish transparency frameworks covering safety-standard compliance, model capabilities, catastrophic-risk potential and incident-response procedures whenever they deploy a new or substantially modified frontier model. It adds to a widening patchwork of US state-level AI governance that enterprises must now track. 🔗 https://www.jurist.org/news/2026/07/illinois-governor-enacts-legislation-holding-ai-companies-accountable-for-safety-risks/
