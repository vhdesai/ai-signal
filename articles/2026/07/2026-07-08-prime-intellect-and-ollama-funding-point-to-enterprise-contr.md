# Prime Intellect and Ollama funding point to enterprise-controlled AI stacks

Prime Intellect raised $130 million at a $1 billion valuation for enterprise agent training and evaluation, while Ollama raised $65 million after growing to nearly 9 million monthly developers. Together, the rounds point to demand for local/open execution, proprietary workflow tuning, and reduced dependence on frontier-lab APIs.
