# AI-Chip Maker SambaNova Raises $1B at $11B Valuation; JPMorganChase Named Inference Partner

SambaNova closed a first tranche of a $1B Series F at an $11B post-money valuation, led by General Atlantic with Intel Capital, BlackRock, Qatar Investment Authority, and T. Rowe Price participating. Alongside the raise, SambaNova said JPMorganChase selected its SN40L and SN50 systems as an inference-infrastructure partner for secure, on-premises AI — a notable enterprise validation for non-GPU inference. The round marks a sharp reset from an earlier ~$1.6B Intel acquisition discussion.
