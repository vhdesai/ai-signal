# Big Tech's AI debt load doubles to $350B as infrastructure spending strains balance sheets

Alphabet, Amazon, Meta, Microsoft, and Oracle have collectively added about $350 billion in debt over five years to fund data-center buildouts, according to Bloomberg data cited by Yahoo Finance. Investors gave Amazon's $25 billion issuance a cool reception, and S&P cut Oracle to its lowest investment-grade rating over AI spending.
