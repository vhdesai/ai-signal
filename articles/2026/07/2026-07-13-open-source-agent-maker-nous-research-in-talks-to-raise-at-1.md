# Open-source agent maker Nous Research in talks to raise at $1.5B valuation

Nous Research, the startup behind the open-source Hermes agent, is finalizing a round led by Robot Ventures with participation from Union Square Ventures at a $1.5 billion valuation, according to three sources. The company is raising at least $75 million and drew strong investor interest. It had previously raised roughly $70 million from backers including Paradigm and Balaji Srinivasan — evidence that open-weight agent players continue to command premium valuations.
