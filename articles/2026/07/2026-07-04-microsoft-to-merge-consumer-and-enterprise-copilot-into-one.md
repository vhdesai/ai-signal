# Microsoft to Merge Consumer and Enterprise Copilot Into One App

Microsoft told its ~11,000-person Copilot team the product must "earn the right to exist," alongside plans to merge every consumer and enterprise Copilot into a single unified app by August, cut under-adopted features (Copilot Podcasts and Labs), and introduce a paid tier of background "AutoPilot" agents. The blunt memo signals a consolidation push and a pivot toward monetizable, agentic capabilities amid scrutiny of paid-seat adoption rates.
