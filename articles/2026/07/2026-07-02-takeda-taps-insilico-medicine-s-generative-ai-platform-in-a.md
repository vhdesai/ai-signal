# Takeda taps Insilico Medicine's generative-AI platform in a deal worth up to ~$600M

Insilico Medicine entered a strategic collaboration with Takeda to apply its end-to-end Pharma.AI platform to discover and optimize drug candidates across Takeda's core therapeutic areas. Insilico receives about $60 million upfront and near-term, with milestones potentially bringing the total to roughly $600 million; Insilico leads AI-driven discovery while Takeda advances selected molecules through clinical validation. Another data point that generative AI is moving from pilot to production in pharma R&D economics.
