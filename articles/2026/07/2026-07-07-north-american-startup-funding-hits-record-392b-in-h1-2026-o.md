# North American Startup Funding Hits Record $392B in H1 2026, Overwhelmingly AI-Driven

U.S. and Canadian startups raised a record $392 billion in H1 2026, with Q2 alone at $137.2 billion. About 80% of Q2 dollars went to AI-focused companies, and a single $65 billion Anthropic raise (at ~$965B post-money) accounted for roughly half the quarter's AI total. The pattern is extreme capital concentration into a few frontier-scale players rather than broadening.
