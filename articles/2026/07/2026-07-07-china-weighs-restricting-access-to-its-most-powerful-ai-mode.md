# China Weighs Restricting Access to Its Most Powerful AI Models

Chinese authorities have held talks with Alibaba, ByteDance, and Z.ai about restricting foreign access to their most advanced models — including some not yet released — per Reuters. Options reportedly sketched include barring public release or limiting models to domestic use, a sharp reversal of the open-weight strategy that drove Chinese models' global adoption despite trailing top U.S. systems by roughly seven months. For enterprises that adopted Chinese open models to cut costs, this introduces real supply-and-continuity risk.
