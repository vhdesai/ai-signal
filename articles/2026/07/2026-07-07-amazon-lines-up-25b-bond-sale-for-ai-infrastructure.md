# Amazon Lines Up $25B Bond Sale for AI Infrastructure

Amazon is preparing a $25 billion bond sale to fund AI infrastructure expansion, including data centers and custom chip development. The offering would be one of the largest corporate bond sales of 2026 and follows similar capital raises by Alphabet and Meta.
