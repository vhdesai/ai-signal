# Google's deepfake detector system used to debunk McConnell hoax pic

Google's SynthID watermarking system was used by Snopes to debunk a viral AI-generated image purporting to show Senator Mitch McConnell in medical distress. This is a meaningful real-world validation of invisible AI watermarking, while also highlighting ecosystem gaps: provenance systems only work at scale when major model providers participate.
