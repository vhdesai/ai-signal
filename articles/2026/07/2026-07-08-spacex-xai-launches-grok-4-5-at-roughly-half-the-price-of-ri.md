# SpaceX/xAI launches Grok 4.5 at roughly half the price of rivals

Elon Musk's SpaceX released Grok 4.5, its first model trained specifically for coding and agents and the first product of its ~$60B Cursor acquisition, priced at $2/$6 per million tokens. xAI's headline claim is token efficiency — about 15,954 output tokens per SWE-bench Pro task versus roughly 67,020 for Anthropic's Opus 4.8, a 4.2x gap. First-day independent benchmarks on July 10 ranked it #4 on Artificial Analysis's Intelligence Index with the board's best agentic tool-use score, but flagged a hallucination-rate jump to 54% and a context-window cut to 500K (down from 1M on Grok 4.3); it is not available in the EU at launch.
