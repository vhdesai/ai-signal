# Pritzker signs Illinois bill aimed at artificial intelligence accountability

Illinois Governor JB Pritzker signed SB 315, an AI accountability law requiring transparency frameworks, third-party compliance audits, rapid incident reporting, and penalties for covered AI developers. The notable policy signal is that both OpenAI and Anthropic supported the bill, suggesting frontier labs are increasingly shaping state-level regulation while federal policy remains fragmented.
