# Senator Markey unveils an AI accountability package targeting deployment and infrastructure

Senator Ed Markey introduced a broad AI accountability agenda targeting data-center certification, automated hiring, workplace surveillance, healthcare AI, and child safety. One forthcoming bill would require AI data-center owners to obtain FCC certification weighing air and water quality, energy costs, and grid reliability before construction begins.
