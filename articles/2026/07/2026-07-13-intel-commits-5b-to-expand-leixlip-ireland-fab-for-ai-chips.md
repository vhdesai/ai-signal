# Intel commits €5B to expand Leixlip, Ireland fab for AI chips

Intel announced €5B to expand Fab 34 for Xeon 6 and next-gen processors on Intel 3 node. Follows April's $14.2B Apollo stake buyback. Total Ireland investment >€30B since 1989 — a foundry-strategy statement as much as a capacity one.
