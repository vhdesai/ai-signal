# Cursor is building "Sand," a general-purpose AI agent for non-developers

reporting details Cursor's internally code-named "Sand," a general-purpose assistant for emails, texts, spreadsheets, and documents aimed at non-developers — Cursor's first product beyond coding, positioning it against Claude Cowork and ChatGPT Work. Internal rollout began in late June; a public launch remains uncertain given the pending ~$60B acquisition of Cursor's parent, Anysphere. Note: secondary reporting on a scoop that originally broke July 9.
