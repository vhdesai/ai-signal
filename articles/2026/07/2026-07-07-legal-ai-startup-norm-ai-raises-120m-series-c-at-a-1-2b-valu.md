# Legal-AI startup Norm Ai raises $120M Series C at a $1.2B valuation

Norm Ai raised $120 million in a Series C led by Khosla Ventures, with Blackstone, Bain Capital Ventures, Craft Ventures, Coatue, Vanguard, New York Life and TIAA participating, valuing the company at $1.2 billion and lifting total funding past $260 million in under three years. Norm Ai builds AI agents that embed legal and regulatory reasoning into automated compliance workflows and runs Norm Law, an AI-native firm supervised by senior attorneys. The raise signals continued enterprise appetite for agentic systems targeting regulated, high-liability work. 🔗 https://ventureburn.com/norm-ai-raises-120-million-series-c/
