# Meta scraps Instagram "Muse Image" likeness tool days after launch amid consent backlash

Meta discontinued a Meta AI feature — built on its new Muse Image model — that let users tag any adult's public Instagram account and generate images using that person's likeness, notifying subjects only through an opt-out. The company confirmed the withdrawal Friday after backlash from SAG-AFTRA, Creative Artists Agency, and Public Citizen, which called it an "egregious invasion" of privacy. Meta Superintelligence Labs head Alexandr Wang told Axios the team is still weighing "next steps," leaving open whether a consent-first version returns. The episode crystallizes the industry's unresolved opt-in-versus-opt-out fight over likeness rights.
