# Microsoft is reportedly preparing another major Copilot revamp — targeted for August — that would merge its consumer…

Microsoft is reportedly preparing another major Copilot revamp — targeted for August — that would merge its consumer and enterprise apps into a single experience. The update is said to add background "AutoPilot" agents that handle routine work such as scheduling and email summaries without direct prompting. If accurate, it signals a continued push toward proactive, agentic assistants over chat-first interfaces.
