# AWS Launches "Forward Deployed Engineering" Org Backed by $1B Amazon Investment

AWS stood up a Forward Deployed Engineering organization, backed by $1B, to embed engineers directly with customers and co-develop agentic AI systems in days. The approach mirrors Palantir's model. Channel partners called it a "force multiplier" rather than competition for systems-integrator work.
