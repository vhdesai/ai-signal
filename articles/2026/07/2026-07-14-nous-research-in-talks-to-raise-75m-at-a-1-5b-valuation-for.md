# Nous Research in talks to raise $75M at a $1.5B valuation for its open-source Hermes agent

Nous Research is reportedly negotiating a round of at least $75M at a $1.5B valuation. Its open-source Hermes AI agent has amassed 214,000+ GitHub stars and roughly 40,000 forks and runs locally on a laptop, VPS, or cloud — a sign investors are treating open agent frameworks as platforms rather than hobby projects.
