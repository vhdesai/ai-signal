# Foxconn's Q2 revenue jumps ~40% on AI-server demand; June sets a record, full-year target raised

Foxconn (Hon Hai) reported Q2 revenue of T$2.513 trillion (~$78.71B), up 39.8% year-on-year and above the LSEG SmartEstimate, with June alone up 52.1% to a record T$821.8B on its Nvidia AI-server division. The world's largest contract manufacturer raised its 2026 revenue target to ~NT$11 trillion (~$350.5B, +36%) and expects AI-server-rack shipments to more than double this year, while again cautioning about a "volatile" global political and economic environment. It is the cleanest signal that the hardware buildout is still accelerating — even as scrutiny grows over whether hyperscaler capex and softening per-token pricing will ultimately pay off.
