# Singapore's dConstruct raises a $125M Series A for GPS-denied robotics

dConstruct Technologies closed a US$125M Series A — one of the largest for a Singapore robotics firm — as the state-backed RoboNexus accelerator wrapped its first cohort. Its d.ASH suite pairs 3D scanning with autonomy software for robots operating indoors and underground where GPS fails, with disclosed clients including SBS Transit, Japan's JR East group and SoftBank Robotics Singapore. It reflects Asia's "embodied AI" wager on deployment and adoption rather than foundation-model building.
