# Chai Discovery raises $400M at a $3.8B valuation for AI drug design

OpenAI-backed Chai Discovery closed a $400 million round led by Index Ventures at a $3.8 billion valuation — roughly tripling its $1.3 billion December 2025 mark in seven months. The company positions itself as “AI infrastructure for pharmaceuticals,” claiming ~20% antibody-design hit rates versus below 0.1% for traditional methods, and on July 13 added Novartis to existing engagements with Pfizer and Eli Lilly. The round is another vote of investor conviction in vertical, applied AI rather than general-purpose models. URL behind paywall. FUNDING
