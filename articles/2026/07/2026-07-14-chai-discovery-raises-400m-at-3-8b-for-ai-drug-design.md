# Chai Discovery raises $400M at $3.8B for AI drug design

OpenAI-backed Chai Discovery closed a $400 million round led by Index at a $3.8 billion valuation, adding Novartis alongside Pfizer and Lilly engagements. The raise is another vote for vertical, applied AI over general-purpose model companies.
