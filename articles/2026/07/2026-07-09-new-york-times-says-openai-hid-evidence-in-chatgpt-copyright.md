# New York Times says OpenAI hid evidence in ChatGPT copyright trial

The New York Times and The Daily News filed a sanctions motion alleging OpenAI concealed internal tools and datasets relevant to detecting copyrighted content in ChatGPT outputs. The case raises direct enterprise risk questions: if courts establish broader evidence of regurgitation or misleading discovery conduct, downstream users may face greater uncertainty around AI-generated content provenance and indemnity.
