# TetraMem and SK hynix Demonstrate Memristor-Based In-Memory Computing SoC

TetraMem and SK hynix published a joint paper in Advanced Intelligent Systems demonstrating analog in-memory computing that performs neural-network operations directly within memory, attacking the data-movement bottleneck capping energy efficiency in conventional AI accelerators. Memory-centric architectures are an emerging path to materially lower energy per operation.
