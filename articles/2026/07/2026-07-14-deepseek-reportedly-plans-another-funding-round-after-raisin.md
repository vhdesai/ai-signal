# DeepSeek reportedly plans another funding round after raising $7.4 billion

The Information reports that DeepSeek is plotting another funding round only weeks after raising $7.4 billion. Details are behind the publication's paywall, but the timing signals continuing capital intensity among Chinese frontier-model companies despite geopolitical and chip-supply constraints. The story also reinforces that leading Chinese AI firms are still trying to scale through private capital rather than relying only on state or platform backing.
