# The Motion Picture Association sent ByteDance a cease-and-desist over its Seedance AI video generator, calling it…

The Motion Picture Association sent ByteDance a cease-and-desist over its Seedance AI video generator, calling it “systemic infringement.” Even so, ByteDance continues expanding in the US — a Santa Monica demo, ~100 US job postings, a Cannes presence, and talks to fund AI films — while industry figures reportedly tolerate the tool on a “don't ask, don't tell” basis. The story underscores the widening gap between studios' public and private stances on generative video.
