# Anthropic extends free Claude Fable 5 access and brings Cowork to mobile and web

Anthropic extended complimentary access to Claude Fable 5 across all paid plans through 11:59 PM PT on July 12, letting subscribers use its latest model at no additional cost until they reach plan usage limits. In parallel, the company began rolling out Claude Cowork — its agentic work environment — on mobile and web, initially for Max subscribers. The moves follow Anthropic’s restoration of Fable 5 and Mythos 5 after a government export-control pause, and read as an aggressive retention play against OpenAI’s GPT‑5.6 rollout.
