# Waze rolls out new Gemini-powered AI features, including Motorcycle and "Less Chatty" modes

Google's Waze introduced a set of AI-powered features led by a Motorcycle mode that accounts for two-wheeler-specific shortcuts and hazards (potholes, speed bumps, narrow bridges) and a "Less Chatty" mode that trims voice guidance. The update also adds AI route personalization based on prior trips, deepening Waze's shift onto Gemini and mirroring recent Google Maps changes.
