# New Filings Sharpen Apple's Trade-Secrets Lawsuit Against OpenAI

Apple's 41-page complaint alleges a former employee exploited a "rare" bug to download confidential files after leaving for OpenAI. Apple frames it as coordinated IP extraction, quoting casual internal messages about unauthorized access. The dispute escalates the AI talent wars ahead of OpenAI's expected confidential IPO filing.
