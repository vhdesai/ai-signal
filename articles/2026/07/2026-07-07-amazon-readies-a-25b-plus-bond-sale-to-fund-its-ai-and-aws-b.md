# Amazon readies a $25B-plus bond sale to fund its AI and AWS buildout

Amazon is preparing to raise at least $25 billion through a US dollar-denominated, eight-part bond offering — one of 2026’s largest corporate debt sales — primarily to fund AI compute capacity across AWS, according to Bloomberg. The longest tranche matures in 2066. It follows a roughly $42 billion multi-part offering in March and mirrors debt-funded expansion by peers; Amazon, Alphabet, Microsoft and Meta are together expected to invest more than $700 billion in AI infrastructure this year. The scale reflects how capital-intensive frontier compute has become. 🔗 https://thetechportal.com/2026/07/07/amazon-could-raise-25bn-through-bond-sale-to-fund-ai-expansion/
