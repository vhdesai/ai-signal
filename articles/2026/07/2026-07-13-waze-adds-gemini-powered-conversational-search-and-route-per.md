# Waze adds Gemini-powered conversational search and route personalization

Google-owned Waze added AI-powered destination search, conversational road-update reporting, personalized route ranking, and a motorcycle mode. Some of the features are powered by Gemini and are rolling out globally or to beta users, depending on the capability. The update is a practical example of Gemini being embedded into high-frequency consumer workflows where utility depends on context and low-friction interaction.
