# Apple Adds Siri Pace and Expressivity Controls in the iOS 27 Beta

TechCrunch reported that Apple's latest iOS 27 beta lets users customize the new AI-powered Siri's speaking pace and expressivity — a further refinement of the revamped Apple Intelligence Siri rolling out after WWDC 2026. The controls give users finer command over how the assistant sounds. Single-source report; treat the specifics as preliminary until the beta ships more widely.
