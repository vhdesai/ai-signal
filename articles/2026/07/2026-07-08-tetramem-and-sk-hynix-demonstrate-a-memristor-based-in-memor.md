# TetraMem and SK hynix Demonstrate a Memristor-Based In-Memory Computing SoC

TetraMem and SK hynix published a joint paper, “A Memristor-based In-Memory Computing SoC with Efficient Depthwise Convolution,” in Advanced Intelligent Systems, where it was selected as the cover feature. The work demonstrates analog in-memory computing that performs neural-network operations directly within memory, attacking the data-movement bottleneck that caps energy efficiency in conventional AI accelerators.
