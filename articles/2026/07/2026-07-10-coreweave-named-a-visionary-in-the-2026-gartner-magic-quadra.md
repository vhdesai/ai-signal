# CoreWeave named a Visionary in the 2026 Gartner Magic Quadrant for Cloud AI Infrastructure

CoreWeave was named a Visionary in Gartner's 2026 Magic Quadrant for Cloud AI Infrastructure, a category distinct from general-purpose cloud. The recognition formalizes a procurement trend already visible in enterprise AI: purpose-built GPU and AI infrastructure clouds are being evaluated as a separate category for training and inference, not merely as hyperscaler alternatives.
