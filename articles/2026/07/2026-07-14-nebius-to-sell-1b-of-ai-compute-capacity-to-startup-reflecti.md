# Nebius to sell $1B of AI compute capacity to startup Reflection

Neocloud provider Nebius agreed to sell roughly $1 billion in AI capacity to AI startup Reflection — another sign of the surging market for dedicated GPU capacity contracts among AI-native companies that cannot or will not build their own data centers.
