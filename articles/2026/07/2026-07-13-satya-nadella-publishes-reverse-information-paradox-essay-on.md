# Satya Nadella publishes “Reverse Information Paradox” essay on the hidden cost of enterprise AI

In an X essay that drew roughly 10M views, Microsoft’s CEO argued enterprises using AI “pay twice” — once in cash, and once in the proprietary know-how models absorb from prompts, tool use, and corrections — and laid out a “five Cs” framework. It is a notable strategic framing of the data-leverage question every large AI adopter now faces.
