# Meta ships low-cost Muse Spark 1.1, undercutting OpenAI and Anthropic on price

Meta’s Muse Spark 1.1 — now in public preview via the Meta Model API — claims parity with Claude Opus 4.8, Gemini 3.1 Pro and GPT-5.5 across agentic, coding and computer-use benchmarks including SWE-bench Verified, Terminal-bench and OSWorld. At $1.25/$4.25 per million input/output tokens, its output pricing runs roughly 86% below GPT-5.5 and more than 90% below Claude Opus 4.8. Analysts expect it to reset enterprise price expectations and strengthen CIOs’ hand in multi-model procurement, even where the model itself isn’t adopted.
