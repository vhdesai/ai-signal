# Prime Intellect raises $130M Series A to help enterprises build their own AI agents

Prime Intellect raised $130 million at a $1 billion valuation for a platform that helps enterprises train and evaluate their own AI agents. The funding reflects growing demand for AI sovereignty: companies want agent systems tailored to proprietary workflows without depending entirely on frontier labs for model access, policy continuity, or cost control.
