# PixVerse raises $439 million as valuation passes $2 billion

Singapore-based video-generation startup PixVerse closed a Series C extension, bringing the round to $439 million and pushing valuation above $2 billion. Investors include Alibaba, Lollapalooza Capital, Ivy Capital, Grand Mount Capital, Eastern Bell Capital, Mirae Asset, BlueFocus, CloudAlpha, iGlobe Partners, and OCBC's Lion X Ventures. The company claims 150 million registered users and is expanding world-model, enterprise, and global go-to-market efforts, signaling continued investor appetite for non-U.S. video-generation platforms.
