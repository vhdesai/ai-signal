# ChatGPT Work launches after U.S. government approval

OpenAI’s ChatGPT Work rollout, tied to the GPT-5.6 family, positions ChatGPT as a broader enterprise work platform rather than a standalone assistant. The move brings agentic execution, multi-step workflow handling, and tool use into a consolidated business product, intensifying competition with Microsoft Copilot, Claude, and Google’s enterprise AI stack.
