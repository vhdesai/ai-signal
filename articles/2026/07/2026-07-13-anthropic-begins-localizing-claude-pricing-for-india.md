# Anthropic begins localizing Claude pricing for India

Anthropic has started showing local pricing for Claude in India — its largest market outside the U.S. — on its website and mobile apps. Notably, it has not yet enabled UPI, India’s dominant instant-payment rail, unlike OpenAI, which rolled out rupee pricing with UPI support last year. The move signals intensifying competition to convert usage into paid subscribers in the world’s most populous market.
