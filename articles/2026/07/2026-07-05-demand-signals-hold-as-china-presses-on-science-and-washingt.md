# Demand signals hold as China presses on science and Washington drafts model-release rules

Over the US Independence Day weekend, hard demand signals outweighed new product news. Foxconn’s Q2 results reaffirmed that AI-server orders are still accelerating — even as Nvidia’s flat 2026 share price shows investors questioning how durable, and how monetizable, the buildout is. No frontier model shipped in the last 24 hours; momentum instead came from China (Alibaba’s AI-driven materials-science discovery, a $2.8B Kling AI raise, and DeepSeek-V4 reaching a major cloud) and from Washington, where a voluntary framework for frontier-model releases moved closer to announcement.
