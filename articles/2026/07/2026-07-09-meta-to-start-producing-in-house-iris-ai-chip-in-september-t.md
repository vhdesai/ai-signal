# Meta to Start Producing In-House "Iris" AI Chip in September, Targeting 14 GW by 2027

An internal memo reviewed by Reuters shows Meta plans to begin manufacturing its "Iris" data-center chip in September. Meta aims for ~7 GW of compute this year and 14 GW in 2027. The move reduces Nvidia dependence and deepens Meta's vertical integration.
