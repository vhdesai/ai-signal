# Popular open source AI developer tool Ollama raises $65M, grows to nearly 9M users

Ollama raised $65 million after growing to nearly 9 million monthly developers and broad Fortune 500 usage. The company's momentum reflects a structural enterprise shift toward local and open-weight model execution, particularly where inference cost, data control, and developer ergonomics make closed frontier APIs less attractive.
