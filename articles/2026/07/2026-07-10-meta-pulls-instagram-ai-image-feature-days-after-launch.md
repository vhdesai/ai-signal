# Meta pulls Instagram AI image feature days after launch

Meta removed a newly launched feature that let users generate images by @-mentioning public Instagram accounts — part of the "Muse Image" generator from Meta Superintelligence Labs — after immediate backlash over privacy. Because the tool did not notify people when their public photos were referenced, critics including a Hollywood union pushed back hard, and Meta conceded the feature "missed the mark." The reversal underscores how quickly consumer-AI features can collide with consent and likeness concerns.
