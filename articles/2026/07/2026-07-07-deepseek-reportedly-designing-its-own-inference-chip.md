# DeepSeek reportedly designing its own inference chip

DeepSeek is reportedly moving to design its own inference chip — hiring engineers and talking to manufacturers — according to a Reuters report, part of a push to reduce reliance on Huawei and NVIDIA silicon. Any resulting chip would likely stay inside China given export controls, but DeepSeek's cost-focused track record makes the effort credible.
