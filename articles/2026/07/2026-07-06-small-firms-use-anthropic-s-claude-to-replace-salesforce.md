# Small Firms Use Anthropic's Claude to Replace Salesforce

Greenleaf Management, an Atlanta-based real estate firm with ~55 employees, saved around $100,000 annually by replacing Salesforce's CRM with a custom application built using AI tools from Replit and Claude Code. The new app costs about $300/month to maintain. While large enterprises are unlikely to vibe-code their way out of SaaS contracts, smaller firms with tighter margins are finding that AI-built replacements can be viable — a trend that could pressure mid-market SaaS pricing.
