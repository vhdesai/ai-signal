# Meta enters the agentic-coding market with Muse Spark 1.1

Meta Superintelligence Labs publicly launched Muse Spark 1.1, a multimodal model built for agentic coding — multistep reasoning, workflow management, and feature deployment in enterprise systems. Meta is late relative to OpenAI and Anthropic but is competing on price, at $1.25 per million input tokens and $4.25 per million output tokens (Reuters, via TechCrunch). The move rounds out a two-day stretch in which all three U.S. leaders shipped, leaving Google’s Gemini 3 as the oldest standing frontier flagship.
