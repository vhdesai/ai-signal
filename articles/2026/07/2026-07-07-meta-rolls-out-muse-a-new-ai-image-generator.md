# Meta rolls out Muse, a new AI image generator

TechCrunch reported that Meta’s Muse Image rollout quickly drew privacy concerns because users can generate images involving public Instagram profiles without the subject being notified, with opt-out controls rather than opt-in consent. The case is a useful governance warning for consumer-facing AI launches: distribution advantage can become regulatory exposure if default data-use assumptions feel too broad.
