# Apple sues OpenAI over alleged AI-hardware trade-secret theft

Apple filed suit against OpenAI in the Northern District of California, alleging misappropriation of trade secrets tied to its unreleased AI-hardware program. The complaint cites 400+ former Apple employees at OpenAI and names former Apple hardware leaders. The case could set precedent for AI talent migration and IP protection.
