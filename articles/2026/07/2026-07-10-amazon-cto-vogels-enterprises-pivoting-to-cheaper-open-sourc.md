# Amazon CTO Vogels: Enterprises Pivoting to Cheaper Open-Source Models

At the UN's AI for Good summit, Amazon CTO Werner Vogels said enterprises alarmed by runaway token bills are shifting from the largest frontier models toward cheaper open-source alternatives. He cited companies burning through AI budgets in months. The comments dovetail with Meta's same-day price-war push.
