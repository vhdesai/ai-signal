# OpenAI completes public rollout of the GPT-5.6 family (Sol, Terra, Luna)

OpenAI moved GPT-5.6 to full public availability on July 10, ending a roughly two-week delay tied to a U.S. government review. The lineup spans three tiers — Sol (flagship, $5/$30 per 1M input/output tokens), Terra (balanced, $2.50/$15, about 2× cheaper than GPT-5.5), and Luna (fast, $1/$6). OpenAI positions Sol as its strongest model to date, citing gains in coding, biology, and cybersecurity.
