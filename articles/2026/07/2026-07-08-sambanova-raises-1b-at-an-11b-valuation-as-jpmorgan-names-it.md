# SambaNova raises $1B at an $11B valuation as JPMorgan names it an inference partner

AI chip maker SambaNova said it raised $1 billion in a late-stage round led by General Atlantic at an $11 billion post-money valuation, with T. Rowe Price, Capital Group, BlackRock-managed funds, Intel Capital and the Qatar Investment Authority participating. The company, which builds custom chips and systems optimized for inference, said JPMorgan Chase has selected it as an inference infrastructure partner and will deploy its SN40 and SN50 systems. The deal underscores investor conviction in dedicated inference silicon as an alternative to GPUs. 🔗 https://money.usnews.com/investing/news/articles/2026-07-08/ai-chip-startup-sambanova-valued-at-11-billion-in-1-billion-funding-round
