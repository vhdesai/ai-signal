# Apple Escalates Trade-Secret Suit Against OpenAI; Next Siri to Run on Google Gemini

Apple's federal complaint accuses OpenAI of trade-secret theft, alleging former Apple employees carried confidential data into OpenAI's consumer-device program. Apple separately confirmed next-gen Siri will run on Gemini — reversing the 2024 ChatGPT-in-iOS deal and signaling that Big Tech AI alliances are giving way to direct competition over hardware, talent, and IP.
