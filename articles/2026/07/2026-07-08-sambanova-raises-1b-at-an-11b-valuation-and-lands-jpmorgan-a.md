# SambaNova raises $1B at an $11B valuation and lands JPMorgan as an inference partner

SambaNova raised $1 billion in a Series F led by General Atlantic at an $11 billion post-money valuation. JPMorgan Chase selected it as an inference-infrastructure partner and will deploy SN40 and SN50 systems, reflecting investor conviction that inference is where near-term AI economics are hardening.
