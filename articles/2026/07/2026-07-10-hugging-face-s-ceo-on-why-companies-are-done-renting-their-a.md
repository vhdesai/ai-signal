# Hugging Face's CEO on why companies are done renting their AI

Hugging Face CEO Clem Delangue argued that enterprises are moving from renting intelligence via closed APIs toward owning and customizing open models. The strategic takeaway is that open-source AI is becoming a cost-control, auditability, and resilience strategy for large companies, especially as reliance on a small number of frontier vendors creates pricing and continuity risk.
