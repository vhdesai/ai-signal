# In the studios' copyright case against image generator Midjourney, a judge had allowed Midjourney to seek information…

In the studios' copyright case against image generator Midjourney, a judge had allowed Midjourney to seek information about the studios' consumer-facing AI tools; Midjourney has now appealed, pressing the court to compel broader disclosure of how the studios use AI internally for tasks like storyboarding and ideation. Midjourney's counsel argued that if the studios are themselves building image models trained on third-party copyrighted work, that is directly relevant. The dispute could set discovery precedent for AI-and-copyright litigation.
