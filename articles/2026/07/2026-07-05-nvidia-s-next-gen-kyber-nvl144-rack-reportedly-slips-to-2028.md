# Nvidia's Next-Gen "Kyber" NVL144 Rack Reportedly Slips to 2028

Research firm SemiAnalysis reports that Nvidia's Kyber NVL144 rack — designed to house 2027's Rubin Ultra chips — has been pushed back more than 12 months to 2028 due to manufacturing problems with a key circuit board. The delay adds to a string of reported setbacks and raises questions about whether Nvidia's aggressive annual product cadence is colliding with production limits. A slip at the high end could open a rare technical window for AMD and Google's TPUs, and complicates 2027 capacity planning for buyers.
