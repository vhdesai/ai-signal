# OpenAI moves GPT-5.6 (Sol, Terra, Luna) to broad availability

OpenAI began rolling GPT-5.6 out globally after a limited, government-vetted preview. Flagship Sol leads on coding, cybersecurity, and science; Terra is the mid-tier default; Luna is the lowest-cost option — and Altman told CNBC Sol is "54% more token efficient" on agentic coding. API pricing runs $5/$30 (Sol), $2.50/$15 (Terra) and $1/$6 (Luna) per million input/output tokens; the ~50% mid-tier cost cut reframes the enterprise buying decision around price-performance rather than headline capability.
