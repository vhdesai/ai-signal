# SK Hynix raises $26.5B in blockbuster US listing

SK Hynix priced one of the largest equity deals on record, raising $26.5B in a Nasdaq listing driven by demand for its AI memory chips; the sale was reportedly more than seven times oversubscribed. A key high-bandwidth-memory supplier to Nvidia, the company has seen its shares climb more than 220% this year. The debut is a direct read on investor appetite for AI memory-supply exposure amid a broader chip-stock rotation.
