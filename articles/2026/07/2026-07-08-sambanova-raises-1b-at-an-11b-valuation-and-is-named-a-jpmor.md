# SambaNova raises $1B at an $11B valuation and is named a JPMorgan inference partner

AI chip maker SambaNova raised $1B at an $11B valuation in a first close of its Series F led by General Atlantic, roughly five months after a $350M Series E accompanying its SN50 inference chip. Alongside the raise, SambaNova said JPMorgan Chase selected it as an “inference-infrastructure partner” to power secure, on-premises inference — a signal, CEO Rodrigo Liang argued, that large banks want heterogeneous infrastructure rather than full cloud dependence. Liang was noncommittal on staying independent after prior Intel acquisition interest (~$1.6B), citing continued inbound. The round underscores intensifying capital and consolidation in the inference-hardware market.
