# OpenAI to open GPT-5.6 to the public July 9 after clearing federal pre-release review

OpenAI said it will make all three GPT-5.6 variants — Sol, Luna and Terra — publicly available on Thursday, July 9, and is expanding preview access globally now. The models were limited in late June to a small group of government-approved partners under a Trump administration order requiring frontier developers to submit their most capable systems for review 30 days before release. OpenAI said it complied to reach public availability sooner but does not believe such government-access processes “should become the long-term default.” The flagship Sol tier is positioned around advanced reasoning and cybersecurity-relevant capability. 🔗 https://www.engadget.com/2210308/openai-rolls-out-gpt5-6-july-9/
