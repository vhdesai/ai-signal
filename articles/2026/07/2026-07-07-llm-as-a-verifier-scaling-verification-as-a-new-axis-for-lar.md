# LLM-as-a-Verifier: Scaling Verification as a New Axis for Large Language Models

Researchers from Stanford, UC Berkeley, and NVIDIA introduced a training-free verification framework that uses the distribution of scoring-token logits rather than a single discrete judge score. The work argues that verification can become a distinct scaling axis for agentic systems, with reported gains across software, robotics, medical-agent, and terminal benchmarks.
