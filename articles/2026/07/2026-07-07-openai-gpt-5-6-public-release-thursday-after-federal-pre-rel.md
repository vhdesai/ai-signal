# OpenAI GPT-5.6 Public Release Thursday After Federal Pre-Release Review

OpenAI will publicly release GPT-5.6 on Thursday after completing the first-ever federal pre-release review of a frontier AI model. The review was conducted under the new voluntary framework established by the AI Safety Institute. The model reportedly shows significant improvements in reasoning, code generation, and multimodal capabilities over GPT-5.5.
