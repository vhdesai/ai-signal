# Slack’s Slackbot can now pull your CRM data, generate charts, and send DocuSigns

Salesforce connected Slackbot to CRM records, Tableau, Data Cloud, Agentforce, and third-party apps through MCP servers, allowing users to query and trigger enterprise workflows from chat. This is a practical example of collaboration platforms becoming agent orchestration layers, with governance implications around permissions, auditability, and shared visibility into agent actions.
