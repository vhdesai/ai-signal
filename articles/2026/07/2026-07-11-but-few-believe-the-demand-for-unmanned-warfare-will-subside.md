# But few believe the demand for unmanned warfare will subside any time soon

But few believe the demand for unmanned warfare will subside any time soon. And in Helsing's drone factory, the outline of the next potential conflict in Europe is already taking shape. In a large storage room, dozens of boxes are stacked several feet high, many containing drones awaiting shipment, not to Ukraine, but to a battle-ready German brigade stationed in Lithuania, near the Belarus border.
