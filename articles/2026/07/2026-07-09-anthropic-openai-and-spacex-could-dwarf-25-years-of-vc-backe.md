# Anthropic, OpenAI, and SpaceX could dwarf 25 years of VC-backed tech exits

TechCrunch, citing the Q2 2026 PitchBook-NVCA Venture Monitor, reported that expected exit value from SpaceX, Anthropic, and OpenAI could exceed the aggregate value of U.S. VC-backed tech exits since 2000. AI capital formation is becoming extraordinarily concentrated across a small number of firms, investors, and infrastructure chokepoints.
