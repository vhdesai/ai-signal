# ECB asks euro-area banks for plans to address AI-driven cyber threats

The European Central Bank has asked euro-area banks to submit plans for addressing AI-driven cybersecurity threats, as banking watchdogs warned that generative AI is amplifying the sophistication and scale of attacks on financial institutions. The request signals that AI risk is moving from principle to supervisory expectation in regulated sectors. Financial-services firms should expect AI-specific operational-resilience requirements to harden over the coming year. URL behind paywall.
