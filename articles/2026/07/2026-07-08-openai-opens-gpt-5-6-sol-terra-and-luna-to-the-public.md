# OpenAI opens GPT‑5.6 — Sol, Terra and Luna — to the public

OpenAI said it would make its GPT‑5.6 family broadly available starting Thursday, roughly two weeks after limiting the June debut to a “small group of trusted partners.” The lineup spans Sol — the new flagship, billed as OpenAI’s “strongest model yet” and more capable in coding, biology and cybersecurity — alongside Terra, a mid-range tier priced at half of GPT‑5.5, and Luna, a fast, low-cost option. The broad release lands the same week rival Anthropic restored access to Claude Fable 5 and Mythos 5, intensifying a race in which both labs have filed confidential IPO paperwork at valuations approaching $1 trillion.
