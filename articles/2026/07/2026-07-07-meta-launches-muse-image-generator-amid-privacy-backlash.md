# Meta Launches Muse Image Generator Amid Privacy Backlash

Meta's Superintelligence Labs unveiled Muse Image (internally “Mango”), a free image generator available in the Meta AI app, Instagram Stories, and WhatsApp. A feature that lets users manipulate other people's public Instagram photos with AI drew immediate privacy criticism and alarm in Hollywood over likeness rights. The rollout is a fresh test of Meta's willingness to trade user-consent optics for distribution scale across its social platforms.
