# A study of more than 26,000 Chinese students found that AI users completed homework faster and posted higher short-term…

A study of more than 26,000 Chinese students found that AI users completed homework faster and posted higher short-term marks, yet performed up to 24% worse on exams. Researchers estimate the full learning cost takes roughly two years to surface, raising questions about how generative tools reshape skill acquisition. The finding adds empirical weight to the ongoing debate over AI's role in education.
